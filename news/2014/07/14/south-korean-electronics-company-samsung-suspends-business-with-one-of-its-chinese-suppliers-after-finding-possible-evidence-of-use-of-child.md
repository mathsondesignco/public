# South Korean electronics company Samsung suspends business with one of its Chinese suppliers after finding possible evidence of use of child labour in one of its plants. 

Samsung said on Monday it had temporarily suspended business with one of its suppliers in China after finding &quot;evidence&quot; of possible illegal child labour at the plant.
