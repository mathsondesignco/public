### [2014-07-29](/news/2014/07/29/index.md)

# The Ukrainian Culture Ministry decides not to issue distribution licenses for two Russian films: "The White Guard", and a film about Ukrainan-born Soviet wrestler Ivan Poddubny, as they "show contempt for the Ukrainian language, people and the state," and "some facts are distorted to benefit Russia."
