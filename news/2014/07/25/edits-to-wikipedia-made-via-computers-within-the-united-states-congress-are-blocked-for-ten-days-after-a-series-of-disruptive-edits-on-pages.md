# Edits to Wikipedia made via computers within the United States Congress are blocked for ten days after a series of disruptive edits on pages created by congressional staff members. 

Recent edits accuse Donald Rumsfeld of being an alien lizard and Cuba of faking the moon landings. By Alex Hern
