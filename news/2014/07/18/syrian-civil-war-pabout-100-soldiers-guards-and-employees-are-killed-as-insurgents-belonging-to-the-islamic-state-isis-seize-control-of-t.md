### [2014-07-18](/news/2014/07/18/index.md)

# Syrian Civil War:About 100 soldiers, guards and employees are killed as insurgents belonging to the Islamic State (ISIS) seize control of the Shaer gas field in Palmyra, central Syria. The United Kingdom-based Syrian Observatory for Human Rights said 115 people were killed in total. 

About 100 soldiers, guards and employees have been killed as jihadis seized a gas field in central Syria, activists say.
