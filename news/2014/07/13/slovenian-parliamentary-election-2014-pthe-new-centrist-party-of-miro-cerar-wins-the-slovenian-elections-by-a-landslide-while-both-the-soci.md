### [2014-07-13](/news/2014/07/13/index.md)

# Slovenian parliamentary election, 2014:The new centrist Party of Miro Cerar wins the Slovenian elections by a landslide while both the Social Democrats and the centre-right parties suffer their worst-ever defeat, and the winner of the  previous elections, Positive Slovenia, does not even pass the electoral threshold.
