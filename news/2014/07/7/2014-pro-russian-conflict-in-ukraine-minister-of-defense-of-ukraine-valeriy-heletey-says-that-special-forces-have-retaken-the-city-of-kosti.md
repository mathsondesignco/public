### [2014-07-7](/news/2014/07/7/index.md)

# 2014 pro-Russian conflict in Ukraine. Minister of Defense of Ukraine Valeriy Heletey says that special forces have retaken the city of Kostiantynivka, Donetsk from armed insurgents. 

Three bridges on roads leading into Donetsk are destroyed ahead of an expected blockade of the city by government forces.
