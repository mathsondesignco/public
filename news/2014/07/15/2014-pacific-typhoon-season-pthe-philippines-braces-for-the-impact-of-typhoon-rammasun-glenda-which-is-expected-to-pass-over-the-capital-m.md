# 2014 Pacific typhoon season:The Philippines braces for the impact of Typhoon Rammasun (Glenda) which is expected to pass over the capital Manila causing flooding. Thousands have fled to evacuation centres and ports and airports in the country are closed 

Thousands are preparing to evacuate Philippines coastal areas as Typhoon Rammasun approaches.
