# Sinking of the MV "Sewol". The Prime Minister of South Korea Jung Hong-won announces his immediate resignation following growing criticism of the response to the sinking of the MV "Sewol". 

Prime Minister Chung Hong-won announces resignation amid criticism of government handling of the Sewol ferry disaster.
