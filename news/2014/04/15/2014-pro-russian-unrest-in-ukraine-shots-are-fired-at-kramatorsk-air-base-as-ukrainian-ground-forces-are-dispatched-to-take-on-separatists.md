### [2014-04-15](/news/2014/04/15/index.md)

# 2014 pro-Russian unrest in Ukraine: Shots are fired at Kramatorsk air base as Ukrainian Ground Forces are dispatched to take on separatists. Unconfirmed reports state that 4 pro-Russian militants have been killed and 2 people have been injured. Some reports claim up to 10 deaths. 

Ukrainian troops defy pro-Russian separatists and take control of an air base in the eastern city of Kramatorsk.
