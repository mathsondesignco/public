# 2014 Garda phone recordings controversy:. Taoiseach Enda Kenny tells the Dil the Prison Service recorded conversations between inmates and their solicitors. 

The Irish Prison Service has confirmed that 139 prisoners had their telephone conversations with solicitors recorded while in custody.
