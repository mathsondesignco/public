# A United States court overturns the conviction of Andrew Auernheimer (known as weev), who was sentenced to 41-months in prison in 2010 for hacking the AT&T website, stealing millions of iPad user e-mails. 

A federal appeals court has ruled that the venue for Auernheimer&#039;s prosecution wasn&#039;t appropriate
