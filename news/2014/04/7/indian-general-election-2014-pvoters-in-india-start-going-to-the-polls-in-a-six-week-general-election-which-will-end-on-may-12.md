### [2014-04-7](/news/2014/04/7/index.md)

# Indian general election, 2014:Voters in India start going to the polls in a six-week General Election which will end on May 12. 

Voting began at 7:00 am in six constituencies in tea-growing and insurgency-wracked areas of the northeast.
