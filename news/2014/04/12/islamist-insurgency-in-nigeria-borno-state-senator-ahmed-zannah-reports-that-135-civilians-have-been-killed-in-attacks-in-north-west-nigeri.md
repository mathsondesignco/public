# Islamist insurgency in Nigeria: Borno State Senator Ahmed Zannah reports that 135 civilians have been killed in attacks in north west Nigeria since Wednesday. 

A senator from north-east Nigeria tells the BBC that 135 civilians have been killed by gunmen there since Wednesday.
