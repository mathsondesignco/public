### [2014-02-4](/news/2014/02/4/index.md)

# The Scottish Parliament votes overwhelmingly (105-18) in favor of a bill legalizing same-sex marriage in Scotland. 

A bill which allows same-sex weddings to take place in Scotland is passed by MSPs in the Scottish Parliament.
