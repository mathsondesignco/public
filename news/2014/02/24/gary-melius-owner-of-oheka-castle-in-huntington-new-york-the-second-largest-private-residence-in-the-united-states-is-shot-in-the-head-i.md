### [2014-02-24](/news/2014/02/24/index.md)

# Gary Melius, owner of Oheka Castle in Huntington, New York, the second largest private residence in the United States, is shot in the head in the castle parking lot. The gunman is still at large. 

A prominent New York real estate developer and socialite told investigators he has no idea who shot him at his expansive Long Island estate, police said.
