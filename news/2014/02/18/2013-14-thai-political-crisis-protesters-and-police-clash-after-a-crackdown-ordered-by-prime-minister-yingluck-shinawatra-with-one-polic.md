# 2013-14 Thai political crisis:: Protesters and police clash after a crackdown ordered by Prime Minister Yingluck Shinawatra with one policeman shot dead and dozens wounded. 

At least four people are killed and dozens injured in violence that erupted as Thai police began clearing protest sites in the capital, Bangkok.
