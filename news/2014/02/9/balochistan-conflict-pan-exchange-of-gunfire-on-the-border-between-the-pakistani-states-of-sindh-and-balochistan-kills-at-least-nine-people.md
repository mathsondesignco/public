### [2014-02-9](/news/2014/02/9/index.md)

# Balochistan conflict:An exchange of gunfire on the border between the Pakistani states of Sindh and Balochistan kills at least nine people. 

Dead include six civilians, clash took place between BRA members and tribesmen.
