# Winter storms of 2013-14 in the United Kingdom:Floods in the United Kingdom continue to worsen as the Met Office issues severe rain weather warnings for southwest England. 

The government urges the insurance industry to deal with flood-related claims as quickly as possible, as more rain hits ground already saturated with water.
