### [2014-02-22](/news/2014/02/22/index.md)

# Euromaidan:The Ukrainian parliament votes to free Yulia Tymoshenko, the former prime minister jailed for more than two years for what supporters say are politically tainted charges. She is released later in the day. 

 President Yanukovych signs agreement as parliament passes law that could free Yulia Tymoshenko
