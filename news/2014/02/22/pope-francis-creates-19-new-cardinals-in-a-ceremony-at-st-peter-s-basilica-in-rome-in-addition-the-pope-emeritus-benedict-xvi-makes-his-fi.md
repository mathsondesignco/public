### [2014-02-22](/news/2014/02/22/index.md)

# Pope Francis creates 19 new cardinals in a ceremony at St Peter's Basilica in Rome. In addition, the Pope emeritus Benedict XVI makes his first public appearance at the Vatican since his resignation in 2013. 

The leader of the Roman Catholic Church in England and Wales is created a cardinal at a ceremony in Rome.
