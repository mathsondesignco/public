### [2014-02-19](/news/2014/02/19/index.md)

# Euromaidan. After protesters had resisted the police, opposition and President Viktor Yanukovych hold talks and reach a truce. The toll of riots over the past couple of days is at least 25 dead and 241 injured. 

Ukrainian police are continuing to storm the main anti-government protest camp in Kiev, after at least 18 people die in the worst violence in weeks.
