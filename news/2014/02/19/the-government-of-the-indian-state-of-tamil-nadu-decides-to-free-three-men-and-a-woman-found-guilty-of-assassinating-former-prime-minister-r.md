### [2014-02-19](/news/2014/02/19/index.md)

# The government of the Indian state of Tamil Nadu decides to free three men and a woman found guilty of assassinating former Prime Minister Rajiv Gandhi in 1991. 

The government in the southern Indian state of Tamil Nadu decides to free seven people convicted of plotting the 1991 assassination of former PM Rajiv Gandhi.
