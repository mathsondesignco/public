# War in Afghanistan (2001-present):Afghanistan releases 65 prisoners from the Parwan Detention Facility despite concerns by the United States that the men were responsible for attacks on NATO and Afghan forces. 

Afghan authorities release 65 detainees from the Bagram detention centre, a move the US condemns as &quot;deeply regrettable&quot;.
