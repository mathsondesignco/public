### [2014-03-30](/news/2014/03/30/index.md)

# French municipal elections, 2014:. Voters in France go to the polls for a second round of voting in municipal elections. The ruling Socialist Party generally performs badly, but their candidate in the Paris mayoral election Anne Hidalgo is set to become the first female mayor of that city.
