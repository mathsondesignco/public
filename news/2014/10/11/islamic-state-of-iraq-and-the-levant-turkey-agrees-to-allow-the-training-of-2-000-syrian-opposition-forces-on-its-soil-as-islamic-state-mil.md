### [2014-10-11](/news/2014/10/11/index.md)

# Islamic State of Iraq and the Levant: Turkey agrees to allow the training of 2,000 Syrian opposition forces on its soil as Islamic State militants approach to within 1 mile of the centre of the border city of Kobani. 

Turkey agrees to train Syrian opposition fighters in Turkey by American and Turkish special forces, as Islamic State militants draw closer to capturing the Syrian city of Kobani.
