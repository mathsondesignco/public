### [2014-10-7](/news/2014/10/7/index.md)

# A major search operation is underway for the ''Sunrise-689'', a Vietnamese vessel carrying 18 crew members and 5,226 tonnes of oil which lost both radio and radar contact on October 3 40 minutes after its departure from Singapore. 

Hunt on for vessel sailing from Singapore to Quang Tri port in Vietnam.
