### [2014-10-1](/news/2014/10/1/index.md)

# The Egyptian government confiscates all copies of a daily print of "Al-Masry Al-Youm", one of the country's largest private newspapers, in order to censor an article, days after President Abdel Fattah el-Sisi vowed in a U.S. television interview that there was "no limitation on freedom of expression in Egypt."
