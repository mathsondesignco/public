### [2014-10-31](/news/2014/10/31/index.md)

# A Maine judge imposes limited restrictions, but rejects state officials' bid to quarantine nurse Kaci Hickox, who  returned to the United States last week after treating Ebola patients in Sierra Leone. 

Maine health officials have asked a court to limit the movements of nurse Kaci Hickox, who defied a voluntary quarantine for medical workers who have treated Ebola patients.
