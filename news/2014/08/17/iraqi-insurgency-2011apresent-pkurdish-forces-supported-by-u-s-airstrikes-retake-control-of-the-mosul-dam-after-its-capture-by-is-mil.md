# Iraqi insurgency (2011-present):Kurdish forces, supported by U.S. airstrikes, retake control of the Mosul Dam after its capture by IS militants. 

Kurdish forces in northern Iraq are largely in control of the Mosul dam previously held by Islamic State militants, Kurdish officials say.
