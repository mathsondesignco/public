# The murder conviction of David Eastman for allegedly killing former Australian Federal Police Assistant Commissioner Colin Winchester is quashed after Eastman has served a 19-year prison sentence. 

David Eastman&#039;s conviction has been quashed and he could soon walk from prison a free man after serving more than 19 years of a life sentence.
