# Yemeni forces kill at least seven al-Qaeda militants in the Wadi Hadramout region. 

Yemeni security forces killed seven suspected al Qaeda militants on Thursday when they tried to attack an army facility in the volatile eastern region of Wadi Hadramout, where the government is perceived to be weak.
