### [2014-08-24](/news/2014/08/24/index.md)

# Syrian Civil War: After more than 18 months of requests, Turkey comes under heavy international pressure to secure its borders to the influx of foreign fighters, oil, and weapons crossing into Syria to fight for ISIS. ISIS fighters have been moving towards the Turkey-Syria border using armored cars looted from Iraqi military bases. 

Islamic State fighters mass on Syria&#x27;s north-western frontier as it seeks to reopen its main artery for foreign fighters
