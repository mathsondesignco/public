### [2014-08-2](/news/2014/08/2/index.md)

# At least 8 killed, hundreds missing in massive landslides fueling flash flood fears downstream, as entire Itani and Kagu settlements in Ramche VDC-5 are swept away and about 1 km Araniko Highway is destroyed. 

At least eight persons were killed and scores went missing when a massive landslide blocked Sunkoshi River at Mankha VDC on Saturday morning
