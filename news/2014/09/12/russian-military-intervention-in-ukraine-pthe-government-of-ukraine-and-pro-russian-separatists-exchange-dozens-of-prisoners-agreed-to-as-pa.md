# Russian military intervention in Ukraine:The Government of Ukraine and pro-Russian separatists exchange dozens of prisoners agreed to as part of a ceasefire a week ago. Both sides still hold thousands of prisoners. 

Ukrainian leader said the crisis has &ldquo;no military solution&rdquo; and urged rebels to seek political accord.
