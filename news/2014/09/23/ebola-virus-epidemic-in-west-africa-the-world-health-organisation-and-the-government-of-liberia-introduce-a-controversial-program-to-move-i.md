### [2014-09-23](/news/2014/09/23/index.md)

# Ebola virus epidemic in West Africa. The World Health Organisation and the Government of Liberia introduce a controversial program to move infected people out of their homes and into "de facto" shelters. 

WHO-backed plan for Ebola is seen as intermediate step between treatment centers and home care.
