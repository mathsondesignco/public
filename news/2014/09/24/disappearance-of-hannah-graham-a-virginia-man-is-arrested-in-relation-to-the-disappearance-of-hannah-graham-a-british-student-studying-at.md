# Disappearance of Hannah Graham: A Virginia man is arrested in relation to the disappearance of Hannah Graham, a British student studying at the University of Virginia. 

A Virginia man is arrested in the abduction of a British-born student who disappeared more than 10 days ago and remains missing.
