### [2014-12-30](/news/2014/12/30/index.md)

# Luise Rainer, German-born American actress ("The Great Ziegfeld", "The Good Earth"), Academy Award winner (1936, 1937), dies of pneumonia at the age of 104. 

Actress Luise Rainer, who became the first winner of consecutive Oscars in the 1930s, dies aged 104.
