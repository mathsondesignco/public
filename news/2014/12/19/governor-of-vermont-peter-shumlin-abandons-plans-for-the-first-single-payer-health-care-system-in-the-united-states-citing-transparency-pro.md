### [2014-12-19](/news/2014/12/19/index.md)

# Governor of Vermont Peter Shumlin abandons plans for the first single-payer health care system in the United States, citing transparency problems and heavy tax increases. 

Tax hikes to state businesses and individual would have been necessary to pay for the single-payer program.
