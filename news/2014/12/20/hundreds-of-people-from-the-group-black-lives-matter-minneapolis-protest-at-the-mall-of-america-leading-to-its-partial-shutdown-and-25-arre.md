### [2014-12-20](/news/2014/12/20/index.md)

# Hundreds of people from the group Black Lives Matter Minneapolis protest at the Mall of America, leading to its partial shutdown and 25 arrests. 

The group Black Lives Matter Minneapolis organized the gathering in the mall&#39;s rotunda.
