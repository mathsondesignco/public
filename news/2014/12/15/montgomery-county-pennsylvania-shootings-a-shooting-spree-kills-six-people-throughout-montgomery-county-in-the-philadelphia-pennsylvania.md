# Montgomery County, Pennsylvania shootings: A shooting spree kills six people throughout Montgomery County in the Philadelphia, Pennsylvania suburbs. The suspect, Bradley William Stone, remains at large. 

Investigators are releasing new details about the murder spree in Montgomery County that left six people dead and one person wounded.
