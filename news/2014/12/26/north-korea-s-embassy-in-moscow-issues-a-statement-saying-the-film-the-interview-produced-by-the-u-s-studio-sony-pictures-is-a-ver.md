### [2014-12-26](/news/2014/12/26/index.md)

# North Korea's embassy in Moscow issues a statement, saying: "The film ''The Interview'', produced by the U.S. studio Sony Pictures, is a very dangerous film product that justifies and promotes terrorism." 

Sony will decide on a possible release in the country in the new year, but the movie has already popped up on Russian torrents
