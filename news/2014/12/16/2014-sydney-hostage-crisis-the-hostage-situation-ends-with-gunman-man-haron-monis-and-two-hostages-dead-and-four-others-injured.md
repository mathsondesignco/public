# 2014 Sydney hostage crisis. The hostage situation ends with gunman Man Haron Monis and two hostages dead and four others injured. 

Two hostages and the gunman at the centre of a 16-hour siege at a cafe in Sydney&#039;s CBD have died, police confirm.
