### [2014-12-16](/news/2014/12/16/index.md)

# The court jury finds Apple Inc. in a nine-year lawsuit not guilty of violating antitrust laws and deleting music content for security reasons. 

After just a few hours of deliberation, jury nukes a 10-year antitrust case.
