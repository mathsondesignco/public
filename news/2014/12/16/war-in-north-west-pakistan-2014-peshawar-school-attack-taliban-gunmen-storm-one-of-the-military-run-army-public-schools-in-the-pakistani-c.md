### [2014-12-16](/news/2014/12/16/index.md)

# War in North-West Pakistan: 2014 Peshawar school attack: Taliban gunmen storm one of the military-run Army Public Schools in the Pakistani city of Peshawar, killing at least 141, including 132 children and nine employees, with most of five hundred students evacuated. 

The Pakistan Taliban launch their deadliest attack ever, leaving 132 children and nine adults dead at a school in Peshawar.
