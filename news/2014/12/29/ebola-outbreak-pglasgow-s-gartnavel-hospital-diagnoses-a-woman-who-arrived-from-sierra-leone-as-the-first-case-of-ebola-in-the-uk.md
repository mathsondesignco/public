# Ebola outbreak:Glasgow's Gartnavel Hospital diagnoses a woman, who arrived from Sierra Leone, as the first case of Ebola in the UK. 

A healthcare worker who has just returned from West Africa is diagnosed with Ebola and is being treated in hospital in Glasgow.
