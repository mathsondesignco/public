### [2014-12-25](/news/2014/12/25/index.md)

# Somali civil war. Al-Shabab militants attack AMISOM's headquarters in Mogadishu, leaving three peacekeeping soldiers and a civilian contractor dead. Five of the attackers are also killed. 

At least three African Union soldiers, five fighters and one civilian contractor killed in attack in Mogadishu.
