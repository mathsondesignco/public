### [2014-12-12](/news/2014/12/12/index.md)

# Sony Pictures Entertainment hack. Sony Pictures Entertainment reportedly shuts production on several sets because of computer network issues regarding employee disbursement of pay following a massive computer hack. 

Sony Pictures has abandoned film shoots because hackers have crippled its computer network, The Times of London reported Saturday.
