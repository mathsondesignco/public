### [2014-06-10](/news/2014/06/10/index.md)

# Five American soldiers, an Afghan soldier, and an interpreter in the Zabul province of Afghanistan are killed when a NATO air strike accidentally targeted them. 

Five American soldiers have been killed accidentally by their own side in southern Afghanistan, US military sources confirm.
