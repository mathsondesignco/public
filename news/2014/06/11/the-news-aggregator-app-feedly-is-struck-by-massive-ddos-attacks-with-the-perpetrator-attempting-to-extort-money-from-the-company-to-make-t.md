### [2014-06-11](/news/2014/06/11/index.md)

# The news aggregator app Feedly is struck by massive DDoS attacks, with the perpetrator attempting to extort money from the company to make the cyber-attacks stop. 

The note-taking app Evernote and news aggregator Feedly are being targeted by cyber-attacks, which is preventing some members from accessing their services.
