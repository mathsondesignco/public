# Al-Qaeda insurgency in Yemen:The entire country of Yemen remains without electricity for a second day after insurgents sabotaged power lines linking the capital Sana'a and the contested Ma'rib Governorate. 

Assault on lines linking Sanaa with al-Qaeda stronghold Marib trips national grid, cutting off power to all provinces.
