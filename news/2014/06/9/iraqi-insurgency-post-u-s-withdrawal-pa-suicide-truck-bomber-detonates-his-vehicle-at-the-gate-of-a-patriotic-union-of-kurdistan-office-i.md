### [2014-06-9](/news/2014/06/9/index.md)

# Iraqi insurgency (post-U.S. withdrawal):A suicide truck bomber detonates his vehicle at the gate of a Patriotic Union of Kurdistan office in Tuz Khormato, Iraq, a second truck bomb detonated as people were gathering around the first one. Over 15 are killed and at least 110 injured.
