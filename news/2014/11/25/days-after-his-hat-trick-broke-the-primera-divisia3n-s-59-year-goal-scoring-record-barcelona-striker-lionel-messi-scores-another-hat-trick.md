### [2014-11-25](/news/2014/11/25/index.md)

# Days after his hat-trick broke the Primera Division's 59-year goal-scoring record, Barcelona striker Lionel Messi scores another hat-trick in Nicosia to become the UEFA Champions League all-time top scorer. 

Lionel Messi became the all-time leading scorer in the Champions League after scoring twice for Barcelona in their Group F clash against Apoel Nicosia
