# Killing of Sergeant Almog Shiloni: A Palestinian man fatally stabs an Israeli soldier in Tel Aviv.2014 Alon Shvut stabbing attack: In a separate attack at a bus stop near Alon Shvut in the West Bank, a Palestinian man runs over three people with a vehicle then stabs all three, resulting in the death of 26-year-old occupational therapist Dahlia Lemkus. 

Girlfriend of Almog Shiloni, who was stabbed to death in Tel Aviv attack, says they were set to get married, calls him &#8216;an angel&#8217;
