### [2014-11-21](/news/2014/11/21/index.md)

# The World Health Organization reports that an outbreak of bubonic plague in Madagascar has killed 40 people. 

An outbreak of plague in Madagascar has killed 40 people and infected dozens of others, the World Health Organization says.
