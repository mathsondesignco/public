### [2014-11-27](/news/2014/11/27/index.md)

# Sudanese nomadic conflicts:: At least 133 people were killed and 100 wounded, in the aftermath of clashes between Awlad Omran and Al-Ziyoud groups of the Mesiria tribe. The clashes occurred in the Kwak area of the West Kurdufan state, Sudan.
