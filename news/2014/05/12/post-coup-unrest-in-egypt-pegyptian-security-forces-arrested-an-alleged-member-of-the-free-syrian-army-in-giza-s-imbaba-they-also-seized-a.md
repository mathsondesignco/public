### [2014-05-12](/news/2014/05/12/index.md)

# Post-coup unrest in Egypt:Egyptian security forces arrested an alleged member of the Free Syrian Army in Giza's Imbaba. They also seized a number of explosives they claimed would allegedly be used in terror attacks in the country before the upcoming presidential elections.
