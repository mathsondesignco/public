### [2014-05-12](/news/2014/05/12/index.md)

# The ninth and final phase of election concluded in India, with an all time high voter turnout of 66.64%, which is overall the highest in the history of Lok Sabha. Final results will be declared on May 16.

The overall voter turnout surpassed the previous best of 64.01% in 1984 in the wake of the assassination of former Prime Minister Indira Gandhi.
