### [2014-05-25](/news/2014/05/25/index.md)

# Colombian presidential election, 2014:. Voters in Colombia go to the polls for the first round in the presidential election. Opposition candidate Oscar Ivan Zuluaga wins a plurality of votes and will face the incumbent Juan Manuel Santos in the second round.
