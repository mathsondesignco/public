# War in Somalia (2009-present):: An al-Shabaab-led assault at the Parliament of Somalia in Mogadishu leaves at least 10 dead, including police and militants. 

At least 10 people are reported killed as al-Shabab militants attack parliament in the Somali capital Mogadishu.
