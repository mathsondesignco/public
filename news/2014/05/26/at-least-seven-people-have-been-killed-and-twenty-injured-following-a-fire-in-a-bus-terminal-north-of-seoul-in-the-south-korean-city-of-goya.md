### [2014-05-26](/news/2014/05/26/index.md)

# At least seven people have been killed and twenty injured following a fire in a bus terminal north of Seoul in the South Korean city of Goyang. 

 At least seven people were killed and 20 others injured in a fire at a local bus terminal on Monday, firefighters and hospital officials said.
