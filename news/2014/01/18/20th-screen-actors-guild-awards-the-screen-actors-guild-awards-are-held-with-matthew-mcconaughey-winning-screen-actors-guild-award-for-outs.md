### [2014-01-18](/news/2014/01/18/index.md)

# 20th Screen Actors Guild Awards: The Screen Actors Guild Awards are held with Matthew McConaughey winning Screen Actors Guild Award for Outstanding Performance by a Male Actor in a Leading Role for his role in "Dallas Buyers Club" and Cate Blanchett winning Screen Actors Guild Award for Outstanding Performance by a Female Actor in a Leading Role for her role in "Blue Jasmine". 

The awards season now turns on a delicate dance between &quot;American Hustle&quot; and &quot;12 Years a Slave.&quot;
