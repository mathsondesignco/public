### [2014-01-29](/news/2014/01/29/index.md)

# More than 40 people are killed on Mindanao between the Philippine government and armed groups opposed to the peace deal between the Philippines and the Moro Islamic Liberation Front. 

A two-day military offensive kills at least 37 Philippine rebels who opposed a recent peace deal aimed at ending the long-running insurgency, the military says.
