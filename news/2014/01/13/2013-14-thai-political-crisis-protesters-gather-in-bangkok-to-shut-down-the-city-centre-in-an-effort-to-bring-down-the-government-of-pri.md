# 2013-14 Thai political crisis:: Protesters gather in Bangkok to shut down the city centre in an effort to bring down the government of Prime Minister of Thailand Yingluck Shinawatra. 

Thai protesters launch what they are calling a shutdown of Bangkok in a bid to oust the government before snap elections on 2 February.
