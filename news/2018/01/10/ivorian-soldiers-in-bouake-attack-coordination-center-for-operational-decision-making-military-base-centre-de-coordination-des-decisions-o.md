### [2018-01-10](/news/2018/01/10/index.md)

# Ivorian soldiers in Bouak attack Coordination Center for Operational Decision-Making military base ("Centre de Coordination des Dcisions Oprationnelles"), seizing their weaponry and setting the base on fire after accusations that the unit was spying on them. Last year the soldiers were involved in a series of mutinies.
