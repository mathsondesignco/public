### [2005-06-14](/news/2005/06/14/index.md)

#  A TNS-MRBI Irish Times opinion poll predicts that Ireland, previously seen as certain to vote "yes", is likely to vote "no" in its planned referendum on the European Constitution. Only 30% of voters indicated that they would vote for the constitution, the lowest level in any country in the European Union to date.
