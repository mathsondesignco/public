### [2005-06-26](/news/2005/06/26/index.md)

#   The Socialists win a plurality with 31.44% of the vote, with incumbent Prime Minister and ex-Tsar Simeon Saxe-Coburg receiving 20.13%. Despite the plurality, the results fall far short of pre-election forecasts for the Socialists.
