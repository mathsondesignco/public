### [2005-06-10](/news/2005/06/10/index.md)

#  In India, Subrata Roy, chairman of Sahara Group, goes public to state that he is healthy. Roy had been out of the public eye since April and his absence had aroused rumors of death, illness, intrafamily conflict and political pressure.
