### [2005-06-29](/news/2005/06/29/index.md)

#  In Serbia, Belgrade court convicts 10 officials from the government of Slobodan Milosevic for an assassination attempt against then-opposition leader Vuk Draskovic. they include special police commander Milorad Ulemek and chief of state security Radomir Markovic
