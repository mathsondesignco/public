### [2005-06-22](/news/2005/06/22/index.md)

#  In Colombia, congress approves a draft bill that offers sentences of only eight years to those members of paramilitary militias who give up their weapons. It demands that they confess, return the stolen property and disarm. Critics of president lvaro Uribe say that the law is too lenient
