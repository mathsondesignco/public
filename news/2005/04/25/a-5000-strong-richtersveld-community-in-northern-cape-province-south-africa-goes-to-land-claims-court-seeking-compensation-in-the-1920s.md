### [2005-04-25](/news/2005/04/25/index.md)

#  A 5000-strong Richtersveld community in Northern Cape province, South Africa, goes to Land Claims Court seeking compensation. In the 1920s they were evicted from their land, that turned out to be rich in diamonds and was mined by Alexkor mining company.
