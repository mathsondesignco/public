### [2005-04-26](/news/2005/04/26/index.md)

#  Israeli-Palestinian Conflict: The Israeli soldier killed in the West Bank on Monday night was shot by his comrades.  It was believed he was killed by an Unarmed Taxi Driver, Iyad Dueik, who was shot seven times and whose car ran over the man, but news that he was shot questions this assessment.
