### [2005-04-24](/news/2005/04/24/index.md)

#  Togolese presidential election, 2005: Three killed, amid claims of fraud in tense election in Togo to choose the successor to President Gnassingb Eyadma, hardline ruler for 38 years, from amongst Faure Gnassingb, the 39-year-old son of the late leader, and several challengers led by Emmanuel Bob-Akitani.
