### [2005-11-9](/news/2005/11/9/index.md)

#  Archaeologists report that two lines of a Phoenician or Hebrew alphabet on a stone dating to the 10th century BCE were discovered in July in Tel Zayit tell in Israel. The discovery suggests that literacy existed in ancient Israel earlier than had been thought.
