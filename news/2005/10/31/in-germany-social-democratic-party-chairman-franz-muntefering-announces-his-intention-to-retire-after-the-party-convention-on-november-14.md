### [2005-10-31](/news/2005/10/31/index.md)

#  In Germany, Social Democratic Party chairman Franz Mntefering announces his intention to retire after the party convention on November 14. The decision came after his candidate for Secretary General of the SPD, Kajo Wasserhvel, lost to left-wing candidate Andrea Nahles.
