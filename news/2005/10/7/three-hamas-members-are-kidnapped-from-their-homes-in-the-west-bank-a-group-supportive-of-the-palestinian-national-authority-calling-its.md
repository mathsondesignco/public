### [2005-10-7](/news/2005/10/7/index.md)

#  Three Hamas members are kidnapped from their homes in the West Bank. A group, supportive of the Palestinian National Authority, calling itself the "Omar Ben al-Khatib Warriors" claims responsibility for the kidnappings of the three, and one is later freed. 

3 Hamas members kidnapped in West Bank following abduction of PA security man on Thursday. One person released early Friday.
