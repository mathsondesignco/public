### [2005-02-24](/news/2005/02/24/index.md)

#  In Colombia, there is a ceremony to mark the three-year anniversary of the capture of Ingrid Btancourt, former Colombian presidential candidate who is still a FARC hostage. She was captured February 2002. Her relatives are pressing government to make a prisoner swap for her and other hostages.
