### [2005-02-28](/news/2005/02/28/index.md)

#  In Haiti, police shoots at demonstrators who march in support of the former president Jean-Bertrand Aristide. Two deaths and several injuries are reported. Aristide left the country February 29 2004 

At least 2 killed during pro-Aristide demonstration in Haiti.
