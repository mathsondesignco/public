### [2005-09-25](/news/2005/09/25/index.md)

#  Polish parliamentary election, 2005: As predicted by polls, support for Prime Minister Marek Belka's post-communist Alliance of the Democratic Left drops sharply to about 11%, while center-right opposition parties Law and Justice and Civic Platform will obtain about 28% and 26%, respectively.
