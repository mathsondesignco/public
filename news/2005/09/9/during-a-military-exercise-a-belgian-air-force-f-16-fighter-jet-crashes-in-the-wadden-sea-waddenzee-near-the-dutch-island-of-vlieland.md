### [2005-09-9](/news/2005/09/9/index.md)

#  During a military exercise, a Belgian Air Force F-16 fighter jet crashes in the Wadden Sea (Waddenzee), near the Dutch island of Vlieland. The pilot failed to eject, and died in the crash.
