### [2005-09-4](/news/2005/09/4/index.md)

#  In Bregenz, Austria, a German woman attacks the Roy Lichtenstein painting "Nudes in Mirror" with a jackknife. Witnesses say that the woman claimed that the painting was not authentic. Although there were several slashes in the painting, valued at -4 million, it can be repaired.
