### [2005-09-23](/news/2005/09/23/index.md)

#  Israeli-Palestinian Conflict:. At least 10 Palestinians die and 80 are injured when a truck carrying home-made explosives explodes during a Friday salat parade organised by Hamas. Hamas blames Israel, claiming an Israeli plane blew up the vehicle, while Israel and the Palestinian Authority blames Hamas.
