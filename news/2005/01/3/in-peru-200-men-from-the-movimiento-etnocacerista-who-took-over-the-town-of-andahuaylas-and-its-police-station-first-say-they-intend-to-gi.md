### [2005-01-3](/news/2005/01/3/index.md)

#  In Peru, 200 men from the Movimiento Etnocacerista who took over the town of Andahuaylas and its police station first say they intend to give up their weapons, then retract, saying the government had reneged on a surrender deal.
