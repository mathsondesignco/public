### [2005-01-27](/news/2005/01/27/index.md)

#  French national railways SNCF are severely disrupted by a 24-hour walkout by staff to protest the rape of a ticket inspector on a Toulouse - Cahors train on Tuesday. The inspector had been attempting to charge a man for not having a ticket. A 24-year-old man was later arrested. The strike is due to end at 1500 UTC.
