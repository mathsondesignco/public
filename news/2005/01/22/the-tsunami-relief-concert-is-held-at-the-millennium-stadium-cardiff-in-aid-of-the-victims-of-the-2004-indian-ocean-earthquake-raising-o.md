### [2005-01-22](/news/2005/01/22/index.md)

#  The Tsunami relief concert is held at the Millennium Stadium, Cardiff, in aid of the victims of the 2004 Indian Ocean earthquake, raising over PS1.25 million (EUR1.8 million or $2.4 million), making it the biggest charity concert in the United Kingdom since Live Aid in 1985.
