### [2005-01-14](/news/2005/01/14/index.md)

#  Saudi Arabia's supreme judicial council announced that the ritual of the Day of Arafah would take place Wednesday rather than Thursday as expected. This means that Eid ul-Adha will begin a day earlier than thought.
