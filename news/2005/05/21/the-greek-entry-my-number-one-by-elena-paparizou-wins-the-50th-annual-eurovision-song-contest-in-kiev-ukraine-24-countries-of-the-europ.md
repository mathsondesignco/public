### [2005-05-21](/news/2005/05/21/index.md)

#  The Greek entry "My Number One" by Elena Paparizou wins the 50th Annual Eurovision Song Contest in Kiev, Ukraine. 24 countries of the European Broadcasting Union participate in the final, fifteen other countries having been eliminated in Thursdays' semi-finals.
