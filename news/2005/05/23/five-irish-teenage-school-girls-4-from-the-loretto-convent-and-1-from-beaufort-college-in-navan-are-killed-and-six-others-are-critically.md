### [2005-05-23](/news/2005/05/23/index.md)

#  Five Irish teenage school girls, 4 from the Loretto Convent and 1 from Beaufort College in Navan, are killed and six others are critically injured in a bus crash in County Meath. The bus was carrying students from the Loretto Convent, the Mercy Convent, St. Patrick's Classical School and Beaufort College, all in Navan and did not have fitted seatbelts.
