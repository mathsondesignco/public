### [2005-05-6](/news/2005/05/6/index.md)

#  United Kingdom general election, 2005: At 04:28 BST, The Labour Party wins a parliamentary majority. Despite a substantially reduced majority, Tony Blair becomes the first Labour Prime Minister to lead his party to three election victories.
