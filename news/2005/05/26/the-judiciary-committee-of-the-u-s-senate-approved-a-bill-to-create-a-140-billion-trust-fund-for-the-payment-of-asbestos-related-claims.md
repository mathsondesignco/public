### [2005-05-26](/news/2005/05/26/index.md)

#  The Judiciary Committee of the U.S. Senate approved a bill to create a $140 billion trust fund for the payment of asbestos-related claims. The bill now goes to the Senate floor, no analogous bill is yet before the House of Representatives.
