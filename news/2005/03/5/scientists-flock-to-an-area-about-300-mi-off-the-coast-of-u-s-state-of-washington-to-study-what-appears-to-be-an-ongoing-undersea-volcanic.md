### [2005-03-5](/news/2005/03/5/index.md)

#  Scientists flock to an area about 300 mi off the coast of U.S. state of Washington to study what appears to be an ongoing undersea volcanic eruption. Accompanying earthquakes measuring up to magnitude 4.0 have numbered thousands per day.
