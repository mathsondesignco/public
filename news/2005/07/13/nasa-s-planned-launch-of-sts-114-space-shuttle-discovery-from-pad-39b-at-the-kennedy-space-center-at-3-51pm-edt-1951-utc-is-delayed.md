### [2005-07-13](/news/2005/07/13/index.md)

#  NASA's planned launch of STS-114, Space Shuttle ''Discovery'' from pad 39B at the Kennedy Space Center at 3:51pm EDT (1951 UTC) is delayed due to a problem with the fuel level sensors. This would have been the first manned space launch by NASA since the loss of ''Columbia'' over 2 years ago.
