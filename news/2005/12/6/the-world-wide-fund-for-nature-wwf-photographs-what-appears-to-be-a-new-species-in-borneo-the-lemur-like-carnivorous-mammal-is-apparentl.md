### [2005-12-6](/news/2005/12/6/index.md)

#  The World Wide Fund for Nature (WWF) photographs what appears to be a new species in Borneo. The lemur-like carnivorous mammal is apparently a member of the viverrid family, related to the mongoose and civet.
