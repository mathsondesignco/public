# 2015-16 UK and Ireland windstorm season: 2015 Great Britain and Ireland floods: The 18th century Tadcaster Bridge, in Tadcaster, North Yorkshire, partially collapses into the River Wharfe as a result of flooding. British Army soldiers evacuated people from nearby homes. 

Military drafted in to help carry out evacuation in storm-hit Yorkshire town after Environment Agency warns of significant risk to life
