### [2015-12-8](/news/2015/12/8/index.md)

# The prime ministers of Britain and France joined condemnation around the globe of U.S. Republican presidential candidate Donald Trump's call for a "total and complete ban" on Muslims entering the United States. 

Critics around the globe wonder whether the leading GOP presidential contender has finally gone too far.
