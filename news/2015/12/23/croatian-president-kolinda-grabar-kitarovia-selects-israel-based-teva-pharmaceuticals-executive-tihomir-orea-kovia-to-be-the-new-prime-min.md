### [2015-12-23](/news/2015/12/23/index.md)

# Croatian President Kolinda Grabar-Kitarovic selects Israel-based Teva Pharmaceuticals executive Tihomir Oreskovic to be the new prime minister following the inconclusive November 8 election. The "technocrat" is the choice of conservatives and reformists, who have 78 parliamentary seats, enough for confirmation.
