### [2015-12-23](/news/2015/12/23/index.md)

# Tornadoes of 2015 At least six people die and dozens injured as major storms, packed with tornadoes, move through the American South and Midwest. Three people died when a tornado hit northern Mississippi, one was killed in Arkansas and two more in Tennessee. Officials continue  searching into the night for the missing. 

Days of severe weather have left deaths, injuries and damage in the South. Here are the latest impacts.
