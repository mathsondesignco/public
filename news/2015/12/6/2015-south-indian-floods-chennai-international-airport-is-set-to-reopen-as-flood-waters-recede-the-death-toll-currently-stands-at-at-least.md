### [2015-12-6](/news/2015/12/6/index.md)

# 2015 South Indian floods. Chennai International Airport is set to reopen as flood waters recede. The death toll currently stands at at least 280 people. 

The Regional Meteorological Centre has forecast light rains in the next 24 hours for the city and suburbs.
