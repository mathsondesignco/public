### [2015-12-6](/news/2015/12/6/index.md)

# Syrian Civil War: Military intervention against ISIL: A series of air strikes in Syria's Raqqa province, believed to be carried out by U.S.-led coalition warplanes, leaves at least 32 Islamic State fighters killed and 40 more wounded. The final toll could rise as casualty figures were collected from a single hospital. The jets hit IS headquarters and bases to the north, east, and southeast of provincial capital Raqqa city. 

Fighters killed in some 15 strikes on IS Raqqa stronghold.
