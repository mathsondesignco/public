### [2015-12-11](/news/2015/12/11/index.md)

# 2015 South Indian floods: Major UK-based reinsurance broker Aon Benfield estimates India has suffered over US$3 billion (over Rs. 200 billion) worth of losses as a result of the flooding, and rates them as the costliest floods and the eighth-costliest natural disaster of 2015.

Reinsurance broker Aon Benfield says the country suffered a $3 bn loss to its economy from severe rainfall and flooding in November and early December
