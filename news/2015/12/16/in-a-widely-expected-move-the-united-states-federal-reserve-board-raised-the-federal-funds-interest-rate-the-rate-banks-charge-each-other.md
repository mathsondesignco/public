### [2015-12-16](/news/2015/12/16/index.md)

# In a widely expected move, the United States Federal Reserve Board raised the federal funds interest rate, the rate banks charge each other for overnight loans, from the 0-.25% range to the .25-.50% range.  U.S. stock markets rallied on the news.
