### [2015-12-21](/news/2015/12/21/index.md)

# Iraqi Civil War (2014-present): Military intervention against ISIL: Around 20 people, including civilians and Islamic State militants, are killed in airstrikes on Mosul in northern Iraq. 

About 20 people, including at least 12 civilians, were killed on Monday in the northern Iraqi city of Mosul, in two air strikes that destroyed houses believed to be used by Islamic State militants, six eyewitnesses and a medical source said.
