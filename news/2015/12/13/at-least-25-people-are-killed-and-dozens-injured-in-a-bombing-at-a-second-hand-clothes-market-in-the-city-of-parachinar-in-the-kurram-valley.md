### [2015-12-13](/news/2015/12/13/index.md)

# At least 25 people are killed and dozens injured in a bombing at a second-hand clothes market in the city of Parachinar in the Kurram Valley. 

Sectarian outfit Lashkar-e-Jhangvi al-Alami claims responsibility for the attack
