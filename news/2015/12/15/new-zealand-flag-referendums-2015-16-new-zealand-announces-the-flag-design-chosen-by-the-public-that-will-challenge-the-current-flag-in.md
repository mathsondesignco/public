### [2015-12-15](/news/2015/12/15/index.md)

# New Zealand flag referendums, 2015-16: New Zealand announces the flag design chosen by the public that will challenge the current flag in a March 2016 vote. Voters will choose between the current flag, which features the British Union Jack with a dark blue background, and the challenger, a silver fern and four red stars on a black and blue background.
