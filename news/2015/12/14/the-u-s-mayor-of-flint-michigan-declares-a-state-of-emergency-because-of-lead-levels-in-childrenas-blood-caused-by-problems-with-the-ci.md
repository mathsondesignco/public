### [2015-12-14](/news/2015/12/14/index.md)

# The U.S. mayor of Flint, Michigan, declares a state of emergency because of lead levels in children's blood caused by problems with the city's water system. 

Michigan city asks for more federal help while it deals with the fallout from water problems
