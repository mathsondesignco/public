### [2015-08-22](/news/2015/08/22/index.md)

# Mei Xiang, a female giant panda at the Smithsonian National Zoological Park in Washington, D.C., delivers two cubs. The identity of the father is not known. 

A second cub was born less than five hours after its sibling, launching a new chapter in the public romance with the bears.
