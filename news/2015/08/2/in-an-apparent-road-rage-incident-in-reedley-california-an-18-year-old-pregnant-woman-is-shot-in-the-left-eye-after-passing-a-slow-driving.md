# In an apparent road rage incident in Reedley, California, an 18-year-old pregnant woman is shot in the left eye after passing a slow-driving SUV which subsequently pulls up alongside and opens fire.

The shooting happened after an SUV driven by the victim&#039;s husband passed another vehicle as it was driving too slowly.
