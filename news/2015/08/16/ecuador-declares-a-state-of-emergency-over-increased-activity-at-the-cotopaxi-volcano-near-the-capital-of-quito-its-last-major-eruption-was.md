### [2015-08-16](/news/2015/08/16/index.md)

# Ecuador declares a state of emergency over increased activity at the Cotopaxi volcano near the capital of Quito. Its last major eruption was in 1877. 

Officials evacuate several hundred people amid increasing activity in Cotopaxi volcano, near the capital Quito.
