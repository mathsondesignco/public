### [2015-08-29](/news/2015/08/29/index.md)

# The Secretary-General of the United Nations Ban Ki-moon calls for a "collective political response" to the crisis as the death toll from the latest boat to sink in the Mediterranean Sea rises to 171. 

The UN says &quot;much more is required&quot; to prevent fatalities among migrants fleeing to Europe, after hundreds are reported dead in recent days.
