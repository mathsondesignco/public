### [2015-08-20](/news/2015/08/20/index.md)

# North and South Korea exchange artillery fire over the border at Yeoncheon, prompting South Korea to order evacuations of its citizens in the area. 

Reports say Pyongyang fired a shell at a South Korean border town, which prompted Seoul to respond with artillery fire.
