### [2015-08-21](/news/2015/08/21/index.md)

# President Barack Obama declares a state of emergency across northeastern and central Washington as the amount of burned land in the state reaches nearly a half-million acres. 

Winds will continue to challenge firefighters across North Central Washington. President Obama has declared a state of emergency.
