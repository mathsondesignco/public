### [2015-08-25](/news/2015/08/25/index.md)

# North Korea and South Korea agree to stop airing propaganda at the border and stand down preparations for war. 

South Korea has agreed to stop blaring propaganda from speakers across the border and the North has agreed to lift its semi-war status.
