### [2015-11-3](/news/2015/11/3/index.md)

# Territorial disputes in the South China Sea. Japan-Vietnam relations. Japan has delivered two more ships to Vietnam that will be refurbished into patrol boats. The ships, which arrived in the port city of Da Nang, are the second delivery of a 2014 deal in which Tokyo is to provide Vietnam with six used fishing vessels that will be converted into patrol boats for Vietnam's coast guard in the South China Sea.
