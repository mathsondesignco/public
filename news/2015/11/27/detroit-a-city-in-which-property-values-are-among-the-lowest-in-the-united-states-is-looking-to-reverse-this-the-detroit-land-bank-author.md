### [2015-11-27](/news/2015/11/27/index.md)

# Detroit, a city in which property values are among the lowest in the United States, is looking to reverse this. The Detroit Land Bank Authority is demolishing structures that are beyond repair and auction (bids start at $1,000) ones that are salvageable.  

City leaders target eight promising neighborhoods, moving quickly with demolitions, renovations and sales.
