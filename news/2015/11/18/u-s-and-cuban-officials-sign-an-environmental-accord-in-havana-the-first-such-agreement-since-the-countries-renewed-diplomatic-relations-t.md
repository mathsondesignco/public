# U.S. and Cuban officials sign an environmental accord in Havana, the first such agreement since the countries renewed diplomatic relations this summer. 

On Wednesday, U.S. and Cuban officials signed an environmental accord in Havana, the first such agreement since the diplomatic thaw.
