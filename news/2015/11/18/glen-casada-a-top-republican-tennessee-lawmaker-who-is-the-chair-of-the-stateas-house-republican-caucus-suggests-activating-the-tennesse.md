### [2015-11-18](/news/2015/11/18/index.md)

# Glen Casada, a top Republican Tennessee lawmaker who is the chair of the state's House Republican Caucus, suggests activating the Tennessee National Guard to 'round up' Syrian refugees and remove them from the southeastern state, saying "Tennessee is a sovereign state, if the federal government is forsaking the obligation to protect our citizens, we need to act". 

Glen Casada: &quot;We need to ... take them back to the ICE center and say, &#39;They’re not coming to Tennessee, they’re yours.&#39; &quot;
