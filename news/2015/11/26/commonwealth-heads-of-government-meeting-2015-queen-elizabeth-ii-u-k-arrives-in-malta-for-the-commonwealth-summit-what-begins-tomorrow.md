### [2015-11-26](/news/2015/11/26/index.md)

# Commonwealth Heads of Government Meeting 2015. Queen Elizabeth II (U.K.) arrives in Malta for the Commonwealth summit what begins tomorrow. The then-future Queen and the Duke of Edinburgh lived on the island from 1949-51. The Commonwealth is a group of 53 nations; most member states were once part of the British Empire, and 16 retain the Queen as head of state. 

Armed Forces band of Malta sets the tone as they chose to play Thanks For The Memory while Her Majesty inspects guard of honour in Valletta
