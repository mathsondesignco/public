### [2015-11-13](/news/2015/11/13/index.md)

# Islamic State in Iraq and the Levant: The United States conducts airstrikes targeting Kuwaiti-British militant Jihadi John. Later, the U.S. says it is "reasonably certain" a drone strike in Syria killed Jihadi John, ne Mohammed Emwazi. 

A Pentagon spokesman says the US is &quot;reasonably certain&quot; that a drone strike in Syria killed the Islamic State militant known as Jihadi John.
