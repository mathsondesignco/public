### [2015-11-8](/news/2015/11/8/index.md)

# According to a new World Bank report, climate change could drive more than 100 million people into poverty by 2030 largely due to difficulties producing crops. 

Poverty levels will drastically increase across the world if governments fail to take action against climate change, a new World Bank report claims.
