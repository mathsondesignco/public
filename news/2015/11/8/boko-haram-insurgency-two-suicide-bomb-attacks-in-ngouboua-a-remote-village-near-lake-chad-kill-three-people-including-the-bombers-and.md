### [2015-11-8](/news/2015/11/8/index.md)

# Boko Haram insurgency. Two suicide bomb attacks in Ngouboua, a remote village near Lake Chad, kill three people, including the bombers, and injure four others, according to Chadian Communication Minister Hassan Sylla Bakari. 

At least three people were killed in a double suicide bombing in a village near the border with Nigeria in southwestern Chad.
