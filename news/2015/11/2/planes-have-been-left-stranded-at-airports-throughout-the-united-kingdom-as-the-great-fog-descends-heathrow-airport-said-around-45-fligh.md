### [2015-11-2](/news/2015/11/2/index.md)

#  Planes have been left stranded at airports throughout the United Kingdom as the "Great Fog" descends. Heathrow Airport said around 45 flights had been cancelled there while London City Airport was closed for several hours. Other affected airports include Manchester, Leeds Bradford, Glasgow, Belfast, Southampton and Cardiff. 

Scores of flights are cancelled and many more delayed by a second day of dense fog across much of the UK.
