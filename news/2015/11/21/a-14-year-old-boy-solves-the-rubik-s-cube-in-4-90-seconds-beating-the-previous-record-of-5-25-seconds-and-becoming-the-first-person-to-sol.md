# A 14-year-old boy solves the Rubik's Cube in 4.90 seconds, beating the previous record of 5.25 seconds, and becoming the first person to solve it in under 5 seconds 

World Cube Association confirms speedcuber Lucas Etter, 14, has beaten previous 3x3 record, with reported time of just 4.9 seconds
