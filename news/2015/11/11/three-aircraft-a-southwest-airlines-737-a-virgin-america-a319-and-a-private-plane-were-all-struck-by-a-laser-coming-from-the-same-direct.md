# Three aircraft, a Southwest Airlines 737, a Virgin America A319, and a private plane, were all struck by a laser coming from the same direction as they were about to land at Dallas's Love Field, according to the U.S. FAA. 

Three airplanes were hit by a laser coming from the same direction as they were inbound to a Dallas airport, the FAA said on Wednesday.
