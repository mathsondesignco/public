### [2015-11-11](/news/2015/11/11/index.md)

# Syrian Civil War: Spillover of the Syrian Civil War: The Israeli Air Force reportedly conducts an airstrike near Damascus International Airport, in Syria's capital, the target apparently a weapons shipment reportedly intended for Lebanese terrorist organization Hezbollah, according to Syrian opposition groups. 

According to unconfirmed reports by opposition groups, target was a weapons shipment intended for Hezbollah
