### [2015-11-19](/news/2015/11/19/index.md)

# The World Economic Forum in its annual Global Gender Gap Report says that it would take 118 years before women and men have equal pay for similar occupations, such as white collar jobs. 

Almost no progress on wage equality around the globe in past seven years, according to report
