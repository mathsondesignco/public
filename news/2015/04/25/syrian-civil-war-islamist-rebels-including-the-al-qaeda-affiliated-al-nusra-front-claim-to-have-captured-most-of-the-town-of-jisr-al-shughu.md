### [2015-04-25](/news/2015/04/25/index.md)

# Syrian Civil War: Islamist rebels including the al-Qaeda-affiliated al-Nusra Front claim to have captured most of the town of Jisr al-Shughur in the Idlib Governorate. 

Islamist rebels have captured much of the north-western Syrian town of Jisr al-Shughur from government forces, activists say.
