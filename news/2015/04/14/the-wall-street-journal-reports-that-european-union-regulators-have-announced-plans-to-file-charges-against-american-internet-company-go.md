### [2015-04-14](/news/2015/04/14/index.md)

# The ''Wall Street Journal'' reports that European Union regulators have announced plans to file charges against American Internet company Google following a European Union investigation. 

EU regulators decided Tuesday that they would file charges against Google stemming from an antitrust investigation, according to reports.
