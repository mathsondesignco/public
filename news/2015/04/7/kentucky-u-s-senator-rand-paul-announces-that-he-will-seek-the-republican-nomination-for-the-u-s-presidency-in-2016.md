### [2015-04-7](/news/2015/04/7/index.md)

# Kentucky U.S. Senator Rand Paul announces that he will seek the Republican nomination for the U.S. presidency in 2016. 

Kentucky Sen. Rand Paul kicked off his presidential campaign today, vowing to buck the &quot;Washington machine&quot; and expand the Republican Party to include young people and minorities.
