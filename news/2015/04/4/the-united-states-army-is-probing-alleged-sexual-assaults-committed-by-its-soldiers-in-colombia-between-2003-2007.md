### [2015-04-4](/news/2015/04/4/index.md)

# The United States Army is probing alleged sexual assaults committed by its soldiers in Colombia between 2003-2007. 

Army criminal investigators plan to probe allegations with officials in Colombia that American soldiers and contractors sexually assault civilians there, an Army spokesman said on Friday.
