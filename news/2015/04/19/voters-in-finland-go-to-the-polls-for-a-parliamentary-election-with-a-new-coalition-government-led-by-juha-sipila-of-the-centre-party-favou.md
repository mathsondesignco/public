### [2015-04-19](/news/2015/04/19/index.md)

# Voters in Finland go to the polls for a parliamentary election with a new coalition government led by Juha Sipila of the Centre Party favoured to form government after negotiation. 

Finns are tipped to oust apro-European and pro-NATO prime minister in elections on Sundayand put in power an opposition coalition that may includeEurosceptic nationalists to revive a sagging economy.
