### [2015-01-11](/news/2015/01/11/index.md)

# Croatian presidential election, 2014-15: Former diplomat and foreign minister Kolinda Grabar-Kitarovic from the Croatian Democratic Union narrowly defeats incumbent president Ivo Josipovic and will become the first female president of Croatia on February 18, 2015. 

Opposition challenger Kolinda Grabar-Kitarovic becomes the first female president of Croatia, winning by the narrowest of margins.
