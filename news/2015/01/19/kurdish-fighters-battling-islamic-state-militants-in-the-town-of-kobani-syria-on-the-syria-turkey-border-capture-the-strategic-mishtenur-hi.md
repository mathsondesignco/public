### [2015-01-19](/news/2015/01/19/index.md)

# Kurdish fighters battling Islamic State militants in the town of Koban, Syria on the Syria-Turkey border capture the strategic Mishtenur hill killing eleven fighters. 

Kurdish fighters have captured a strategic hilltop, reports say, in their battle with Islamic State militants for control of the town of Kobane.
