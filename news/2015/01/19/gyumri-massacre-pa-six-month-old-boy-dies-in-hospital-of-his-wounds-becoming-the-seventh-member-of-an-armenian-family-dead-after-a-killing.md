# Gyumri massacre:A six-month-old boy dies in hospital of his wounds, becoming the seventh member of an Armenian family dead after a killing spree by a Russian soldier. 

The death toll in the slaying of a family in Armenia blamed on a Russian soldier has risen to seven after a 6-month-old infant has died of his wounds.
