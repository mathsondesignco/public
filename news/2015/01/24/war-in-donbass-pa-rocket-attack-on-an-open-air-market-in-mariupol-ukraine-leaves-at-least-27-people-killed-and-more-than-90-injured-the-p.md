### [2015-01-24](/news/2015/01/24/index.md)

# War in Donbass:A rocket attack on an open-air market in Mariupol, Ukraine, leaves at least 27 people killed and more than 90 injured. The pro-Russian rebel leader, Alexander Zakharchenko, announces an offensive on Mariupol. 

Pro-Russian rebels claim responsibility for the attack on Mariupol city that killed at least 27 people.
