# Sectarianism in Pakistan. Sunni militants linked to the Pakistani Taliban kill at least 40 people and injure 50 in a bombing at a Shiite mosque in the Shikarpur District of Pakistan's Sindh province. 

A bomb blast at a Shia mosque in southern Pakistan kills at least 40 people and injures many more, officials say.
