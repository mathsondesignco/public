### [2015-01-16](/news/2015/01/16/index.md)

# The Obama Administration imposes restrictions on state and local police's ability to seize personal property under the guidelines of the Equitable Sharing federal program. 

The action represents a sweeping check on the power of police to confiscate under federal law.
