### [2015-01-29](/news/2015/01/29/index.md)

# The Philippine economy grew by more than 6% in 2014 making it the second fastest growing economy in Asia after China. This is the longest sustained growth period in the country since the 1950s. 

The Philippines has been blighted by decades of corrupt governments, besides it is also vulnerable to frequent natural disasters such as typhoons and floods.
