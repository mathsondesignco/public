### [2015-06-8](/news/2015/06/8/index.md)

#  ''Zivotofsky v. Clinton'' / ''Zivotofsky v. Kerry''The Supreme Court of the United States decides 6-3 to strike down a law passed by the United States Congress allowing people born in Jerusalem to list Israel as their birthplace in their passports, on the grounds that it is the President's prerogative to extend recognition of sovereignty in matters of foreign affairs.

In landmark ruling backing president&#8217;s stance, legal body strikes down law permitting US citizens to cite Jewish state on passport
