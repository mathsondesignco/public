### [2015-06-3](/news/2015/06/3/index.md)

# The search continues for survivors of the ''Dong Fang Zhi Xing'' which sank on China's Yangtze River with 450 passengers on board. So far, 18 people have been confirmed dead with 14 people rescued. 

“18 confirmed dead, 14 rescued; over 150 divers carrying out search and rescue operations on #YangtzeRiver”
