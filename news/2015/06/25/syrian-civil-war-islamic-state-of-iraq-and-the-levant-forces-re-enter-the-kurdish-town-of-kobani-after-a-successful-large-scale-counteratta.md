# Syrian Civil War. Islamic State of Iraq and the Levant forces re-enter the Kurdish town of Koban after a successful large-scale counterattack against the Kurds. 

Islamic State militants fight Kurdish troops in the northern Syrian city of Kobane, months after their symbolic defeat there.
