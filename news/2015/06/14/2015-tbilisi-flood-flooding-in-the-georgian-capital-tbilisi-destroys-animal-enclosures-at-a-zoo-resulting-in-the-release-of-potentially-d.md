### [2015-06-14](/news/2015/06/14/index.md)

# 2015 Tbilisi flood. Flooding in the Georgian capital, Tbilisi, destroys animal enclosures at a zoo resulting in the release of potentially dangerous animals and the death of three zookeepers and six other people. 

At least 12 people are reported to have died and a number of others were missing following flooding that destroyed enclosures at the zoo in Georgia&#39;s capital of Tbilisi, leaving animals including lions and tigers roaming the city.
