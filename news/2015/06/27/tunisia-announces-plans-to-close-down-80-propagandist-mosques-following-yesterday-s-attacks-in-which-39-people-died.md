### [2015-06-27](/news/2015/06/27/index.md)

# Tunisia announces plans to close down 80 "propagandist" mosques following yesterday's attacks in which 39 people died. 

Tunisia deploys extra security to tourist sites and vows to close down mosques accused of inciting violence, after a deadly attack at a beach resort.
