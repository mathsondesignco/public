### [2015-06-16](/news/2015/06/16/index.md)

# Rupert Murdoch confirms stepping down as the CEO of 21st Century Fox to be succeeded by his son James on July 1, 2015. Rupert will continue as its executive chairman with his eldest son, Lachlan, as a future executive co-chairman. 

As expected, 21st Century Fox board approved a proposal to promote James Murdoch as CEO
