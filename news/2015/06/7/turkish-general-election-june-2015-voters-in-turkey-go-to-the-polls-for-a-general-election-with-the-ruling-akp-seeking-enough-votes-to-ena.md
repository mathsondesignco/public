### [2015-06-7](/news/2015/06/7/index.md)

# Turkish general election, June 2015: Voters in Turkey go to the polls for a general election with the ruling AKP seeking enough votes to enable them to change the constitution. However, early projections show that they will lose their majority in the new parliament.
