# A Hercules C-130 military aircraft with 113 people onboard crashes in a residential area in the Indonesian city of Medan, resulting in at least 116 deaths. 

More than 100 are feared killed as a military transport plane crashes in a residential area of the Indonesian city of Medan, officials say.
