### [2015-06-11](/news/2015/06/11/index.md)

# After a poor 1st quarter earnings report, Twitter CEO Dick Costolo steps down with co-founder Jack Dorsey taking over as interim CEO effective July 1. 

Twitter CEO Dick Costolo is stepping down effective July 1, replaced by Interim CEO Jack Dorsey.
