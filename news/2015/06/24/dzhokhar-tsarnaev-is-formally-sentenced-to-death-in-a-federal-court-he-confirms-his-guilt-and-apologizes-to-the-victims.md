### [2015-06-24](/news/2015/06/24/index.md)

# Dzhokhar Tsarnaev is formally sentenced to death in a federal court. He confirms his guilt and apologizes to the victims. 

Since the federal government reinstated the death penalty in 1988, only three men have been executed.
