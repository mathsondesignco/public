### [2015-06-24](/news/2015/06/24/index.md)

# Xinjiang Conflict. A police checkpoint on the outskirts of Kashgar in Xinjiang is attacked by ethnic Uighurs, with at least 18 killed according to US-based Radio Free Asia. 

At least 18 people are killed in the Chinese region of Xinjiang during a clash at a police checkpoint, a US media report says.
