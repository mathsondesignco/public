### [2015-10-10](/news/2015/10/10/index.md)

# Palestinian militants fire 3 rockets into Israel during the night after several violent riots throughout Israel and the West Bank. 

Palestinian militants in the Gaza Strip have fired a rocket into southern Israel, the Israeli military says, hours after six Palestinians were shot dead.
