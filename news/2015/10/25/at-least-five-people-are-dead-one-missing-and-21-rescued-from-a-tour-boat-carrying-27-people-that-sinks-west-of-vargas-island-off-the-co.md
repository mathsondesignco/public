### [2015-10-25](/news/2015/10/25/index.md)

# At least five people are dead, one missing, and 21 rescued, from a tour boat carrying 27 people that sinks west of Vargas Island, off the coast of Tofino on Vancouver Island, British Columbia, Canada. The RCMP is responsible for the missing person now that the search has been called off. 

A massive search-and-rescue effort was launched after the vessel made a mayday call.
