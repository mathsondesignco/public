### [2015-10-25](/news/2015/10/25/index.md)

# Voters in Guatemala go to the polls for the second round of voting in a presidential election with former TV comedian Jimmy Morales winning a landslide victory. 

Jimmy Morales, a former TV comedy actor who promised to clean up Guatemala’s corrupt politics, has won a landslide victory in the country’s presidential election on Sunday.
