### [2015-10-27](/news/2015/10/27/index.md)

# Syrian Civil War. Destruction of cultural heritage by ISIL. The Syrian Observatory for Human Rights reports the Islamic State, on Sunday, executed three detainees in the ancient Syrian city of Palmyra by strapping them to pillars and blowing them up with the antiquities. ISIL has yet to tell locals the identities of the three individuals or say why they had been killed.
