# War in Afghanistan (2015-present). Taliban insurgents overrun two security checkpoints near Kandahar in the southern Helmand Province, killing at least 29 border police officers, according to local officials. 

Taliban insurgents overran two checkpoints in southern Helmand province and killed 29 border police officers, a provincial official said.
