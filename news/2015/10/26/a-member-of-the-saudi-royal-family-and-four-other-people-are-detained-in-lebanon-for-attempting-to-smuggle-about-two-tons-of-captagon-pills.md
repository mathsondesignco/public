### [2015-10-26](/news/2015/10/26/index.md)

# A member of the Saudi Royal Family and four other people are detained in Lebanon for attempting to smuggle about two tons of Captagon pills and some cocaine on a private jet. This is the largest drug seizure in the history of Beirut's Rafik Hariri International Airport, according to Lebanon's state-run National News Agency. 

Prince among five Saudis detained over seizure of two tonnes of amphetamines and cocaine at Lebanese capital&#39;s airport.
