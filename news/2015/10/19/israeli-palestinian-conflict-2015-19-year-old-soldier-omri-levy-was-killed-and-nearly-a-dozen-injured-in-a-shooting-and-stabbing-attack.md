### [2015-10-19](/news/2015/10/19/index.md)

# Israeli-Palestinian conflict (2015): 19-year-old soldier Omri Levy was killed and nearly a dozen injured in a shooting and stabbing attack in the central bus station in the southern city of Beersheba carried out by an Israeli Bedouin Arab, 21-year old Mohind al-Okbi, who was killed after a gun battle. 

A gunman killed an Israeli soldier and wounded at least 11 other people at a bus station in southern Israel on Sunday.
