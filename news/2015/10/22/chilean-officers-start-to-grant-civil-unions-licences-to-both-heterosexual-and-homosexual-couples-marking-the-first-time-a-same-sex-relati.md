### [2015-10-22](/news/2015/10/22/index.md)

#  Chilean officers start to grant civil unions licences to both heterosexual and homosexual couples, marking the first time a same-sex relationship is officially recognized in the country. 

A new law comes into force in Chile granting unmarried heterosexual and same-sex couples many of the rights enjoyed by married couples.
