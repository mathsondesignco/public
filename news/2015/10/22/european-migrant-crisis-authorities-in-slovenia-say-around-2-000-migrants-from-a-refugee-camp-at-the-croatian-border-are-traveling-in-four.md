### [2015-10-22](/news/2015/10/22/index.md)

# European migrant crisis. Authorities in Slovenia say around 2,000 migrants from a refugee camp at the Croatian border are traveling in four trains toward Austria, three to the overloaded crossing at Sentilj and the other to Jesenice. 

Miloš Zeman dismisses accusations of Islamophobia amid UN condemnation of country’s detention policy and strip-searching of refugees
