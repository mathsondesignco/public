### [2015-10-1](/news/2015/10/1/index.md)

# An Israeli couple are murdered in a shooting attack on their car in the northern West Bank; four of the couple's children are in the vehicle at the time but are not hit by the gunfire. Hamas praises the "heroic attack" and calls for "more high-quality attacks." 

Victims identified as Eitam and Naama Henkin; their 4 children were in the car, but were unhurt in the gunfire; IDF deploys 4 battalions to aid in hunt for killers
