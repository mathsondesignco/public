### [2015-10-12](/news/2015/10/12/index.md)

# Islamist militants kill two Tunisian soldiers and injure four others who were seeking to free a man kidnapped  in a mountainous area near the border with Algeria. 

Islamists militants killed two Tunisian soldiers who were seeking to free a man kidnapped on Monday in a mountainous area near the border with Algeria, an army spokesman said.
