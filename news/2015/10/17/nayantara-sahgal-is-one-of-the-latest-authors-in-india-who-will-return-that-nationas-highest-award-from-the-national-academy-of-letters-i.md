### [2015-10-17](/news/2015/10/17/index.md)

# Nayantara Sahgal, is one of the latest authors in India who will return that nation's highest award from the National Academy of Letters in protest against what Salman Rushdie has characterized as "thuggish violence" creeping into Indian life under the Hindu nationalist government of Prime Minister Narendra Modi.
