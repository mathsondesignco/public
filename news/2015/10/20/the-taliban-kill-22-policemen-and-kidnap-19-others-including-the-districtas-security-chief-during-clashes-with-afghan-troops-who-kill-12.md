### [2015-10-20](/news/2015/10/20/index.md)

# The Taliban kill 22 policemen and kidnap 19 others, including the district's security chief, during clashes with Afghan troops who kill 12 of the insurgents in the battle in Faryab Province in northern Afghanistan. 

Taliban forces advanced on the capital of the volatile southern Afghan province of Helmand on Tuesday amid fierce fighting with government forces that threatened to cut off a major highway and prompted many families to flee.
