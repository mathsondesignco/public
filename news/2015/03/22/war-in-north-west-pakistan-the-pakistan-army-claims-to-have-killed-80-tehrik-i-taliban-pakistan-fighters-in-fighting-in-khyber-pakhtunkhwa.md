### [2015-03-22](/news/2015/03/22/index.md)

# War in North-West Pakistan: The Pakistan Army claims to have killed 80 Tehrik-i-Taliban Pakistan fighters in fighting in Khyber Pakhtunkhwa in recent days while the Taliban claims to have killed at least six soldiers. 

Pakistani troops kill 80 militants in heavy clashes in the country&#039;s lawless north-west, a spokesman says.
