# Death of Lee Kuan Yew: Lee Kuan Yew, a founding father of Singapore as well as its first prime minister, dies at the age of 91 after a prolonged illness. 

Lee Kuan Yew, the statesman who transformed Singapore from a port city into a wealthy global hub, dies at the age of 91.
