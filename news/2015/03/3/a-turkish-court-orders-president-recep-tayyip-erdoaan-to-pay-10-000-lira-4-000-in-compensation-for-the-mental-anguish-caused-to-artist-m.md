### [2015-03-3](/news/2015/03/3/index.md)

# A Turkish court orders President Recep Tayyip Erdogan to pay 10,000 lira ($4,000) in compensation for the mental anguish caused to artist Mehmet Aksoy for having called his sculpture "Monument to Humanity" - meant to promote reconciliation between Turkey and Armenia - a "monstrosity" in 2011.
