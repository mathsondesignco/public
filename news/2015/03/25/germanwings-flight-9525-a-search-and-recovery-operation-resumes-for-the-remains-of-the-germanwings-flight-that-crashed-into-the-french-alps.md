# Germanwings Flight 9525. A search and recovery operation resumes for the remains of the Germanwings flight that crashed into the French Alps on March 24. 

The German, French and Spanish leaders visit the site in the French Alps where a Germanwings plane crashed, killing all 150 people on board.
