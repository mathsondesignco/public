### [2015-07-9](/news/2015/07/9/index.md)

# The South Carolina House of Representatives agrees to a bill previously passed by the Senate to remove the Confederate flag from the capitol grounds with Governor Nikki Haley signing the legislation into law. 

South Carolina Gov. Nikki Haley signed into law a bill that removes the Confederate battle flag from the capitol.
