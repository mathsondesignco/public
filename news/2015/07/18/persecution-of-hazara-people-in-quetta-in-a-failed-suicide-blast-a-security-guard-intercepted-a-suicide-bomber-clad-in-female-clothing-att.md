### [2015-07-18](/news/2015/07/18/index.md)

# Persecution of Hazara people in Quetta: In a failed suicide blast, a security guard intercepted a suicide bomber clad in female clothing attempting to enter Quetta-s Hazara town, who then detonated their device that contained 6|to|7|kg|abbr=on of explosive material, killing themselves and the guard. 

Attacker prematurely detonates explosives after interception by a security guard, while attempting to enter Hazara town.
