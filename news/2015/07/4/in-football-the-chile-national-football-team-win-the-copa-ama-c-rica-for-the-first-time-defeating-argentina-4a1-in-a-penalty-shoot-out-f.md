### [2015-07-4](/news/2015/07/4/index.md)

#   In football, the Chile national football team win the Copa America for the first time, defeating Argentina 4-1 in a penalty shoot-out following a 0-0 draw.

Chile win Copa America for the first time in their history after a 4-1 victory on penalties over Argentina
