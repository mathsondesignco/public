### [2015-07-3](/news/2015/07/3/index.md)

# Research shows that during the Ebola virus epidemic in West Africa, the ReEBOV Antigen Rapid Test, developed by Corgenix, showed promise as a bedside rapid Ebola test, successfully detecting Ebolavirus. 

A new test can accurately diagnose the Ebola virus disease within minutes at the point of care.
