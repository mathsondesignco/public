### [2015-07-21](/news/2015/07/21/index.md)

# Burundian presidential election, 2015. Voters in Burundi go to the polls for a presidential election amidst gunfire and violence with President Pierre Nkurunziza seeking a third term and opposition parties boycotting the election. At least two people have been killed in election violence.
