### [2015-07-2](/news/2015/07/2/index.md)

# The Kim Nirvana ferry capsizes off the Philippines en route from the city of Ormoc to Camotes island with 173 people onboard; at least 33 people are confirmed dead while at least 50 people have been rescued. 

The death toll from the ferry that capsized in the central Philippines rises to 41 after rescuers recover three more bodies from the hull.
