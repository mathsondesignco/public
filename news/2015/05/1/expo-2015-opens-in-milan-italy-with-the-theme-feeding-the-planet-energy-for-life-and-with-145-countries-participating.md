### [2015-05-1](/news/2015/05/1/index.md)

# Expo 2015 opens in Milan, Italy, with the theme "Feeding the planet, energy for life" and with 145 countries participating.

A major global commercial fair - Milan Expo 2015 - opens in northern Italy, but violence breaks out in the city as protesters clash with police.
