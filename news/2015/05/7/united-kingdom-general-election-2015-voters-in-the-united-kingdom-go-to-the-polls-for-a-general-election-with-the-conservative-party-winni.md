### [2015-05-7](/news/2015/05/7/index.md)

# United Kingdom general election, 2015: Voters in the United Kingdom go to the polls for a general election with the Conservative Party winning a narrow majority in the House of Commons after the election. 

Preliminary results of final Guardian/ICM campaign poll show Labour and Conservatives tied at 35% each
