### [2015-05-7](/news/2015/05/7/index.md)

# The wreck of the migrant boat which sank in April 2015 with 700 people on board is located by the Italian Navy 136km off the Libyan coast at a depth of 375m. 

The Italian navy has found the sunken wreck of a migrant boat in which 700 people are feared to have died last month.
