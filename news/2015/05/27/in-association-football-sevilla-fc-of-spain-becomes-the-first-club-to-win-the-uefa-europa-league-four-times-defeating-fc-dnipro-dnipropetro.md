### [2015-05-27](/news/2015/05/27/index.md)

# In association football, Sevilla FC of Spain becomes the first club to win the UEFA Europa League four times defeating FC Dnipro Dnipropetrovsk of Ukraine 3-2 with Colombian striker Carlos Bacca scoring twice in the final. 

Dnipro Dnipropetrovsk winger Matheus Nascimento collapses during the Europa League final against Sevilla in Warsaw.
