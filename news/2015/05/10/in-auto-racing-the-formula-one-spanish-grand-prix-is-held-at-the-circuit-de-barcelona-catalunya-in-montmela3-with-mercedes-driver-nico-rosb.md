### [2015-05-10](/news/2015/05/10/index.md)

# In auto racing, the Formula One Spanish Grand Prix is held at the Circuit de Barcelona-Catalunya in Montmelo with Mercedes driver Nico Rosberg finishing ahead of Lewis Hamilton. 

Nico Rosberg wins the Spanish Grand Prix as Mercedes team-mate Lewis Hamilton fights back from a slow start to take second.
