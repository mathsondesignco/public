# Syrian Civil War: Hezbollah and Syrian Army gain strategic territories in Qalamoun Mountains north of Damascus, which was held by opposition factions, particularly al-Nusra Front. 

State TV credits fighters from &quot;Lebanese resistance&quot; with forcing fighters from mountainous area north of Damascus.
