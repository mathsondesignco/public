# 2015 Burundian unrest: After a failed coup d'tat the President of Burundi, Pierre Nkurunziza, fires three Ministers, including the Minister of Defence. 

An official says Burundi&#8217;s president has fired his defense minister and two other Cabinet ministers after a failed coup attempt.
