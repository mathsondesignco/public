# Thirty-fourth Amendment of the Constitution (Marriage Equality) Bill 2015: The counting of votes for yesterday's referendum occurs with the Republic of Ireland becoming the first country to adopt same sex marriage by referendum. 

With all of the results in, Ireland has voted a historic yes in its gay marriage referendum
