### [2015-05-23](/news/2015/05/23/index.md)

# The Salvadoran Archbishop Oscar Romero, assassinated at the start of the Salvadoran Civil War, is beatified in Pope Francis's name, by Cardinal Angelo Amato, Prefect of the Congregation for the Causes of Saints, in San Salvador.
