# War in Donbass: Pro-Russian separatist military leader colonel Alexei Mozgovoi is confirmed killed in an ambush on his car. 

One of the top rebel commanders in eastern Ukraine, Alexei Mozgovoi, is killed in an attack on his car, media say.
