### [2015-05-9](/news/2015/05/9/index.md)

# Ebola virus epidemic in West Africa. The World Health Organization declares that the outbreak of the Ebola virus has ceased in Liberia after weeks of no cases there. 

The World Health Organization (WHO) declares Liberia free of the Ebola virus, as the country has had no new cases in 42 days.
