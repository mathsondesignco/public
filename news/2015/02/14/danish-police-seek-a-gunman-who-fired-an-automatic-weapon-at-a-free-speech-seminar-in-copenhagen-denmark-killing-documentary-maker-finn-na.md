### [2015-02-14](/news/2015/02/14/index.md)

# Danish Police seek a gunman who fired an automatic weapon at a free speech seminar in Copenhagen, Denmark, killing documentary maker Finn Norgaard and injuring Swedish artist Lars Vilks known for controversial drawings of the prophet Mohammed. Among the injured are also three police officers who had been guarding the seminar. 

One person dies after shots are fired at a Copenhagen cafe where a controversial cartoonist was attending a free speech debate.
