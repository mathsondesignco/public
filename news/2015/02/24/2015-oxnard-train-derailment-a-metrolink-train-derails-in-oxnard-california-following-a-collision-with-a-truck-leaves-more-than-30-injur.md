# 2015 Oxnard train derailment: A Metrolink train derails in Oxnard, California, following a collision with a truck, leaves more than 30 injured with four in critical condition. 

California Metrolink train derails, injuring 28, including the engineer, officials say. A triage area is set up at the scene.
