### [2015-02-11](/news/2015/02/11/index.md)

# Rite Aid, one of the largest chains of pharmacies in the United States, announces that it is purchasing EnvisionRx, a pharmacy benefits manager, from TPG Capital, for $2 billion. 

Drugstore operator Rite Aid Corp (RAD.N) agreed to buy pharmacy benefit manager EnvisionRx for about $2 billion, looking to cut drug procurement costs as reimbursement rates fall.
