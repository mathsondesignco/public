# 2014-15 Australian region cyclone season. Cyclone Marcia makes landfall near Shoalwater Bay in central Queensland as a Category 5 tropical cyclone with wind gusts of up to 285 kilometres per hour. 

Tropical Cyclone Marcia is downgraded to a category two storm after battering the central Queensland coast.
