# 2006 Mumbai train bombings. A Mumbai, India court convicts twelve men with one man acquitted for their roles in the bombing which killed 209 people and injured 714. 

A special court convicts 12 out of 13 men accused of planning bombings on commuter trains that killed 187 people.
