### [2015-09-30](/news/2015/09/30/index.md)

# Russian involvement in the Syrian Civil War: The upper chamber of the Russian parliament approves a law allowing the use of the Russian Armed Forces outside its borders, following a request by Russian President Vladimir Putin. 

The escalation comes after Russia&rsquo;s parliament backed the use of military force in Syria.
