# Malaysia Airlines Flight 370:French prosecutors state that they believe "with certainty" that a piece of debris that had washed ashore on Reunion Island came from the missing plane. 

French prosecutors say &quot;with certainty&quot; that a wing part found on Reunion Island in July came from missing flight MH370.
