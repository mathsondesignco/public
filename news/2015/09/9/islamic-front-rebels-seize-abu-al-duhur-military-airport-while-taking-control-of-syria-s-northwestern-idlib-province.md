### [2015-09-9](/news/2015/09/9/index.md)

# Islamic Front rebels seize Abu al-Duhur military airport while taking control of Syria's northwestern Idlib province. 

A coalition of mainly Islamist Syrian rebels seize a key airbase in north-western province of Idlib after a long battle.
