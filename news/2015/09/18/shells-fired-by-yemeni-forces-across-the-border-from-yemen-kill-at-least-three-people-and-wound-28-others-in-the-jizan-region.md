### [2015-09-18](/news/2015/09/18/index.md)

# Shells fired by Yemeni forces across the border from Yemen kill at least three people and wound 28 others in the Jizan region. 

Shelling from across the border in Yemen killed three people in Saudi Arabia Friday and wounded 28, the civil defense agency said.
