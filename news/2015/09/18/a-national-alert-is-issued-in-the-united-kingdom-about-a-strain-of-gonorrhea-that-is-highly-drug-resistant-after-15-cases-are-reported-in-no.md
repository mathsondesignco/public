### [2015-09-18](/news/2015/09/18/index.md)

# A national alert is issued in the United Kingdom about a strain of gonorrhea that is highly drug resistant after 15 cases are reported in northern England. 

A national alert has been issued after an outbreak of highly drug-resistant gonorrhoea in Britain where authorities are extremely worried about its spread.
