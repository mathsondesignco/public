### [2015-09-13](/news/2015/09/13/index.md)

# Turkey-PKK conflict. A PKK car bombing on a checkpoint kills two Turkish police officers and injures five others in south-eastern Turkey. Also, Turkish security forces impose a curfew in the region's largest city, Diyarbakir.
