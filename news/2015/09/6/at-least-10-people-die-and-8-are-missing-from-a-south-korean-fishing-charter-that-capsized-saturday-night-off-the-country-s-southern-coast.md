### [2015-09-6](/news/2015/09/6/index.md)

# At least 10 people die and 8 are missing from a South Korean fishing charter that capsized Saturday night off the country's southern coast. Coast Guard officers said three people survived by clinging to the wreckage for 10 hours. 

At least 10 people have died after clinging to their capsized charter boat for 10 hours.
