### [2015-09-6](/news/2015/09/6/index.md)

# In North Carolina, the Charlotte-Mecklenburg Police Department announces an unprecedented 11 people have been shot, 5 fatally, over the Labor Day weekend, including a boy shot at a birthday party. 

Charlotte-Mecklenburg police are stepping up patrols around the city after a spike in violence over Labor Day weekend.
