### [2015-09-29](/news/2015/09/29/index.md)

# Two police officers are gunned down by suspected PKK militants on the streets of Adana, southern Turkey. 

The attack comes the same day a pro-Kurdish news agency was raided by Turkish police and journalists were arrested
