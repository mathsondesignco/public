# Malcolm Turnbull launches a challenge for the leadership of the Liberal Party of Australia against incumbent Prime Minister Tony Abbott and wins 54&ndash;44. 

The Prime Minister says he is confident of beating challenger Malcolm Turnbull in a Liberal leadership vote this evening.
