### [2015-09-19](/news/2015/09/19/index.md)

# Hungarian security forces confiscate a Croatian train carrying 1000 migrants and disarm 40 Croatian police officers accompanying the migrants. Hungarian authorities repatriate the officers. 

Hungary accuses Croatia of &quot;human smuggling&quot; as its neighbour refuses to stop sending people back across the border.
