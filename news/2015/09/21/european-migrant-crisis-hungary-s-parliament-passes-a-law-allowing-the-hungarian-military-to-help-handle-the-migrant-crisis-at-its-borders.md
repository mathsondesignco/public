### [2015-09-21](/news/2015/09/21/index.md)

# European migrant crisis. Hungary's parliament passes a law allowing the Hungarian military to help handle the migrant crisis at its borders with Serbia and Croatia, including the right to use non-lethal force such as rubber bullets, pyrotechnical devices, tear gas grenades or net guns. 

The latest donation brings total U.S. aid over four years to $4.5 billion, more than any other country.
