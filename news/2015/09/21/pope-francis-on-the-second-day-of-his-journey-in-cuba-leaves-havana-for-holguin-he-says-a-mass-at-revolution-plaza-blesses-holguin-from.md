### [2015-09-21](/news/2015/09/21/index.md)

# Pope Francis, on the second day of his journey in Cuba, leaves Havana for Holguin. He says a Mass at Revolution Plaza, blesses Holguin from the Loma de la Cruz area, arrives in Santiago de Cuba, flies to Santiago de Cuba, meets with the Cuban Bishops at Saint Basil the Great Seminary, and says a prayer with them at the Minor Basilica of the Basilica Santuario Nacional de Nuestra Senora de la Caridad del Cobre (Shrine of Our Lady of del Cobre).
