# September 2015 lunar eclipse. The night of September 28 sees a lunar eclipse (sometimes described as a "blood moon") with asupermoon. 

Seen from the UK, a rare blood-red eclipse started at 1:10am (BST) on Monday - join us for the best of the photographs and reaction
