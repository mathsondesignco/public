### [2015-09-1](/news/2015/09/1/index.md)

# Al-Shabaab claims to have killed scores of African Union troops in a successful attack that captured a compound in Janaale, south of Mogadishu. 

Residents say Somali armed group controls AMISOM base in Janaale, but African Union insists its troops are in control.
