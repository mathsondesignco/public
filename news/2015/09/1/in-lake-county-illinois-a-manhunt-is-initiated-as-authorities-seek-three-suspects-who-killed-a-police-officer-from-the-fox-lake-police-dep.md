### [2015-09-1](/news/2015/09/1/index.md)

# In Lake County, Illinois, a manhunt is initiated as authorities seek three suspects who killed a police officer from the Fox Lake Police Department, Lieutenant Charles Joseph Gliniewicz. 

A Fox Lake police officer was shot Tuesday morning, and a massive manhunt with dogs and helicopters was underway along Rollins Road in Lake County.
