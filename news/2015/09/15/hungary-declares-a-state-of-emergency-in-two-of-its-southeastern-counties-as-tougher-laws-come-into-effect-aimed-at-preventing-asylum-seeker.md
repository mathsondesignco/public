### [2015-09-15](/news/2015/09/15/index.md)

# Hungary declares a state of emergency in two of its southeastern counties as tougher laws come into effect aimed at preventing asylum-seekers and other migrants from entering the country. 

Hungary declares a state of emergency in two southern counties as tough new laws to stop migrants entering illegally come into force.
