### [2015-09-15](/news/2015/09/15/index.md)

# Saudi Arabian-led intervention in Yemen: Houthi militants backed by Yemeni troops reportedly seize control of al-Rabu-ah, a town in Saudi Arabia's southwestern Asir region near the Yemeni border, forcing Saudi forces to pull out of the area. 

Yemeni Ansarullah fighters, backed by allied forces, seize al-Rabu’ah town in Saudi Arabia’s southwestern Asir region.
