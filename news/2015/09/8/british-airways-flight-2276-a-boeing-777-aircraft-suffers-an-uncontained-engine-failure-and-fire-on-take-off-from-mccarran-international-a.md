### [2015-09-8](/news/2015/09/8/index.md)

# British Airways Flight 2276, a Boeing 777 aircraft, suffers an uncontained engine failure and fire on take-off from McCarran International Airport, Las Vegas, Nevada, United States. Fourteen of the 172 on board are injured and taken to a nearby hospital. 

A British Airways plane bound for London catches fire at Las Vegas airport, forcing the evacuation of all 170 people on board on emergency slides.
