### [2013-01-8](/news/2013/01/8/index.md)

# Belfast City Hall flag protests:. In a fifth night of protests in Belfast, protestors throw rocks, "missiles", and petrol bombs at police, with law enforcement responding with water cannons and plastic bullets. 

Police fired plastic bullets during a fifth night of rioting in Belfast over a decision to reduce the days when the union flag is flown at Belfast City Hall.
