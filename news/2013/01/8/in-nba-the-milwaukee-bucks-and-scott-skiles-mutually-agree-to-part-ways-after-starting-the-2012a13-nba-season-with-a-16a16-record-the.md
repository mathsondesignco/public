### [2013-01-8](/news/2013/01/8/index.md)

# In NBA, the Milwaukee Bucks and Scott Skiles mutually agree to part ways after starting the 2012-13 NBA season with a 16-16 record. The team promotes assistant Jim Boylan as interim head coach. 

 Assistant Jim Boylan will assume head coaching duties for the remainder of the season. Boylan, 57, has been the lead assistant coach for the Bucks the past four seasons. NBAE / Gett
