### [2013-01-23](/news/2013/01/23/index.md)

# European champions Chelsea are defeated by Swansea City after a 0-0 draw in the second semifinal match; the game is marred by an incident involving Chelsea midfielder Eden Hazard, who is sent off after violently kicking a ball boy in the midriff during the game. 

As the FA investigates the Chelsea star&#039;s kick, details emerge of the privileged &quot;hustler&quot; lifestyle of ballboy Charlie Morgan.
