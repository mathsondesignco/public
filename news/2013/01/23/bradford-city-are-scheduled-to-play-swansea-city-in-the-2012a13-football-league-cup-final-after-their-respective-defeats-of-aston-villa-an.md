### [2013-01-23](/news/2013/01/23/index.md)

# Bradford City are scheduled to play Swansea City in the 2012-13 Football League Cup Final after their respective defeats of Aston Villa and Chelsea in the semifinals. 

Swansea reach the Capital One Cup final with a 2-0 aggregate win over Chelsea, who have Eden Hazard sent off for kicking a ball boy.
