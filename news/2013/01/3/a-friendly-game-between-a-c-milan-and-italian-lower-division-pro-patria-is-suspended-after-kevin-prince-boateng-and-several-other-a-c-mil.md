# A friendly game between A.C. Milan and  Italian lower division Pro Patria is suspended after Kevin-Prince Boateng and several other A.C. Milan were the target of racists chants by Pro Patria crowd. 

A friendly between AC Milan and Italian lower division club Pro Patria is suspended after players walk off because of racist chants.
