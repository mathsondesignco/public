# Belfast City Hall flag protests:Violent protests occur in Northern Ireland over flying the Union Jack at Belfast City Hall, twenty-nine police officers are injured. 

Twenty-nine police officers are injured in rioting in east Belfast following a loyalist protest over the union flag.
