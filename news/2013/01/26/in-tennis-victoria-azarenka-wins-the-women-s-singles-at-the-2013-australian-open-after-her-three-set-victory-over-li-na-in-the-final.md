### [2013-01-26](/news/2013/01/26/index.md)

# In tennis, Victoria Azarenka wins the women's singles at the 2013 Australian Open after her three-set victory over Li Na in the final. 

Victoria Azarenka retains her Australian Open title and kept hold of the world number one spot with victory over Li Na in the Melbourne final.
