# War in Afghanistan (2001-present):A suicide bombing at a market in the Afghan city of Kunduz kills at least ten people and wounds up to twenty others. 

At least 10 people have been killed and many injured in a suicide attack in a busy market in the north-eastern city of Kunduz, officials say.
