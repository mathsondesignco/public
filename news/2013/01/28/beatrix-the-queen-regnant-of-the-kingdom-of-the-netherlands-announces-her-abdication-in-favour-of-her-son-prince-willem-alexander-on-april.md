### [2013-01-28](/news/2013/01/28/index.md)

# Beatrix the Queen regnant of the Kingdom of the Netherlands announces her abdication in favour of her son, Prince Willem-Alexander, on April 30, 2013. 

Queen Beatrix of the Netherlands announces she is abdicating, and will hand the crown to her son, Prince Willem-Alexander, on 30 April.
