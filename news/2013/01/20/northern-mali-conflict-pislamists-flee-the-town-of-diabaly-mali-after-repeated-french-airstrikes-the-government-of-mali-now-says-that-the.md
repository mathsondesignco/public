# Northern Mali conflict:Islamists flee the town of Diabaly, Mali, after repeated French airstrikes. The government of Mali now says that they control the city. 

Malian forces appeared close to recapturing key town of Diabaly where bands of al Qaeda-linked fighters had holed up
