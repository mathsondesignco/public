### [2013-01-25](/news/2013/01/25/index.md)

# Tennessee Highway Patrol officer Michael Slagle is killed after his car hit an ice patch and crashed and the responding fire truck hit the same ice patch and fell on top of his vehicle in northeast Knox County, Tennessee, United States. 

Trooper Michael Slagle reportedly suffered a fatal heart attack during the incident Friday morning.
