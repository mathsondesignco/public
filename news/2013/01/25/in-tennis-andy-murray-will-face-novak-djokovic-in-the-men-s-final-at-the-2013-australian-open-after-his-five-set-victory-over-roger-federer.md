### [2013-01-25](/news/2013/01/25/index.md)

# In tennis, Andy Murray will face Novak Djokovic in the men's final at the 2013 Australian Open after his five-set victory over Roger Federer in the semifinal. 

Andy Murray beats Roger Federer 6-4 6-7 6-3 6-7 6-2 to set up an Australian Open final against Novak Djokovic.
