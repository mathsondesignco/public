### [2013-01-4](/news/2013/01/4/index.md)

# 9 people are killed by a car bomb at a petrol station in the Syrian capital of Damascus. The bomb reportedly hit the Barzeh al-Balad district, as large numbers of people were queuing for fuel. 

At least nine people have been killed and many injured by a car bomb at a petrol station in Damascus, say activists.
