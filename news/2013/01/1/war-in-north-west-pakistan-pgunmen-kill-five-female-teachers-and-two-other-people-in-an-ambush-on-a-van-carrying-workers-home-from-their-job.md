### [2013-01-1](/news/2013/01/1/index.md)

# War in North-West Pakistan:Gunmen kill five female teachers and two other people in an ambush on a van carrying workers home from their jobs at a community center in Khyber Pakhtunkhwa province, Pakistan. 

Seven charity workers, six of them women, are killed by gunmen in north-west Pakistan, police say.
