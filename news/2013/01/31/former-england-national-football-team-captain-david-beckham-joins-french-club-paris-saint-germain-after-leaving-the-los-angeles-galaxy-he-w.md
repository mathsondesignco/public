# Former England national football team captain David Beckham joins French club Paris Saint-Germain after leaving the Los Angeles Galaxy. He will also donate his entire salary to charity. 

Former England captain David Beckham arrives in Paris to complete the formalities of a deadline day move to Paris St-Germain.
