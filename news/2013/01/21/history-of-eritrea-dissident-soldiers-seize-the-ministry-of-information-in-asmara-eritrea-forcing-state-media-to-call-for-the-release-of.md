### [2013-01-21](/news/2013/01/21/index.md)

# History of Eritrea:. Dissident soldiers seize the ministry of information in Asmara, Eritrea, forcing state media to call for the release of political prisoners. Other buildings are also surrounded while television and radio later went off air. The apparent coup attempt against President Isaias Afewerki reportedly fails. 

Eritrean soldiers surround the ministry of information as state TV is taken off air, unconfirmed reports say.
