### [2013-01-21](/news/2013/01/21/index.md)

# The public portion of Barack Obama's second inauguration takes place at nation's capital in Washington, D.C., a day after he was officially sworn into office in the White House for his second term as President of the United States. 

Barack Obama takes the oath of office in Washington DC to inaugurate his second term as US president, before hundreds of thousands of spectators.
