# Northern Mali conflict (2012-present):: French forces prepare an attack on Timbuktu to defeat Islamists, as more African troops are sent into Mali. 

French and Malian forces move on Timbuktu after recapturing other northern towns from rebels, as African states discuss sending more troops.
