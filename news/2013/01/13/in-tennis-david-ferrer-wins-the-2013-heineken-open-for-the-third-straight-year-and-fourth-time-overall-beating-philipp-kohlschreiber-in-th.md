### [2013-01-13](/news/2013/01/13/index.md)

# In tennis, David Ferrer wins the 2013 Heineken Open for the third straight year and fourth time overall, beating Philipp Kohlschreiber in the final with a 7-6, 6-1 score in Auckland, New Zealand, as part of the 2013 ATP World Tour. 

Top seed David Ferrer beat second-seeded Philipp Kohlschreiber 7-6 (5), 6-1 on Saturday to win the Heineken Open title for the third straight year and fourth time overall.
