### [2013-01-24](/news/2013/01/24/index.md)

# French football is reported to be stunned at yesterday evening's red card for Chelsea midfielder Eden Hazard, who violently kicked a ball boy in the midriff during the 2012-13 Football League Cup game in Wales. The Belgian midfielder, who previously played in Ligue 1, was thought to have had an exemplary disciplinary record before the assault.
