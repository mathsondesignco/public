# Libyan factional fighting:. The Foreign and Commonwealth Office urges British nationals to leave the Libyan city of Benghazi immediately due to a "specific and imminent" threat to Westerners. 

British and other foreign nationals are advised to leave the Libyan city of Benghazi immediately because of an &quot;imminent&quot; threat to Westerners.
