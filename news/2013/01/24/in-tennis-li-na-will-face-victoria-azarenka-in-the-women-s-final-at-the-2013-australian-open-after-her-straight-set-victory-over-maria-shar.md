### [2013-01-24](/news/2013/01/24/index.md)

# In tennis, Li Na will face Victoria Azarenka in the women's final at the 2013 Australian Open after her straight-set victory over Maria Sharapova in the semifinal. 

Victoria Azarenka overcomes Sloane Stephens and set up an Australian Open final with Li Na who beat Maria Sharapova.
