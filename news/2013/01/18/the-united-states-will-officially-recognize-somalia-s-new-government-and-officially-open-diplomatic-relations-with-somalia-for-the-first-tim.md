### [2013-01-18](/news/2013/01/18/index.md)

# The United States will officially recognize Somalia's new government and officially open diplomatic relations with Somalia for the first time since the Battle of Mogadishu. 

The United States has officially recognised the government of Somalia for the first time in more than 20 years.
