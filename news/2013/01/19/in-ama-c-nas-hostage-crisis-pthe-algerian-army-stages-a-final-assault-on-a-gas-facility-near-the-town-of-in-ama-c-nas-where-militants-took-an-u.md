### [2013-01-19](/news/2013/01/19/index.md)

# In Amenas hostage crisis:The Algerian army stages a final assault on a gas facility near the town of In Amenas, where militants took an unknown number of foreign workers hostage on January 16. Totally at least 23 hostages and 32 terrorists were killed for the four days of crisis. 

Algerian troops end a siege at a gas facility in the Sahara desert which left 23 hostages and 32 kidnappers dead, officials say, with several Western workers still missing.
