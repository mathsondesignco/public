### [2013-01-19](/news/2013/01/19/index.md)

# The 2013 Dakar Rally ends today in Santiago, Chile. Stephane Peterhansel wins a record 11th overall Dakar Rally, while French compatriot Cyril Despres captures a fifth motorcycling crown. 

Stephane Peterhansel clinched his 11th Dakar Rally victory with a calm run to the finish in Santiago on Saturday
