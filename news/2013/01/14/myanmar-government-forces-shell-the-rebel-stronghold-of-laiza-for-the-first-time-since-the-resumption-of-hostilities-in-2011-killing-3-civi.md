### [2013-01-14](/news/2013/01/14/index.md)

# Myanmar government forces shell the rebel stronghold of Laiza for the first time since the resumption of hostilities in 2011, killing 3 civilians and injuring 6 others. 

At least three civilians killed and six wounded in attack on Kachin Independence Army headquarters in Laiza, rebels say.
