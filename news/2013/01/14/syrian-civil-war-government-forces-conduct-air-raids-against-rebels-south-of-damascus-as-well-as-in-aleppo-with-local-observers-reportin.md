### [2013-01-14](/news/2013/01/14/index.md)

# Syrian civil war:: Government forces conduct air raids against rebels south of Damascus, as well as in Aleppo, with local observers reporting dozens of deaths, including children. 

Eight children reported killed in airstrikes southwest of capital, amid calls for Syria to be charged with war crimes.
