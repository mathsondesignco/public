### [2013-02-25](/news/2013/02/25/index.md)

# Syrian civil war:For the first time since the start of the Syrian civil war, President Bashar al-Assad's government offers to talk with rebels in hopes of a diplomatic solution to the conflict. 

The Syrian opposition reverses its decision to boycott international talks in Rome, after the US and the UK promise to increase their support.
