### [2013-02-9](/news/2013/02/9/index.md)

# Four people are wounded after a shooting in Bourbon Street, New Orleans, United States, while it was crowded with party-goers and people setting up for Mardi Gras parades. 

The New Orleans police said they were seeking three men in the shooting, which left one man critically wounded after an argument among partygoers.
