### [2013-02-10](/news/2013/02/10/index.md)

# War in Afghanistan (2001-present):. American general Joseph F. Dunford, Jr. takes over command of NATO forces in Afghanistan, replacing John R. Allen. 

NATO forces in Afghanistan get a new commander as the coalition prepares to pull out combat troops.
