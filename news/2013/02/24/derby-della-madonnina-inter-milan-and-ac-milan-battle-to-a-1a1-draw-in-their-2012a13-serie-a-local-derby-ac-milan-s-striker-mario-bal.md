### [2013-02-24](/news/2013/02/24/index.md)

# Derby della Madonnina:  Inter Milan and AC Milan battle to a 1-1 draw in their 2012-13 Serie A local derby. AC Milan's striker Mario Balotelli was the target of racial abuse from fans of his ex-club Inter Milan. 

Inter Milan are fined 50,000 euros after their fans are found guilty of racial abuse towards AC Milan striker Mario Balotelli.
