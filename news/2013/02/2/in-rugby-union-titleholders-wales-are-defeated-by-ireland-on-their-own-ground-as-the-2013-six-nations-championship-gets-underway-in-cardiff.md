# In rugby union, titleholders Wales are defeated by Ireland on their own ground as the 2013 Six Nations Championship gets underway in Cardiff. 

Ireland hold off a stirring second-half Wales revival to launch their Six Nations campaign with a thrilling victory in Cardiff.
