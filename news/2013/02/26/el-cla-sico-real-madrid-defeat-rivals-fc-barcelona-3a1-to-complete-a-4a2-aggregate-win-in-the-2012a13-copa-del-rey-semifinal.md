# El Clasico: Real Madrid defeat rivals FC Barcelona 3-1 to complete a 4-2 aggregate win in the 2012-13 Copa del Rey semifinal. 

Real Madrid inflict a painful 3-1 defeat on Barcelona, to complete a 4-2 aggregate win in the Copa del Rey semi-final.
