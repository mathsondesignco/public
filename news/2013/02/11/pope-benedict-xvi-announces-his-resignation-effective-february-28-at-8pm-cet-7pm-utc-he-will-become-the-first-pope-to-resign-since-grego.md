### [2013-02-11](/news/2013/02/11/index.md)

# Pope Benedict XVI announces his resignation, effective February 28 at 8pm CET (7pm UTC). He will become the first pope to resign since Gregory XII in 1415. 

Pope Benedict XVI becomes the first pontiff to resign in nearly 600 years, saying his health is deteriorating.
