### [2013-02-13](/news/2013/02/13/index.md)

# Syrian civil war:Rebels clash with the Syrian military at an army base protecting Aleppo's international airport. The opposition claims that 40 Syrian soldiers were killed in the fighting, as large parts of the base are overrun. 

Assault on al-Jarrah airfield in Aleppo comes a day after opposition seized a key dam.
