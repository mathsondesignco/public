### [2013-02-6](/news/2013/02/6/index.md)

# 2013 Solomon Islands earthquake:A tsunami warning is issued for a wide range of islands in the South Pacific after a magnitude 8.0 earthquake occurs off the coast of the Solomon Islands. 

SIX bodies, including that of a child, have been found in the sodden wreckage left by a tsunami that smashed into villages in the Solomon Islands.
