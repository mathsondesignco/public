### [2013-02-4](/news/2013/02/4/index.md)

# Europol announces that a total of 680 football matches are under investigation for match fixing.  Of these, 380 are in Europe, including World Cup and European Championship qualifying matches as well as Champions League matches in England.  

A Champions League tie played in England is one of 680 matches investigators believe was fixed by a crime syndicate.
