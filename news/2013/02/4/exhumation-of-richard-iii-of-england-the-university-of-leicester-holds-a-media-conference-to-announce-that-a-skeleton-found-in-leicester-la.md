### [2013-02-4](/news/2013/02/4/index.md)

# Exhumation of Richard III of England: The University of Leicester holds a media conference to announce that a skeleton found in Leicester last year during an archaelogical dig is that of former King Richard III of England. 

An image of a skull which historians believe could be that of Richard III is released ahead of DNA test results being announced.
