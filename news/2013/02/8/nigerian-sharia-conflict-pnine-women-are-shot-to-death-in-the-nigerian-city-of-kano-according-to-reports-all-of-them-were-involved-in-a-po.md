### [2013-02-8](/news/2013/02/8/index.md)

# Nigerian Sharia conflict:Nine women are shot to death in the Nigerian city of Kano. According to reports, all of them were involved in a polio vaccination policy and were most probably killed by the Islamist movement Boko Haram, which has previously threatened to target those involved in vaccinations.
