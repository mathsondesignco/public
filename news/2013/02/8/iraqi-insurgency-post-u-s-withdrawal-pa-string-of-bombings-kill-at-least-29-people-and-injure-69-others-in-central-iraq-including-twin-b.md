### [2013-02-8](/news/2013/02/8/index.md)

# Iraqi insurgency (post-U.S. withdrawal):A string of bombings kill at least 29 people and injure 69 others in central Iraq, including twin blasts in Baghdad's Kadhimiya neighborhood, as well as the city of Hilla. 

At least 31 people killed and dozens wounded in car bombings in Shia-dominated areas.
