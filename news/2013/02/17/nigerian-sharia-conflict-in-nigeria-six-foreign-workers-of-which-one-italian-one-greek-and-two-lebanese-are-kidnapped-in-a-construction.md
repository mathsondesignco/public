### [2013-02-17](/news/2013/02/17/index.md)

# Nigerian Sharia conflict:: In Nigeria six foreign workers, of which one Italian, one Greek and two Lebanese, are kidnapped in a construction site, one security guard is killed. 

Seven foreign workers have been kidnapped by gunmen in northern Nigeria, in the biggest such incident in recent years.
