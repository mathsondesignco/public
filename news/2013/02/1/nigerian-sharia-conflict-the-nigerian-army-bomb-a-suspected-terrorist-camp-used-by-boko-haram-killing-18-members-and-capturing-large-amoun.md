### [2013-02-1](/news/2013/02/1/index.md)

# Nigerian Sharia conflict:. The Nigerian army bomb a suspected terrorist camp used by Boko Haram killing 18 members and capturing large amounts ammunition, guns and weapons. 

The JTF said it lost one of its officials in the attack in Northern Nigeria
