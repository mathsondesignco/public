### [2013-02-28](/news/2013/02/28/index.md)

# Iraqi insurgency (post-U.S. withdrawal):At least 23 people are killed and 60 others injured in a series of bombings in Shiite districts of Baghdad and surrounding suburbs. 

Bombings target predominantly Shia Muslim areas in and around Baghdad against a backdrop of rising sectarian tensions.
