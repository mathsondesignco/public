### [2013-03-12](/news/2013/03/12/index.md)

# NASA holds a media conference to announce new discoveries about Mars made by the Curiosity rover. It was found that the planet could have once supported life. 

NASA will reveal new discoveries about Mars gleaned by Curiosity rover on March 12, 2013.
