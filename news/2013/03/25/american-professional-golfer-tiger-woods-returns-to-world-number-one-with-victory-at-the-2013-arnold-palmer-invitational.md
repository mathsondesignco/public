### [2013-03-25](/news/2013/03/25/index.md)

# American professional golfer Tiger Woods returns to World Number One with victory at the 2013 Arnold Palmer Invitational. 

Tiger Woods, who last held the No. 1 ranking in October 2010, fell as low as No. 58 as he coped with a personal crisis and injuries to his left leg.
