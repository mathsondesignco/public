# Terrorism in Pakistan:A suspected militant suicide bombing at a court and prison complex in the Pakistani city of Peshawar kills at least 4 people and injures 47 others. 

Militants kill at least four people and injure 29 in an attack on a court and prison complex in the north-west Pakistani city of Peshawar.
