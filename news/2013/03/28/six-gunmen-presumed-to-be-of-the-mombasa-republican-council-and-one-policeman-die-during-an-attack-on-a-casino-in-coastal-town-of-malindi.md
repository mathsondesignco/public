# Six gunmen (presumed to be of the Mombasa Republican Council) and one policeman die during an attack on a casino in coastal town of Malindi, Kenya. 

Six gunmen and one policeman die in fierce gunbattles during raid by mob on casino in coastal town of Malindi.
