### [2013-03-11](/news/2013/03/11/index.md)

# The U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission accuses the state of Illinois of securities fraud by misleading investors about the health of the state's public pension fund. 

In announcing a settlement, the agency said the state had misled investors about the health of its pension system.
