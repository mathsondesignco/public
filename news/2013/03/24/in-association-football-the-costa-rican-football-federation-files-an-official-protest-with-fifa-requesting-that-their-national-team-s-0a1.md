### [2013-03-24](/news/2013/03/24/index.md)

# In association football, the Costa Rican Football Federation files an official protest with FIFA requesting that their national team's 0-1 loss to the USA on March 22 in near-blizzard conditions at Dick's Sporting Goods Park in Commerce City, Colorado, be replayed.
