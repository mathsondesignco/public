# 2012-13 Cypriot financial crisis: Cyprus President Nicos Anastasiades begins a series of emergency meetings in Brussels in a last-ditch attempt to finalize a bailout. 

Eurozone finance ministers agree a deal on a bailout for Cyprus to prevent its banking system collapsing, but the island faces deep recession.
