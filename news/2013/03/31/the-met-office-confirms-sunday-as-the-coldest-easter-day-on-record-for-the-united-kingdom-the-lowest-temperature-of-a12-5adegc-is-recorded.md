### [2013-03-31](/news/2013/03/31/index.md)

# The Met Office confirms Sunday as the coldest Easter Day on record for the United Kingdom. The lowest temperature of -12.5degC is recorded in Braemar in the Scottish Highlands. 

Easter Sunday is the coldest Easter day since modern records began, with temperatures as low as -12.5C in Braemar in the Scottish Highlands.
