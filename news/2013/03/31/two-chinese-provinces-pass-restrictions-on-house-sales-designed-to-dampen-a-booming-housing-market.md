### [2013-03-31](/news/2013/03/31/index.md)

# Two Chinese provinces pass restrictions on house sales designed to dampen a booming housing market. 

Beijing and Shanghai announced they would put in place new restrictions and penalties on housing sales to ensure that prices do not continue to soar, fueling what many say is a real estate bubble.
