### [2013-03-31](/news/2013/03/31/index.md)

# 2013 NCAA Men's Division I Basketball Tournament:University of Louisville basketball player Kevin Ware breaks his right leg during a game at Lucas Oil Stadium in Indianapolis, Indiana. He was taken to Methodist Hospital of Indianapolis. 

Top overall seed Louisville will face Wichita State at the Georgia Dome next Saturday, while Michigan takes on Syracuse in the other national semifinal. The winners advance to the April 8 championship.
