# 2013 Mayflower oil spill: An Exxon Mobil crude oil pipeline ruptures near the town of Mayflower in the U.S. state of Arkansas, spilling thousands of barrels from the tar sands. 

Thousands of barrels spill near town of Mayflower in Arkansas state in second crude oil spill in the US this week.
