### [2013-03-27](/news/2013/03/27/index.md)

# South Thailand insurgency:. The Thailand government prepares for peace talks with Muslim separatist groups with the hope of ending a decade-long insurgency. 

Five people are reported killed as Thai government begins talks with separatists to end decades-long armed struggle.
