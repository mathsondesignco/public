### [2013-03-5](/news/2013/03/5/index.md)

# A black American political candidate "was dragged and set on fire" during his murder in the U.S. state of Mississippi, say his family. The corpse of Marco McMillian was found bruised and burned near a levee on the Mississippi River. 

One of the first openly gay political candidates in the southern US state of Mississippi was beaten, dragged and set on fire during his murder, his family has claimed.
