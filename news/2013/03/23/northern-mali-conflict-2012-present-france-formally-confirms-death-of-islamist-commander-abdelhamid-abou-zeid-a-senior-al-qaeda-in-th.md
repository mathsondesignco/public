# Northern Mali conflict (2012-present):: France formally confirms death of Islamist commander Abdelhamid Abou Zeid, a senior al-Qaeda in the Islamic Maghreb in February, after DNA tests. 

The French presidency confirms &quot;with certainty&quot; reports that Islamist commander Abdelhamid Abou Zeid was killed in fighting in Mali in late February.
