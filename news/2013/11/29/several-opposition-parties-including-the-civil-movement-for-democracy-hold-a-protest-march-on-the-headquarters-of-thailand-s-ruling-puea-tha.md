# Several opposition parties including the Civil Movement for Democracy hold a protest march on the headquarters of Thailand's ruling Puea Thai party in Bangkok. 

About 1,500 anti-government protesters have forced their way into the compound of the Royal Thai Army headquarters in Bangkok.
