### [2013-11-5](/news/2013/11/5/index.md)

# Chicago chef Charlie Trotter, who operated his namesake restaurant in Chicago (from 1987 until its closure in 2012), is found dead by his son at his Lincoln Park home. 

Famed Chicago chef Charlie Trotter died this morning, according to a family friend and police sources. Trotter, 54, was found unconscious and not breathing.
