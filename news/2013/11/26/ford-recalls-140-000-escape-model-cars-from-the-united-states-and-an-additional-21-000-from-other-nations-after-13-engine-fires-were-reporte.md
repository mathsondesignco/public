### [2013-11-26](/news/2013/11/26/index.md)

# Ford recalls 140,000 Escape model cars from the United States and an additional 21,000 from other nations after 13 engine fires were reported. 

Fire hazards from possible oil and fuel leaks are cited for the 1.6-liter 4-cyinder engine.
