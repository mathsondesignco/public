### [2013-11-15](/news/2013/11/15/index.md)

# Typhoon Haiyan:The United Nations tallies the death toll to around 4,400. The Red Cross estimates that around 20,000 are still missing, many of whom are feared dead. This is contrary to a previous estimate by Benigno Aquino III, who put the death toll to a low 2,000.
