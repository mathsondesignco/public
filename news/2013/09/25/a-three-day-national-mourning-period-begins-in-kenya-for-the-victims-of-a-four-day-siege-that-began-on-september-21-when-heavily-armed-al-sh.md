### [2013-09-25](/news/2013/09/25/index.md)

# A three-day national mourning period begins in Kenya for the victims of a four-day siege that began on September 21 when heavily armed Al-Shabaab militants stormed the Westgate shopping mall in the capital, Nairobi, killing 61 civilians and 6 soldiers. 

Kenya mourns the victims of the militant attack at a Nairobi shopping centre as dramatic footage of a family being rescued emerges.
