### [2013-09-8](/news/2013/09/8/index.md)

# The International Olympic Committee votes to reinstate wrestling as an Olympic sport at the 2020 Summer Olympics in Tokyo. 

Wrestling has regained its place in the 2020 Tokyo Olympics after beating out squash and a combined baseball-softball bid
