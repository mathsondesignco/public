### [2013-09-24](/news/2013/09/24/index.md)

# In road cycling, Ellen van Dijk won the women's time trial at the 2013 UCI Road World Championships. 

The Dutch cyclist led from start to finish along the 21.8km course and created a 24.1 second gap with No 2 Linda Villumsen.
