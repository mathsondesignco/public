### [2013-09-11](/news/2013/09/11/index.md)

# Syrian civil war:The UN accuses both the Syrian government and, to a lesser extent, the rebels of war crimes. Out of 9 massacres they investigated, 8 were attributed to the Syrian Army. The remaining massacre was attributed to insurgents. 

Investigators say government forces and rebel fighters have killed, tortured and raped on a huge scale.
