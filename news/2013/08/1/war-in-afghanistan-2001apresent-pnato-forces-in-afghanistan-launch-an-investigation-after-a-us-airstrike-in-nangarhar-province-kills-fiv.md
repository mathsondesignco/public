# War in Afghanistan (2001-present):NATO forces in Afghanistan launch an investigation after a US airstrike in Nangarhar Province kills five Afghan policemen and injures another two. 

Five Afghan policeman die in a US air strike during a battle with insurgents near a police checkpoint.
