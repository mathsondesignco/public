### [2013-08-1](/news/2013/08/1/index.md)

# NATO forces in Afghanistan launch an investigation after a US airstrike in Nangarhar Province kills five Afghan policemen and injures another two. 

Five Afghan policeman die in a US air strike during a battle with insurgents near a police checkpoint.
