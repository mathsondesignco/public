### [2013-08-14](/news/2013/08/14/index.md)

# Nigerian Sharia conflict:: Two Boko Haram commanders Mohammad Bama and Abubakar Zakariya Yau are killed by the Nigerian army in a gun battle in the north-east Adamawa State. 

The radical group fighting for an Islamic state in Nigeria is seen as the biggest threat to the West African nation&#039;s stability.
