### [2013-04-2](/news/2013/04/2/index.md)

# In the 2012-13 UEFA Champions League quarter-finals, Paris Saint-Germain secures a 2-2 draw against Barcelona while Bayern Munich beats Juventus 2-0. 

Zlatan Ibrahimovic scores as Paris St-Germain draw with his former side Barcelona in an absorbing Champions League tie.
