### [2013-04-2](/news/2013/04/2/index.md)

# War in North-West Pakistan:At least 7 people are killed in an attack by militants, suspected to be Tehrik-i-Taliban Pakistan, on a power station in the Pakistani city of Peshawar. Ten hostages are reportedly taken by the militants. 

At least seven people are killed as dozens of heavily-armed militants attack an electricity plant in the Pakistani city of Peshawar, officials say.
