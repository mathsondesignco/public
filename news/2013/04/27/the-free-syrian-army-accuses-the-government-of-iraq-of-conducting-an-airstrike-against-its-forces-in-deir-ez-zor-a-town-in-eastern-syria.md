### [2013-04-27](/news/2013/04/27/index.md)

# The Free Syrian Army accuses the government of Iraq of conducting an airstrike against its forces in Deir ez-Zor, a town in eastern Syria. 

Activists say an attack in the east may have originated in Iraq.
