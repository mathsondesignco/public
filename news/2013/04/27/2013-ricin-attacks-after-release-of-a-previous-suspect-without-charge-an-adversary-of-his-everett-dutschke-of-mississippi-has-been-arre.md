# 2013 ricin attacks:: After release of a previous suspect without charge, an adversary of his, Everett Dutschke of Mississippi, has been arrested for mailing letters containing ricin to the President of the United States, a senator, and a federal judge. 

A man is charged in the US state of Mississippi in connection with sending letters containing the poison ricin to President Barack Obama and others.
