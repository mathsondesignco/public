### [2013-04-1](/news/2013/04/1/index.md)

# A report alleges that Ed T. Rush, head of men's basketball officiating for the Pac-12 Conference, offered $5,000 or a trip to Cancun to any referee who would call a technical foul against or eject Arizona Wildcats head coach Sean Miller during the 2013 Pac-12 Conference Men's Basketball Tournament.
