# War in Afghanistan (2001-present):At least 46 people are killed and more than 100 injured following an attack by Taliban militants armed with suicide vests on a courthouse in Farah, Afghanistan. 

A suicide bomb and gun attack on a courthouse in the Afghan city of Farah leaves more than 50 people dead and 90 wounded, most of them civilians.
