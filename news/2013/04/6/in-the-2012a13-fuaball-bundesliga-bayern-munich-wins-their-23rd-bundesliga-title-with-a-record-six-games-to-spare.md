### [2013-04-6](/news/2013/04/6/index.md)

# In the 2012-13 Fussball-Bundesliga, Bayern Munich wins their 23rd Bundesliga title with a record six games to spare. 

Bayern Munich clinch the Bundesliga title with six games to spare after a win 1-0 at Eintracht Frankfurt.
