### [2013-04-6](/news/2013/04/6/index.md)

# Iraqi insurgency (post-U.S. withdrawal):. At least 22 people are killed and more than 60 others injured when a suicide bomber blows himself up in a busy election campaign tent in the Iraqi city of Baquba. 

A suicide bomber killed 22 people and wounded 60 in a crowded election campaign tent in the Iraqi city of Baquba on Saturday, police and medics said.
