### [2013-04-7](/news/2013/04/7/index.md)

# In road bicycle racing, Fabian Cancellara wins 2013 Paris-Roubaix ahead of Sep Vanmarcke and Niki Terpstra. 

The heavy favorite settles accounts first on the road, then on the Roubaix velodrome, taking his third victory ahead of Sep Vanmarcke
