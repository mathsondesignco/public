### [2013-04-18](/news/2013/04/18/index.md)

# Authorities in the United Arab Emirates say they have arrested seven suspected members of an al-Qaeda-linked "terrorist cell" seeking to carry out operations in the country and the region. 

The statement, carried by the official WAM news agency, says the latest arrests include seven people of Arab nationalities, but gave no further details.
