### [2013-04-10](/news/2013/04/10/index.md)

# Liu Zhijun, the leader of the construction of the Chinese bullet train system and Minister of Railways, is arrested on corruption charges for allegedly taking bribes and abusing his power as Minister of Railways. 

China charges once-powerful former Railways Minister Liu Zhijun with corruption and abuse of power, state media reports.
