### [2013-04-5](/news/2013/04/5/index.md)

# War in Afghanistan (2001-present):: An explosive-laden donkey is used in an attack on a police security post in the Alingar District of Laghman Province in eastern Afghanistan, killing a policeman and wounding three civilians. 

An official says a bomb attached to a donkey has exploded, killing a policeman and wounded three civilians.
