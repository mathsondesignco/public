### [2013-04-28](/news/2013/04/28/index.md)

# War in North-West Pakistan:. At least eight people are dead and dozens injured as a result of Tehrik-i-Taliban Pakistan attacks on election candidates in Kohat and Peshawar. 

The attacks, mostly by the Taliban, added to a growing toll of militant violence in the run-up to elections scheduled for May 11.
