# Boston Marathon bombings:: Two explosions in Boston, United States, near the finish line of the 2013 Boston Marathon leave 3 people dead and 183 injured. 

At least three people are killed and more than 100 injured, some seriously, in two explosions at the finish line of the Boston Marathon.
