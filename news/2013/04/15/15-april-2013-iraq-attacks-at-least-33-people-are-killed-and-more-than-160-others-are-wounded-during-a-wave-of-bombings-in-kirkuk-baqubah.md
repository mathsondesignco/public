### [2013-04-15](/news/2013/04/15/index.md)

# 15 April 2013 Iraq attacks:. At least 33 people are killed and more than 160 others are wounded during a wave of bombings in Kirkuk, Baqubah, Tikrit, Fallujah, Nassiriyah, Mussayab and Baghdad. 

Officials say at least 31 people have been killed and more than 200 others wounded in a series of early morning explosions across Iraq.
