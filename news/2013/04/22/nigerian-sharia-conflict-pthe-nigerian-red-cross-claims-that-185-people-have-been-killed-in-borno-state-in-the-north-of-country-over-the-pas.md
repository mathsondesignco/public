### [2013-04-22](/news/2013/04/22/index.md)

# Nigerian Sharia conflict:The Nigerian Red Cross claims that 185 people have been killed in Borno State in the north of country over the past few days as a result of fighting between militants and government state forces. The local military claim the numbers are inflated.
