# 2013 Bangalore blast:Sixteen people, including eight policemen, are injured in a bomb blast at a BJP office in Bangalore, India, weeks ahead of elections in the state of Karnataka. 

Sixteen people are injured in an explosion near the office of a political party in the southern Indian city of Bangalore, police say.
