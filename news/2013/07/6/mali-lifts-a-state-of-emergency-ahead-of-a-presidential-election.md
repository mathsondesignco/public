### [2013-07-6](/news/2013/07/6/index.md)

# Mali lifts a state of emergency ahead of a presidential election. 

Mali has lifted its state of emergency to allow campaigning for a presidential election being held on July 28 after a French-led offensive to drive Islamist militants from the West African country.
