### [2013-07-2](/news/2013/07/2/index.md)

# Portuguese political crisis, 2013:: Portuguese Minister of Foreign Affairs Paulo Portas quits after disagreeing with the prime minister's choice for Finance Minister, Maria Luis Albuquerque. The Minister for the Environment and Agriculture and the Minister of Social Security are also set to resign.
