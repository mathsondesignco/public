### [2013-07-21](/news/2013/07/21/index.md)

# Voters in Japan go to the polls for a House of Councillors (Japan's upper house) election with the governing coalition led by Shinzo Abe winning control of the upper house. 

Reviving the long-stagnant economy is the top priority for voters.
