### [2013-07-26](/news/2013/07/26/index.md)

# Qatar Airways grounds one of its Boeing 787 Dreamliners following a "minor" technical issue, as pressure mounts on Boeing over possible new electrical problems with the newly released jet. 

Qatar Airways said on Friday it had taken one of its Boeing 787 Dreamliners out of service following what it described as a &quot;minor&quot; technical issue, as pressure mounted on the plane maker over possible new electrical problems with the advanced jet.
