### [2013-07-26](/news/2013/07/26/index.md)

# Mass rival demonstrations are held across Egypt three weeks after Mohamed Morsi was deposed as president in a military coup d'tat. At least eight people die and hundreds are injured in violence during the rallies. 

Millions attend rival rallies; fighting begins after pro-military protesters burn pictures of ousted president Mohammed Morsi
