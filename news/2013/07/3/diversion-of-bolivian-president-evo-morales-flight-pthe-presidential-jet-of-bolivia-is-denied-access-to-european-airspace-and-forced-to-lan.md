### [2013-07-3](/news/2013/07/3/index.md)

# Diversion of Bolivian president Evo Morales' flight:The presidential jet of Bolivia is denied access to European airspace and forced to land in Vienna amid concerns that Edward Snowden is on board. 

Bolivia accuses European states of &quot;aggression&quot; after its presidential plane was apparently forced to land and searched for Edward Snowden.
