### [2013-06-30](/news/2013/06/30/index.md)

# Former President of the Philippines Joseph Estrada is sworn-in as the newly elected Mayor of Manila, the nation's capital. 

Exactly 15 years after he was sworn as Philippine president, Joseph Estrada takes his oath as the chief executive of Manila
