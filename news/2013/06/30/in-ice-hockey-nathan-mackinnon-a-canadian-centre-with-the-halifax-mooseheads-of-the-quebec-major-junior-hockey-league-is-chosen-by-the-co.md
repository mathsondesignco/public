### [2013-06-30](/news/2013/06/30/index.md)

# In ice hockey, Nathan MacKinnon, a Canadian centre with the Halifax Mooseheads of the Quebec Major Junior Hockey League, is chosen by the Colorado Avalanche as the first pick in the 2013 NHL Entry Draft. 

The Colorado Avalanche have selected center Nathan MacKinnon with the first pick of the NHL draft.
