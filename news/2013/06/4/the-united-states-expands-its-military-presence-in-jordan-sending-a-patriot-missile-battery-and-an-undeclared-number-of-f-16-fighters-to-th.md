### [2013-06-4](/news/2013/06/4/index.md)

# The United States expands its military presence in Jordan, sending a Patriot missile battery and an undeclared number of F-16 fighters to the area. 

Washington says weapons may remain there after exercise to help Amman deal with threat from conflict in Syria.
