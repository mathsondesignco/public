### [2013-06-11](/news/2013/06/11/index.md)

# U.S. government surveillance programs (including PRISM):European Union officials send a letter to U.S. attorney general Eric Holder with seven detailed questions requesting concrete explanations about the country's data snooping programs and how it affects EU citizens ahead of a meeting in Dublin this Friday. 

The European Commission says it will ask the US for assurances about the legality of its data harvesting this week.
