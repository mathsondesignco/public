### [2013-06-9](/news/2013/06/9/index.md)

# A former CIA employee named Edward Snowden comes forward as the source of recent NSA leaks in the United States. Fearful of prosecution for his actions, he defects to Hong Kong. 

Some in the administration and Congress say whoever leaked the information should be prosecuted.
