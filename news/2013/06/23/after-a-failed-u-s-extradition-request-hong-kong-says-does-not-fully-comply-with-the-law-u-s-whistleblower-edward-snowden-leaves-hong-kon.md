### [2013-06-23](/news/2013/06/23/index.md)

# After a failed U.S. extradition request Hong Kong says does not fully comply with the law, U.S. whistleblower Edward Snowden leaves Hong Kong for Moscow. 

Former US intelligence contractor reportedly arrives in Moscow and applies for asylum in Ecuador.
