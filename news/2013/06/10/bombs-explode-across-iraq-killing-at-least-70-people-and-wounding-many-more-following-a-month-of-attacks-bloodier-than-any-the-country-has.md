### [2013-06-10](/news/2013/06/10/index.md)

# Bombs explode across Iraq, killing at least 70 people and wounding many more, following a month of attacks bloodier than any the country has experienced since June 2008. 

At least 70 people have been killed and many more wounded in a series of bombings and suicide attacks in northern and central Iraq, officials say.
