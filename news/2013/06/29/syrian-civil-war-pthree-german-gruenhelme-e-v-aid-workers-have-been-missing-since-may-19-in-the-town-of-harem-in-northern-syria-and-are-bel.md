### [2013-06-29](/news/2013/06/29/index.md)

# Syrian civil war:Three German Gruenhelme e.V. aid workers have been missing since May 19 in the town of Harem in northern Syria and are believed to have been kidnapped. 

Troops loyal to Damascus regime attempt to recapture rebel-held area at center of two year conflict.
