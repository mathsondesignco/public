# Jiroemon Kimura, who had been the world's oldest living person and the verified longest lived man ever, dies in the Japanese city of Kyotango. 

Japan&#039;s Jiroemon Kimura, recognised by Guinness World Records as the oldest man in recorded history, has died at the age of 116.
