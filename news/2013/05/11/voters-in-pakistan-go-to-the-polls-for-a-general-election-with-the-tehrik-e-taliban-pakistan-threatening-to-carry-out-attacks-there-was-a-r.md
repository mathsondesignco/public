### [2013-05-11](/news/2013/05/11/index.md)

# Voters in Pakistan go to the polls for a general election with the Tehrik-e-Taliban Pakistan threatening to carry out attacks. There was a record turnout despite 24 people being killed. 

Nawaz Sharif tells his supporters that voters have handed him a landmark third term as Pakistan&#039;s PM.
