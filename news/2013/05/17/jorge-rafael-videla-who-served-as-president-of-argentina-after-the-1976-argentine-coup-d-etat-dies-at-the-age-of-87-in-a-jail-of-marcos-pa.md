### [2013-05-17](/news/2013/05/17/index.md)

# Jorge Rafael Videla, who served as President of Argentina after the 1976 Argentine coup d'tat, dies at the age of 87 in a jail of Marcos Paz, Buenos Aires. 

Videla died of natural causes while serving life sentences for crimes against humanity.
