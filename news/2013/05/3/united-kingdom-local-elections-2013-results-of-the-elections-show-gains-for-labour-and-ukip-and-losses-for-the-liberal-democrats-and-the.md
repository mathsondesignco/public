# United Kingdom local elections, 2013:: Results of the elections show gains for Labour and UKIP and losses for the Liberal Democrats and the Conservative Party.

UKIP leader Nigel Farage hails gains in council elections across England - while David Cameron pledges to &quot;work hard to win back voters&quot;.
