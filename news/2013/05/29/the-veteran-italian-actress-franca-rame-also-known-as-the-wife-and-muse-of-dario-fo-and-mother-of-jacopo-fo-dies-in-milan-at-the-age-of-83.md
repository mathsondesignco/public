### [2013-05-29](/news/2013/05/29/index.md)

# The veteran Italian actress Franca Rame, also known as the wife and muse of Dario Fo and mother of Jacopo Fo, dies in Milan at the age of 83. Dario Fo dedicated his 1997 Nobel Prize in Literature to her.
