# Afghanistan War:Afghan security forces battle Taliban insurgents in central Kabul.   An Afghan policeman, a Nepalese soldier, and numerous insurgents are killed. 

Afghan security forces battle Taliban insurgents for hours in central Kabul, following an explosion that shook the capital.
