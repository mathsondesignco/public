# Eric Holder, the Attorney-General of the United States, confirms that four US citizens have been killed in drone aircraft attacks since 2011 including radical cleric Anwar al-Awlaki killed in 2011. 

Only one of those Americans, Anwar al-Awlaki, was targeted for his involvement in various terror plots
