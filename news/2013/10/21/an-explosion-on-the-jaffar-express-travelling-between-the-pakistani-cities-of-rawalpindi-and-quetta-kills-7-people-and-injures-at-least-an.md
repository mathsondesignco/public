### [2013-10-21](/news/2013/10/21/index.md)

# An explosion on the Jaffar Express, travelling between the Pakistani cities of Rawalpindi and Quetta, kills 7 people and injures at least another 10. 

At least 10 people were also injured in the explosion and were taken to Dera Murad Jamali for treatment.
