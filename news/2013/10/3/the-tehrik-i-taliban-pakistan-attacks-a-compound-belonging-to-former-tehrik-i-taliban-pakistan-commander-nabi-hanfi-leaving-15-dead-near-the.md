### [2013-10-3](/news/2013/10/3/index.md)

# The Tehrik-i-Taliban Pakistan attacks a compound belonging to former Tehrik-i-Taliban Pakistan commander Nabi Hanfi leaving 15 dead near the border of North Waziristan. 

Fifteen people are killed as five attackers, including at least three suicide bombers, launch an attack in northwest Pakistan.
