### [2013-10-11](/news/2013/10/11/index.md)

# The Islamic State of Iraq and the Levant is accused by Human Rights Watch of killing at least 190 villagers in pro-government areas and kidnapping hundreds more. 

Human Rights Watch says Latakia residents were gunned down or stabbed during a planned attack.
