# 2013 Tiananmen Square incident: Police in China name two suspects from Xinjiang linked to an apparent suicide attack car crash outside the Forbidden City on Tiananmen Square in the capital Beijing. 

Chinese police seek two suspects from Xinjiang linked to a &quot;major incident&quot; in Beijing, after the deadly car crash in Tiananmen Square, state media say.
