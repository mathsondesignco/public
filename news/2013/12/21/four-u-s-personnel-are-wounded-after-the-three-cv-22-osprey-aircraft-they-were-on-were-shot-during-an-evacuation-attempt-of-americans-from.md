# Four U.S. personnel are wounded after the three CV-22 Osprey aircraft they were on were shot during an evacuation attempt of Americans from Bor, South Sudan. 

Four US service personnel are wounded as aircraft involved in evacuating US citizens come under fire in the spreading South Sudan conflict.
