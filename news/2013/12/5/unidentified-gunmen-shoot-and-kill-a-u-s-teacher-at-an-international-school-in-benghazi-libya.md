### [2013-12-5](/news/2013/12/5/index.md)

# Unidentified gunmen shoot and kill a U.S. teacher at an international school in Benghazi, Libya. 

Shooting comes 15 months after an attack on US consulate that killed the ambassador to Libya and three others.
