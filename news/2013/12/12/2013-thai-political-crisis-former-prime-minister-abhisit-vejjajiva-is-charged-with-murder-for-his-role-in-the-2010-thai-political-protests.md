# 2013 Thai political crisis:: Former Prime Minister Abhisit Vejjajiva is charged with murder for his role in the 2010 Thai political protests. 

Former Thai PM Abhisit Vejjajiva is formally charged with murder over the 2010 crackdown, as protests continue in Bangkok.
