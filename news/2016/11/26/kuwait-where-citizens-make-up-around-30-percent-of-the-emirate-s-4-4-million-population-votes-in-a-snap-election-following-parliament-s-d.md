### [2016-11-26](/news/2016/11/26/index.md)

# Kuwait, where citizens make up around 30 percent of the emirate's 4.4 million population, votes  in a snap election following parliament's dissolution on 16 October amid the impact of falling oil prices. This is the first election the opposition has not boycotted in nearly four years.
