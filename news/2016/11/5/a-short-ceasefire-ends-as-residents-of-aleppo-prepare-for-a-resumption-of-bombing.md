### [2016-11-5](/news/2016/11/5/index.md)

# A short ceasefire ends as residents of Aleppo prepare for a resumption of bombing. 

Syrian rebels and civilians refuse to leave the besieged city of Aleppo as a ceasefire expires and a renewed bombing campaign looms.
