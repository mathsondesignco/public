# Hillary Clinton email controversy: Federal Bureau of Investigation chief James Comey tells the United States Congress that he has found no evidence of criminality by Hillary Clinton in relation to a new batch of emails. 

An FBI investigation finds no evidence of criminality in the latest batch of Hillary Clinton emails.
