### [2016-11-1](/news/2016/11/1/index.md)

# Two weeks after his initial announcement, Bob Day, leader of the Family First Party, confirms his immediate resignation from the Australian Senate due to the liquidation of his business. 

Family First senator Bob Day tenders his resignation, effective immediately.
