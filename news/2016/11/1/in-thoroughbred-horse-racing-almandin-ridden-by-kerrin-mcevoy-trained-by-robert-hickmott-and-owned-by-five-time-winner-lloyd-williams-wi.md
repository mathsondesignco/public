### [2016-11-1](/news/2016/11/1/index.md)

# In thoroughbred horse racing, Almandin, ridden by Kerrin McEvoy, trained by Robert Hickmott and owned by five-time winner Lloyd Williams, wins the 156th Melbourne Cup. 

Almandin, ridden by Kerrin McEvoy, wins the 156th Melbourne Cup at Flemington, narrowly taking the victory from Heartbreak City in the race that stops the nation.
