# Former Philippine president Fidel Ramos quits as the Philippines' special envoy to China amid a falling out with his mentee current Filipino president Rodrigo Duterte. 

FORMER President Fidel V. Ramos on Monday resigned as the countrys special envoy to China after criticizing President Rodrigo Dutertes repeated tirades against the United States and an apparent tilt towards China.
