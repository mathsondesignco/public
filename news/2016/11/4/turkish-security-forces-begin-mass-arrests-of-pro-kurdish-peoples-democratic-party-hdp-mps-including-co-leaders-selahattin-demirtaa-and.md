### [2016-11-4](/news/2016/11/4/index.md)

# Turkish security forces begin mass arrests of pro-Kurdish Peoples' Democratic Party (HDP) MPs, including co-leaders Selahattin Demirtas and Figen Yuksekdag. Internet access is reportedly blocked across southeastern Turkey. 

Selahattin Demirtaş, HDP co-leader known as the ‘Kurdish Obama’, held with at least 11 MPs as post-coup crackdown continues
