### [2016-11-29](/news/2016/11/29/index.md)

# LaMia Airlines Flight 2933: A chartered Avro RJ85 plane carrying at least 81 people, including the Chapecoense football team, crashes near Medellin, Colombia. Rescuers report at least six survivors have been found in the wreckage. The 2016 Copa Sudamericana Finals are suspended. 

A plane carrying a Brazilian football team crashes in Colombia, killing 71 people on board.
