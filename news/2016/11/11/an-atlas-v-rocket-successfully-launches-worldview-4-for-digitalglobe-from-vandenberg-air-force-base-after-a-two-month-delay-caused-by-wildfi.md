# An Atlas V rocket successfully launches WorldView-4 for DigitalGlobe from Vandenberg Air Force Base after a two-month delay caused by wildfires near the spaceport. 

After nearly two months of delay due to wildfires, United Launch Alliance successfully launched an Atlas 5 rocket Friday carrying a DigitalGlobe satellite.
