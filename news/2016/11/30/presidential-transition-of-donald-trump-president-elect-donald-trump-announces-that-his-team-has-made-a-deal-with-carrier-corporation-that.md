### [2016-11-30](/news/2016/11/30/index.md)

# Presidential transition of Donald Trump. President-elect Donald Trump announces that his team has made a deal with Carrier Corporation that would save around 1,000 jobs at an Indiana factory. Carrier had planned to move production from a key factory in that state to Nuevo Leon, Mexico. 

The incoming Trump Administration and UTX have reached an agreement that will keep close to 1,000 jobs at Carrier in Indiana.
