### [2016-11-30](/news/2016/11/30/index.md)

# The Indian Supreme Court orders all cinemas must play the national anthem, and the audience must stand  during its performance, before a film is screened. Order is effective in 10 days. 

Further, the bench said people must show respect to the national anthem and the national flag and called it “imperative” to show them honour and respect.
