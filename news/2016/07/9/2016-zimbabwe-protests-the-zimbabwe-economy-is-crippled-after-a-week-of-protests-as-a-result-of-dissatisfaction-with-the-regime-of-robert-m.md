# 2016 Zimbabwe protests: The Zimbabwe economy is crippled after a week of protests as a result of dissatisfaction with the regime of Robert Mugabe. 

As their country is sucked into a the depths of a new economic crisis, Zimbabweans are beginning to run out of patience.
