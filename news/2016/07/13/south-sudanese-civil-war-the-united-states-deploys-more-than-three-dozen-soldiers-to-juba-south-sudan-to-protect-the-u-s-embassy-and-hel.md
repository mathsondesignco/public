# South Sudanese Civil War. The United States deploys more than three dozen soldiers to Juba, South Sudan, to protect the U.S. embassy and help evacuate non-essential embassy staff due to local clashes. 

South Sudan: U.S. troops were rushed to evacuate Americans and embassy personnel in the capital amid renewed fighting that is creating a humanitarian crisis.
