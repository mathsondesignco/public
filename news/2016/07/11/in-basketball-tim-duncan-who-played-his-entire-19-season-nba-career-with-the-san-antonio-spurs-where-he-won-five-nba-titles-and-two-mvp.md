### [2016-07-11](/news/2016/07/11/index.md)

#  In basketball, Tim Duncan, who played his entire 19-season NBA career with the San Antonio Spurs, where he won five NBA titles and two MVP awards, made 15 All-Star appearances and made the All-NBA first team 10 times, announces his retirement. 

Tim Duncan, who turned 40 in April, announced Monday that he is retiring from the NBA after 19 seasons with the San Antonio Spurs.
