### [2016-07-27](/news/2016/07/27/index.md)

# At least 44 people are killed in a twin bombing in the Kurdish-controlled city of Al-Qamishli on the Syria-Turkey border. ISIL claims responsibility for the attack. 

Hundreds of thousands face running out of food in besieged city, while Isis launches bloody attack in border town of Qamishli
