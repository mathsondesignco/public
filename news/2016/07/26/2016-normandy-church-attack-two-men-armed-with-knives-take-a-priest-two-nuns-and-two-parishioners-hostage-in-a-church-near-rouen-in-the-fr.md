# 2016 Normandy church attack: Two men armed with knives take a priest, two nuns and two parishioners hostage in a church near Rouen in the French region of Normandy. French police kill the hostage-takers after the 86-year-old priest, Jacques Hamel, was killed by slitting his throat. 

French President Francois Hollande blamed the &quot;cowardly acts&quot; on ISIS-linked terrorists.
