# Cross-Strait relations. Taiwanese President Tsai Ing-wen rejects a supposed deadline set by Chinese President Xi Jinping for accepting the 1992 Consensus, saying such a deadline is against the will of the people. 

The People&rsquo;s Republic has cut off official communications with the island.
