### [2016-02-10](/news/2016/02/10/index.md)

# North Korea reportedly executes Ri Yong-gil, Chief of the General Staff of the Korean People's Army on charges of corruption, according to South Korean media. 

North Korea has executed its army chief of staff Ri Yong-gil, according to unconfirmed South Korean media reports.
