# Zika virus outbreak. The Polynesian state of Tonga declares a Zika virus epidemic with two confirmed cases and 265 suspected cases. 

Tonga has declared a Zika epidemic after confirming two people have contracted the mosquito-borne virus, with 265 suspected cases.
