### [2016-02-18](/news/2016/02/18/index.md)

# Kurdish-Turkish conflict. February 2016 Diyarbakir bombing. A roadside bomb hits a military convoy, killing six Turkish Army soldiers in the southeast Diyarbakir Province. 

The Turkish Prime Minister blames Kurdish militants for a deadly car bombing in Ankara that killed 28 people.
