### [2016-02-18](/news/2016/02/18/index.md)

# Ethnic violence in South Sudan. Fighting occurs between Shilluk and Dinka youths at a United Nations peacekeeping compound in the city of Malakal which lasts around three hours. Medecins Sans Frontieres reports at least 18 people are killed, including two MSF South Sudanese staff members, and dozens wounded. 

Fighting at a United Nations compound sheltering people fleeing conflict in South Sudan has killed 18 people, including two Medicins Sans Frontieres workers, the international medical aid group said.
