# Tornadoes of 2016: At least two people are dead and 30 injured after a suspected tornado hits a trailer park near the village of Convent, Louisiana. At least one other person has died in the Deep South of the United States. 

A suspected tornado slammed into a trailer park in Sugar Hill RV park near Convent, Louisiana damaging dozens of trailers
