### [2016-02-19](/news/2016/02/19/index.md)

# Libyan Civil War (2014-present): Military intervention against ISIL: United States Air Force warplanes carry out airstrikes on ISIL in western Libya, near the Tunisian border, reportedly killing at least 38 people. 

The US carries out air strikes on so-called Islamic State in Libya, targeting a top militant linked to an attack on Tunisia that killed 30 Britons.
