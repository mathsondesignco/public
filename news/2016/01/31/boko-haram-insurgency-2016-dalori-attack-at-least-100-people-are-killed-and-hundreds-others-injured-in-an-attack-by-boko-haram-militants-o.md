# Boko Haram insurgency: 2016 Dalori attack: At least 100 people are killed and hundreds others injured in an attack by Boko Haram militants on Dalori Village 4 kilometers from Maiduguri, Nigeria. 

At least 100 people were killed and over hundred others injured when the Boko Haram terrorists attacked Dalori Village some 4 kilometers from Maiduguri yesterday evening.
