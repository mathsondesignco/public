### [2016-01-20](/news/2016/01/20/index.md)

# A suicide bomber in Kabul, Afghanistan, targets Tolo News, killing four and wounding 20. 

Seven employees of a popular Afghan TV channel are killed after their bus is targeted by a suicide car bomber in Kabul.
