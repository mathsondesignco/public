### [2016-01-13](/news/2016/01/13/index.md)

# A major indication that relations between Cuba and the U.S. are increasingly normalized will be the island nation's participation later this month at an annual Caribbean security conference in Jamaica, according to the outgoing head of the United States Southern Command, General John F. Kelly. 

MIAMI — A U.S. military official says Cuba will attend an annual Caribbean security conference for the first time in another sign of normal relations between the long-hostile nations.
