### [2016-01-3](/news/2016/01/3/index.md)

# The United Kingdom designates Ascension Island and its surrounding waters in the Atlantic Ocean as a Marine protected area. The reserve will be almost as big as the UK with just over half of the protected area completely closed to fishing. 

The government is to create a marine reserve almost as big as the UK in the Atlantic waters of Ascension Island.
