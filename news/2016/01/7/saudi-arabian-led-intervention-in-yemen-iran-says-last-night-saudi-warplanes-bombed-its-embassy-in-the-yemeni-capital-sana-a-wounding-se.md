### [2016-01-7](/news/2016/01/7/index.md)

# Saudi Arabian-led intervention in Yemen. Iran says last night, Saudi warplanes bombed its embassy in the Yemeni capital, Sana'a, wounding several of its guards. The Associated Press reports there is no visible damage to the building. A Saudi-alliance spokesman promises to investigate the accusations. 

Iran has accused Saudi Arabia of using warplanes to attack the Iranian embassy in the Yemeni capital Sana&#039;a. Some guards were reportedly wounded in the attack, according to state news channel IRIB.
