### [2016-01-5](/news/2016/01/5/index.md)

# Reform of the People's Liberation Army in China:: The Second Artillery Corps is re-organized into the People's Liberation Army Rocket Force.: The People's Liberation Army Strategic Support Force is founded, with Gao Jin as its inaugural commander and Liu Fulian as Political Commissar.: The Ground Force is re-organized under the People's Liberation Army Ground Force Command, under commander Li Zuocheng.
