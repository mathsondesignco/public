# Indonesia blocks access to radical Islamist websites and social media sites after the recent terror attacks in Jakarta. 

Indonesia shut down at least 11radical websites and several social media accounts on Saturdayafter a deadly gun and bomb attack claimed by Islamic Staterocked the capital earlier in the week.
