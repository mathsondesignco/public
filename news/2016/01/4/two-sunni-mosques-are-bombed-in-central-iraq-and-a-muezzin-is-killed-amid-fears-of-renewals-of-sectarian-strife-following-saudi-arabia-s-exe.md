# Two Sunni mosques are bombed in central Iraq and a muezzin is killed amid fears of renewals of sectarian strife following Saudi Arabia's execution of Shi'ite cleric Nimr al-Nimr in Saudi Arabia. 

HILLA, Iraq (AFP) - Blasts rocked two Sunni mosques in central Iraq Monday (Jan 4), amid fears of renewed sectarian strife following Saudi Arabia&#039;s execution of a prominent Shi&#039;ite cleric, police and medics said.. Read more at straitstimes.com.
