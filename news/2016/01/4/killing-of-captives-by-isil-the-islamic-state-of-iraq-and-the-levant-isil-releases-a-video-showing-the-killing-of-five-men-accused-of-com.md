### [2016-01-4](/news/2016/01/4/index.md)

# Killing of captives by ISIL: The Islamic State of Iraq and the Levant (ISIL) releases a video showing the killing of five men accused of committing espionage on the militant group for the United Kingdom. The British Foreign and Commonwealth Office released a statement saying that they are examining the content of the video. 

BEIRUT - The Islamic State group threatened Britain in an Internet video Sunday showing the killing of five &#39;&#39;spies&#39;&#39; it said worked with the international coalition fighting IS in Iraq and Syria.
