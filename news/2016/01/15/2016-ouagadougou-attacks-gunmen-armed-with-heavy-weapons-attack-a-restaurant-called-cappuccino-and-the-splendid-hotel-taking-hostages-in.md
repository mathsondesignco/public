### [2016-01-15](/news/2016/01/15/index.md)

# 2016 Ouagadougou attacks: Gunmen armed with heavy weapons attack a restaurant called Cappuccino and the Splendid Hotel, taking hostages, in the heart of Burkino Faso's capital, Ouagadougou. At least 20 people are reportedly killed. 

Exchanges of fire continue at a luxury hotel in Burkina Faso as special forces seek to break a deadly siege by suspected Islamist gunmen.
