### [2016-06-27](/news/2016/06/27/index.md)

# Israel-Turkey relations: Israeli Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu and Turkish Prime Minister Binali Yildirim announce their countries have agreed on a pact to normalize diplomatic relations that have been strained for six years since the Gaza flotilla raid when nine Turkish activists were killed. 

After six years of estrangement over a deadly clash in the waters outside of Gaza, Israel and Turkey announced a deal to normalize ties.
