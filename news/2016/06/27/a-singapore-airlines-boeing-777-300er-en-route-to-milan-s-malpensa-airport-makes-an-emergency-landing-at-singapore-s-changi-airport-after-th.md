### [2016-06-27](/news/2016/06/27/index.md)

# A Singapore Airlines Boeing 777-300ER en-route to Milan's Malpensa Airport makes an emergency landing at Singapore's Changi Airport after the pilots received an engine oil warning message, whereafter the plane's right engine caught fire. Singapore Changi Airport's aircraft rescue and firefighting services extinguished the fire and Singapore Airlines confirmed that all 222 passengers and 19 crew members evacuated safely. 

A Singapore Airlines plane has erupted in flames shortly after making an emergency landing at Singapore&#039;s Changi Airport early on Monday morning , witnesses said.
