### [2016-06-27](/news/2016/06/27/index.md)

# Underdogs Iceland defeat England 2-1 at the Allianz Riviera in Nice to reach the quarter-finals of the UEFA European Championship. 

England suffer a humiliating exit from Euro 2016 as they go out to minnows Iceland in the last 16 in Nice.
