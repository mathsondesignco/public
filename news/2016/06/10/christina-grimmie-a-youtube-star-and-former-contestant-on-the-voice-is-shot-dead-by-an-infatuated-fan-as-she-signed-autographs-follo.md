### [2016-06-10](/news/2016/06/10/index.md)

# Christina Grimmie, a YouTube star and former contestant on ''The Voice'', is shot dead by an "infatuated" fan as she signed autographs following a concert appearance with Before You Exit at The Plaza Live in Orlando, Florida. The shooter, 27-year-old Kevin James Loibl, shot himself dead after being tackled by Grimmie's Brother. Grimmie died in a hospital just before 11 p.m. 

The investigation into the shooting death of The Voice star Christina Grimmie has been closed, officials say.
