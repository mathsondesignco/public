### [2016-06-19](/news/2016/06/19/index.md)

# Italian local elections, 2016: Voters in the Italian capital of Rome go to the polls for a runoff election to select a new Mayor of Rome with exit polls showing Virginia Raggi of the Five Star Movement will become the first female Mayor. 

The anti-establishment Five Star Movement wins key mayoral races in the capital, Rome, and Turin, cementing its role in Italian politics.
