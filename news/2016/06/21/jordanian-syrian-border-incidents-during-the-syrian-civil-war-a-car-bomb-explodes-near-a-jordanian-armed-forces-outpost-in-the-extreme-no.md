### [2016-06-21](/news/2016/06/21/index.md)

# Jordanian-Syrian border incidents during the Syrian Civil War. A car-bomb explodes near a Jordanian Armed Forces outpost in the extreme north-eastern point of Rukban, a makeshift Syrian refugee camp, leaving 6 soldiers dead and 14 injured. 

Six Jordanian security personnel are killed in a suicide truck bomb attack near a makeshift refugee camp on the border with Syria, officials say.
