### [2016-06-4](/news/2016/06/4/index.md)

# In horse racing, Harzand trained by Dermot Weld wins the Epsom Derby. It is the fifth horse owned by His Highness The Aga Khan IV to win the Derby. 

Two-time Melbourne Cup winning trainer Dermot Weld won the Epsom Derby for the first time on Saturday with Harzand, who had been a doubtful runner earlier in the day.
