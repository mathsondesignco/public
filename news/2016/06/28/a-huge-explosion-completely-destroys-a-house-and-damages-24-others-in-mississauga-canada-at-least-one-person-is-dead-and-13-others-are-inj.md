### [2016-06-28](/news/2016/06/28/index.md)

# A huge explosion completely destroys a house and damages 24 others in Mississauga, Canada. At least one person is dead and 13 others are injured, according to Mississauga Fire and Emergency Services. Thousands of residents are forced to evacuate and many spend the night at a local community shelter. 

One person is dead after a devastating explosion levelled a house in Mississauga, Ont., and damaged 24 others.
