### [2016-06-24](/news/2016/06/24/index.md)

# 2016 West Virginia flood: West Virginia police report powerful flash floods last night killed at least 26 people and swept away a toddler from Ravenswood. Up to 10 inches (25 cm) of rain fell in White Sulphur Springs in the southern part of the state. A state of emergency had been declared in 44 counties. Almost 500,000 customers are without power including thousands in Virginia. 

Historic flooding in West Virginia has caused dangerous conditions that have killed at least 23 people and left thousands without power.
