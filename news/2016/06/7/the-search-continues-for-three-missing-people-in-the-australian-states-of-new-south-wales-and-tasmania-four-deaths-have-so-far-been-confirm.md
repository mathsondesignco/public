### [2016-06-7](/news/2016/06/7/index.md)

# The search continues for three missing people in the Australian states of New South Wales and Tasmania. Four deaths have so far been confirmed in the Australian Capital Territory, Tasmania and NSW. 

Deadly storms in NSW and the ACT have claimed three lives amid grave concerns for three people missing in floods in Tasmania, and one man off Bondi Beach.
