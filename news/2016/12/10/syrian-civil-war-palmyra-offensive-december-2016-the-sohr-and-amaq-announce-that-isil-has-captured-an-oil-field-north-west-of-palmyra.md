### [2016-12-10](/news/2016/12/10/index.md)

# Syrian Civil War: Palmyra offensive (December 2016): The SOHR and Amaq announce that ISIL has captured an oil field north-west of Palmyra. 

The US says it is sending 200 troops to help Syrian rebels recapture the IS stronghold of Raqqa.
