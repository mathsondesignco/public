# Hitrino train derailment. At least seven people have been killed and 25 others are injured after an explosion caused by the derailment of a train carrying propane and butane in the Bulgarian village of Hitrino, near Shumen. 

A freight train derails and explodes in a north-east Bulgarian village, killing five people.
