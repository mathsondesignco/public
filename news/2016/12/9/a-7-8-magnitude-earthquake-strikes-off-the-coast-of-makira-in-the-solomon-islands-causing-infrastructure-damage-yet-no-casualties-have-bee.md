# A 7.8 magnitude earthquake strikes off the coast of Makira in the Solomon Islands, causing infrastructure damage, yet no casualties have been reported in the hours following the quake. 

Residents in Solomon Islands are hit by aftershocks after a magnitude-7.8 earthquake strikes the country.
