### [2016-12-14](/news/2016/12/14/index.md)

# The Committee to Protect Journalists reports a record 259 journalists, 81 in Turkey, are jailed worldwide as of December 1. 

The number of reporters behind bars has reached a record high in 2016, according to the Committee to Protect Journalists.
