### [2016-12-11](/news/2016/12/11/index.md)

# The death toll from yesterday's church collapse in Uyo, Nigeria, rises to 160 with the death toll expected to rise further. 

The roof of a crowded church collapses in southern Nigeria, reportedly killing at least 160 people.
