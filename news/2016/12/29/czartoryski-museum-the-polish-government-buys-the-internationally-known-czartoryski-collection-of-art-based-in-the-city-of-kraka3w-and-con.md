### [2016-12-29](/news/2016/12/29/index.md)

# Czartoryski Museum: The Polish government buys the internationally-known Czartoryski collection of art. Based in the city of Krakow and consisting of around 86,000 pieces, this features works by Leonardo da Vinci, Rembrandt and Pierre-Auguste Renoir. 

Poland buys the Czartoryski collection of about 86,000 artefacts for about 5% of its market value.
