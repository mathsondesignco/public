### [2016-12-29](/news/2016/12/29/index.md)

# U.S. President Barack Obama declares the Bears Ears in Utah and Gold Butte area of Nevada as National Monuments. Both are known for the presence of Native American populations and archaeological sites. 

President Barack Obama unilaterally designates two new &quot;national monument&quot; sites in Nevada and Utah.
