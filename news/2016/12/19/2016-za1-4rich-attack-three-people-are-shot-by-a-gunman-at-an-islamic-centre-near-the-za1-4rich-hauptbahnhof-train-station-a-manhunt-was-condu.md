# 2016 Zurich attack. Three people are shot by a gunman at an Islamic centre near the Zurich Hauptbahnhof train station. A manhunt was conducted for the assailant, a 24-year-old Swiss citizen of Ghanian descent, whose body was was found close to the Islamic centre. 

 A man stormed into a Zurich mosque on Monday evening and opened fire on people praying, injuring three, Swiss police said.
