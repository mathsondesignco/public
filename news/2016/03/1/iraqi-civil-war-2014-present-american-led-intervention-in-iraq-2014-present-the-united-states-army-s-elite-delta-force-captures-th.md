# Iraqi Civil War (2014-present). American-led intervention in Iraq (2014-present). The United States Army's elite Delta Force captures their first suspected ISIL operative during a raid in northern Iraq. The detainee is being interrogated by the U.S. and is expected to be turned over to Iraqi officials in the coming days.
