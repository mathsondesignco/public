### [2016-03-17](/news/2016/03/17/index.md)

# Three construction workers are shot dead in Jayapura, Papua, by a supposed armed group, according to Indonesian police. 

Police in Indonesia&#39;s Papua region say at least three construction workers were shot dead by an armed group in Puncak Jaya district on Tuesday.
