### [2016-03-8](/news/2016/03/8/index.md)

# U.S. carrier Alaska Airlines reschedules Flight 870 from Anchorage, Alaska, to Honolulu, Hawaii, to depart at 2:00 p.m. local time (23:00 UTC) so passengers can view 2016's only total solar eclipse for 1 minute and 59 seconds, 695 miles north of Honolulu at 37,000 feet. 

The departure time is being adjusted so passengers can get a rare look over the Pacific Ocean.
