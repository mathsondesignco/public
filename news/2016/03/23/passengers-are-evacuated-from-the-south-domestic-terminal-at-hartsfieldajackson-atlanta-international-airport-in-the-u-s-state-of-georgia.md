### [2016-03-23](/news/2016/03/23/index.md)

# Passengers are evacuated from the south domestic terminal at Hartsfield-Jackson Atlanta International Airport in the U.S. state of Georgia after a suspicious package is found. The package is declared safe by a bomb squad. 

Atlanta police say passengers who were evacuated from a terminal at Atlanta&#39;s airport are now being allowed back in after a bomb squad investigated and cleared a suspicious package.
