### [2016-03-7](/news/2016/03/7/index.md)

# War in North-West Pakistan: A Taliban suicide bomber hits a court complex in the town of Shabqadar killing at least 13 people and injuring 36. Jamaat-ul-Ahrar, a Pakistani Taliban faction, says the attack is retaliation for Mumtaz Qadri's execution last week. 

At least 13 people died in a suicide bomb attack at a court building in Charsadda, Pakistan, police said.
