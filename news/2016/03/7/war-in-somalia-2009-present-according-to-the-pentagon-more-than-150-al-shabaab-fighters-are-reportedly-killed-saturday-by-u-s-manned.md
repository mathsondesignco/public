### [2016-03-7](/news/2016/03/7/index.md)

# War in Somalia (2009-present): According to the Pentagon, more than 150 al-Shabaab fighters are reportedly killed Saturday by U.S. manned and unmanned aircraft strikes targeting a training camp near the central Somali town of Buloburde. 

More than 150 al-Shabaab fighters were killed in a U.S. military strike Saturday using manned aircraft and drones, a U.S. official said Monday.
