# Google's DeepMind AlphaGo artificial intelligence program defeats South Korean grand master Lee Se-dol in the ancient game of Go. 

A Google computer program trounced one of the world&#039;s top players on Wednesday in a round of Go, which is believed to be the most complex board game ever created.
