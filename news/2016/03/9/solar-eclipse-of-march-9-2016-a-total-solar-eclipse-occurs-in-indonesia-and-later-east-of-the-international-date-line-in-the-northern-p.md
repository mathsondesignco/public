### [2016-03-9](/news/2016/03/9/index.md)

# Solar eclipse of March 9, 2016: A total solar eclipse occurs in Indonesia, and later, east of the international date line, in the northern Pacific Ocean. A partial eclipse occurs in northern Australia, South-East Asia, and the Pacific. 

A solar eclipse sweeps across the vast Indonesian archipelago, with sky gazers turning out to watch the spectacle.
