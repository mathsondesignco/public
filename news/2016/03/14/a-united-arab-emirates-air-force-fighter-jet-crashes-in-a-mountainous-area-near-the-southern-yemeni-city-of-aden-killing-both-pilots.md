### [2016-03-14](/news/2016/03/14/index.md)

# A United Arab Emirates Air Force fighter jet crashes in a mountainous area near the southern Yemeni city of Aden, killing both pilots. 

Two Emirati pilots were killed when their jet crashed during a mission against Houthi rebels in Yemen, the Saudi-led coalition says.
