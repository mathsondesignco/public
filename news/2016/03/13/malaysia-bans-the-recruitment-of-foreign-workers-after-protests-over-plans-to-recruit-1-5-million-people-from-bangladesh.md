# Malaysia bans the recruitment of foreign workers after protests over plans to recruit 1.5 million people from Bangladesh. 

Malaysia banned the recruitment of new foreign workers after widespread criticism of its decision to bring in 1.5 million Bangladeshis to work in the country.
