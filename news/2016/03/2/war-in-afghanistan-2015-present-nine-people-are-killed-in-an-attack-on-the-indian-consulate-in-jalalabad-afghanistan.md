# War in Afghanistan (2015-present): Nine people are killed in an attack on the Indian consulate In Jalalabad, Afghanistan. 

In the fourth attack since 2007, heavily-armed terrorists, including suicide bombers, today struck the Indian Consulate in Afghanistan&#039;s Jalalabad city, killing nine persons including an Afghan security personnel and causing damage to the chancery.
