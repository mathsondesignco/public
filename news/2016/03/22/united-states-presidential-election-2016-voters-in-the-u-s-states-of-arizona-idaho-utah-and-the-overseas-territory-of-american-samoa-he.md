# United States presidential election, 2016. Voters in the U.S. states of Arizona, Idaho, Utah and the overseas territory of American Samoa head to the polls for Republican and Democratic Party primaries and caucuses. 

Republicans will face off in Arizona and Utah, while Democrats battle it out in all three states.
