# Saudi Arabian-led intervention in Yemen. Airstrikes near the Yemeni port city of Al Mukalla, Hadhramaut Governorate, reportedly kill at least 30 Al-Qaeda militants. 

Saudi-led airstrikes targeting al Qaeda in eastern Yemen killed and wounded dozens on Tuesday, a provincial governor and medics said.
