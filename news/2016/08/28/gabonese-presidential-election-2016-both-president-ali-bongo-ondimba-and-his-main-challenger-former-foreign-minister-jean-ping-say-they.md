### [2016-08-28](/news/2016/08/28/index.md)

# Gabonese presidential election, 2016. Both President Ali Bongo Ondimba and his main challenger, former Foreign Minister Jean Ping, say they won yesterday's election for a seven-year term as president. Official results are expected Tuesday.
