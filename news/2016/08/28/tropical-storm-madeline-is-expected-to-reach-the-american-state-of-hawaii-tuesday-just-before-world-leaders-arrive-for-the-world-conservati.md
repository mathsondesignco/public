### [2016-08-28](/news/2016/08/28/index.md)

# Tropical Storm Madeline is expected to reach the American state of Hawaii Tuesday, just before world leaders arrive for the World Conservation Congress. 

The President of the United States will arrive in Honolulu Wednesday evening, and city officials are warning the public of major road closures during rush hour.
