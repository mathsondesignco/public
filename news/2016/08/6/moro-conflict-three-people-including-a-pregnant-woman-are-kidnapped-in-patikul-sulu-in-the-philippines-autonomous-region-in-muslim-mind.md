### [2016-08-6](/news/2016/08/6/index.md)

# Moro conflict. Three people, including a pregnant woman, are kidnapped in Patikul, Sulu, in the Philippines Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao. No group has claimed responsibility, though Abu Sayyaf is suspected.
