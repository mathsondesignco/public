### [2016-08-24](/news/2016/08/24/index.md)

# August 2016 Myanmar earthquake: A 6.8 magnitude earthquake hits central Myanmar, 25km west of the town of Chauk, and was also felt in Thailand, Bangladesh and India, according to reports. At least three people are killed. 

A 6.8 magnitude earthquake strikes central Myanmar, damaging ancient temples and leaving at least one person dead.
