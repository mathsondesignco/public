### [2016-08-31](/news/2016/08/31/index.md)

# Cuba-United States relations: United States-Cuban Thaw: The first commercial flight from the United States to Cuba since 1961 takes place as "JetBlue Flight 387" lands in Santa Clara, Villa Clara province, after a 51-minute flight from Fort Lauderdale, Florida. "JetBlue Flight 387" had 150 passengers, including United States Secretary of Transportation Anthony Foxx. 

The flight from Fort Lauderdale to Santa Clara in central Cuba on Wednesday morning took only 51 minutes.
