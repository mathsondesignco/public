### [2016-08-12](/news/2016/08/12/index.md)

# 2016 Summer Olympics Singaporean swimmer Joseph Schooling defeats American swimmer Michael Phelps in the 100m butterfly event, attaining Singapore's first-ever Olympic gold; Schooling's winning time of 50.39 seconds is an Olympic, Asian, and national record.
