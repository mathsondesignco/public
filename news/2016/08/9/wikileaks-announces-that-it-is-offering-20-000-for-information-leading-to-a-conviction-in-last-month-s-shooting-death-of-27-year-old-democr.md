### [2016-08-9](/news/2016/08/9/index.md)

# WikiLeaks announces that it is offering $20,000 for information leading to a conviction in last month's shooting death of 27-year-old Democratic National Committee (DNC) staffer Seth Rich in Bloomingdale, Washington, D.C.. Police say they believe Rich was likely killed in an attempted robbery. 

Julian Assange seemed to suggest Seth Rich was the source of DNC email leak.
