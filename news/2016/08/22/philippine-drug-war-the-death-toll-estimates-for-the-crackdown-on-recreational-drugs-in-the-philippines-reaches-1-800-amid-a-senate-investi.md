# Philippine Drug War: The death toll estimates for the crackdown on recreational drugs in the Philippines reaches 1,800 amid a senate investigation of the killings. 

Senator Leila de Lima, a staunch critic of the president, started a two-day congressional inquiry into the &quot;unprecedented&quot; killings.
