### [2016-10-6](/news/2016/10/6/index.md)

# An al-Shabaab attack on a village near the Kenyan town of Mandera results in six deaths. 

Islamist militants from the Somali group al Shabaab killed six people in an attack in northeast Kenya on Thursday, the latest in a series of raids by the group in the region.
