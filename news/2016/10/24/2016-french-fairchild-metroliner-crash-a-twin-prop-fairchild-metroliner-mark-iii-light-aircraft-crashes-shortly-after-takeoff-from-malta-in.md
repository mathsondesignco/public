### [2016-10-24](/news/2016/10/24/index.md)

# 2016 French Fairchild Metroliner crash: A twin-prop Fairchild Metroliner Mark III light aircraft crashes shortly after takeoff from Malta International Airport, killing all five French nationals on board. Malta officials say that the aircraft was part of a French customs surveillance operation tracing routes of illicit trafficking, of humans and drugs on Libyan coasts, and that the flight was heading for the Libyan city of Misrata. 

A plane has crashed in Malta, killing all five people on board in the country&#039;s worst peacetime air disaster. Maltese officials said French customs officials were among victims on the flight, which was heading for the city of Misrata in Libya.
