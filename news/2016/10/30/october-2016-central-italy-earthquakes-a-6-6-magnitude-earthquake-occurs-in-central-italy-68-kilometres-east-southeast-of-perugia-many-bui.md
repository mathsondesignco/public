### [2016-10-30](/news/2016/10/30/index.md)

# October 2016 Central Italy earthquakes. A 6.6 magnitude earthquake occurs in Central Italy 68 kilometres east southeast of Perugia. Many buildings are reported as having been destroyed. 

A earthquake measuring magnitude-6.6 strikes in central Italy on Sunday, the United States Geological Survey says.
