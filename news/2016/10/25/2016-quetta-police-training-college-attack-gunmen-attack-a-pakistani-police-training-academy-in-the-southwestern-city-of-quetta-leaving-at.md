# 2016 Quetta police training college attack. Gunmen attack a Pakistani police training academy in the southwestern city of Quetta, leaving at least 59 people dead and 117 others injured. ISIL - Khorasan Province claims responsibility for the attack. 

Dozens of people are killed after militants mount an attack on a police training college in the Pakistani city of Quetta, officials say.
