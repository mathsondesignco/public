# Clashes between rival gangs in at least two prisons in Brazil, leave at least 18 people killed. 

Prison clashes between rival gangs killed at least 18 inmates in two penitentiaries in remote parts of northern and western Brazil, including six men who were decapitated and their bodies set on fire, local media reported on Monday.
