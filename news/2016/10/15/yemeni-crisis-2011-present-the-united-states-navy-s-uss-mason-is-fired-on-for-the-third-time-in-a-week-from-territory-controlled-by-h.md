### [2016-10-15](/news/2016/10/15/index.md)

# Yemeni Crisis (2011-present). The United States Navy's USS "Mason" is fired on for the third time in a week from territory controlled by Houthi forces in Yemen, while in international waters of the Red Sea. The ship deployed countermeasures and was not struck, according to U.S. officials. 

At least one missile was fired at the destroyer, but the ship deployed countermeasures and was not struck. It was targeted twice earlier this week.
