### [2016-10-7](/news/2016/10/7/index.md)

# Philippines-United States relations: The Philippines Secretary of National Defense Delfin Lorenzana suspends participation in any joint patrols with the United States in the disputed South China Sea. Lorenzana will ask a detachment of 107 U.S. troops providing counterterrorism assistance on the southern island of Mindanao to leave as President Rodrigo Duterte requested. 

Defense Secretary Delfin Lorenzana said Friday that he told the United States military that plans for joint patrols and naval exercises in the disputed South China Sea have been put on hold.
