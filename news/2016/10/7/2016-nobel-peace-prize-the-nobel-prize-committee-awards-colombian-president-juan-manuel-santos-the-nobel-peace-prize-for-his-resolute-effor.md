### [2016-10-7](/news/2016/10/7/index.md)

# 2016 Nobel Peace Prize: The Nobel Prize committee awards Colombian president Juan Manuel Santos the Nobel Peace Prize for his resolute efforts to bring the country's more than 50-year-long civil war to an end, a war that has cost the lives of at least 220 thousand Colombians and displaced close to six million people. 

Award comes despite peace deal being voted down in a referendum
