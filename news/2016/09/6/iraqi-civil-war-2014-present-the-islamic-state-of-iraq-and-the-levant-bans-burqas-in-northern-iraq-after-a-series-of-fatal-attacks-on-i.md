### [2016-09-6](/news/2016/09/6/index.md)

# Iraqi Civil War (2014-present). The Islamic State of Iraq and the Levant bans burqas in northern Iraq after a series of fatal attacks on its members by veiled women. 

The group has made a U-turn on its burka stance following reports of a veiled woman targeting fighters.
