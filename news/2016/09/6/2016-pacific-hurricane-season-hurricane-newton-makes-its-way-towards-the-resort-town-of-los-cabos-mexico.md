# 2016 Pacific hurricane season: Hurricane Newton makes its way towards the resort town of Los Cabos, Mexico. 

Tropical storm Newton, located 350 kilometers southeast of Cabo San Lucas, reached hurricane strength as of 4:00pm CDT.
