### [2016-09-10](/news/2016/09/10/index.md)

# Philippine Drug War. The sister of Filipino actress Maritoni Fernandez and daughter of British hereditary peer Antony Moynihan, 3rd Baron Moynihan is shot dead in a suburb of Manila. Through deliberate evidence left at the scene, the perpetrator's motive appears to be an implication that Moynihan had been a drug dealer. 

Ma. Aurora Moynihan, an alleged drug pusher to celebrities, was the sister of actress Maritoni Fernandez
