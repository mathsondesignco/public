### [2016-09-18](/news/2016/09/18/index.md)

# Kashmir insurgency. Four Jaish-e-Mohammed militants attack an Indian Army base near the Pakistan border with at least 17 soldiers and four militants killed. 

At least 17 soldiers have been killed, while 4 militants were shot dead by Indian forces.
