### [2016-09-4](/news/2016/09/4/index.md)

# Hong Kong legislative election, 2016: Voters in Hong Kong go to the polls for a Legislative Council election, the first major election since the 2014 pro-democracy street protests. Several young pro-independence candidates win seats in the election.
