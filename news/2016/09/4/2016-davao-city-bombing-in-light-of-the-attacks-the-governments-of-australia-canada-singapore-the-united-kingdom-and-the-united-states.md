# 2016 Davao City bombing: In light of the attacks, the governments of Australia, Canada, Singapore, the United Kingdom, and the United States issue travel warnings against going to Mindanao, Philippines. 

At least 5 countries issue travel warnings after the Davao City explosion that killed 14 and left more than 60 others wounded
