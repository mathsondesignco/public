### [2016-09-12](/news/2016/09/12/index.md)

# The Piton de la Fournaise (Peak of the Furnace) shield volcano on France's Reunion island in the Indian Ocean erupts for the second time this year. It is one of the world's most active. 

The Peak of the Furnace volcano on Reunion Island in the Indian Ocean erupted on Sunday -- the second time this year.
