# Iranian presidential election, 2017: Former President Mahmoud Ahmadinejad declines to run for a third term in the upcoming election. Ayatollah Ali Khamenei has previously advised Ahmadinejad to not seek the presidency next year. 

TEHRAN (Tasnim) – Former Iranian president Mahmoud Ahmadinejad said he does not have any plans for running in the country’s 12th presidential election due to be held on May 19, 2017.
