### [2016-05-2](/news/2016/05/2/index.md)

# Russia-United States relations: United States Chief of Naval Operations Admiral John M. Richardson says Russian jets in the Baltics buzzing a U.S. military ship and planes are escalating tension between the two nations. Richardson added he doesn't think the Russians are trying to provoke an incident but these actions increase the chance of a "tactical miscalculation." 

Another close Russian fighter jet fly-by of a U.S. aircraft prompts plea from Admiral John M. Richardson
