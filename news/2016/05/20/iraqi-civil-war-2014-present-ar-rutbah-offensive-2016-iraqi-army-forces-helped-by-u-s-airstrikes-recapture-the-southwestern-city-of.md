### [2016-05-20](/news/2016/05/20/index.md)

# Iraqi civil war (2014-present): Ar-Rutbah offensive (2016): Iraqi Army forces helped by U.S. airstrikes recapture the southwestern city of Ar-Rutbah from the Islamic State of Iraq and the Levant group. The victory will allow for the re-opening of the Baghdad-Amman highway. 

US military officials say the small town in the country&#39;s southwestern region retaken with little resistance from ISIL.
