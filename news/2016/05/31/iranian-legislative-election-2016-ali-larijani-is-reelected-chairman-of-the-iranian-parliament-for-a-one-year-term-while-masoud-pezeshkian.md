### [2016-05-31](/news/2016/05/31/index.md)

# Iranian legislative election, 2016. Ali Larijani is reelected chairman of the Iranian Parliament for a one year term while Masoud Pezeshkian and Ali Motahari are elected deputy chairpersons. All three elected members were supported by List of Hope in the recent election.
