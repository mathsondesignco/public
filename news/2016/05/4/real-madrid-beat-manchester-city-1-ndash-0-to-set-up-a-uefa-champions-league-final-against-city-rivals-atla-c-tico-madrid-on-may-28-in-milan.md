### [2016-05-4](/news/2016/05/4/index.md)

# Real Madrid beat Manchester City 1&ndash;0 to set-up a UEFA Champions League Final against city rivals Atletico Madrid on May 28, in Milan, Italy. It will be a repeat of the 2014 UEFA Champions League Final.
