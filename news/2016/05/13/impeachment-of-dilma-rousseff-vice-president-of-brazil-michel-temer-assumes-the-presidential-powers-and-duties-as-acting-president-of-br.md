#  Impeachment of Dilma Rousseff: Vice President of Brazil, Michel Temer, assumes the presidential powers and duties as Acting President of Brazil. 

Brazil&#x27;s Senate voted to impeach President Dilma Rousseff early Thursday amid the worst economic crisis there since the 1930s.
