### [2016-05-16](/news/2016/05/16/index.md)

# Colombian conflict (1964-present): Colombian police announce the seizure of about 8|tonne|lb of cocaine in an operation targeting the Clan Usuga gang. This would make it the biggest seizure of drugs in the history of Colombia. 

Police in Colombia say they have seized nearly eight tonnes of cocaine in the town of Turbo, their biggest domestic haul.
