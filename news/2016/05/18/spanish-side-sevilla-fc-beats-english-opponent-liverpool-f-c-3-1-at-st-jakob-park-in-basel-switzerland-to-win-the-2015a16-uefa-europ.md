### [2016-05-18](/news/2016/05/18/index.md)

# Spanish side Sevilla FC beats English opponent Liverpool F.C., 3-1, at St. Jakob-Park in Basel, Switzerland, to win the 2015-16 UEFA Europa League. It is their third straight UEFA Europa League title win. 

Spanish side Sevilla survived a first-half battering before hitting back with three second-half goals to beat English club Liverpool 3-1 in Basel and claim their third Europa League title in a row.
