#   Turkish president Recep Tayyip Erdogan threatens unilateral action against Islamic State of Iraq and Syria inside Syria.  

Turkey is preparing to take action against the Islamic State of Iraq and the Levant (ISIL) in Syria, President Recep Tayyip Erdoğan has said, adding that the country has not received the desired support from allies with armed forces in the region
