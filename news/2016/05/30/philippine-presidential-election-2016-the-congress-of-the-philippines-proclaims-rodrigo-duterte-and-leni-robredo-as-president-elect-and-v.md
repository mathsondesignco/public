#  Philippine presidential election, 2016. The Congress of the Philippines proclaims Rodrigo Duterte and Leni Robredo as President-elect and Vice President-elect of the Philippines, respectively. 

Congress proclaims Rodrigo Duterte as president-elect and Leni Robredo as vice president-elect after approving the joint canvassing committee report on Monday.
