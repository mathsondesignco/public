### [2016-05-15](/news/2016/05/15/index.md)

# Capital punishment in the Philippines. President-elect of the Philippines, Rodrigo Duterte, vows to reintroduce capital punishment and give security forces permission to shoot to kill for organized crime figures and people resisting arrest. Duterte is due to take office on June 30, 2016. 

The Philippine president-elect says he will seek to reintroduce the death penalty and give security forces shoot-to-kill powers in certain circumstances.
