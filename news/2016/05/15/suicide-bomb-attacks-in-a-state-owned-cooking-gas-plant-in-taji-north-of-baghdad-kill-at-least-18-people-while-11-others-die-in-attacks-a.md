### [2016-05-15](/news/2016/05/15/index.md)

# Suicide bomb attacks in a state owned cooking gas plant in Taji, north of Baghdad, kill at least 18 people, while 11 others die in attacks across the country. 

ISIL attacks on a natural gas plant and other targets in or close to Baghdad killed at least 29 people on Sunday.
