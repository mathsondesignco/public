### [2016-05-14](/news/2016/05/14/index.md)

# In football, FC Barcelona wins La Liga for the 24th time. It is FC Barcelona's second straight title and sixth in the last eight years. 

Cristiano Ronaldo scored twice to lead Real Madrid, but Luis Suarez netted a hat trick for Barcelona, and the Catalan side lifts the trophy again.
