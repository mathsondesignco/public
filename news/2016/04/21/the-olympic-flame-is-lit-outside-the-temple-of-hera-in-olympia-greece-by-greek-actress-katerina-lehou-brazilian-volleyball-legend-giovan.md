### [2016-04-21](/news/2016/04/21/index.md)

# The Olympic flame is lit outside the Temple of Hera in Olympia, Greece, by Greek actress Katerina Lehou.  Brazilian volleyball legend Giovane Gavio starts the relay that will reach the capital Brasilia on May 3 for transport throughout the South American country by 12,000 torchbearers, arriving at Maracana Stadium in Rio de Janeiro on August 5. 

Flame for Rio de Janeiro Olympics has been lit at the birthplace of the ancient games.
