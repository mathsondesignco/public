# A bus carrying members of the Bharati Gananatya opera troupe crashes into a gorge in India's Odisha state resulting in 25 people killed and 11 injured. 

A bus carrying members of an opera troupe plunges 76 metres into a gorge in eastern India, killing at least 25 people, media reports say.
