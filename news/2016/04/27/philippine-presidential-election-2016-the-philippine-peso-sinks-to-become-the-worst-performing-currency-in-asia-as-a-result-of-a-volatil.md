# Philippine presidential election, 2016: The Philippine peso sinks to become the "worst performing currency in Asia" as a result of a volatile and unpredictable presidential election. 

A controversial city mayor’s tightening grip on the Philippine presidential race has made the nation’s currency Asia’s worst performer this month.
