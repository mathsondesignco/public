### [2016-04-8](/news/2016/04/8/index.md)

# ISIL militants reportedly execute 175 workers who were captured earlier this week at a cement factory situated to the east of Damascus. 

Islamic State militants have killed 175 workers who were captured at a cement factory situated to the east of Damascus, Sputnik Arabic reported, citing its sources.
