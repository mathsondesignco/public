### [2016-04-8](/news/2016/04/8/index.md)

# North Korea-South Korea relations. North Korean defectors. A group of 13 North Koreans working at a restaurant in an unidentified country defect en masse to South Korea, according to Seoul's Ministry of Unification. 

Friday&#39;s public announcement is unusual for South Korea, which typically keeps a low-profile about defections.
