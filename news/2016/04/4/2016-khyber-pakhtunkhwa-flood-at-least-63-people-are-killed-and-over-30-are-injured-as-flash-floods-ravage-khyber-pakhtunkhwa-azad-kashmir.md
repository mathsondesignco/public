# 2016 Khyber Pakhtunkhwa flood: At least 63 people are killed and over 30 are injured as flash floods ravage Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, Azad Kashmir and other parts of northwest Pakistan. 

At least 63 killed and more than 30 injured as incessant rains cause havoc particularly in Khyber Pakhtunkhwa province.
