### [2016-04-12](/news/2016/04/12/index.md)

#  Iran - Italy relations. During a visit to Iran, Prime Minister of Italy Matteo Renzi signs various bilateral agreements to enhance relations between the two countries, including plans for cooperation between the Italian electricity and gas company Enel and the National Iranian Oil Company.
