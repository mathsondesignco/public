### [2016-04-9](/news/2016/04/9/index.md)

# Moro Conflict. 2016 Battle of Basilan. At least 18 soldiers are killed and 52 injured in clashes with Abu Sayyaf militants and allied gunmen in Basilan island, Philippines. 

Philippine military officials say at least 18 soldiers have been killed in fierce daylong fighting with Muslim militants in the south.
