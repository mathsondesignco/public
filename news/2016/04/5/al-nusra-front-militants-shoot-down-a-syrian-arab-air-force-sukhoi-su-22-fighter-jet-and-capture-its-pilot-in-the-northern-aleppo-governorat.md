### [2016-04-5](/news/2016/04/5/index.md)

# Al-Nusra Front militants shoot down a Syrian Arab Air Force Sukhoi Su-22 fighter jet and capture its pilot in the northern Aleppo Governorate. 

The Syrian al-Qaeda faction Jabhat al-Nusra has captured an Assad regime pilot after his Russian-made jet was shot down near a town that has been subject to intense fighting in the north of the country.
