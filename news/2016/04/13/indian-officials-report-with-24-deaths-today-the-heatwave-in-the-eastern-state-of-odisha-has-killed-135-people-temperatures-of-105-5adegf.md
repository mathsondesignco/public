### [2016-04-13](/news/2016/04/13/index.md)

# Indian officials report, with 24 deaths today, the heatwave in the eastern state of Odisha has killed 135 people. Temperatures of 105.5degF (40.8degC) were recorded yesterday at 19 locations across the state. Meteorologists say the heat, an after-effect of El Nino, has delayed the traditional April rains.
