# The government of Malawi declares a state of emergency across the country over worsening food shortages caused by a severe drought that is affecting much of Southern Africa with about 2.8 million Malawians, or 20% of the population, facing food insecurity. 

About 2.8 million Malawians face food insecurity, making country one of the worst hit in southern African drought.
