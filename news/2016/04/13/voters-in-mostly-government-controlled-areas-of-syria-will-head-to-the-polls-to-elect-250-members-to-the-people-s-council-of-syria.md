### [2016-04-13](/news/2016/04/13/index.md)

# Voters in mostly government controlled areas of Syria will head to the polls to elect 250 members to the People's Council of Syria. 

The parliamentary elections in Syria will be held on April 13, 2016, said a statement issued by Syrian President Bashar Assad.
