### [2016-04-13](/news/2016/04/13/index.md)

# In basketball, the 2015-16 Golden State Warriors team sets a new NBA record for regular season wins with 73 wins this season. The 1995-96 Chicago Bulls previously held the record with 72. 

Stephen Curry and the Warriors stand alone in NBA history after a historic 73-9 regular season.
