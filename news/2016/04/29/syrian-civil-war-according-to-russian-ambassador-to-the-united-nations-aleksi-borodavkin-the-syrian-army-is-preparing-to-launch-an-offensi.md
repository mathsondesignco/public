### [2016-04-29](/news/2016/04/29/index.md)

# Syrian Civil War: According to Russian ambassador to the United Nations Aleksi Borodavkin, the Syrian Army is preparing to launch an offensive against the ISIL strongholds of Al-Raqqah and the countryside around Deir ez-Zor and that the Russian Air Force will be providing the ground campaign with air support. 

Sources in city say three clinics in rebel-held areas of Aleppo have been destroyed in bombardment.
