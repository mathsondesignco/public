### [2016-04-29](/news/2016/04/29/index.md)

#  Zika virus outbreak (2015-present): The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention reports the first Zika virus related death in the United States, an elderly man in Puerto Rico. 

The man died from internal bleeding resulting from a rare immune reaction to his Zika infection.
