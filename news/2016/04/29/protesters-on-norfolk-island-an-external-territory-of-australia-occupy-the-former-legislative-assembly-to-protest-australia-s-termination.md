### [2016-04-29](/news/2016/04/29/index.md)

# Protesters on Norfolk Island, an external territory of Australia, occupy the former Legislative Assembly to protest Australia's termination of the island's autonomy. In July, Norfolk Island is set to become part of the State of New South Wales. 

Protesters on Norfolk Island are occupying the grounds of the former legislative assembly and refusing to leave.
