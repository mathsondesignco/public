# Macedonian police fire tear gas at refugees as they attempted to break through a fence at the Greece-Macedonia border near Idomeni, sparking clashes that injured 200 people. 

Hundreds injured as Macedonian police fire tear gas and rubber bullets at refugees trying to cross over from Greece.
