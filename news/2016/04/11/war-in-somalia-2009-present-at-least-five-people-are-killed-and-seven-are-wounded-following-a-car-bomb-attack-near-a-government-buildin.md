# War in Somalia (2009-present): At least five people are killed and seven are wounded following a car bomb attack near a government building in Somalia's capital, Mogadishu. Al-Shabaab claims responsibility for the attack. 

At least five people are killed after a suicide bomb attack on a restaurant near a local government building in the Somali capital, Mogadishu.
