# A severe heat wave grips Malaysia as much of Southeast Asia struggles with unusually hot and dry weather created by the El Nino season. Temperatures in Malaysia soared above 37degC, prompting more than 250 schools to close. 

Intense spell of hot and dry weather brought on by El Nino hits food and water supplies across parts of SE Asia.
