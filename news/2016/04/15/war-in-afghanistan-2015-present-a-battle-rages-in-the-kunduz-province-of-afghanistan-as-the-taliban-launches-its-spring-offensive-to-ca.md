### [2016-04-15](/news/2016/04/15/index.md)

# War in Afghanistan (2015-present). A battle rages in the Kunduz Province of Afghanistan as the Taliban launches its spring offensive to capture the city of Kunduz. According to a Taliban spokesperson, several outposts already fell to them but this could not be verified immediately while a police chief says that the security forces were keeping "the situation under control". 

Police chief says at least 40 Taliban fighters killed after launching offensive to seize key city of Kunduz.
