### [2016-04-26](/news/2016/04/26/index.md)

# European migrant crisis: Clashes break out between migrants and riot police at the Moria camp on the Greek island of Lesbos after a policeman reportedly hit a minor. The violence occurred as a Greek migration minister was visiting the center. Several refugees/migrants are reported injured. 

Clashes break out between migrants and riot police at a detention centre on the Greek island of Lesbos, police say.
