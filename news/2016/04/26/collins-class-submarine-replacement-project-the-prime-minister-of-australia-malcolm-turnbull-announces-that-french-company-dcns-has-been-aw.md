# Collins-class submarine replacement project: The Prime Minister of Australia Malcolm Turnbull announces that French company DCNS has been awarded a $50 billion contract to build a new fleet of submarines for the Royal Australian Navy with the subs to be built in Adelaide. 

French company wins the contract to build Australia&#039;s next fleet of submarines, with Prime Minister Malcolm Turnbull promising thousands of jobs.
