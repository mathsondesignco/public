# China denies a Hong Kong port call by the U.S. Carrier Strike Group 3, which includes the aircraft carrier John C. Stennis and accompanying vessels. 

China recently denied a request from a U.S. aircraft carrier for a port visit in Hong Kong, the State Department said.
