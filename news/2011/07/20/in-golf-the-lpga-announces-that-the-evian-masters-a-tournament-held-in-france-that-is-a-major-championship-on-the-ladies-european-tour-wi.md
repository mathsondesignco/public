### [2011-07-20](/news/2011/07/20/index.md)

# In golf, the LPGA announces that the Evian Masters, a tournament held in France that is a major championship on the Ladies European Tour, will become the LPGA's fifth major in 2013. 

The Evian Masters in France will become the fifth major on the LPGA Tour starting in 2013.
