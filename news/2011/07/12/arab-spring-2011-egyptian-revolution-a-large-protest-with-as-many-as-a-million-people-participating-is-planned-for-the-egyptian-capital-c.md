### [2011-07-12](/news/2011/07/12/index.md)

# Arab Spring: 2011 Egyptian revolution:: A large protest with as many as a million people participating is planned for the Egyptian capital Cairo over perceived lack of progress since the 2011 Egyptian revolution. 

Thousands of Egyptians are protesting in Cairo to demand faster political reform, despite warnings and fresh concessions from Egyptian officials.
