### [2011-07-12](/news/2011/07/12/index.md)

# Guatemalan police arrest two men in relation to the assassination of Argentine singer Facundo Cabral as fans across the continent mourn his shooting and a tribute concert is held in his memory. 

Police in Guatemala arrest two men in connection with the killing of Argentine singer Facundo Cabral on Saturday.
