### [2011-07-2](/news/2011/07/2/index.md)

# 11 members of one family are killed by a roadside bomb in southern Afghanistan while traveling to Pakistan. 

Members of family travelling through Ghazni reportedly killed, a day after a similar attack in Nimroz left 13 dead.
