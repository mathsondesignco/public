# European sovereign debt crisis: Eurozone finance ministers agree to give a further -12 billion over the next two weeks to Greece as it fights bankruptcy. 

Eurozone finance ministers agree to release a further 12bn euros of emergency aid to Greece to help it avoid bankruptcy.
