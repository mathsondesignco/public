### [2011-07-14](/news/2011/07/14/index.md)

# Chairman of Ireland's governing Fine Gael parliamentary party Charlie Flanagan calls for Papal Nuncio Giuseppe Leanza to be expelled from the country after the publication of a report into the sexual abuse of children by priests in the Roman Catholic Diocese of Cloyne, while Tanaiste Eamon Gilmore attacks the Vatican's interference in the scandal. 

The chairman of the Fine Gael parliamentary party has called for the expulsion of the Papal Nuncio following revelations in the Cloyne Report.
