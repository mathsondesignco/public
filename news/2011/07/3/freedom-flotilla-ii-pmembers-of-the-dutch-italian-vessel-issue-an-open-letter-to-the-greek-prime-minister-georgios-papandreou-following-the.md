### [2011-07-3](/news/2011/07/3/index.md)

# Freedom Flotilla II:Members of the Dutch-Italian vessel issue an open letter to the Greek prime minister Georgios Papandreou following the alleged sabotage of two ships and the prevention of the rest from setting sail for Gaza. 

Pro-Palestinian activists have their sights set on sailing to Gaza, despite Greek authorities blocking them.
