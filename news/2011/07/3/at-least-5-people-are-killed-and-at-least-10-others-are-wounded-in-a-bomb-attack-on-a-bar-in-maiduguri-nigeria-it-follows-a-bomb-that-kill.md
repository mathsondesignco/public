### [2011-07-3](/news/2011/07/3/index.md)

# At least 5 people are killed and at least 10 others are wounded in a bomb attack on a bar in Maiduguri, Nigeria. It follows a bomb that killed 25 people this day last week. 

At least 10 people die in attacks blamed on Islamist militants in the north-eastern Nigerian city of Maiduguri, a week after a similar attack killed 25.
