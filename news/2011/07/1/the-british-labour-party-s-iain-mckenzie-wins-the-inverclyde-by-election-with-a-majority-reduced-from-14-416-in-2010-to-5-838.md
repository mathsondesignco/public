### [2011-07-1](/news/2011/07/1/index.md)

# The British Labour Party's Iain McKenzie wins the Inverclyde by-election with a majority reduced from 14,416 in 2010 to 5,838. 

Labour wins the Inverclyde by-election but its majority is more than halved.
