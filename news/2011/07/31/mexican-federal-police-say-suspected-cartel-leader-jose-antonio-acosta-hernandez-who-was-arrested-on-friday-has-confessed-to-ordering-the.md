### [2011-07-31](/news/2011/07/31/index.md)

# Mexican Federal Police say suspected cartel leader Jos Antonio Acosta Hernndez, who was arrested on Friday, has confessed to ordering the murder of 1,500 people in the country's northern state of Chihuahua. 

A man arrested in the northern Mexican city of Chihuahua on Friday admits to ordering the murder of 1,500 people, police say.
