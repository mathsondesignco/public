### [2011-07-31](/news/2011/07/31/index.md)

# Ramadan Massacre:At least 121 people are killed in a Syrian Army tank raid on the town of Hama and over 150 people are reportedly killed across the country. 

At least 136 people reportedly killed in violence throughout the country, a day ahead of the start of Ramadan.
