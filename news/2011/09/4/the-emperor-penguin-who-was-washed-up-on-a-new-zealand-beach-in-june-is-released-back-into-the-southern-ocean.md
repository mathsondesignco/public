### [2011-09-4](/news/2011/09/4/index.md)

# The Emperor penguin who was washed up on a New Zealand beach in June is released back into the Southern Ocean. 

An emperor penguin known as Happy Feet is released into the ocean south of New Zealand after being found washed up on a beach in June.
