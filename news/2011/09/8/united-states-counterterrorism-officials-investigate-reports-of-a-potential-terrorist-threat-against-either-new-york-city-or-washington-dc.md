### [2011-09-8](/news/2011/09/8/index.md)

# United States counterterrorism officials investigate reports of a potential terrorist threat against either New York City or Washington DC, linked to the 10th anniversary of the September 11 attacks on Sunday. 

The Department of Homeland Security said a new report of a threat of a car or truck bomb attack in New York City or Washington was “specific, credible, but unconfirmed.”
