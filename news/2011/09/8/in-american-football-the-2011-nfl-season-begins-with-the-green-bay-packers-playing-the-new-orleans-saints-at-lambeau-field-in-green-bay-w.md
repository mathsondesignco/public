### [2011-09-8](/news/2011/09/8/index.md)

# In American football, the 2011 NFL season begins, with the Green Bay Packers playing the New Orleans Saints at Lambeau Field in Green Bay, Wisconsin with the Packers winning a shootout 42-34. 

The National Football League (NFL) season gets underway with a bang on Thursday as the past two Super Bowl champions, the Green Bay Packers and the New Orleans Saints, go head-to-head at a packed Lambeau Field.
