### [2011-09-5](/news/2011/09/5/index.md)

# Turkish hackers gain access to the websites of several companies, including Vodafone, the "Daily Telegraph" and UPS, redirecting visitors to a site set up by the group. 

Turkish hackers managed to re-direct visitors away from high-profile sites, including Vodafone and the Daily Telegraph.
