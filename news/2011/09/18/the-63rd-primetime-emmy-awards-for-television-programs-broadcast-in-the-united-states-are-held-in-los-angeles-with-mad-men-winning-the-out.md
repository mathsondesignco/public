### [2011-09-18](/news/2011/09/18/index.md)

# The 63rd Primetime Emmy Awards for television programs broadcast in the United States are held in Los Angeles with "Mad Men" winning the outstanding drama series and "Modern Family" winning the Outstanding Comedy. 

The hit shows repeated the feat, winning the top awards for comedy and drama, while some first-timers pulled a few upsets.
