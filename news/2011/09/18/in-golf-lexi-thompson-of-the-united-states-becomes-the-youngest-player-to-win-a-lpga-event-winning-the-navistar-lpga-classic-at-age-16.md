### [2011-09-18](/news/2011/09/18/index.md)

# In golf, Lexi Thompson of the United States becomes the youngest player to win a LPGA event winning the Navistar LPGA Classic at age 16. 

Sixteen-year-old Lexi Thompson closed with a 2-under 70 on Sunday to win the Navistar LPGA Classic by five strokes over Tiffany Joh.
