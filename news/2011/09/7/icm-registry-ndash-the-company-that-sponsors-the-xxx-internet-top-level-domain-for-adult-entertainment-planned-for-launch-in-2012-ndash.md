### [2011-09-7](/news/2011/09/7/index.md)

# ICM Registry &ndash; the company that sponsors the .xxx Internet top-level domain for adult entertainment, planned for launch in 2012 &ndash; is giving organisations and people in the public eye the opportunity to block their names from being used by websites with the suffix. 

Companies and celebrities are being given the chance to protect their names from hijacking ahead of the launch of .xxx.
