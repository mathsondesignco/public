### [2011-09-14](/news/2011/09/14/index.md)

# Two days after his release from prison, British Conservative peer Lord Hanningfield is arrested on suspicion of fraudulently claiming expenses during his time as leader of Essex County Council. 

Lord Hanningfield, who was jailed for expenses fraud, has been arrested over money he claimed from Essex County Council, the BBC understands.
