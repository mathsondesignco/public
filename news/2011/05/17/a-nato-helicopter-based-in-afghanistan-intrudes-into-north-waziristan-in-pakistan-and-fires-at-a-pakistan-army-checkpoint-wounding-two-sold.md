### [2011-05-17](/news/2011/05/17/index.md)

# A NATO helicopter based in Afghanistan intrudes into North Waziristan in Pakistan and fires at a Pakistan Army checkpoint, wounding two soldiers. 

The Pakistani army protests after two of its soldiers are injured in a clash with Nato helicopters in the border region of North Waziristan.
