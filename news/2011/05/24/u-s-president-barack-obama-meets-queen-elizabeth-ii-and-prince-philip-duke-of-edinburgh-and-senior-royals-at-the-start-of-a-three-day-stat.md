# U.S. President Barack Obama meets Queen Elizabeth II and Prince Philip, Duke of Edinburgh and senior royals at the start of a three-day state visit to the United Kingdom. 

Barack Obama has met the Queen and senior royals at the start of a three-day state visit as he hails the &quot;essential relationship&quot; between the US and UK.
