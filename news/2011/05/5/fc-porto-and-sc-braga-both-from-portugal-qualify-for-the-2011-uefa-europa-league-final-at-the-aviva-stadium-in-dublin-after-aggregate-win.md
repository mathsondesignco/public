### [2011-05-5](/news/2011/05/5/index.md)

# FC Porto and SC Braga, both from Portugal, qualify for the 2011 UEFA Europa League Final at the Aviva Stadium in Dublin, after aggregate wins over Villarreal CF and Benfica respectively. The final is also the first ever time that two finalists have been situated less than 50 kilometers from each other.
