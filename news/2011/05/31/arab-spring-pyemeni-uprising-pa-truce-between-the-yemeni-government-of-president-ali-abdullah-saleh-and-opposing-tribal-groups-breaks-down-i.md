### [2011-05-31](/news/2011/05/31/index.md)

# Arab Spring:Yemeni uprising:A truce between the Yemeni government of President Ali Abdullah Saleh and opposing tribal groups breaks down increasing the chance of a civil war. 

More than 50 people have been killed in demonstrations in the southern Yemeni city of Taiz since Sunday, the UN human rights office says.
