### [2011-05-31](/news/2011/05/31/index.md)

# In Germany fourteen people have been reported dead from hemolytic-uremic syndrome outbreak as of 30 May, with another 329 confirmed and up to 1,200 suspected cases.

German officials held crisis talks, with reports at least 14 people have died amid a food poisoning outbreak blamed on imported cucumbers.
