# Belgian professional cyclist Wouter Weylandt is killed in a crash in the 2011 Giro d'Italia. 

Belgium&rsquo;s Wouter Weylandt was killed in a horrific crash while descending the Passo Bocco 25km from the finish of stage three of the Giro d&rsquo;Italia in Rapello on Monday.
