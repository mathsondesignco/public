### [2011-05-26](/news/2011/05/26/index.md)

# The United States announces the removal of a number of troops from Pakistan, following a request from the Pakistani government. 

The US military says it is withdrawing some of the more than 200 troops it has in Pakistan, after a request from the government in Islamabad.
