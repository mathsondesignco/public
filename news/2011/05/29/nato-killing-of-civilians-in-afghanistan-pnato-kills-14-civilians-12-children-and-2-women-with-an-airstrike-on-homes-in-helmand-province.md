### [2011-05-29](/news/2011/05/29/index.md)

# NATO killing of civilians in Afghanistan:NATO kills 14 civilians, 12 children and 2 women, with an airstrike on homes in Helmand Province. 

Deadly attack in volatile southwestern province followed small arms fire on US Marines.
