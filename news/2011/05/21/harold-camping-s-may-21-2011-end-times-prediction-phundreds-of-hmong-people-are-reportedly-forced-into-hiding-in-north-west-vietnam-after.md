# Harold Camping's May 21, 2011 end times prediction:Hundreds of Hmong people are reportedly forced into hiding in north-west Vietnam, after security forces disperse thousands awaiting the supposed Second Coming of Christ. 

A prophecy that Saturday is Judgment Day, and the start of the five-month end of the world, is dividing families.
