### [2011-05-21](/news/2011/05/21/index.md)

# "Dawn", Pakistan's largest English-language newspaper, begins publication of thousands of U.S. diplomatic cables it has obtained in a deal with WikiLeaks and Julian Assange. The cables show that the Pakistani military asked the United States to increase its drone attacks against insurgents on Pakistani territory, a request Pakistani authorities have not admitted in public. 

Leaked US diplomatic messages show Pakistani military requested more drone strikes and help from US special forces.
