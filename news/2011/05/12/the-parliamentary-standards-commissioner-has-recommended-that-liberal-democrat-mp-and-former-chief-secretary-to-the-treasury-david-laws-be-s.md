### [2011-05-12](/news/2011/05/12/index.md)

# The Parliamentary Standards Commissioner has recommended that Liberal Democrat MP and former Chief Secretary to the Treasury David Laws be suspended from the House of Commons for 7 days over wrongly claimed expenses. 

Lib Dem MP David Laws will be suspended from the Commons for seven days after being found guilty of &quot;a series of serious breaches of the rules&quot; over his expenses.
