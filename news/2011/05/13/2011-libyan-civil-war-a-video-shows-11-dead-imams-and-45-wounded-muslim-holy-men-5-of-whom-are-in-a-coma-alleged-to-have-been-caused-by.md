### [2011-05-13](/news/2011/05/13/index.md)

# 2011 Libyan civil war:: A video shows 11 dead imams and 45 wounded Muslim holy men, 5 of whom are in a coma, alleged to have been caused by a NATO airstrike. Those attacked were said to have been at rest and sleeping while participating in a long peace march; Muslims and Christians unite in condemnation of the attack. 

The Libyan leader responds to claims by the Italian foreign minister in an audio statement aired on state television.
