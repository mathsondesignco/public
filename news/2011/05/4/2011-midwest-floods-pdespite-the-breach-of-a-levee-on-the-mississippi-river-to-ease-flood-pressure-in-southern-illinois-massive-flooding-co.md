# 2011 Midwest floods:Despite the breach of a levee on the Mississippi River to ease flood pressure in southern Illinois, massive flooding continues from Minnesota to Louisiana and hundreds of square miles of mostly farmland in Missouri are under water. 

The Mississippi river spilled out across huge swaths of farmland in the American South and Midwest on Wednesday, creating massive flooding from Minnesota to Louisiana.
