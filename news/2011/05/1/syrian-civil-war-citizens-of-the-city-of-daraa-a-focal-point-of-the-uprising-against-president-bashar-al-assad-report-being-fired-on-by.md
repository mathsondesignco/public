### [2011-05-1](/news/2011/05/1/index.md)

# Syrian civil war:. Citizens of the city of Daraa, a focal point of the uprising against President Bashar al-Assad, report being fired on by government soldiers and tanks. 

Syrian soldiers and tanks have been firing in the city of Deraa, a centre of anti-government protests, residents say.
