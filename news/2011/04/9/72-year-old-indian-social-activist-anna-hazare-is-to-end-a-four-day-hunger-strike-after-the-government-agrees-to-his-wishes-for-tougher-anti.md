### [2011-04-9](/news/2011/04/9/index.md)

# 72-year-old Indian social activist Anna Hazare is to end a four-day hunger strike after the government agrees to his wishes for tougher anti-corruption laws which have gained widespread public support. 

Indian anti-corruption activist Anna Hazare calls off his four-day-old hunger strike after the government agrees to all of his demands.
