# Over 120 rockets are fired into southern Israel this weekend, wounding 5 people. The Iron Dome missile system intercepts 10 rockets. 

Iron Dome intercepts 10 rockets; Israel reviewing cease-fire proposal; Hamas figure involved in Schalit kidnapping killed.
