### [2011-04-4](/news/2011/04/4/index.md)

# Prince Albert II of Monaco and his fiancee Charlene Wittstock arrive in Ireland for a state visit, 50 years after the visit of Prince Rainier and Hollywood actress Princess Grace. They are to dine at a state banquet hosted by President Mary McAleese and her husband at Aras an Uachtarain. 

Prince Albert II of Monaco and his fiancée Charlene Wittstock today began a three-day State visit to Ireland.
