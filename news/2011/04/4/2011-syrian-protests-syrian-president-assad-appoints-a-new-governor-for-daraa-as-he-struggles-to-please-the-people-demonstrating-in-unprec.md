### [2011-04-4](/news/2011/04/4/index.md)

# 2011 Syrian protests:. Syrian president Assad appoints a new governor for Daraa as he struggles to please the people demonstrating in unprecedented protests against his regime. 

In a bid to appease protesters, the Syrian president continues to make new government appointments.
