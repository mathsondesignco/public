# 2011 Libyan civil war in Tunisia:Muammar Gaddafi brings the 2011 Libyan civil war over the border into Tunisia, prompting complaints from the Tunisian foreign ministry. 

Tunisia condemns a violation of its territory after fighting between Libyan rebels and forces loyal to Colonel Gaddafi spills over the border.
