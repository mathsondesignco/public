### [2011-04-28](/news/2011/04/28/index.md)

# India chooses two European fighters for a key $11 billion military contract, ignoring lobbying from United States president Barack Obama, with United States Ambassador to India Timothy J. Roemer saying he is "deeply disappointed". Roemer resigns from his post. 

India shortlists two European fighter jets and rules out two US rivals for a key $11bn contract, a move the US ambassador calls &quot;deeply disappointing&quot;.
