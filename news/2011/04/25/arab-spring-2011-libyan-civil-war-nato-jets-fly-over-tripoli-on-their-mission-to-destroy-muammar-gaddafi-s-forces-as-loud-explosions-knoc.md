# Arab Spring: 2011 Libyan Civil War:: NATO jets fly over Tripoli on their mission to destroy Muammar Gaddafi's forces as loud explosions knock 3 television stations (Libyan Television, Jamahiriya TV and Shababiya) off-the-air. 

Syrian security forces backed by tanks advance into the restive city of Deraa, in an apparent escalation of a crackdown on the protest movement.
