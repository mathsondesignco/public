# Scottish football:UK police express relief after an Old Firm match between Celtic and Rangers plays out without descending into expected violence related to parcel bombs sent amid increasing sectarian tensions. 

Rangers and Celtic, the top two teams in the Scottish Premier League standings, played to a scoreless tie in the Old Firm match in Glasgow Sunday.
