### [2011-06-29](/news/2011/06/29/index.md)

# In tennis, Jo-Wilfried Tsonga comes back to defeat Roger Federer at Wimbledon, the first loss Federer has sustained from being two sets ahead at a Grand Slam. 

Frenchman defeats six-time champion to reach semi-final against second seed Djokovic, while Murray will take on Nadal.
