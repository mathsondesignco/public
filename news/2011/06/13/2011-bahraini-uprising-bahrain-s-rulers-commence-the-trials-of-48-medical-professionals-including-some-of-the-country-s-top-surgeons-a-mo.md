### [2011-06-13](/news/2011/06/13/index.md)

# 2011 Bahraini uprising: Bahrain's rulers commence the trials of 48 medical professionals, including some of the country's top surgeons, a move seen as the hounding of those who treated injured protesters during the popular uprising which was crushed by the military intervention of Saudi Arabia. 

Trial of 48 medical workers accused of attempting to topple the monarchy opens amid condemnation by rights groups.
