### [2011-06-7](/news/2011/06/7/index.md)

# A massive wildfire burning in the US state of Arizona already covering almost 500 square miles remains completely uncontained and is sending dense smoke billowing across several other states; evacuations have been ordered for several communities. 

Dry, windy conditions whipped flames and spread smoke across several states as a massive Arizona wildfire threatened mountain communities.
