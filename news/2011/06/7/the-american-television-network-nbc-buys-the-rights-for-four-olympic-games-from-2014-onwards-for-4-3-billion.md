### [2011-06-7](/news/2011/06/7/index.md)

# The American television network NBC buys the rights for four Olympic Games from 2014 onwards for $4.3 billion. 

NBC extended its Olympic reign to 2020 as Comcast, its parent company, agreed Tuesday to acquire the rights.
