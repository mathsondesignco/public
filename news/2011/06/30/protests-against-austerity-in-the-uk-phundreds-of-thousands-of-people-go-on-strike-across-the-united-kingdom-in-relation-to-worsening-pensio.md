### [2011-06-30](/news/2011/06/30/index.md)

# Protests against austerity in the UK:Hundreds of thousands of people go on strike across the United Kingdom in relation to worsening pension conditions being discussed by the government and opposition, while MPs from both the government and opposition condemn them for going on strike. 

Schools, courts and jobcentres are among the services affected across the UK as public sector workers strike against proposed pension changes.
