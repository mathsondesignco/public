### [2011-06-30](/news/2011/06/30/index.md)

# Bangladesh's parliament overturns a requirement, dating from the mid-1990s, that general elections be watched over by non-partisan caretaker governments; the opposition protests over fears that future elections could be rigged. 

The Bangladeshi parliament overturns a 15-year-old requirement that general elections are overseen by non-partisan caretaker governments.
