### [2011-06-30](/news/2011/06/30/index.md)

# The United Nations extends MONUSCO, its mission in the Democratic Republic of the Congo, for a thirteenth year, with 5 million people dead so far. 

Peacekeepers to stay another year as war estimated to have taken five million lives rages on.
