### [2011-06-30](/news/2011/06/30/index.md)

# A by-election is held in the UK Parliament constituency of Inverclyde to elect a new MP following the death of the previous incumbent, Labour's David Cairns in May. 

Labour wins the Inverclyde by-election but its majority is more than halved.
