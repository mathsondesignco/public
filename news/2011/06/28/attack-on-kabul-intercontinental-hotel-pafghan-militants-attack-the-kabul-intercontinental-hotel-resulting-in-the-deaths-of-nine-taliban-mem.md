### [2011-06-28](/news/2011/06/28/index.md)

# Attack on Kabul InterContinental Hotel:Afghan militants attack the Kabul InterContinental Hotel resulting in the deaths of nine Taliban members and five or six hotel workers or guests. 

Six bombers said to have stormed the Intercontinental, popular with foreigners and officials, after a firefight outside.
