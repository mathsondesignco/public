# British Prime Minister David Cameron has announced major changes to plans for reforms of the NHS in England saying that the government has made mistakes, but he insists it has not made "a humiliating U-turn". 

David Cameron agrees to major changes to plans for the NHS in England, but insists the government has not made &quot;a humiliating U-turn&quot;.
