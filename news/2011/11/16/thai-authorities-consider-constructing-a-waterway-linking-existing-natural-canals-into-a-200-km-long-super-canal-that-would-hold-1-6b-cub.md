### [2011-11-16](/news/2011/11/16/index.md)

# Thai authorities consider constructing a waterway linking existing natural canals into a 200-km long "super-canal" that would  hold 1.6B cubic meters of water and speed the draining of run-off to prevent flooding like that currently inundating Bangkok and other parts of the country.
