# Frisking of A. P. J. Abdul Kalam by the United States:Former President of India A. P. J. Abdul Kalam is frisked and has his clothes inspected for explosives by officials in New York, with the United States apologising for its breach of protocol. 

The U.S. government has apologized to India and to former Indian President A.P.J. Abdul Kalam for not following “appropriate procedures” during the security screening of Mr. Kalam on Sept. 29 at John F. Kennedy airport in New York.
