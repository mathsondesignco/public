### [2011-11-13](/news/2011/11/13/index.md)

# An Israeli cabinet committee passes legislation backed by prime minister Binyamin Netanyahu to limit potentially tens of millions in foreign funding to non-government organizations. 

PM supports legislation which passes with majority of 11 to 5; new law bans foreign donations from exceeding NIS 20,000.
