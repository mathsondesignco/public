### [2011-11-27](/news/2011/11/27/index.md)

# In Canada, Chinook windstorms tear through Alberta and Saskatchewan, producing a dust storm in downtown Calgary and causing millions of dollars in damage. The windstorms fuel a grass fire that grows to two kilometres wide near Lethbridge, Alberta, before being contained, and gusts reach a maximum speed of {{convert|149|km/h|mph|abbr=on}}.
