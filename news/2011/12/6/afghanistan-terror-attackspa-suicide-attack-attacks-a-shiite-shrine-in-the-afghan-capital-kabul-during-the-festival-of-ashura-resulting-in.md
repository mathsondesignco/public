### [2011-12-6](/news/2011/12/6/index.md)

# Afghanistan terror attacksA suicide attack attacks a Shiite shrine in the Afghan capital Kabul during the festival of Ashura, resulting in at least 50 people killed and hundreds injured. Hours later another attack takes place at Mazar-i-Sharif, killing at least 4.
