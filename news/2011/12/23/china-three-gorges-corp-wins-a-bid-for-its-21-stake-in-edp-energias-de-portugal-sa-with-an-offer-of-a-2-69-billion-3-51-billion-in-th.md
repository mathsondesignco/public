### [2011-12-23](/news/2011/12/23/index.md)

# China Three Gorges Corp. wins a bid for its 21% stake in EDP-Energias de Portugal SA with an offer of EUR2.69 billion ($3.51 billion), in the first of a series of sales of state-owned assets under Portugal's austerity program. This is the first time a mainland Chinese firm has acquired a significant stake in a southern European company.
