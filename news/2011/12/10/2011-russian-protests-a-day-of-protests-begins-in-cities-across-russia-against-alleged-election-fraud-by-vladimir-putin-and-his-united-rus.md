### [2011-12-10](/news/2011/12/10/index.md)

# 2011 Russian protests:: A day of protests begins in cities across Russia against alleged election fraud by Vladimir Putin and his United Russia party, with fifty thousand people marching in Moscow and 25 people arrested in Khabarovsk.
