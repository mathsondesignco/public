### [2011-12-10](/news/2011/12/10/index.md)

# At least six people are killed in clashes in the Democratic Republic of the Congo in the aftermath of disputed general elections in which Joseph Kabila was named President. 

At least six people have been killed in the capital Kinshasa as opposition supporters face off with police.
