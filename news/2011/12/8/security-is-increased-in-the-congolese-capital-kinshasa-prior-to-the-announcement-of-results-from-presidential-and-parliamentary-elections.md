### [2011-12-8](/news/2011/12/8/index.md)

# Security is increased in the Congolese capital Kinshasa prior to the announcement of results from presidential and parliamentary elections. 

Electoral body says all 60,000 ballots boxes have not been counted and will wait until Friday to release results.
