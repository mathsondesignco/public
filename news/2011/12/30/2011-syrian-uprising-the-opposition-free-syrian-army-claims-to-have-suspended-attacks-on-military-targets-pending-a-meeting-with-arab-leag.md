# 2011 Syrian uprising:: The Opposition Free Syrian Army claims to have suspended attacks on military targets pending a meeting with Arab League monitors. 

Syrian troops fire on tens of thousands of protesters as they hold rallies in front of visiting Arab League monitors, killing 35, activists say.
