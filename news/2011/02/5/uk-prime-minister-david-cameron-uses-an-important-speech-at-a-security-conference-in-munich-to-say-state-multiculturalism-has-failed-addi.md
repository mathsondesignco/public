### [2011-02-5](/news/2011/02/5/index.md)

# UK prime minister David Cameron uses an important speech at a security conference in Munich to say "state multiculturalism" has failed, adding that the UK needs a stronger national identity and promising to promote Western values. 

David Cameron criticises &quot;state multiculturalism&quot; in his first speech as prime minister on radicalisation and the causes of terrorism.
