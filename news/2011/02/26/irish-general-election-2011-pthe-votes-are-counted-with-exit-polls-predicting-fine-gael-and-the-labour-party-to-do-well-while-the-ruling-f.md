# Irish general election, 2011:The votes are counted, with exit polls predicting Fine Gael and the Labour Party to do well while the ruling Fianna Fail is expected to do poorly. 

Counting of votes has begun around the country as an exit poll indicated Fine Gael will lead the government for the first time&hellip;
