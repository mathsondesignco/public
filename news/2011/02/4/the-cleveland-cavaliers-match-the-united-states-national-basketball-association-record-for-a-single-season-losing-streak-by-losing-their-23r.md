### [2011-02-4](/news/2011/02/4/index.md)

# The Cleveland Cavaliers match the United States National Basketball Association record for a single-season losing streak by losing their 23rd straight match to the Memphis Grizzlies. 

The Cleveland Cavaliers matched the longest single-season losing streak in NBA history, dropping their 23rd straight with a 112-105 loss to the Memphis Grizzlies on Friday night.
