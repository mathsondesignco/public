### [2011-02-8](/news/2011/02/8/index.md)

# 2011 Egyptian protests: The protests continue in Egypt for a fifteenth day, hours after a detained Google executive is released with the crowds the biggest yet. 

Pro-democracy supporters hold fresh rallies in Cairo, just hours after the release of a detained Google executive.
