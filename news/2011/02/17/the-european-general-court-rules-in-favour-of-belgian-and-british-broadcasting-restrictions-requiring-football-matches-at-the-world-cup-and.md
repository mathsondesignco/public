### [2011-02-17](/news/2011/02/17/index.md)

# The European General Court rules in favour of Belgian and British broadcasting restrictions requiring football matches at the World Cup and European Championships on to be available on free-to-air TV. 

The European General Court (EGC) says the UK is within its rights to show all World Cup and European Championship games on free-to-air TV.
