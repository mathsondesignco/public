### [2011-02-10](/news/2011/02/10/index.md)

# Utah Jazz head coach Jerry Sloan, the longest-tenured coach in any of North America's four major leagues, announces his resignation effective immediately. Sloan, inducted in the Basketball Hall of Fame in 2009, had been with the Jazz since 1988. 

Jerry Sloan resigned as head coach of the Utah Jazz at a news conference Thursday.
