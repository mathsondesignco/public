### [2011-02-6](/news/2011/02/6/index.md)

# 2011 Egyptian protests:People demonstrate against the Mubarak regime for the thirteenth consecutive day, dubbed the "Day of Martyrs" in memory of those killed. 

Videos uploaded on YouTube appear to show graphic scenes of recent violence in Cairo and Alexandria.
