### [2011-08-22](/news/2011/08/22/index.md)

# Jerry Leiber, the American lyricist of the Leiber and Stoller duo that wrote many of the most popular songs in the early years of rock and roll, dies at the age of 78 in Los Angeles. 

With his partner, Mike Stoller, Mr. Leiber wrote a string of hits including &#8220;Hound Dog&#8221; and &#8220;Stand By Me.&#8221;
