# 2011 Atlantic hurricane season:: Tropical Storm Harvey is expected to cross the coast of Belize with a tropical storm watch in place for Guatemala and parts of Honduras. 

A tropical storm formed in the Caribbean Friday and is expected to move across the coast of Belize Saturday afternoon or night.
