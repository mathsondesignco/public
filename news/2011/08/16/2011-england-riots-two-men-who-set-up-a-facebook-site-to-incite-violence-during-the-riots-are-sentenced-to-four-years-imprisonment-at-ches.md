### [2011-08-16](/news/2011/08/16/index.md)

# 2011 England riots:. Two men who set up a Facebook site to incite violence during the riots are sentenced to four years imprisonment at Chester Crown Court. 

Two men are jailed for four years each for using Facebook to incite disorder during riots in England last week.
