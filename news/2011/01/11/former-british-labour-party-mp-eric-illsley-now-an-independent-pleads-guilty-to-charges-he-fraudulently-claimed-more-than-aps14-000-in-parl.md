# Former British Labour Party MP Eric Illsley, now an Independent, pleads guilty to charges he fraudulently claimed more than PS14,000 in parliamentary expenses. 

MP Eric Illsley is facing calls to quit after pleading guilty to fraudulently claiming thousands of pounds in parliamentary expenses.
