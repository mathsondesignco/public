# Taoiseach Brian Cowen tells his government he is to take on the foreign affairs portfolio after his challenger Micheal Martin's resignation yesterday. President Mary McAleese accepts Martin's resignation. 

Irish prime minister Brian Cowen has told the government he will be taking on the Foreign Affairs portfolio.
