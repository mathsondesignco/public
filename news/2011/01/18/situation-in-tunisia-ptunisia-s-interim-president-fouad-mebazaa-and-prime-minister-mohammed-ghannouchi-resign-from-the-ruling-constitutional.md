### [2011-01-18](/news/2011/01/18/index.md)

# Situation in Tunisia:Tunisia's interim President Fouad Mebazaa and Prime Minister Mohammed Ghannouchi resign from the ruling Constitutional Democratic Rally party amid a row over the make-up of the new government. 

The French foreign minister defends her handling of the Tunisian crisis, saying France was as surprised as anyone else.
