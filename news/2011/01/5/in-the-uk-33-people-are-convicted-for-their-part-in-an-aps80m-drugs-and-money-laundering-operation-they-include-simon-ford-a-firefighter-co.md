# In the UK 33 people are convicted for their part in an PS80m drugs and money laundering operation. They include Simon Ford, a firefighter commended for his actions during the 7 July London bombings. 

A firefighter commended for his work in the 7 July London bombings is one of 33 people convicted for drugs and money-laundering crimes.
