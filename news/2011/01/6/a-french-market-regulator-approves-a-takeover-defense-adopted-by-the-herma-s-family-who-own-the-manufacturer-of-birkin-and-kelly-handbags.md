### [2011-01-6](/news/2011/01/6/index.md)

# A French market regulator approves a takeover defense adopted by the Hermes family, who own the manufacturer of Birkin and Kelly handbags. This improves the odds that Hermes will preserve its independence in the face of a bid by LVMH.
