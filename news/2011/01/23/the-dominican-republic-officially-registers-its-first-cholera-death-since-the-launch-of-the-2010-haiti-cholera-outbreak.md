### [2011-01-23](/news/2011/01/23/index.md)

# The Dominican Republic officially registers its first cholera death since the launch of the 2010 Haiti cholera outbreak. 

The Dominican Republic confirms a Haitian man is the first to die of cholera in the country since an epidemic broke out in neighbouring Haiti.
