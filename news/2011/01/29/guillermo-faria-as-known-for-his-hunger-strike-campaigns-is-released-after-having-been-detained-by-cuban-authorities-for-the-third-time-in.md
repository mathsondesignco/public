# Guillermo Farinas, known for his hunger strike campaigns, is released after having been detained by Cuban authorities for the third time in 48 hours. 

Cuban dissident Guillermo Farinas is released after being arrested three times in 48 hours, as he defied official warnings to keep a low profile.
