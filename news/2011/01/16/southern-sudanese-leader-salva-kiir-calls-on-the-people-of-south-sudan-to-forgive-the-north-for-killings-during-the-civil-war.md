### [2011-01-16](/news/2011/01/16/index.md)

# Southern Sudanese leader Salva Kiir calls on the people of South Sudan to forgive the north for killings during the civil war. 

The South Sudanese leader calls on his people to forgive the north for killings during more than 20 years of civil war.
