### [2011-01-2](/news/2011/01/2/index.md)

# The Canada Border Services Agency confirms the deportation of a Toronto student to Mexico, who immigrated with his sister, who cited homophobia as the reason for claiming refugee status. Reports state he boarded the plane unwillingly. 

Canada Border Services Agency has confirmed an 18-year-old Toronto student has been deported to Mexico despite fears from his supporters he could be a target for violence.
