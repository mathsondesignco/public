### [2011-10-17](/news/2011/10/17/index.md)

# Apple Inc. releases its sales report for the iPhone 4S smartphone, which has reportedly sold over 4 million units worldwide in under four days, breaking company sales records. 

Apple today said that it had sold more than 4 million iPhone 4S smartphones since the device reached retail last Friday.
