### [2011-10-17](/news/2011/10/17/index.md)

# Egypt's ruling Supreme Council of the Armed Forces announces a law banning all forms of discrimination, in the wake of recent violence between Coptic protesters and security forces. 

Measure comes a week after 26 people were killed in clashes involving minority Coptic Christian protesters and military.
