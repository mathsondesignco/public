### [2011-10-15](/news/2011/10/15/index.md)

# The United States Department of Commerce reports that U.S. retail sales rose at their fastest rate for seven months in September, having risen by 1.1% over the previous month. 

US retail sales rose by 1.1% in September, with vehicle sales showing the biggest gain, but grocery store sales fall.
