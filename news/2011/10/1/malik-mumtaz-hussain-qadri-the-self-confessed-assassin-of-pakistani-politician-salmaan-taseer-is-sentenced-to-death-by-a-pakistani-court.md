### [2011-10-1](/news/2011/10/1/index.md)

# Malik Mumtaz Hussain Qadri, the self-confessed assassin of Pakistani politician Salmaan Taseer, is sentenced to death by a Pakistani court. 

Mumtaz Qadri&#039;s supporters took to the streets to denounce the sentence soon after it was handed down.
