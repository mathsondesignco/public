# English auto racing driver Dan Wheldon dies in a crash in the final race of the 2011 IndyCar Series at the Las Vegas Motor Speedway, with three other drivers reportedly hospitalised in the crash. 

IndyCar champion Dan Wheldon has died after a dramatic 15-car pile-up in Las Vegas, in which cars flew through the air before bursting into flames.
