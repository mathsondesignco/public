### [2011-10-20](/news/2011/10/20/index.md)

# Britain's three main political parties order their MPs to vote against a Parliamentary motion calling for a referendum on the UK's membership of the European Union, due to be debated on Monday. 

Members of the three largest UK parties are being told to vote against a motion calling for a referendum to be held on UK membership of the European Union.
