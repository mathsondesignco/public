### [2011-10-24](/news/2011/10/24/index.md)

# Kenyan-Somalian military operation:Kenyan and Somali forces prepare to take the Somali town of Afmadow, defended by Islamist al-Shabaab militiamen. 

Twin attacks in central Nairobi come two days after the US issued a terrorism alert in the Kenyan capital.
