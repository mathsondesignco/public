### [2011-03-22](/news/2011/03/22/index.md)

# John-Paul Langbroek stands down as the Leader of the Opposition in the Australian state of Queensland with the sitting Lord Mayor of Brisbane Campbell Newman favoured to take his place. 

Queensland Opposition Leader John-Paul Langbroek has resigned, less than two hours after high-profile Brisbane Lord Mayor Campbell Newman confirmed that he was after his job.
