# Tokyo Electric Power Co. resumes its policy of rolling electricity blackouts following ongoing problems caused by the 2011 Tohoku earthquake and tsunami. 

Tokyo Electric Power will have to compensate farmers for losses caused by the nuclear radiation leaks.
