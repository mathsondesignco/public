### [2011-03-24](/news/2011/03/24/index.md)

# Japanese earthquake, tsunami and nuclear incidentsSeveral governments in the Asia-Pacific region including Australia, Hong Kong and Singapore ban the import of food from areas near the Fukushima I Nuclear Power Plant following the recent incidents. 

Singapore reports finding low levels of radioactivity in vegetables from Japan as more countries ban food imports.
