### [2011-03-12](/news/2011/03/12/index.md)

# A Palestinian militant armed with a knife infiltrates the West Bank settlement of Itamar and stabs to death two Israeli parents and three of their children. 

The attack in Itamar, a religious settlement in the northern West Bank, was the first against settlers in months.
