### [2011-03-4](/news/2011/03/4/index.md)

# Real Madrid soccer coach Jos Mourinho evades being stabbed to death after a man wielding a knife attacks a member of the club's security staff at an airport in A Corua, Galicia. 

A man armed with a dagger is reported to have tried to stab Real Madrid manager Jose Mourinho as he signed autographs in a Spanish airport.
