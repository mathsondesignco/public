### [2011-03-8](/news/2011/03/8/index.md)

# A St Louis police officer and two U.S. Marshals are shot, one fatally, while serving an arrest warrant in south St. Louis, Missouri.  The gunman is killed.

Suspect, who was shot and killed, was wanted in assault on officer.
