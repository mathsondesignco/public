### [2011-03-23](/news/2011/03/23/index.md)

# A U.S. federal court judge dismisses part of a lawsuit brought by Lions Gate Entertainment against its shareholder, Carl Icahn, holding that Icahn had met certain statutory disclosure requirements. 

A Manhattan federal judge dismissed part of a lawsuit by Lions Gate Entertainment Corp LGF.N against Carl Icahn that accused the activist investor of plotting to merge the studio with Metro-Goldwyn-Mayer.
