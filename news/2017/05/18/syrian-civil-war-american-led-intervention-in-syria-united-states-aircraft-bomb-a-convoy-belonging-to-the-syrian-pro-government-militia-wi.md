# Syrian Civil War. American-led intervention in Syria. United States aircraft bomb a convoy belonging to the Syrian pro-government militia within a so-called "deconfliction zone" around Al-Tanf. 

CBS News correspondent David Martin reports Syrian regime vehicles have violated the deconfliction zone twice within the past few days
