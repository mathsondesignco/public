### [2017-05-23](/news/2017/05/23/index.md)

# Newark Liberty International Airport in northern New Jersey was closed for almost two hours this evening  after flames were spotted coming from one of the  United Airlines Boeing 757 jet engines. All 124 passengers and seven crew members safely evacuated the plane. 

Newark Liberty International Airport in New Jersey reopened late on Tuesday after being shut down when 131 people were evacuated from a United Airlines flight over flames seen emerging from an aircraft engine.
