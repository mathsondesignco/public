### [2017-05-21](/news/2017/05/21/index.md)

# Mountaineers led by Tim Mosedale confirm that Mount Everest's famous Hillary Step has completely collapsed. It has been  suspected that Nepal's April 2015 earthquake had damaged the feature but due to heavy snow it could not be confirmed.
