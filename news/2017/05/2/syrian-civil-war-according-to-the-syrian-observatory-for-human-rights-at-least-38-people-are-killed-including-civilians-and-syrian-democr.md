### [2017-05-2](/news/2017/05/2/index.md)

# Syrian Civil War. According to the Syrian Observatory for Human Rights, at least 38 people are killed, including civilians and Syrian Democratic Forces fighters, after ISIL militants attack a camp for displaced Syrian and Iraqi refugees near Al-Shaddadah, Al-Hasakah Governorate. 

Many civilians are among more than 30 people killed in the incident near the Iraqi border.
