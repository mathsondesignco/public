### [2017-05-12](/news/2017/05/12/index.md)

# A large scale international cyber-attack using the WannaCry ransomware package disrupts computer and telephone systems in thousands of cases across 99 countries. Affected organisations include: England's NHS, with doctors reporting messages demanding money; MegaFon and Sberbank in Russia; Telefonica, Iberdrola, and Gas Natural in Spain; and FedEx. 

Patient data at many GP surgeries and hospitals has been encrypted by ransomware and cannot be accessed by staff.
