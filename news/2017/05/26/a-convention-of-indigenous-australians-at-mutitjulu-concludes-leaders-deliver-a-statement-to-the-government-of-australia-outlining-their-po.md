### [2017-05-26](/news/2017/05/26/index.md)

# A convention of Indigenous Australians at Mutitjulu concludes. Leaders deliver a statement to the Government of Australia outlining their position on amendments to the Constitution of Australia, including constitutional recognition. 

In the shadow of Uluru, Indigenous people will present a historic position on changes to the constitution today.
