# American comedian and ''Late Show'' host Stephen Colbert is to be investigated by the Federal Communications Commission over a sexually explicit joke about U.S. president Donald Trump's relationship with Russian president Vladimir Putin. 

TV network CBS faces a fine if the joke, perceived by some to be homophobic, is deemed &quot;indecent&quot;.
