### [2017-05-15](/news/2017/05/15/index.md)

# According to a current and a former unnamed U.S. government official, President Donald Trump revealed classified information that jeopardized a critical source of intelligence on the Islamic State during his Oval Office meeting with the Russian Foreign Minister and Ambassador on May 10. 

The president was boasting of the &ldquo;great intel&rdquo; he receives when he discussed intelligence provided by a U.S. partner.
