# 1,200 Canadian Forces personnel are deployed in Quebec to help with rising floodwaters. 126 communities are affected and more than 1,000 residents have been evacuated from their homes. 

Thousands of Canadians are spending the weekend in a desperate struggle with rising floodwaters caused by unusually persistent rainfall.
