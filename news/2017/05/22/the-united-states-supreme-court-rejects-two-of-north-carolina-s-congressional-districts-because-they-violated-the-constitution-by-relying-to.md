### [2017-05-22](/news/2017/05/22/index.md)

# The United States Supreme Court rejects two of North Carolina's congressional districts because they violated the Constitution by relying too heavily on race in drawing them. 

Redistricting is part of a bare-knuckled fight in North Carolina, where Democrats and Republicans uneasily share political power.
