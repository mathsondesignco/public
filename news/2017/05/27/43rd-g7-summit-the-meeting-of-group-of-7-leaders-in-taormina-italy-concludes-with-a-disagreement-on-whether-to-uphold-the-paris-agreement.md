### [2017-05-27](/news/2017/05/27/index.md)

# 43rd G7 summit. The meeting of Group of 7 leaders in Taormina, Italy, concludes with a disagreement on whether to uphold the Paris Agreement; U.S. President Donald Trump is the only leader not to confirm commitment to the agreement, due to his climate change denial. 

Germany called the climate change talks &quot;very unsatisfactory&quot;, describing them as &quot;six against one&quot;.
