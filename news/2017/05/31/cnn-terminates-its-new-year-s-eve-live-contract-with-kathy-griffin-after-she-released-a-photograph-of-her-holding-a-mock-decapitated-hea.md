# CNN terminates its ''New Year's Eve Live'' contract with Kathy Griffin after she released a photograph of her holding a mock decapitated head of President Donald Trump. 

After her severed-Trump-head photo, Kathy Griffin will no longer appear with Anderson Cooper on CNN&#39;s New Year&#39;s Eve special, the network said Wednesday.
