### [2017-05-14](/news/2017/05/14/index.md)

# North Korea and weapons of mass destruction. 2017 North Korean missile tests. U.S. and South Korean officials confirm that North Korea launched a test missile from the west coast city of Kusong. The projectile travelled to the east for 30 minutes and 435|mi|km reaching an altitude of more than 1245|mi|km before landing in the Sea of Japan, 60|mi|km south of the Russian city of Vladivostok. 

Japan says it reached an altitude of 2,000km which, if confirmed, could show a technological advance.
