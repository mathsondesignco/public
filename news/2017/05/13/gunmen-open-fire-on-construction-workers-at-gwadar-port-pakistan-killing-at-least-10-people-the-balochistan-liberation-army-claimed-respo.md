### [2017-05-13](/news/2017/05/13/index.md)

# Gunmen open fire on construction workers at Gwadar Port, Pakistan, killing at least 10 people. The Balochistan Liberation Army claimed responsibility for the attack. 

Attack was claimed by the Baloch Liberation Army, which has been fighting for an independent homeland for the Baloch.
