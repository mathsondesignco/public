### [2017-05-4](/news/2017/05/4/index.md)

# The United States House of Representatives votes in favor of the repeal of the Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act and the passage of the American Health Care Act into a vote in the United States Senate, with a narrow vote of 217 to 213 with Republicans voting in favor and Democrats against. 

House Republicans plan to vote Thursday on the latest version of the American Health Care Act – their answer to ObamaCare. Here’s what’s the bill does.
