### [2017-07-21](/news/2017/07/21/index.md)

# White House Press Secretary Sean Spicer announced his intention to resign as White House Press Secretary becauseDonald Trump appoints Anthony Scaramucci as the White House Communications Director,and will formally step down from the role in August 2017. Sarah Huckabee Sanders will take over as the White House Press Secretary in August 2017. 

Mr. Spicer quit after telling President Trump he vehemently disagreed with the appointment of the financier Anthony Scaramucci as communications director.
