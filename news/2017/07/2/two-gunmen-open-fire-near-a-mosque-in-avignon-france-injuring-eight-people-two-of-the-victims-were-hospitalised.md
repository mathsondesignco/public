### [2017-07-2](/news/2017/07/2/index.md)

# Two gunmen open fire near a mosque in Avignon, France, injuring eight people. Two of the victims were hospitalised. 

Muslim worshippers were leaving the mosque around 10:30 p.m. when the men reportedly opened fire.
