### [2017-07-9](/news/2017/07/9/index.md)

# UNESCO declares eight new sites as world heritage sites; including Lake District in North West England and Iran's Historic City of Yazd. 

It joins the likes of the Grand Canyon and Machu Picchu as it gets heritage status.
