### [2017-07-26](/news/2017/07/26/index.md)

# The United States Senate rejects "repeal only" propositions of the Affordable Care Act. United States President Donald Trump lashes out at Republican Senator Lisa Murkowski, who voted against the proposal. 

The legislative showdown will continue Wednesday as the Senate considers various measures to replace the health law.
