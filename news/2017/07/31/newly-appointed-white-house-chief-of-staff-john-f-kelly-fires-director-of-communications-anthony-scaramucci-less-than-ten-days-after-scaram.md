### [2017-07-31](/news/2017/07/31/index.md)

# Newly-appointed White House Chief of Staff John F. Kelly fires Director of Communications Anthony Scaramucci less than ten days after Scaramucci took up his post. 

The decision to remove Mr. Scaramucci came at request of the new chief of staff, John Kelly, people close to the decision said.
