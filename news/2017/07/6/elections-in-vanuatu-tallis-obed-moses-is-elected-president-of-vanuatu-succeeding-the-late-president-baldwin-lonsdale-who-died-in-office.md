# Elections in Vanuatu. Tallis Obed Moses is elected President of Vanuatu, succeeding the late President Baldwin Lonsdale, who died in office in June. 

There was applause and a sense of relief in Vanuatu&#39;s parliament today as Tallis Obed Moses was voted in as Vanuatu&#39;s new President.
