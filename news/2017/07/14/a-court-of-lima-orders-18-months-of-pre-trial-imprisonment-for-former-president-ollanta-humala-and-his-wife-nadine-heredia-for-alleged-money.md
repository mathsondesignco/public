### [2017-07-14](/news/2017/07/14/index.md)

# A court of Lima orders 18 months of pre-trial imprisonment for former president Ollanta Humala and his wife Nadine Heredia for alleged money laundering. Both have turned themselves in. 

The former leader and his wife hand themselves in while corruption charges are prepared.
