# The test series between New Zealand and the British and Irish Lions concludes in a 1-1 draw after the third test match in Eden Park finishes 15-15. 

The British &amp; Irish Lions and the All Blacks have made rugby history with a tied Test series in New Zealand thanks to a hard-fought 15-15 draw at Eden Park in Auckland
