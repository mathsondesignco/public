### [2017-06-8](/news/2017/06/8/index.md)

# United Nations Ocean Conference: At a United Nations-Oceans summit, delegates from China, Indonesia, the Philippines and Thailand, four of the top polluters of plastic into the oceans, pledge to reduce their levels of oceanic plastic pollution. 

China, Thailand, Indonesia and the Philippines say they will act to stop plastic polluting the oceans.
