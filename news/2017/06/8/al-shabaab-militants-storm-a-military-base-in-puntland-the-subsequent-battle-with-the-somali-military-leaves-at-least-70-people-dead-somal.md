### [2017-06-8](/news/2017/06/8/index.md)

# Al-Shabaab militants storm a military base in Puntland. The subsequent battle with the Somali military leaves at least 70 people dead. Somali officials called the attack the deadliest in the country in years. 

Gun battle in military base in Somalia&#39;s semi-autonomous Puntland region kills at least 70 soldiers.
