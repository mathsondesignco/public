# War in Afghanistan. June 2017 Kabul mosque attack. A terrorist attack, involving a blast and gunfire, occurs at a Shia mosque in Kabul, Afghanistan, killing several people. 

An attack hits a mosque in an area of the Afghan capital Kabul dominated by minority Shia Muslims.
