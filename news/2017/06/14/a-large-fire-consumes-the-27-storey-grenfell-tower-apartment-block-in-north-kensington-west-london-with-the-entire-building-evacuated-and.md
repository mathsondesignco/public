### [2017-06-14](/news/2017/06/14/index.md)

# A large fire consumes the 27-storey Grenfell Tower apartment block in North Kensington, West London, with the entire building evacuated and over 40 fire engines and 200 firefighters battling the blaze. A number of fatalities are reported and more people are treated for injuries, with more people possibly being trapped inside. 

Eyewitnesses say people are trapped and there are fears the residential building could collapse.
