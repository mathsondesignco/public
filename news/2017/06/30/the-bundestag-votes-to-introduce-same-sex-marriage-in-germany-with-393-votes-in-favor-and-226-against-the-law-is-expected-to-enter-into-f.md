### [2017-06-30](/news/2017/06/30/index.md)

# The Bundestag votes to introduce same-sex marriage in Germany.  With 393 votes in favor and 226 against, the law is expected to enter into force before the end of the year. 

MPs pass bill granting gay and lesbian couples full rights, including on adoption
