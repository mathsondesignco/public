### [2017-06-16](/news/2017/06/16/index.md)

# The U.S. Office of Government Ethics certifies a financial report form submitted by President Donald Trump where he declares over $315.6 million in liabilities and exceeding $1.4 billion in assets as of mid-2017, for revenues of more than $594 million over 2016 and early 2017. 

President Donald Trump had personal liabilities of at least $315.6 million to German, U.S. and other lenders as of mid-2017, according to a federal financial disclosure form released late on Friday by the U.S. Office of Government Ethics.
