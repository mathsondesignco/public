### [2017-06-16](/news/2017/06/16/index.md)

# Whole Foods Market: Amazon agrees to buy Whole Foods Market for $13.7 billion. The takeover deal is expected to be completed in the second half of the year. 

The deal marks the biggest push yet by Amazon into traditional bricks and mortar retailing.
