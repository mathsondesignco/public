### [2017-06-2](/news/2017/06/2/index.md)

# At least 36 people are killed in a deliberately lit fire at Resorts World Manila in the Philippines. The perpetrator also fired gun shots before committing suicide. ISIL has claimed responsibility the attack; however, authorities contend it is not terror related.
