# Notorious arms dealer Jacques Monsieur is sentenced to three years of imprisonment and a fine of 300,000 euros. 

One of the most notorious arms dealers in Belgian history, Jacques Monsieur, has received three years&#039; imprisonment, a heavier punishment than the public prosecutor had demanded. He also has to cough up a fine of 300,000 euros.
