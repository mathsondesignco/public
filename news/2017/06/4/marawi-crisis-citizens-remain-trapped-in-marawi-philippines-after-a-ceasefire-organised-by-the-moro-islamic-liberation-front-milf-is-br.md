### [2017-06-4](/news/2017/06/4/index.md)

# Marawi crisis: Citizens remain trapped in Marawi, Philippines, after a ceasefire organised by the Moro Islamic Liberation Front (MILF) is broken. Up to 2,000 people remain in need of help after nearly two weeks without food. 

Fighting breaks despite a four-hour ceasefire, preventing hundreds of residents from leaving besieged city of Marawi.
