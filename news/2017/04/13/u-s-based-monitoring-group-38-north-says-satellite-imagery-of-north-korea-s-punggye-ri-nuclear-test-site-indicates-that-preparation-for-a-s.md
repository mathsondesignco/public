### [2017-04-13](/news/2017/04/13/index.md)

# U.S.-based monitoring group 38 North says satellite imagery of North Korea's Punggye-ri Nuclear Test Site indicates that preparation for a sixth nuclear weapons test is underway. 

North’s Punggye-ri site appears to be ready for what would be the country’s sixth nuclear test since 2006
