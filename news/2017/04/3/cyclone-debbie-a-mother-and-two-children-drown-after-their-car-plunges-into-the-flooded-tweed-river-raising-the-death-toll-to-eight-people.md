# Cyclone Debbie. A mother and two children drown after their car plunges into the flooded Tweed River, raising the death toll to eight people. A ten-year-old girl escapes and alerts authorities. 

A MOTHER and her young son and daughter are believed to have drowned after their car plunged into a flood swollen river in northern NSW this afternoon.
