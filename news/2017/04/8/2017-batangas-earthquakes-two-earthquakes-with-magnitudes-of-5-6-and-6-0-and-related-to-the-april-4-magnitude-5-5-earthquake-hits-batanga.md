# 2017 Batangas earthquakes. Two earthquakes, with magnitudes of 5.6 and 6.0 and related to the April 4 magnitude 5.5 earthquake, hits Batangas, and are felt as far as Metro Manila 

(7th UPDATE) Batangas is the epicenter of both earthquakes, which measured magnitude 5.6 and 6.0, respectively
