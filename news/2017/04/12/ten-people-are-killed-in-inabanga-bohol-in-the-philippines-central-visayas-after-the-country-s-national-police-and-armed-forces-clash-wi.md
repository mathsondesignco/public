### [2017-04-12](/news/2017/04/12/index.md)

# Ten people are killed in Inabanga, Bohol, in the Philippines' Central Visayas, after the country's National Police and Armed Forces clash with suspected members of Abu Sayyaf. 

PNP Chief Ronald Dela Rosa says, if confirmed the armed men are members of the terror group, their goal is to carry out kidnappings in Bohol and Cebu.
