### [2017-04-15](/news/2017/04/15/index.md)

# 2017 Nangarhar airstrike: The death toll according to a Nangarhar official source now stands at 94 militants, including 4 commanders. Former president Hamid Karzai says the strike was in violation of national sovereignty. 

Nangarhar police said that the bombing, a Massive Ordnance Air Blast (MOAB), destroyed Daesh hideouts, tunnels and weapon caches.
