# The Alabama Ethics Commission finds there is probable cause that Governor Robert Bentley violated the state's ethics and campaign finance laws, and refers the case to Montgomery County District Attorney Daryl Bailey. 

Commission found &quot;probable cause&quot; Gov. Robert Bentley violated campaign finance laws.
