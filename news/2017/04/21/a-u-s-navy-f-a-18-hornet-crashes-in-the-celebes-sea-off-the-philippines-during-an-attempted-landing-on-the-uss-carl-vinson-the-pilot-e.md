# A U.S. Navy F/A-18 Hornet crashes in the Celebes Sea off the Philippines during an attempted landing on the USS ''Carl Vinson''. The pilot ejected and was recovered safely. 

A Navy F/A-18E pilot has been safely recovered after ejecting from his fighter jet on final approach to the carrier Carl Vinson.
