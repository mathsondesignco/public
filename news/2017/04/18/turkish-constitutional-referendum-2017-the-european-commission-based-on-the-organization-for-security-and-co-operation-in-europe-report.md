# Turkish constitutional referendum, 2017. The European Commission, based on the Organization for Security and Co-operation in Europe report, calls on Turkey to launch an investigation into allegations of referendum voting irregularities. 

The EU called on Turkey to launch an investigation into allegations of irregularities following a report stating its referendum was not genuinely democratic.
