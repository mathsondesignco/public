### [2017-04-19](/news/2017/04/19/index.md)

# German federal election, 2017. Alternative for Germany chairwoman Frauke Petry announces that she will not lead the party in the September federal election, citing other party leaders' insistence on remaining a "fundamental opposition party", unwilling to join a coalition.
