# 2017 Atlantic hurricane season: Hurricane and storm surge watches are issued for Texas and Louisiana as the tropical system Harvey strengthens into a tropical depression and is predicted to bring both heavy rainfall and storm surge. 

The Gulf Coast landfall will only be part of the danger.
