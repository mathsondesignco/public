### [2017-08-22](/news/2017/08/22/index.md)

# Viacom International Media Networks has announced it will no longer pursue a Nickelodeon underwater theme park and resort in Palawan, Philippines. The project, announced last January, attracted controversy due to criticisms from environmentalist groups who fear the resort might destroy the area's marine ecosystem, home to two UNESCO World Heritage sites. 

Viacom International Media Networks (VIMN) will no longer pursue the controversial Nickelodeon theme park in Palawan.
