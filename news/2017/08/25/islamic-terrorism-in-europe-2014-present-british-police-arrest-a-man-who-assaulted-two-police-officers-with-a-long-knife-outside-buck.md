# Islamic terrorism in Europe (2014-present): British police arrest a man who assaulted two police officers with a "long knife" outside Buckingham Palace. The officers suffered minor injuries. No members of the royal family were present in the palace at the time. 

A man with a &quot;large knife&quot; who assaulted two police officers outside Buckingham Palace on Friday night has been arrested, British police said.
