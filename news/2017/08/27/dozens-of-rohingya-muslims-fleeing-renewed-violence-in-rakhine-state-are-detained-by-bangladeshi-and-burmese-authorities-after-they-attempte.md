### [2017-08-27](/news/2017/08/27/index.md)

# Dozens of Rohingya Muslims fleeing renewed violence in Rakhine State are detained by Bangladeshi and Burmese authorities after they attempted to cross the border from Myanmar to Bangladesh. Many of those detained were en route to Kutupalong Refugee Camp, a refugee camp in Ukhia, Bangladesh, mostly inhabited by Rohingya refugees.
