### [2017-08-27](/news/2017/08/27/index.md)

# A dry cargo vessel broke in two in the Black Sea off Turkey-s northwest coast, television images showed early on Sunday, while the 11-man crew was rescued. Leonardo, a 114-meter-long dry cargo ship flagged from Mongolia, started buckling and broke in two while on anchor off Istanbul-s Kilyos coast.
