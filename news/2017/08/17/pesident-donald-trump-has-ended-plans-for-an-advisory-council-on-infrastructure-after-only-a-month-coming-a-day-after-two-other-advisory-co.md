# Pesident Donald Trump has ended plans for an Advisory Council on Infrastructure after only a month, coming a day after two other advisory councils were scrapped. No reason was given. 

President Donald Trump will not move forward with a planned Advisory Council on Infrastructure, a person familiar with the matter said Thursday.
