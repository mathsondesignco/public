### [2017-08-1](/news/2017/08/1/index.md)

#  Jacinda Ardern is elected the new leader of the Labour Party, after Andrew Little resigns on the back of poor opinion poll performance ahead of the 23 September election. 

New Labour leader Jacinda Ardern says the party is about to &quot;run the campaign of our lives&quot;.
