### [2017-08-30](/news/2017/08/30/index.md)

# A shooting at a motel on Auburn Boulevard, Sacramento County, California, leaves a Sacramento County Sheriff's Department deputy dead and two California Highway Patrol officers injured. The suspect was later arrested. 

Sheriff’s deputy Robert French was killed in the 2600 block of Auburn Boulevard, the Sacramento County Sheriff’s Department reports.
