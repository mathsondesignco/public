### [2017-08-11](/news/2017/08/11/index.md)

# The Kenyan electoral commission announces that President Uhuru Kenyatta has re-election. Kenyatta leads ODM candidate Raila Odinga by more than 1.4 million votes. The opposition National Super Alliance rejects this finding as fraudulent.
