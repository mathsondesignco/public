### [2017-08-31](/news/2017/08/31/index.md)

# The United States State Department orders Russia to close the Consulate-General of Russia in San Francisco and two other buildings by September 2, 2017, invoking "parity" over a Russian measure in July. 

The US orders Russia to close its San Francisco consulate in response to &quot;unwarranted&quot; actions.
