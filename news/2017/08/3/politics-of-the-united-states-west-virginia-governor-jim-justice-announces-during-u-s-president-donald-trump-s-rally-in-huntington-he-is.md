### [2017-08-3](/news/2017/08/3/index.md)

# Politics of the United States. West Virginia Governor Jim Justice announces, during U.S. President Donald Trump's rally in Huntington, he is changing his party affiliation from Democratic to Republican. 

Trump won all 55 counties in the state, including 43 counties where he received more than 70 percent of votes.
