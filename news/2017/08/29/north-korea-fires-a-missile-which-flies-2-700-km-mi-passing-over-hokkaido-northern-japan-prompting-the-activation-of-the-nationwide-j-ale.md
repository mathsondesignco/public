### [2017-08-29](/news/2017/08/29/index.md)

# North Korea fires a missile which flies 2,700|km|mi, passing over Hokkaido, northern Japan, prompting the activation of the nationwide J-Alert warning system. This is first time since 1998 that a North Korean missile has passed over Japanese territory. 

North Korea has test fired a missile over Japan in an unprecedented act that is likely to further enflame tensions in the region, Japan&#039;s government has confirmed.
