### [2017-08-2](/news/2017/08/2/index.md)

# RAISE ActUnited States President Donald Trump, together with Senators Tom Cotton and David Perdue, unveils a plan that will improve the immigration process by reducing family-based chain migration and replacing it with a merit-based (employment and skills) system. 

The bill is expected to face fierce resistance from congressional Democrats and immigrant rights groups.
