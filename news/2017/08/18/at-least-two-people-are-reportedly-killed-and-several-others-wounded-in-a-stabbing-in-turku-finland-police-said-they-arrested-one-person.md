# At least two people are reportedly killed, and several others wounded in a stabbing in Turku, Finland. Police said they arrested one person after a shootout and had launched a manhunt for other possible attackers. 

Several people were stabbed in the Finnish city of Turku, police said on Friday, adding they had shot at and arrested one person.
