### [2017-08-4](/news/2017/08/4/index.md)

# Rwandan presidential election, 2017. Voters in Rwanda go to the polls to elect their president. Paul Kagame, incumbent since 2000, referred to the election as a formality, despite challenges from the Democratic Greens' Frank Habineza and independent Philippe Mpayimana. Kagame is re-elected with 98.66 of the vote. 

Rwandan President Paul Kagame has extended his 17 years in power after winning the presidential election.
