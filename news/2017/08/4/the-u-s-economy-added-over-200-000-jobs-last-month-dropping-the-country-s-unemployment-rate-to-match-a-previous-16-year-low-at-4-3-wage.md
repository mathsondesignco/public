### [2017-08-4](/news/2017/08/4/index.md)

# The U.S. economy added over 200,000 jobs last month, dropping the country's unemployment rate to match a previous 16-year low at 4.3%. Wage growth remained below the Federal Reserve Board's target of 3.5%. 

The U.S. economy added a strong 209,000 jobs in July, and the unemployment rate matched a 16-year low.
