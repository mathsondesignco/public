### [2017-12-13](/news/2017/12/13/index.md)

# Belgium's biggest government party says that they will not accept the energy transition agreement that was reached on December 11 by Belgium's four ministers of energy, citing mainly financial arguments to currently refuse the closure of the nuclear power stations. The agreement is inspired by Germany's Energiewende.
