# Mount Hope in a disputed region of Antarctica claimed by the United Kingdom is found to be the highest mountain in British territory. Ben Nevis in Scotland is the highest mountain in Great Britain or Northern Ireland. 

Scientists re-measure the tallest mountains in the Antarctic territory claimed by Britain.
