# During a visit to Syria, his first, President Vladimir Putin announced that Russian forces will withdraw from Syria now that ISIS has been defeated in the country. 

Russian President Vladimir Putin ordered the withdrawal of Russian troops from Syria during a surprise visit to Khmeimim Airbase.
