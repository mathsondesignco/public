### [2017-12-26](/news/2017/12/26/index.md)

# According to aid sources and almasirah television, Saudi Arabian coalition air strikes kill 20 people and wound 60 at al-Shahra souq, a popular market in Taiz Governorate. 

At least 20 people killed and dozens more wounded in a series of Saudi-led raids on a market in Taiz province.
