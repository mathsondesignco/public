### [2017-12-14](/news/2017/12/14/index.md)

# Iran dismisses the United States' allegation of having supplied the missile fired last month on Saudi Arabia from Yemen as an "unfounded" claim based on purported evidence. The United States declare that the displayed missile parts are undeniable, "irrefutable" evidence of Iran having violated its international obligations.
