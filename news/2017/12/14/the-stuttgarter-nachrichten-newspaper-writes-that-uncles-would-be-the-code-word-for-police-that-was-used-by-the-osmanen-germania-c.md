### [2017-12-14](/news/2017/12/14/index.md)

# The Stuttgarter Nachrichten newspaper writes that """'uncles""'" would be the code word for police that was used by the "Osmanen Germania" criminal group, the pro-Erdogan "European Turkish Democrats" lobby and the AKP party mentioned in the 2016 organized harassment of German satirist Jan Bohmermann, the so-called Erdogate--according to research by that same newspaper and ZDF magazine "Frontal 21".
