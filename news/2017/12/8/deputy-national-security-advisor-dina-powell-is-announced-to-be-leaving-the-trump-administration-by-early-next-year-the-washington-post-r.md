### [2017-12-8](/news/2017/12/8/index.md)

# Deputy National Security Advisor Dina Powell is announced to be leaving the Trump administration by early next year. The "Washington Post" reports she is "leaving on good terms". 

Deputy national security adviser Dina Powell will step down from her position early next year, the White House said Friday.
