### [2017-12-8](/news/2017/12/8/index.md)

# Rebels in North Kivu kill at least 14 United Nations peacekeepers and wound 53 others, most of them Tanzanian and Congolese. An unknown number of the rebels are killed. Nearly 300 peacekeepers have been killed since the mission started in 1999. 

Peacekeeping mission in DRC is largest, most expensive in world; aimed at calming armed groups in vast, mineral-rich nation
