### [2017-12-18](/news/2017/12/18/index.md)

# At least three people are killed and many others injured when an Amtrak passenger train derails on an Interstate 5 overpass in Pierce County, Washington. This train is part of a new high-speed "Cascades" service from Tacoma that launched this morning. 

First responders are treating the derailment in Washington state as a mass casualty incident
