### [2017-12-5](/news/2017/12/5/index.md)

#  As police arrive at the stateless Mikheil Saakashvili's house in Kiev to detain him, Saakashvili goes up on the roof to protest verbally. Police then detain him and try to take him away in a blue minivan while hundreds of people block the street. Finally the supporters free him out of the van. Speeching again, he urges Ukrainians to "be afraid of nothing". 

Crowds of supporters demonstrate as anti-corruption campaigner is taken away by police from his home in Kiev
