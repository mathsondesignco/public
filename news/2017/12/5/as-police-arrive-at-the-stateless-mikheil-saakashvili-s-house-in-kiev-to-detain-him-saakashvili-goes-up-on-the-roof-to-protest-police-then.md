# As police arrive at the stateless Mikheil Saakashvili's house in Kiev to detain him, Saakashvili goes up on the roof to protest. Police then detain him and try to take him away in a blue minivan while hundreds of people block the street. Prosecutor General of Ukraine Yuriy Lutsenko claims in a briefing that Saakashvili received $500,000 from the fugitive Serhiy Kurchenko for his activities in Ukraine. 

Crowds of supporters demonstrate as anti-corruption campaigner is taken away by police from his home in Kiev
