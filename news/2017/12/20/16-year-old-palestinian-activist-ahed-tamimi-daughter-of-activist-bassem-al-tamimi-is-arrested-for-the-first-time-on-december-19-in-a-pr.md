### [2017-12-20](/news/2017/12/20/index.md)

# 16-year old Palestinian activist Ahed Tamimi, daughter of activist Bassem al-Tamimi, is arrested (for the first time) on December 19 in a pre-dawn raid on her home in Nabi Salih by the Israeli army. Later in the day, her mother Nariman is allegedly arrested too when visiting her daughter at a police station. 

Detention of mother follows arrest of Ahed Tamimi, 16, by Israeli soldiers in West Bank village in continuing crackdown.
