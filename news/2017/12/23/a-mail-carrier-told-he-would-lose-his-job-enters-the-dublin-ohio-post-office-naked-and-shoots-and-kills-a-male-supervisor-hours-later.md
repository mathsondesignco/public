# A mail carrier, told he would lose his job, enters the Dublin, Ohio, post office, naked, and shoots and kills a male supervisor. Hours later, still unclothed, the suspect drives to the Columbus apartment of a female supervisor, who dies instantly from blunt-force trauma to her head. The 24-year-old man is arrested.
