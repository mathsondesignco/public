### [2017-12-6](/news/2017/12/6/index.md)

# An Institute for Science and International Security study indicates that--between March 2014 and September 2017 and to varying degrees--a total of 49 countries violated United Nations sanctions against North Korea. 

A new report names 49 countries that have violated international sanctions against North Korea in the last three years.
