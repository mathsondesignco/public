# A blast occurs in a supermarket in the Russian city of Saint Petersburg. At least 10 people are injured, while police say they are treating the blast as attempted murder. 

A blast ripped through a supermarket in the Russian city of St Petersburg on Wednesday evening, injuring several shoppers, Russian news agencies reported.
