# Under the title "NATO Expansion: What Gorbachev Heard", the National Security Archive at George Washington University releases documents from five countries relating to assurances the Soviet Union was given regarding NATO expansion, including James Baker's legendary dictum "not one inch eastward" to Mikhail Gorbachev.
