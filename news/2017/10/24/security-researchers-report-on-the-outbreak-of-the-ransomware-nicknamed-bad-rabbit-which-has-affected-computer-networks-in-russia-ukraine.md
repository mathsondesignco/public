### [2017-10-24](/news/2017/10/24/index.md)

# Security researchers report on the outbreak of the ransomware nicknamed Bad Rabbit, which has affected computer networks in Russia, Ukraine, and has been detected throughout the world. 

Bad Rabbit, a new Petya-style ransomware attack, has been spreading quickly among organizations located in Russia and Ukraine.
