# The head of the Financial Conduct Authority (FCA) admits to meeting oil giant Saudi Aramco over a potential GBP1.5 trillion listing on the London Stock Exchange. 

The regulator was criticised for proposing changes to UK listing rules to accommodate the oil giant.
