### [2017-10-11](/news/2017/10/11/index.md)

# "Nature" publishes research from The Institute of Astrophysics of Andalusia at the Glorieta de la Astronomia of Granada, Spain, describing the discovery that a ring system orbits the trans-Neptunian dwarf planet Haumea. 

It’s the only dwarf planet to have a ring — as far as we know
