# Apollo-group-type asteroid 2012 TC4 (approximately 15 meters in diameter) passes 0.0003352 AU (50,150 km; 31,160 mi) from Earth, well within the Moon's orbit. 

Asteroid 2012 TC4 is zipping past Earth on Thursday, allowing scientists and emergency preparedness teams to practise for an impact.
