### [2017-10-7](/news/2017/10/7/index.md)

# A shooting at the Al-Salam Royal Palace in Jeddah, Saudi Arabia, kills two guards and injures three others. Security guards killed the Saudi-national assailant. 

Two royal palace security guards are shot dead by a 28-year-old Saudi national, state media say.
