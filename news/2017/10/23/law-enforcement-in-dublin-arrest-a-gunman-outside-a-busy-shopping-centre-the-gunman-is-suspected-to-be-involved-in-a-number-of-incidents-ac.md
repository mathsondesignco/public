### [2017-10-23](/news/2017/10/23/index.md)

# Law enforcement in Dublin arrest a gunman outside a busy shopping centre. The gunman is suspected to be involved in a number of incidents across the city, including several assaults and carjackings. 

A man in his 30s has been arrested and a suspected gun seized in a busy shopping centre after a near eight-hour manhunt.
