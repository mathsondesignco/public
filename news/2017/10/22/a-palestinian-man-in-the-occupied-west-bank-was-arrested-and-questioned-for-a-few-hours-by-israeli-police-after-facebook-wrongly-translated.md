# A Palestinian man in the occupied West Bank was arrested and questioned for a few hours by Israeli police after Facebook wrongly translated his Arabic-language message saying "good morning" into Modern Hebrew for "attack them". 

The builder&#039;s morning took a turn for the worse after his Arabic post was wrongly translated to &quot;attack them&quot; in Hebrew.
