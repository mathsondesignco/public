### [2017-10-22](/news/2017/10/22/index.md)

# Lombard autonomy referendum, 2017;Set on the same day, two regions of Italy hold non-binding referendums aimed to decide whether the citizen want greater autonomy or not. 

Central government says the polls are unnecessary but they are permitted under the constitution.
