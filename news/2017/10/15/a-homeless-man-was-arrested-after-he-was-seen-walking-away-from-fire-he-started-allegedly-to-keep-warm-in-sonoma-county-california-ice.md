### [2017-10-15](/news/2017/10/15/index.md)

#  A homeless man was arrested after he was seen walking away from fire he started allegedly "to keep warm" in Sonoma County, California. ICE asked to detain him for deportation but Sheriff Rob Giordano declined. The Sheriff determined the suspect was unlikely to be connected to any of the deadlyOctober 2017 Northern California wildfires

Right-wing media sites Breitbart and InfoWars – without evidence – link the arrest of a 29-year-old Latino man Sunday October 15, 2017 to the catastrophic wine country fires that have killed at least 28 people in the area.
