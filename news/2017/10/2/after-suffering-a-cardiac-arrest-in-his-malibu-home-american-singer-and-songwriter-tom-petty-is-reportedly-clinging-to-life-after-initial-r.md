# After suffering a cardiac arrest in his Malibu home, American singer and songwriter Tom Petty is reportedly clinging to life after initial reports that he had died. 

U.S. rocker Tom Petty was reported on Monday to be clinging to life in a southern California hospital after conflicting reports that he had died.
