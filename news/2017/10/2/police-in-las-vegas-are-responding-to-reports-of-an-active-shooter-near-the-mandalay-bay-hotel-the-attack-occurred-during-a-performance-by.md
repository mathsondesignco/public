### [2017-10-2](/news/2017/10/2/index.md)

# Police in Las Vegas are responding to reports of an active shooter near the Mandalay Bay Hotel. The attack occurred during a performance by country music singer Jason Aldean. 

Multiple people are being treated for gunshot wounds after at least one shooter opened fire at the Mandalay Bay hotel and casino.
