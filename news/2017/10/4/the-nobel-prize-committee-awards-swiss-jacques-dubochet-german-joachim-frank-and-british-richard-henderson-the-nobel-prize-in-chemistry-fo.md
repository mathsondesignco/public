# The Nobel Prize committee awards Swiss Jacques Dubochet, German Joachim Frank, and British Richard Henderson the Nobel Prize in Chemistry for developing cryo-electron microscopy for the high-resolution structural determination of biomolecules in a solution. 

This year’s prize has been awarded for developing cryo-electron microscopy for the high resolution structure determination of biomolecules in solution – join us live for all the news and reaction
