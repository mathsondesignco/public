# Opposition leader Katrn Jakobsdttir of the Left-Green Movement receives the mandate to form a government with its coalition partners: the Social Democratic Alliance, the Progressives and the Pirates. 

Icelandic Left Green Movement leader Katrin Jakobsdottir has been given a mandate to explore a governing coalition from President Gudni Th. Johannesson.
