# President Robert Mugabe got fired as the leader of ZANU-PF and was replaced with Emmerson Mnangagwa, the former Vice President of Zimbabwe. 

Zanu PF has sacked the president and replaced him with Emmerson Mnangagwa, the deputy he dismissed two weeks ago
