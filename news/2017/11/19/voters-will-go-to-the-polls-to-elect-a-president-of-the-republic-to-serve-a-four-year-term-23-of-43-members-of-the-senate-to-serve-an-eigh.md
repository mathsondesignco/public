### [2017-11-19](/news/2017/11/19/index.md)

# Voters will go to the polls to elect: a President of the Republic to serve a four-year term, 23 of 43 members of the Senate to serve an eight-year term in the National Congress, the full 155 members of the Chamber of Deputies to serve a four-year term in the National Congress, the full 278 members of the regional boards to serve a four-year term. 

Eight candidates are standing, with ex-president Sebastián Piñera seen as a clear favourite.
