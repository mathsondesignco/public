# U.S. President Donald Trump re-tweets three videos designed to spread hatred for Muslims that were originally tweeted by a member of British far-right political party Britain First, prompting international condemnation. One of the videos is revealed to be fake. 

President Trump on Wednesday re-tweeted a series of alarming videos, one of which allegedly depicts a Muslim ‘migrant’ attacking a ‘Dutch boy.’ The videos were originally posted to social media by a far-right British leader. The panel discusses.
