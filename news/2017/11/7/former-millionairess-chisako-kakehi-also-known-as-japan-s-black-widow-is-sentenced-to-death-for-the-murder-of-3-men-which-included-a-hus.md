### [2017-11-7](/news/2017/11/7/index.md)

# Former millionairess Chisako Kakehi (also known as Japan's "Black Widow") is sentenced to death for the murder of 3 men which included a husband. The case had been considered high-profile in the country. 

Chisako Kakehi, 70, was found guilty of fatally poisoning her elderly partners for insurance payouts.
