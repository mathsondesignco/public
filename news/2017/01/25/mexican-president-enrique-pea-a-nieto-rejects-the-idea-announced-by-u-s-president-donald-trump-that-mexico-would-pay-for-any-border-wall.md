# Mexican President Enrique Pena Nieto rejects the idea, announced by U.S. President Donald Trump, that Mexico would pay for any border wall between the United States and his country. 

Mexico&#39;s president has hit back at Donald Trump&#39;s decision to build a border wall, repeating that his country would not pay for it.
