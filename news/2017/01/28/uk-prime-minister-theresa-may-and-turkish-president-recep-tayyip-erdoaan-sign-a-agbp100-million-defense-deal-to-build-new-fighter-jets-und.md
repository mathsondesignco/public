### [2017-01-28](/news/2017/01/28/index.md)

# UK Prime Minister Theresa May and Turkish President Recep Tayyip Erdogan sign a GBP100 million defense deal to build new fighter jets. Under the deal, BAE Systems will collaborate with Turkish companies to build the TAI TFX. 

Prime minister also uses last leg of diplomatic tour to issue warning to Turkish president to respect human rights obligations
