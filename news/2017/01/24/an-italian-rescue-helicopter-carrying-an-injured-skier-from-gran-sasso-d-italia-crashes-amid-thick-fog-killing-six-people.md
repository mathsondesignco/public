### [2017-01-24](/news/2017/01/24/index.md)

# An Italian rescue helicopter, carrying an injured skier from Gran Sasso d'Italia, crashes amid thick fog, killing six people. 

The helicopter had just picked up an injured skier from a resort in central Italy when it went down.
