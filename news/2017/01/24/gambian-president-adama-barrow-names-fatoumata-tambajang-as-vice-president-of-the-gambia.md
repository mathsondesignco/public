### [2017-01-24](/news/2017/01/24/index.md)

# Gambian President Adama Barrow names Fatoumata Tambajang as Vice-President of the Gambia. 

New deputy leader had previously vowed to prosecute ex-ruler Yahya Jammeh for alleged crimes committed by his regime.
