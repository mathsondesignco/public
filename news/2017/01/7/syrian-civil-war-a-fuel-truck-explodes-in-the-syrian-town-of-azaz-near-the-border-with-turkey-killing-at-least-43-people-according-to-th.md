# Syrian Civil War: A fuel truck explodes in the Syrian town of Azaz, near the border with Turkey, killing at least 43 people, according to the Syrian Observatory for Human Rights. 

Fuel truck explodes in front of a courthouse in Azaz, and news agency says car bomb planted by Islamic State was responsible
