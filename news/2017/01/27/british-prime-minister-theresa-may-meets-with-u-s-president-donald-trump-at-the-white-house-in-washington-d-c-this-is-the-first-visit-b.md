### [2017-01-27](/news/2017/01/27/index.md)

# British Prime Minister  Theresa May meets with U.S. President Donald Trump at the White House in Washington, D.C.. This is the first visit by a foreign leader since Trump's inauguration last Friday. 

The UK prime minister is the first world leader to meet US President Donald Trump for talks.
