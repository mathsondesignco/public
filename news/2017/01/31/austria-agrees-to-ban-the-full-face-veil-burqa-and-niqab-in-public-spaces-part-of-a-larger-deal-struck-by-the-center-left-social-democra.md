### [2017-01-31](/news/2017/01/31/index.md)

# Austria agrees to ban the full-face veil (burqa and niqab) in public spaces, part of a larger deal struck by the center-left Social Democratic Party and the center-right Austrian People's Party to avoid the collapse of the ruling coalition government. 

The governing coalition may also prevent civil servants from wearing religious symbols.
