# January 2017 Central Italy earthquakes: Four earthquakes of magnitude 5.1 or higher strike near Amatrice, Italy, forcing evacuation of some schools and the Rome Metro. No deaths are reported from the quakes. 

A string of moderate earthquakes hit central Italy on Wednesday, shaking an area that&#039;s still rebuilding from last year&#039;s devastating tremors.
