### [2017-01-29](/news/2017/01/29/index.md)

# Syrian Civil War. Siege of Wadi Barada. The Syrian Army recaptures all of the towns and villages in the Barada river valley near Damascus from rebel forces. 

The advance of government forces comes a week after fruitless negotiations between Damascus and rebels in Astana.
