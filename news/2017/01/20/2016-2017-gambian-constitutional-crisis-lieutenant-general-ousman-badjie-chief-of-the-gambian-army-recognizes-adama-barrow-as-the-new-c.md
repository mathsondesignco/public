### [2017-01-20](/news/2017/01/20/index.md)

# 2016-2017 Gambian constitutional crisis. Lieutenant General Ousman Badjie, chief of the Gambian army, recognizes Adama Barrow as the new commander-and-chief of the armed forces and says that his troops will not fight ECOWAS soldiers. 

The Gambian strongman Yahya Jammeh has agreed to step down, officials involved in negotiations say.
