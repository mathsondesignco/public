# War in Somalia. A car bomb explodes at a checkpoint near the Villa Somalia, the President of Somalia Mohamed Abdullahi Mohamed's official residence in Mogadishu, killing at least five people. 

The blast occurred at a checkpoint near the presidential palace just as the new government was unveiled.
