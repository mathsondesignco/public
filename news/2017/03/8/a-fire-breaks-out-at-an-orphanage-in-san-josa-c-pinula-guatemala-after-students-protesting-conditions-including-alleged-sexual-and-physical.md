### [2017-03-8](/news/2017/03/8/index.md)

# A fire breaks out at an orphanage in San Jose Pinula, Guatemala after students protesting  conditions including alleged sexual and physical abuse set fire to their bedding, killing at least 19 children. 

The teenage girls die a day after a riot erupted at the shelter near Guatemala City.
