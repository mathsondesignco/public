### [2017-03-8](/news/2017/03/8/index.md)

# Syrian Civil War: American-led intervention in Syria: The United States Marine Corps' 11th Marine Expeditionary Unit arrives in Syria ahead of a Syrian Democratic Forces operation to take control of ISIL's de facto capital of Raqqa. 

The deployment marks a new escalation in the war in Syria.
