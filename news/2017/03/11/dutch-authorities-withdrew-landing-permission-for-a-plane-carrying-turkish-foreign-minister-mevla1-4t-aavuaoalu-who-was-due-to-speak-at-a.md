### [2017-03-11](/news/2017/03/11/index.md)

#  Dutch authorities withdrew landing permission for a plane carrying Turkish Foreign Minister Mevlut Cavusoglu, who was due to speak at a Rotterdam rally in support of a 'Yes' vote in April's constitutional referendum. 

Turkey vows a &quot;harsh response&quot; as a diplomatic row with the Netherlands escalates.
