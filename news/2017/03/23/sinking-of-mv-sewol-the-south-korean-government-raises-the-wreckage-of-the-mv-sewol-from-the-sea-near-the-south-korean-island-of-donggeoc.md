# Sinking of MV Sewol. The South Korean government raises the wreckage of the MV "Sewol" from the sea near the South Korean island of Donggeochado. The ferry sank in April 2014, killing 304 people. 

The Sewol sank on 16 April 2014 killing 304 people, almost all school children on a trip.
