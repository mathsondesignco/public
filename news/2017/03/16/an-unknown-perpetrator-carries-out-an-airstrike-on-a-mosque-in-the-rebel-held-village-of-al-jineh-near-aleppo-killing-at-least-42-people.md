### [2017-03-16](/news/2017/03/16/index.md)

# An unknown perpetrator carries out an airstrike on a mosque in the rebel-held village of al-Jineh near Aleppo, killing at least 42 people. 

The US investigates reports that it killed dozens of civilians but denies it hit a mosque.
