### [2017-03-29](/news/2017/03/29/index.md)

# The Swedish Academy announces that American singer-songwriter Bob Dylan has agreed to accept his Nobel Prize in Literature this weekend at a private location in Stockholm. 

The American singer, who snubbed a ceremony in December, says no media must be present.
