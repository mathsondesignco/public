# At a NATO meeting, German foreign minister Sigmar Gabriel rejects as "quite unrealistic" the belief that Germany would spend 2% of GDP on the military. He says other spending such as development aid should be taken into account. 

Foreign minister says it is unrealistic to expect Germany to spend 2% of GDP on defence.
