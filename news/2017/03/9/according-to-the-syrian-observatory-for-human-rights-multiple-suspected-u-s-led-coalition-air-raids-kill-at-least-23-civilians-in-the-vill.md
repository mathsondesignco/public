### [2017-03-9](/news/2017/03/9/index.md)

# According to the Syrian Observatory for Human Rights, multiple suspected U.S.-led coalition air raids kill at least 23 civilians in the village of al-Matab in northern Syria. 

Fighter jets believed to belong to the US-led coalition killed dozens in air raids on ISIL-held village, monitor says.
