### [2017-03-30](/news/2017/03/30/index.md)

# Cyclone Debbie. Authorities of New South Wales issue a State Emergency Service for the towns of Murwillumbah and Lismore, and at least 12 flood rescues are underway as former Cyclone Debbie moves south from north Queensland. 

Residents in parts of Lismore have been ordered to immediately evacuate as northern NSW is lashed by heavy rain and major flooding caused by the remnants of ex-tropical cyclone Debbie.
