### [2017-09-30](/news/2017/09/30/index.md)

# In Australian rules football, the Richmond Football Club defeats the Adelaide Football Club in the 2017 AFL Grand Final by 108 points to 60. Dustin Martin won the Norm Smith Medal for best on ground after having won the 2017 Brownlow Medal for the best and fairest player in the competition. 

Tigers superstar Dustin Martin became the first player in history to win the Brownlow and Norm Smith medals in the same week.
