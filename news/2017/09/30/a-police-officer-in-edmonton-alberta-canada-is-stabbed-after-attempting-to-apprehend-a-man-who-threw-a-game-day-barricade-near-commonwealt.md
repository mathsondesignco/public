### [2017-09-30](/news/2017/09/30/index.md)

# A police officer in Edmonton, Alberta, Canada is stabbed after attempting to apprehend a man who threw a game-day barricade near Commonwealth Stadium. The suspect fled the scene and remains at large. 

The suspect remains at large, but police have not provided a description.
