### [2017-09-18](/news/2017/09/18/index.md)

# Toys "R" Us files for Chapter 11 bankruptcy protection in the United States, and also files for bankruptcy in Canada, as it attempts to restructure its debts. 

The once dominant toy retailer has struggled against larger rivals such as Amazon and Walmart.
