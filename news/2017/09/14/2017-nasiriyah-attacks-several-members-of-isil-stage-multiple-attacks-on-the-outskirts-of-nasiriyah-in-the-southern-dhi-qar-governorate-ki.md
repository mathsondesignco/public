### [2017-09-14](/news/2017/09/14/index.md)

# 2017 Nasiriyah attacks: several members of ISIL stage multiple attacks on the outskirts of Nasiriyah in the southern Dhi Qar Governorate, killing at least 50 people and injuring 87 others. 

Gunmen and a suicide bomber storm a restaurant and open fire before a bomb explodes at a checkpoint.
