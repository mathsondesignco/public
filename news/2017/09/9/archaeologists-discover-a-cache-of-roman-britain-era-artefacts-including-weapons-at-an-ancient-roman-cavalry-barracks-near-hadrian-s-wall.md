# Archaeologists discover a cache of Roman Britain-era artefacts, including weapons, at an ancient Roman cavalry barracks near Hadrian's Wall in northern England. Archaeologists liken the discovery to winning the lottery. 

Dig team stumble across thousands of pristine artefacts at ancient garrison site in Northumberland, writes Dalya Alberge
