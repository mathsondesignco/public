# Apple Inc. announces three new iPhone models: the iPhone 8, iPhone 8 Plus, and the iPhone X. The Apple Watch Series 3 and a new Apple TV are also announced. 

Apple unveiled a new high-end smartphone called the iPhone X, as well as an iPhone 8 and 8 Plus and other products. Read our analysis of the event.
