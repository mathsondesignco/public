### [2017-09-12](/news/2017/09/12/index.md)

# The U.S. Supreme Court rules that the Trump administration can block international refugees from entering the United States. 

The U.S. Supreme Court on Tuesday allowed President Donald Trump to broadly implement a ban on refugees entering the country from around the world.
