### [2017-02-8](/news/2017/02/8/index.md)

# War in Afghanistan (2015-present): Six Afghan Red Cross workers are shot dead by suspected ISIL militants in the northern Jowzjan Province. Two other Red Cross workers are reportedly abducted. 

The International Committee of the Red Cross says it is putting its work in Afghanistan on hold.
