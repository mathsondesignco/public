### [2017-02-2](/news/2017/02/2/index.md)

# The White House cautions Israel that building new settlements or expanding existing settlements in the West Bank, "... may not be helpful in achieving (peace)." 

A carefully worded statement from White House Press Secretary Sean Spicer on Thursday marked the first public disagreement between Trump and Netanyahu since they had what the administration described as a &#x27;very warm&#x27; conversation by phone late last month.
