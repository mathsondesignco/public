# Yemeni Civil War (2015-present): An air raid on a funeral in a village north of Sana'a kills at least eight women and children. The Houthis blames the Saudi-led coalition for the attack. 

Houthi rebels blame Arab coalition for attack on Shiraa village 40km north of capital Sanaa.
