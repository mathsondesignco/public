# A rebel commander is killed by a bomb in eastern Ukraine while three Ukrainian Army soldiers are killed in fighting. At least 33 people have been killed in fighting over the past week. 

A top rebel commander in eastern Ukraine has been killed in a car bombing that rebels are blaming on Ukraine&#039;s special services.
