### [2017-02-3](/news/2017/02/3/index.md)

# 2016-17 Rohingya persecution in Myanmar: The United Nations Human Rights Council releases a report detailing mass atrocities by the military and police forces against Myanmar's Rohingya minority, including gang-rapes, mass killings, and possible ethnic cleansing, which could amount to crimes against humanity. 

A UN report accuses security in Myanmar forces of gang-raping Rohingya women and killing babies.
