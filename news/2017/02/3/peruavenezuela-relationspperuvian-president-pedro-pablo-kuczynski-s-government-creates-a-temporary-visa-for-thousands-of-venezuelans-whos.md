### [2017-02-3](/news/2017/02/3/index.md)

# Peru-Venezuela relationsPeruvian  President Pedro Pablo Kuczynski's government creates a temporary visa for thousands of Venezuelans whose country  is mired in a deep economic crisis. Visa holders will be able to study, work, and receive health services in Peru for a year. "This is part of our migratory policy that aims to build bridges not walls", says Interior Minister Carlos Basombrio.
