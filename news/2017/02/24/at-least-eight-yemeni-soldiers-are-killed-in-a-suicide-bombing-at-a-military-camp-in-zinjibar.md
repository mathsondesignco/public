### [2017-02-24](/news/2017/02/24/index.md)

# At least eight Yemeni soldiers are killed in a suicide bombing at a military camp in Zinjibar. 

Bomber wearing a military uniform was driving a car that exploded at the entrance of military camp in Abyan province.
