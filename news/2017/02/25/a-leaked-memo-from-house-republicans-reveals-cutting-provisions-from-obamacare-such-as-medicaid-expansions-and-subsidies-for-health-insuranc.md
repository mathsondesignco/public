# A leaked memo from House Republicans reveals cutting provisions from Obamacare such as Medicaid expansions and subsidies for health insurance. 

The 105-page draft legislation, obtained by NBC News, repeals much of the current law within the next few years and sets in place a Republican vision of healthcare.
