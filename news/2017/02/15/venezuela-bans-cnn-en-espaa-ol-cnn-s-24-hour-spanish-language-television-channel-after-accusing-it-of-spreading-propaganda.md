### [2017-02-15](/news/2017/02/15/index.md)

# Venezuela bans CNN en Espanol, CNN's 24-hour Spanish language television channel, after accusing it of spreading propaganda. 

A report alleged the vice-president was tainted by the sale of passports to terror-linked individuals in Iraq.
