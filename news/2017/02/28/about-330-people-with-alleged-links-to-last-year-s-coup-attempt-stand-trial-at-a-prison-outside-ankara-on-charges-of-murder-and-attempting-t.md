### [2017-02-28](/news/2017/02/28/index.md)

# About 330 people with alleged links to last year's coup attempt stand trial at a prison outside Ankara on charges of murder and attempting to overthrow the government, the largest trial to date. 

Charges for those allegedly involved in failed July military coup range from murder to trying to overthrow government.
