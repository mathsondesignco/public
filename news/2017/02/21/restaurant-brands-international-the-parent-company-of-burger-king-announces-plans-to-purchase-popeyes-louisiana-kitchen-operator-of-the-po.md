### [2017-02-21](/news/2017/02/21/index.md)

# Restaurant Brands International. The parent company of Burger King announces plans to purchase Popeyes Louisiana Kitchen, operator of the Popeyes chicken restaurant chain in a US$1.8 billion deal. 

Restaurant Brands International said it would expand Popeyes in the U.S. and globally.
