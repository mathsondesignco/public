### [2017-02-14](/news/2017/02/14/index.md)

# Kurdish-Turkish conflict (1978-present) / 2016-17 Turkish purges. Turkish police arrest over 800 people with alleged links to the outlawed Kurdistan Workers' Party (PKK). 

State media says more than 800 people have been detained in operations carried out in 37 provinces.
