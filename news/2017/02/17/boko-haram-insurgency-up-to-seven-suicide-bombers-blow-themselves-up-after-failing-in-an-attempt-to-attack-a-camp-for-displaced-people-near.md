# Boko Haram insurgency: Up to seven suicide bombers blow themselves up after failing in an attempt to attack a camp for displaced people near Maiduguri, Nigeria. 

Officials say incident near camp for displaced people resulted in deaths of seven people who tried to attack Maiduguri.
