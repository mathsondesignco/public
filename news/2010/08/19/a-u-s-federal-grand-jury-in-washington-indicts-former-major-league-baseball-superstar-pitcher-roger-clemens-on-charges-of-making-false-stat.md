### [2010-08-19](/news/2010/08/19/index.md)

# A U.S. federal grand jury in Washington indicts former Major League Baseball superstar pitcher Roger Clemens on charges of making false statements to Congress about his alleged use of performance-enhancing drugs. 

Former pitcher Roger Clemens has been indicted for lying to Congress about steroid use. He faces six counts - one for obstruction of Congress, three for false statements and two for perjury.
