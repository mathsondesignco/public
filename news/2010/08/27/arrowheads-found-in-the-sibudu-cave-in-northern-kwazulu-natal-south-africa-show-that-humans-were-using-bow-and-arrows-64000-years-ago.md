### [2010-08-27](/news/2010/08/27/index.md)

# Arrowheads found in the Sibudu Cave in northern  KwaZulu-Natal, South Africa show that humans were using bow and arrows 64000 years ago. 

Researchers in South Africa have revealed the earliest direct evidence of human-made arrows.
