# Russian President Dmitry Medvedev arrives in Abkhazia on his first visit there since the territory declared independence from Georgia two years ago. 

Russian President Dmitry Medvedev visits the breakaway Georgian region of Abkhazia, on the anniversary of the 2008 Russia-Georgia war.
