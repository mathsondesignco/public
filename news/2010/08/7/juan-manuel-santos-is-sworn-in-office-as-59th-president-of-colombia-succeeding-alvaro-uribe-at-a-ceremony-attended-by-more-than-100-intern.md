### [2010-08-7](/news/2010/08/7/index.md)

# Juan Manuel Santos is sworn in office as 59th President of Colombia, succeeding lvaro Uribe, at a ceremony attended by more than 100 international delegations. 

Jaun Manuel Santos takes office amid soaring unemployment and a spat with Venezuela.
