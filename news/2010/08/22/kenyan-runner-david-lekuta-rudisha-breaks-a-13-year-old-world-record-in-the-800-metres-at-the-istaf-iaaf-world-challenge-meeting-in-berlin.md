### [2010-08-22](/news/2010/08/22/index.md)

# Kenyan runner David Lekuta Rudisha breaks a 13-year-old world record in the 800 metres at the ISTAF IAAF World Challenge meeting in Berlin, overshadowing Caster Semenya's return to the venue of her triumph. 

Kenya&#039;s David Rudisha became the fastest 800 metres runner in history at Berlin&rsquo;s ISATF meeting on Sunday with a stunning world record of 1min 41.09sec before predicting that he could go even faster.
