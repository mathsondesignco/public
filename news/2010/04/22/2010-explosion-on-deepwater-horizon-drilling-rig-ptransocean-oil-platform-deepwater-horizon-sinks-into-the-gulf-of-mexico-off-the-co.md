# 2010 Explosion on ''Deepwater Horizon'' drilling rig:Transocean oil platform ''Deepwater Horizon'' sinks into the Gulf of Mexico off the coast of Louisiana after an explosion two days earlier that injured 7, with 11 still missing. 

The deepwater oil rig burning off the coast of Louisiana sank into the Gulf of Mexico at 10:20 a.m. Thursday, according to a Coast Guard spokesman.
