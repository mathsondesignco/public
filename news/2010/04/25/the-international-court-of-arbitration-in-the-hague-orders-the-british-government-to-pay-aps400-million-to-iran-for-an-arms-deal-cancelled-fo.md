### [2010-04-25](/news/2010/04/25/index.md)

# The International Court of Arbitration in The Hague orders the British government to pay PS400 million to Iran for an arms deal cancelled following the Iranian Revolution. 

The British government has confirmed that it has been ordered to pay back $650 million to Iran over a 1970s arms deal.
