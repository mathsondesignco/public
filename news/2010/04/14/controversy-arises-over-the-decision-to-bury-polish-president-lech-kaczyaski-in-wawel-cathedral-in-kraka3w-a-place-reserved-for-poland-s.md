### [2010-04-14](/news/2010/04/14/index.md)

# Controversy arises over the decision to bury Polish President Lech Kaczynski in Wawel Cathedral in Krakow-a place reserved for Poland's kings and heroes. Hundreds of people have taken to the streets in protest, and thousands have joined an internet campaign objecting to the plan.
