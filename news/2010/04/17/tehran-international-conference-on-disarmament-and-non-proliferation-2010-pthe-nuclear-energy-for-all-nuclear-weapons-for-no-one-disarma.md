# Tehran International Conference on Disarmament and Non-Proliferation, 2010:The "Nuclear Energy for All, Nuclear Weapons for No One" disarmament conference opens in response to President of the United States Barack Obama's conference in Washington, D.C. 

Tehran summit said to focus on availability of nuclear energy for all nations.
