### [2010-04-26](/news/2010/04/26/index.md)

# By a 6-5 margin, a United States federal appeals court rules that a sex-discrimination lawsuit, the largest employment discrimination case in history, against Wal-Mart can continue. 

Pay and promotions are at the root of a class action case first filed by six women in 2001 against the retailer.
