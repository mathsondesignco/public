### [2010-04-18](/news/2010/04/18/index.md)

# Benedict and child sexual abuse in the Roman Catholic Church:. Pope Benedict XVI concludes his visit to Malta. Reports suggest he is trying to avoid discussing the recent child sex abuse scandal, on his first trip since the scandal broke. 

Pope Benedict XVI met with a group of victims and expressed “shame and sorrow” at their plight.
