### [2010-04-12](/news/2010/04/12/index.md)

# Aftermath of 2010 Polish Air Force Tu-154 crash:. Polish President Lech Kaczynski lies in state in Warsaw as Russia marks a day of mourning and Poland appoints an acting head of the central bank to replace the one killed in Saturday's air disaster near Smolensk. 

Poland is continuing to mourn the death of President Lech Kaczynski who died in a plane crash on Saturday.
