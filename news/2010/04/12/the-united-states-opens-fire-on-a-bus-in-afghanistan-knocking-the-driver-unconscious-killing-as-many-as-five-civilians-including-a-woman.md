### [2010-04-12](/news/2010/04/12/index.md)

# The United States opens fire on a bus in Afghanistan, knocking the driver unconscious, killing as many as five civilians, including a woman, and wounding at least 18 other passengers.

An attack that killed as many as five people infuriated leaders in Kandahar before an important offensive.
