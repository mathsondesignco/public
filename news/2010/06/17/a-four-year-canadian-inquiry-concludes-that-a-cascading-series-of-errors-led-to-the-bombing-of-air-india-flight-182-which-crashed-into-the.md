# A four-year Canadian inquiry concludes that a "cascading series of errors" led to the bombing of Air India Flight 182 which crashed into the Atlantic Ocean south of Ireland on 23 June 1985, killing all 329 people on board. 

A &quot;cascading series of errors&quot; led to the 1985 bombing of an Air India jet that killed 329 people, an inquiry in Canada concludes.
