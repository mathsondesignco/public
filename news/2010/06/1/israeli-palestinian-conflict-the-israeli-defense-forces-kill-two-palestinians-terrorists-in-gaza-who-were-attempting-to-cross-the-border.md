### [2010-06-1](/news/2010/06/1/index.md)

# Israeli-Palestinian conflict. The Israeli Defense Forces kill two Palestinians terrorists in Gaza who were attempting to cross the border fence, after an exchange of gunfire. Israel closes four settlements, Route 323 and schools as a result of the incident. 

Infiltrators shot dead in exchange of fire with IDF soldiers.
