# 2010 Kyrgyzstan crisis:Tensions remain high in Kyrgyzstan, with police patrolling the streets and special forces standing guard in the city of Osh and Jalal-Abad, after ethnic clashes left more than 100 people dead and about 1,400 others injured since fighting broke out Thursday night. 

Authorities struggling to restore order in southern region where ethnic violence has left at least 100 dead.
