### [2010-06-28](/news/2010/06/28/index.md)

# Iranian President Mahmoud Ahmadinejad postpones nuclear talks so as to "punish the West" for United Nations Security Council Resolution 1929 aimed at curbing the alleged development of Iranian nuclear weapons. 

Ahmadinejad says negotiations over nuclear programme will resume only in late August.
