### [2010-06-2](/news/2010/06/2/index.md)

# President of Lombardy, Roberto Formigoni, offers pregnant women EUR4,500 if they do not have abortions: anti-abortion campaigners welcome the move and critics call it "propaganda". 

Pregnant women in northern Italy who face economic hardship are to be offered money not to have an abortion.
