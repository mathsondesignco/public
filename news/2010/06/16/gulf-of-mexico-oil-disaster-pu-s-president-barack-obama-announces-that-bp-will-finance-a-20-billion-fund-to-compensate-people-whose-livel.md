### [2010-06-16](/news/2010/06/16/index.md)

# Gulf of Mexico oil disaster:U.S. President Barack Obama announces that  BP will finance a $20 billion fund to compensate people whose livelihoods have been damaged by the devastating oil spill in the Gulf of Mexico, and the company's chairman apologized for the worst spill in U.S. history. 

BP’s embattled chief executive, Tony Hayward, prepared to tell Congress on Thursday that he was “deeply sorry” for the oil disaster in the gulf.
