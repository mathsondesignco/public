### [2010-06-16](/news/2010/06/16/index.md)

# The National Army of Colombia says an unknown number of informants who aided the rescue of three police officers and a soldier from FARC on Sunday will receive a $1.2 million reward between them. 

Colombian informants share a $1.2m reward for helping the rescue of four security personnel after a decade in rebel hands.
