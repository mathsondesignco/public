### [2010-06-11](/news/2010/06/11/index.md)

# Israeli police fatally shoot a Palestinian driver who was attempting to run them down; the two policeman and three civilians are injured in the incident. 

Tensions have been running high since an Israeli commando raid last month on a Turkish aid ship bound for Gaza.
