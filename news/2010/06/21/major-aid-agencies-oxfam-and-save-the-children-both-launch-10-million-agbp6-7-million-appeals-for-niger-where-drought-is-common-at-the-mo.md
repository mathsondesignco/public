### [2010-06-21](/news/2010/06/21/index.md)

# Major aid agencies Oxfam and Save the Children both launch $10 million (GBP6.7 million) appeals for Niger where drought is common at the moment and half the country has no food. 

Two major aid agencies launch appeals for seven million people facing food shortages in Niger after crop failures.
