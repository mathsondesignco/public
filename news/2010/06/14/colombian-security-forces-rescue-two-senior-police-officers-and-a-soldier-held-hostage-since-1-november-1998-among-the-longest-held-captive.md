### [2010-06-14](/news/2010/06/14/index.md)

# Colombian security forces rescue two senior police officers and a soldier held hostage since 1 November 1998, among the longest-held captives; a fourth hostage is later rescued. 

Colombian forces rescue three of their men held hostage for more than a decade by Farc rebels and are searching for a fourth.
