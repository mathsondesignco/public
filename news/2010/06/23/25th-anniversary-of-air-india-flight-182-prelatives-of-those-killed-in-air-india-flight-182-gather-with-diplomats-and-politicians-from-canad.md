### [2010-06-23](/news/2010/06/23/index.md)

# 25th anniversary of Air India Flight 182:Relatives of those killed in Air India Flight 182 gather with diplomats and politicians from Canada, India and Ireland in Cork to mark the 25th anniversary with a memorial service. 

Diplomats and relatives gathered today in west Cork to commemorate the 25th anniversary of the Air India crash.
