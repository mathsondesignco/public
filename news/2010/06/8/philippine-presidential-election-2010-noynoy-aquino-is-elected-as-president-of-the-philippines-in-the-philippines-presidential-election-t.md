### [2010-06-8](/news/2010/06/8/index.md)

# Philippine presidential election, 2010. Noynoy Aquino is elected as President of the Philippines in the Philippines presidential election, taking 42% of the vote, about 16 percentage points more than second-place Joseph Estrada.
