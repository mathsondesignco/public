### [2010-06-9](/news/2010/06/9/index.md)

# Dutch general election of 2010:. Prime Minister Jan Peter Balkenende resigns as leader of the Christian Democratic Appeal (CDA) after a disappointing election result. 

Figures show Liberal and Labour parties taking 31 seats with far-right Freedom Party on 23.
