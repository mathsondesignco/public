### [2010-06-9](/news/2010/06/9/index.md)

# The UK government brings forward new rules which make it compulsory for immigrants from outside the European Union, particularly South Asia, to understand the English language. 

The coalition government is bringing forward new rules requiring many immigrants marrying UK citizens to learn English.
