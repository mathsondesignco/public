### [2010-11-29](/news/2010/11/29/index.md)

# Irish financial crisis:Fine Gael speaks out against the high interest rate imposed by the International Monetary Fund and the European Central Bank and their insistence that the country pay EUR17 billion out of its own national pension fund. 

The euro and major European markets fall after ministers agree a 85bn euros bail-out for the Republic of Ireland.
