# At least 8 people are killed when a Russian Ilyushin Il-76 cargo plane bound for Khartoum, Sudan, crashes minutes after take off in a residential area of Karachi, Pakistan. 

Plane crashed in navy housing colony minutes after taking off; 11 dead.
