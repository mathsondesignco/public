### [2010-11-10](/news/2010/11/10/index.md)

# American Gabe Watson leaves detention in Australia after serving time for the death of Tina Watson after she drowned in 2007 after he failed to rescue her. He will be extradited to Alabama to face murder charges upon suspicion of murder. 

HONEYMOON killer Gabe Watson will fly out of Brisbane today hours after being released from jail.
