### [2010-11-11](/news/2010/11/11/index.md)

# Following the detonation of explosive devices near the Gaza border, Israel Air Force helicopters targeted an area near the city of Khan Yunis, causing damage to houses and injuring a number militants suspected of involvement in the attack. 

Palestinian sources report of exchanges of fire east of Khan Younis; missiles said to be fired at deserted building
