### [2010-11-12](/news/2010/11/12/index.md)

# The International Cricket Council forms a three man tribunal to hear charges against former Pakistan captain Salman Butt and fast bowlers Mohammad Asif and Mohammad Aamer for involvement in the Pakistan spot fixing controversy. 

The International Cricket Council (ICC) has formed a three-man anti-corruption tribunal which in January will hear the case of three Pakistani players allegedly involved in spot-fixing.
