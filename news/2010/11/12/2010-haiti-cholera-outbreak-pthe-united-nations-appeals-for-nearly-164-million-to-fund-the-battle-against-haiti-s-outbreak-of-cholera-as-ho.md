# 2010 Haiti cholera outbreak:The United Nations appeals for nearly $164 million to fund the battle against Haiti's outbreak of cholera as hospitals overflow and space and time run out. 

The UN appeals for nearly $164m in aid to help fight a cholera outbreak in Haiti which has now claimed 724 lives.
