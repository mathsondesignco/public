# Afghan parliamentary election, 2010:Voters in Afghanistan go to the polls braving threats from the Taliban with voter turnout calculated at 40 per cent. 

Afghan officials hail a parliamentary vote as a success despite low turnout, attacks that killed 14 people and widespread fraud.
