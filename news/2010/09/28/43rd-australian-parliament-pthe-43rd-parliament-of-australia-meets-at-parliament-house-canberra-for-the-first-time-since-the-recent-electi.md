# 43rd Australian Parliament:The 43rd Parliament of Australia meets at Parliament House, Canberra, for the first time since the recent election. 

A traditional smoking ceremony welcomed politicians to Canberra ahead of the official opening of Australia&#039;s 43rd Parliament.
