### [2010-09-28](/news/2010/09/28/index.md)

# Iceland's parliament, the Althing, refers charges of misconduct in office related to the 2008-2012 Icelandic financial crisis against former Prime Minister of Iceland Geir Haarde to the Landsdomur, a special court for such cases.
