# 95 people, including two former mayors and planning chief of Marbella, appear in court in Malaga in one of Spain's biggest corruption trials. 

Almost 100 defendants, including two former mayors, have appeared in court in the Spanish city of Malaga for the first day of one of the country&#039;s biggest ever corruption trials.
