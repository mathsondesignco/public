### [2010-09-2](/news/2010/09/2/index.md)

# Pakistan's high commissioner Wajid Shamsul Hasan says 3 of his country's cricketers connected to allegations of betting corruption by the ''News of the World'' tabloid could have been set up. 

Three Pakistan cricketers embroiled in a betting scandal are facing possible life bans after being charged by the ICC.
