# Israeli soldiers identify a group of armed men approaching Israel from Gaza near Kibbutz Erez, killing three; Palestinian sources claim that two were farmers. 

Soldiers identify a group of armed men approaching security fence near Kibbutz Erez and kill 3; IDF reports no soldier casualties.
