### [2010-09-4](/news/2010/09/4/index.md)

# Australian floods:: Heavy rain in the Australian state of Victoria causes flooding in the towns of Ballarat, Maryborough and Bendigo with landslides blocking roads in the Victorian Alps. 

Emergency crews are evacuating homes in and around Ballarat, as heavy rain falls across Victoria.
