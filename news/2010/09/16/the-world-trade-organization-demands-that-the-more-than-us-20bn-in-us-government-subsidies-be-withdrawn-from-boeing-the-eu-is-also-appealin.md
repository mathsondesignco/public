### [2010-09-16](/news/2010/09/16/index.md)

# The World Trade Organization demands that the more than US$20bn in US government subsidies be withdrawn from Boeing. The EU is also appealing a decision that it was guilty of illegal subsidies. 

The World Trade Organisation has ruled some US government subsidies should be withdrawn from Boeing, say agencies.
