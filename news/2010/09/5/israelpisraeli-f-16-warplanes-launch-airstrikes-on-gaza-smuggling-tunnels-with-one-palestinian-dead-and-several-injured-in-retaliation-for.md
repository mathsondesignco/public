# IsraelIsraeli F-16 warplanes launch airstrikes on Gaza smuggling tunnels, with one Palestinian dead and several injured, in retaliation for recent Palestinian attacks. 

Air force bombs 3 smuggling tunnels in Gaza Strip meant to help kidnap and attack Israelis; Palestinians: One dead, one injured, one missing.
