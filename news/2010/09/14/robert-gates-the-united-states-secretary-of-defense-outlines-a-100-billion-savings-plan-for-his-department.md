# Robert Gates, the United States Secretary of Defense, outlines a $100 billion savings plan for his Department. 

U.S. Defense Secretary Robert Gates Tuesday gave a taste of his $100 billion, five-year savings plan as he tries to push the Pentagon to run more efficiently.
