### [2010-09-14](/news/2010/09/14/index.md)

# Iran releases United States hiker Sarah Shourd from Evin Prison following payment of bail. 

Sarah Shourd, held for over a year with two others on suspicion of spying, arrives in Oman after being released on bail.
