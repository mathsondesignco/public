### [2010-09-10](/news/2010/09/10/index.md)

# Mining in 3 eastern Democratic Republic of the Congo provinces is banned by President Joseph Kabila. 

President Kabila bans mining in three provinces of DR Congo to allow the government to tackle a &quot;mafia&quot; controlling the trade.
