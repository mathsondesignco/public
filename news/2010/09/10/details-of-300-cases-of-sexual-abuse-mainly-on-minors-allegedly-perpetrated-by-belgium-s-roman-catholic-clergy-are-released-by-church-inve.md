# Details of 300 cases of sexual abuse, mainly on minors, allegedly perpetrated by Belgium's Roman Catholic clergy are released by Church investigator Peter Adriaenssens. 13 are found to have committed suicide. 

Harrowing details of alleged sex abuse by Roman Catholic clergy in Belgium are released by an official Church investigator.
