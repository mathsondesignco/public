# Somali Civil War. East African leaders call for 20,000 troops to be deployed across Somalia to support the United Nations-backed Transitional Federal Government and for the United Nations to replace the African Union Mission to Somalia. 

East African leaders have called for 20,000 troops to be deployed across Somalia to support the country&#39;s besieged transitional federal government.
