### [2010-07-17](/news/2010/07/17/index.md)

# At least 50 automobiles sustain burns in Grenoble, France, as people protest the fatal shooting of a man by police. The man was shot during a chase following a robbery after firing on police.   

The French government vows to restore order after rioting in the eastern city of Grenoble over a shooting by police.
