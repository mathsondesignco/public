### [2010-07-25](/news/2010/07/25/index.md)

# African Union summit:At the African Union summit in Kampala, attended by 53 countries, its president, the President of Malawi Bingu wa  Mutharika, states International Criminal Court indictments against the Sudanese president undermine African peace and security and calls for the issue to be solved in a different way. 

AU leaders also pledge to beef up an African force fighting al-Shabaab group in Somalia.
