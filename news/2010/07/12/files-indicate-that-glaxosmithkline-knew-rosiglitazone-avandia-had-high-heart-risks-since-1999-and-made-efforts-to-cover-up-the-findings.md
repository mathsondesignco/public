### [2010-07-12](/news/2010/07/12/index.md)

# Files indicate that GlaxoSmithKline knew Rosiglitazone (Avandia) had high heart risks since 1999, and made efforts to cover up the findings. 

SmithKline Beecham found in a study as early as 1999 that its diabetes medicine, Avandia, posed heart risks.
