### [2010-07-29](/news/2010/07/29/index.md)

# Greek police fire tear gas at demonstrating lorry drivers who were on strike in protest against new license charges. 

Greek police fire tear gas to disperse hundreds of lorry drivers protesting in Athens against a government order to end their strike.
