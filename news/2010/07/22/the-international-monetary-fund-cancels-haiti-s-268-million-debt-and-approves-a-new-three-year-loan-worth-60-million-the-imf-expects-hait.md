### [2010-07-22](/news/2010/07/22/index.md)

# The International Monetary Fund cancels Haiti's $268 million debt and approves a new three-year loan worth $60 million; the IMF expects Haiti to start paying back interest in late 2011. 

New $60m loan approved for quake-devastated nation.
