# Muttiah Muralidaran takes his 800th Test wicket for the Sri Lanka cricket team in his final ball before his retirement and finishes his career as the world record holder for number of wickets. 

World record-holding off-spinner Muttiah Muralidaran retired with 800 Test wickets on Thursday as Sri Lanka thumped India by 10 wickets in their first match.
