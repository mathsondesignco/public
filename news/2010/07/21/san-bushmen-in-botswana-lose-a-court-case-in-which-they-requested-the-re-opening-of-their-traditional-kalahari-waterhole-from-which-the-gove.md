# San bushmen in Botswana lose a court case in which they requested the re-opening of their traditional Kalahari waterhole from which the government forced them out when diamonds were discovered there in the 1980s. 

A group of San bushmen in Botswana have lost a court case to allow them to re-open a vital Kalahari waterhole.
