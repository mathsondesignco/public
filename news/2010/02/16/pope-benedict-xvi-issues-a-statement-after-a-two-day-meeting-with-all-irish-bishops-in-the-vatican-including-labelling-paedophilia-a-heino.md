### [2010-02-16](/news/2010/02/16/index.md)

# Pope Benedict XVI issues a statement after a two-day meeting with all Irish bishops in the Vatican, including labelling paedophilia a "heinous crime", but his nuncio refuses to appear before an inquiry in Dail Eireann. 

The Pope has told Irish bishops at a special summit in the Vatican that paedophilia is a heinous crime, according to a statement.
