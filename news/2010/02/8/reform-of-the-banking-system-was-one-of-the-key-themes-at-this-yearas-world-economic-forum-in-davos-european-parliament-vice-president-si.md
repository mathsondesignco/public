### [2010-02-8](/news/2010/02/8/index.md)

# Reform of the banking system was one of the key themes at this year's World Economic Forum in Davos. European Parliament Vice-President Silvana Koch-Mehrin said "There was general disapproval of the disproportionate self-rewarding in the banking sector".
