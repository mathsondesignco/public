### [2010-02-14](/news/2010/02/14/index.md)

# Nine Irish Roman Catholic bishops and Cardinal Sean Brady arrive in Rome to discuss the Murphy Report and Ryan Report into the Catholic sexual abuse scandal in Ireland with Pope Benedict XVI, the first such meetings there in eight years. 

Members of the Irish Catholic hierarchy are in Rome in advance of their meeting with Pope Benedict XVI and his most senior officials.
