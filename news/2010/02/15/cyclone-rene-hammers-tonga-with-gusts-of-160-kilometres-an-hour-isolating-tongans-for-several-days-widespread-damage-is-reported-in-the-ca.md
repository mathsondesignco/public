### [2010-02-15](/news/2010/02/15/index.md)

# Cyclone Rene hammers Tonga with gusts of 160 kilometres an hour, isolating Tongans for several days. Widespread damage is reported in the capital, Nuku`alofa, and contact is lost with the northern island of Vava`u.
