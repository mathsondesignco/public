### [2010-01-9](/news/2010/01/9/index.md)

# Togo national football team bus attack:The Togo national football team withdraw from the 2010 Africa Cup of Nations as a third member of the team is confirmed dead following the attack on their team bus. 

Three people were reported killed in Friday’s attack on Togo’s team bus in Angola days before the start of the African Nations Cup tournament.
