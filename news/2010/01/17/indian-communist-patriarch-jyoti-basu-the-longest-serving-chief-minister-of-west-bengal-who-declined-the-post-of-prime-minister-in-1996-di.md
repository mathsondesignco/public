### [2010-01-17](/news/2010/01/17/index.md)

# Indian communist patriarch Jyoti Basu, the longest-serving Chief Minister of West Bengal who declined the post of Prime Minister in 1996, dies at the age of 95. 

Jyoti Basu, the patriarchof Indian communism whose pragmatic politics twice brought himclose to becoming prime minister, died on Sunday. He was 95.
