### [2010-01-30](/news/2010/01/30/index.md)

# The leader of the Shia Houthi rebel group in northern Yemen, Abdul-Malik al-Houthi, says they will accept a ceasefire if government actions against them cease. 

Abdel Malik al-Houthi offers Yemeni government conditional truce for fourth time.
