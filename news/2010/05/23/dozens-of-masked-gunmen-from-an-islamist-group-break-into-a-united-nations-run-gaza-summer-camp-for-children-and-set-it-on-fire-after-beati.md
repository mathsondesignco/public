### [2010-05-23](/news/2010/05/23/index.md)

# Dozens of masked gunmen from an Islamist group break into a United Nations-run Gaza summer camp for children and set it on fire, after beating up the guard and destroying the plastic tents. 

UNRWA chief defiant after masked men ask the UN agency to stop organising such camps.
