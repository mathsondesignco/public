### [2010-05-13](/news/2010/05/13/index.md)

# As many as half a million pilgrims from 35 nations, including India, China and Vietnam, attend open-air Mass at Fatima, Portugal, celebrated by Pope Benedict XVI. The Pope criticises abortion and same-sex marriage in a speech. 

Pope Benedict XVI&#39;s outdoor mass at Fatima has taken place amid heavy rain downpours and low temperatures this afternoon.
