# The refusal of Israel to permit entry of internationally renowned American linguist and political activist Noam Chomsky to the West Bank on May 16, sparks intense debate in the country. 

Noam Chomsky, the linguist and icon of the American left, had been barred from entering the occupied West Bank on Sunday.
