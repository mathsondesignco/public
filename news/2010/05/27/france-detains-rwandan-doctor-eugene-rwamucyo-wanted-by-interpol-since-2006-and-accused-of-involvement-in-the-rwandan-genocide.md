### [2010-05-27](/news/2010/05/27/index.md)

# France detains Rwandan doctor Eugene Rwamucyo, wanted by Interpol since 2006 and accused of involvement in the Rwandan Genocide. 

French authorities arrest a Rwandan doctor accused over the 1994 genocide, a move welcomed in Rwanda.
