# Operation Herrick:: Colonel Bob Seddon resigns as principal ammunition technical officer of the Royal Logistic Corps citing concerns about "the pressures on his team operating in Afghanistan". 

Colonel Bob Seddon, Britain&rsquo;s senior bomb disposal officer, has resigned his post after raising concerns for the welfare of his men.
