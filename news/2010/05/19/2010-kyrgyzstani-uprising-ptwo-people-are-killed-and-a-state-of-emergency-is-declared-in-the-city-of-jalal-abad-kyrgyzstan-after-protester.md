# 2010 Kyrgyzstani uprising: Two people are killed and a state of emergency is declared in the city of Jalal-Abad, Kyrgyzstan after protesters attempted to storm an Uzbek university. 

The interim government of Kyrgyzstan declares a state of emergency in the southern city of Jalalabad after clashes killed two people.
