### [2010-05-30](/news/2010/05/30/index.md)

# A second Danish pharmaceutical company, Leo Pharma, suspends sales of medical products in Greece over the government's decision to reduce the cost of medicine there. The government condemns these acts as "unfair". 

A second Danish pharmaceutical company withdraws products from Greece over its decision to cut medicine prices by 25%.
