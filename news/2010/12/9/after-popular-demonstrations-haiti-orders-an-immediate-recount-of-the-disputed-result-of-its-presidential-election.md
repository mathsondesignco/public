### [2010-12-9](/news/2010/12/9/index.md)

# After popular demonstrations Haiti orders an immediate recount of the disputed result of its presidential election. 

Haiti says it will review the disputed result of its presidential election, after angry demonstrations by supporters of a losing candidate.
