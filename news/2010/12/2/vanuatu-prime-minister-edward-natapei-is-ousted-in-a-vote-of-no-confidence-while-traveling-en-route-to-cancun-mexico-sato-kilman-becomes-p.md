# Vanuatu Prime Minister Edward Natapei is ousted in a vote of no confidence while traveling en route to Cancun, Mexico. Sato Kilman becomes Prime Minister. 

The prime minister of Vanuatu, Edward Natapei, is ousted by a vote of no confidence in parliament while out of the country.
