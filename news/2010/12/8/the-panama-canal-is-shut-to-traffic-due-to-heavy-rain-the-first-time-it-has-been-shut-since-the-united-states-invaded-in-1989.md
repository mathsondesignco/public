### [2010-12-8](/news/2010/12/8/index.md)

# The Panama Canal is shut to traffic due to heavy rain, the first time it has been shut since the United States invaded in 1989. 

The Panama Canal is open again after heavy rains forced a temporary closure for the first time in more than 20 years.
