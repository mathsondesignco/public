### [2010-12-8](/news/2010/12/8/index.md)

# Around 1,000 protesters block a key road in Moscow, Russia, following the killing of a fellow football fan blamed on a group from the North Caucasus. 

Moscow football fans block a key city artery in protest at a killing which has put new pressure on Russian race relations.
