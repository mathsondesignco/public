### [2010-12-11](/news/2010/12/11/index.md)

# A crowd of 113,411, the largest in ice hockey history, packs Michigan Stadium for a game between the University of Michigan and cross-state rival Michigan State University. 

Michigan and Michigan State have set a world record for attendance at a hockey game, playing in front of 113,411 at Michigan Stadium.
