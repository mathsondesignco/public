### [2010-12-3](/news/2010/12/3/index.md)

# A Queensland man is missing presumed dead in floodwaters in Bajool while parts of central New South Wales are declared disaster zones after a week of heavy rains in eastern Australia. 

There are concerns flood waters have claimed the life of a man in Queensland, as parts of NSW are declared a disaster zone.
