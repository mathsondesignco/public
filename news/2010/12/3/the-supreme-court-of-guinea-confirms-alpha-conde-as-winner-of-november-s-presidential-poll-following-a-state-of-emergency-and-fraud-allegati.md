### [2010-12-3](/news/2010/12/3/index.md)

# The Supreme Court of Guinea confirms Alpha Cond as winner of November's presidential poll following a state of emergency and fraud allegations made by ex-president Cellou Dalein Diallo. 

Supreme Court confirms Alpha Conde as winner of November 7 presidential poll, rejecting complaints of election fraud.
