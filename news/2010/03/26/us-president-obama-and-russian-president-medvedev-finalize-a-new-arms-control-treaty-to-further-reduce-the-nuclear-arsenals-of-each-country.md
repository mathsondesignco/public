### [2010-03-26](/news/2010/03/26/index.md)

# US President Obama and Russian President Medvedev finalize a new arms control treaty to further reduce the nuclear arsenals of each country still remaining since the Cold War. 

Treaty to be signed on April 8 would cut both arsenals to 1,550 warheads.
