# Middle East:Israel announces the construction of 112 new Jewish homes for the occupied West Bank territory despite the freeze on new settlements initiated in November 2009. 

Israel has approved the construction of 112 new Jewish homes in the occupied West Bank, in spite of a settlement freeze announced last year.
