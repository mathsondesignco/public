# Catholic sex abuse cases:: Pope Benedict XVI signs his letter to Irish Catholics, sent as his first official documented response to the issue of child sex abuse and due to be published by the Vatican on Saturday and read at Sunday Mass. 

Pope Benedict XVI has today signed off on a pastoral letter to the Irish faithful addressing the question of clerical sex abuse&hellip;
