# Middle East:Complaints are raised against a Saudi Arabian writer for allegedly describing a hadith of the Prophet Muhammad as "barbaric" during a program presented by Nadeen Al-Badr on Al-Hurra Channel. 

Jeddah Summary Court sends copy of lawsuit filed against writer to justice minister.
