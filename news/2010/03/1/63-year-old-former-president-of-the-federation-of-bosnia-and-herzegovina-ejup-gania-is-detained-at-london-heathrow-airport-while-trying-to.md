# 63-year-old former President of the Federation of Bosnia and Herzegovina Ejup Ganic is detained at London Heathrow Airport while trying to leave the UK to escape charges of war crimes. 

A former president of Bosnia accused of war crimes was arrested today at Heathrow airport as he tried to leave the country.
