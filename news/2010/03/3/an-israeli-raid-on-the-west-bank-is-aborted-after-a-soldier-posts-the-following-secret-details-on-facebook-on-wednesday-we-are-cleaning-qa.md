### [2010-03-3](/news/2010/03/3/index.md)

# An Israeli raid on the West Bank is aborted after a soldier posts the following secret details on Facebook: "On Wednesday we are cleaning Qatanna, and on Thursday, God willing, going home". 

An Israeli gunner has posted details of an impending West Bank raid on his Facebook page, leading to the mission being aborted.
