# Seven people are arrested in Ireland &mdash; five in Waterford and two in Cork &mdash; over an alleged plot to assassinate Swedish artist Lars Vilks. 

Seven people have been arrested in the southeast by gardaí investigating a conspiracy to kill an individual abroad.
