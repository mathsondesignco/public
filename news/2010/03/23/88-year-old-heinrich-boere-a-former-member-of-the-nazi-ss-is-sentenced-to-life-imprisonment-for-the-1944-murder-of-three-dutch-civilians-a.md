# 88-year-old Heinrich Boere, a former member of the Nazi SS, is sentenced to life imprisonment for the 1944 murder of three Dutch civilians after six decades of legal wrangling. 

A German court has sentenced an 88-year-old former SS member to life in prison for the murder of three men in the Netherlands in 1944.
