### [2010-10-23](/news/2010/10/23/index.md)

# The opposition Social Democrats are awarded control of the Czech senate by the people in mid-term elections, giving them the chance to oppose right-wing government attempts to cut the wages of workers, stop them sending more troops to Afghanistan and make government reforms "socially more tolerable". 

The opposition Social Democrats win control of the Czech senate in mid-term elections, allowing them to slow government plans to cut spending.
