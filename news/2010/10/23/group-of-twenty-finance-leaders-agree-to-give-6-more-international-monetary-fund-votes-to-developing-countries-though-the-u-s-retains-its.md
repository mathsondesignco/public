### [2010-10-23](/news/2010/10/23/index.md)

# Group of Twenty finance leaders agree to give 6% more International Monetary Fund votes to developing countries, though the U.S. retains its veto power, in a meeting in Gyeongju, South Korea. 

Finance ministers from the G20 leading economies agree reforms of the International Monetary Fund, to give major developing nations more of a say.
