# United Nations envoy Margot Wallstrom says Congolese government troops may have committed rape and murder in the Democratic Republic of the Congo weeks after similar attacks by rebels. 

The UN says government troops in the Democratic Republic of Congo might have committed rapes and murders in an area recently attacked by rebels.
