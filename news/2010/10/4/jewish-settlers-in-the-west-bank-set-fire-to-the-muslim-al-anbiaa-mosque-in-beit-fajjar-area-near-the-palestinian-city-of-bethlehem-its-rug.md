### [2010-10-4](/news/2010/10/4/index.md)

# Jewish settlers in the West Bank set fire to the Muslim Al-Anbiaa mosque in Beit Fajjar area near the Palestinian city of Bethlehem. Its rug ground and some parts of the Qur'an are burnt and anti-Islamic and anti-Palestinian slogans are written. 

Emotions run high after mosque in occupied territory is set ablaze by settlers in early morning attack in Beit Fajjar.
