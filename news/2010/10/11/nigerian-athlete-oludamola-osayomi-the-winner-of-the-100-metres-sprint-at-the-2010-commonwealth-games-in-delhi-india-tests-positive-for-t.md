### [2010-10-11](/news/2010/10/11/index.md)

# Nigerian athlete Oludamola Osayomi, the winner of the 100 metres sprint at the 2010 Commonwealth Games in Delhi, India, tests positive for the banned stimulant Methylhexanamine. 

The sprinter awarded a gold medal after Australia&#039;s Sally Pearson was disqualified from the 100m final in Delhi has tested positive for a banned substance.
