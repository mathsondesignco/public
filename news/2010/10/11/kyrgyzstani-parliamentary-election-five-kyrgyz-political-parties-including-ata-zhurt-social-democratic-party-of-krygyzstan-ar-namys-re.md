### [2010-10-11](/news/2010/10/11/index.md)

# Kyrgyzstani parliamentary election:. Five Kyrgyz political parties, including Ata-Zhurt, Social Democratic Party of Krygyzstan, Ar-Namys, Respublika, and Ata Meken, have passed all thresholds at the election thus can obtain parliamentary seats.
