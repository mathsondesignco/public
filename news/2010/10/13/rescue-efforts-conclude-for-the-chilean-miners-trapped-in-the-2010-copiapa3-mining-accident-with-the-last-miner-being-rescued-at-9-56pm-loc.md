### [2010-10-13](/news/2010/10/13/index.md)

# Rescue efforts conclude for the Chilean miners trapped in the 2010 Copiapo mining accident, with the last miner being rescued at 9:56PM local time. 

The men, who remain 600 metres below the surface, will be released, one by one, over the next 36 hours.
