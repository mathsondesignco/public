# 2010 strikes in France:French riot police take over the Grandpuits Total S.A. oil refinery which had been blockaded by strikers for more than ten days. 

Clashes break out outside a major oil refinery in France after riot police move in to clear strikers who blockaded the terminal for 10 days.
