### [2010-10-25](/news/2010/10/25/index.md)

# Voters in Piran, Slovenia elect Ghanaian-born Peter Bossman as its mayor, the first time a person of African descent has been elected mayor in any Slovenian cities and towns. 

A Ghana-born doctor is elected mayor of a town in Slovenia - apparently breaking new ground for the former Yugoslavia.
