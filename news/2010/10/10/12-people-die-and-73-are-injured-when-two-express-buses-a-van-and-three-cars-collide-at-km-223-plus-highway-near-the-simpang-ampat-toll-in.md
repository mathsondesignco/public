### [2010-10-10](/news/2010/10/10/index.md)

# 12 people die and 73 are injured when two express buses, a van and three cars collide at Km 223, Plus Highway near the Simpang Ampat toll in Negri Sembilan, Malaysia. 

A collision involving two buses, a van and three cars left at least 11 people dead in Malaysia on Sunday, state media reported.
