# Sebastian Vettel of Red Bull Racing wins the 2010 Japanese Grand Prix with teammate Mark Webber finishing second. 

Red Bulls Sebastian Vettel won the Japanese Grand Prix on Sunday ahead of team-mate Mark Webber.
