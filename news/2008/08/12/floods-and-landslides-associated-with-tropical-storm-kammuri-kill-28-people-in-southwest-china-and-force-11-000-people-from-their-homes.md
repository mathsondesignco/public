### [2008-08-12](/news/2008/08/12/index.md)

#  Floods and landslides associated with Tropical Storm Kammuri kill 28 people in southwest China and force 11,000 people from their homes. 

Landslides and flash floods have killed at least 28 people in south-west China and 11,200 have been forced to leave their homes, state media reported.
