### [2008-08-12](/news/2008/08/12/index.md)

#  2008 South Ossetia war: President of Russia Dmitry Medvedev announced that the operation of Russian forces in South Ossetia is completed. He added that 'The aggressor has been punished, having sustained considerable losses. Its armed forces have been disorganised'. Medvedev also ordered the Russian Ministry of Defence to consider awarding the  peacekeepers and military personnel that have 'showed their best' during the operation.
