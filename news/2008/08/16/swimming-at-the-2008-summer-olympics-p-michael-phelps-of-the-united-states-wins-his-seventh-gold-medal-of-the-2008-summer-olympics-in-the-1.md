### [2008-08-16](/news/2008/08/16/index.md)

#  Swimming at the 2008 Summer Olympics: Michael Phelps of the United States wins his seventh gold medal of the 2008 Summer Olympics in the 100 metre butterfly, tying Mark Spitz's record for gold medals at an Olympic Games. 

Michael Phelps had a stunning comeback in the men’s 100-meter butterfly, out-touching Milorad Cavic of Serbia to tie Mark Spitz’s record.
