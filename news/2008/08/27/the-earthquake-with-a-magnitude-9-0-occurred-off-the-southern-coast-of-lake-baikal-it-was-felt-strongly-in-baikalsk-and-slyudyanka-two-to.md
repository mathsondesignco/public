### [2008-08-27](/news/2008/08/27/index.md)

#  The earthquake with a magnitude 9.0 occurred off the southern coast of Lake Baikal. It was felt strongly in Baikalsk and Slyudyanka, two towns in Slyudyansky District, Irkutsk Oblast, Russia.
