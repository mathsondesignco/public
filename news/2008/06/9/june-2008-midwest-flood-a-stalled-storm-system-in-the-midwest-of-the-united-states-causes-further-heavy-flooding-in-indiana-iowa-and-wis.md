#  June 2008 Midwest Flood: A stalled storm system  in the midwest of the United States causes further heavy flooding in Indiana, Iowa and Wisconsin with storms on the weekend causing 10 deaths in four states. 

A stalled storm system poured heavy rain on parts of the Midwest, setting off severe flooding in Indiana, Iowa and Wisconsin.
