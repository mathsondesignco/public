#  Cyclone Nargis: Myanmar's State Peace and Development Council declares that five states in the Irrawaddy River delta are a disaster area following the cyclone with at least 351 deaths. 

Myanmar&#39;s state-run television reported on Sunday that Category 3 Cyclone Nargis killed at least 351 people when it slammed into the the Irrawaddy delta and the former capital of Yangon.
