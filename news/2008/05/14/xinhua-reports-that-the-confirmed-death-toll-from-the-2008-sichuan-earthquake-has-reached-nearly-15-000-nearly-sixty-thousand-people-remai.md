### [2008-05-14](/news/2008/05/14/index.md)

#  Xinhua reports that the confirmed death toll from the 2008 Sichuan earthquake has reached nearly 15,000. Nearly sixty thousand people remain missing near the epicentre of the earthquake. 

Two thousand Chinese soldiers are working to plug an &#034;extremely dangerous&#034; cracks in a dam upriver from the earthquake-hit town of Dujiangyan.
