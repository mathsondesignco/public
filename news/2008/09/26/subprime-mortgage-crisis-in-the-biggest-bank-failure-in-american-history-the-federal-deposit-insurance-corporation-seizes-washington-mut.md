### [2008-09-26](/news/2008/09/26/index.md)

#  Subprime mortgage crisis:: In the biggest bank failure in American history, the Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation seizes Washington Mutual and sells most of the bank to JPMorgan Chase. 

Washington Mutual was seized by federal regulators in what is the largest bank failure in American history.
