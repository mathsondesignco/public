### [2008-03-26](/news/2008/03/26/index.md)

#  A Californian federal judge has ruled that the heirs of Jerry Siegel have a valid claim to a share in the United States copyright which Time Warner holds in the Superman character. 

The co-creator of Superman had sold his rights to the character 70 years ago for $130.
