### [2008-02-10](/news/2008/02/10/index.md)

#  Over five hundred people are evacuated from the North Sea oil rig "Safe Scandinavia", 282 km northeast of Aberdeen, Scotland after a hoax bomb scare. 

Personnel are understood to be returning to a North Sea oil rig after a security threat earlier today.
