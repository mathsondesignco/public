### [2008-02-5](/news/2008/02/5/index.md)

#  Philippine House Speaker Jose de Venecia was ousted by the majority of the representatives of the House of Representatives and Davao City Rep. Prospero Nograles was elected as the 13th House Speaker and the first Speaker from Mindanao.
