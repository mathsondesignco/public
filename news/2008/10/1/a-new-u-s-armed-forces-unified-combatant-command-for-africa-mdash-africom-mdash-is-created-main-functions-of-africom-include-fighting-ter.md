### [2008-10-1](/news/2008/10/1/index.md)

#  A new U.S. Armed Forces Unified Combatant Command for Africa&mdash;AFRICOM&mdash;is created. Main functions of AFRICOM include fighting terror, securing oil supplies in Africa, and supporting U.S. foreign policy in the region where Chinese influence is growing.
