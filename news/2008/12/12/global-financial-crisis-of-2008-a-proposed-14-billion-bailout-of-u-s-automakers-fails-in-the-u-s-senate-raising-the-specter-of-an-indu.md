### [2008-12-12](/news/2008/12/12/index.md)

#  Global financial crisis of 2008: A proposed $14 billion bailout of U.S. automakers fails in the U.S. Senate, raising the specter of an industry collapse that sent Asian markets reeling and sparked fears it could deepen the recession. 

The Bush administration said it would consider dipping into the $700 billion rescue fund to help automakers.
