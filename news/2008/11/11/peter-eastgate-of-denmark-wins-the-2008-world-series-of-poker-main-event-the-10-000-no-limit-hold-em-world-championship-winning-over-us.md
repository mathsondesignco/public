### [2008-11-11](/news/2008/11/11/index.md)

#  Peter Eastgate of Denmark wins the 2008 World Series of Poker main event, the $10,000 No-Limit Hold'em World Championship, winning over US$9.1 million. 

Peter Eastgate shined through the two-day final table and became the 2008 WSOP main event champion.
