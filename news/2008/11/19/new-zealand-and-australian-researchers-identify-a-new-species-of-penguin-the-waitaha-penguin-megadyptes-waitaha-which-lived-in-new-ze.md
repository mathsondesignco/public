### [2008-11-19](/news/2008/11/19/index.md)

#  New Zealand and Australian researchers identify a new species of penguin, the Waitaha penguin ("Megadyptes waitaha"), which lived in New Zealand until its extinction 500 years ago. 

Australian and New Zealand researchers have used ancient DNA to identify a previously unknown penguin species.
