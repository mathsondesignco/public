### [2006-02-9](/news/2006/02/9/index.md)

#  U.S. forces are searching for the {{USS|Cole|DDG-67|06}} attacker who escaped from prison last Friday. According to Interpol, an al-Qaida operative who had been sentenced to death for plotting the bombing of the USS ''Cole'' in 2000 escaped with a group of convicts from their prison last week in Sanaa, Yemen.
