### [2006-02-3](/news/2006/02/3/index.md)

#  Jamal al-Bedawi, who masterminded the USS "Cole" bombing, and Fawaz al-Rabeiee, who planned the 2002 attack on the French tanker "Limburg", escape from a prison in Yemen along with 22 other prisoners, 12 of whom were convicted members of Al-Qaida.
