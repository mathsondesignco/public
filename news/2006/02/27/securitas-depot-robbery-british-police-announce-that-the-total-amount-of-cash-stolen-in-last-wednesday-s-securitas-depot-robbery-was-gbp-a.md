### [2006-02-27](/news/2006/02/27/index.md)

#  Securitas depot robbery: British police announce that the total amount of cash stolen in last Wednesday's Securitas depot robbery was GBP GBP53 million (USD $92.6 million, EUR77.8 million). Five more people have been arrested in the last 24 hours and 10 properties searched in the ongoing investigation.
