### [2006-08-20](/news/2006/08/20/index.md)

#  Iran test fires the Saegheh missile, a short-range surface-to-surface missile with a range of between 80 and 250 kilometres in the desert near Kashan. It comes a day after the army commenced exercises. 

Iran on Sunday test-fired a surface-to-surface short-range missile a day after its army launched large-scale military exercises, state-run television reported.
