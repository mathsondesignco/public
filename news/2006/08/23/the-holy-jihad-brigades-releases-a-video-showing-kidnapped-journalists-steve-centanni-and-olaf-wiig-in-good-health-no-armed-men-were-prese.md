### [2006-08-23](/news/2006/08/23/index.md)

#  The Holy Jihad Brigades releases a video showing kidnapped journalists Steve Centanni and Olaf Wiig in good health. No armed men were present. The HJB demands that the United States release "Muslim prisoners" within 72 hours.
