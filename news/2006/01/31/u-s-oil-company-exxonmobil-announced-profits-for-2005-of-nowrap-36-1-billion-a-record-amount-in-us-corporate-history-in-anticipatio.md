### [2006-01-31](/news/2006/01/31/index.md)

#  U.S. oil company ExxonMobil announced profits for 2005 of {{Nowrap|$36.1 billion}}, a record amount in US corporate history. In anticipation of a public backlash, the company simultaneously posted newspaper advertisements in the US to explain its success.
