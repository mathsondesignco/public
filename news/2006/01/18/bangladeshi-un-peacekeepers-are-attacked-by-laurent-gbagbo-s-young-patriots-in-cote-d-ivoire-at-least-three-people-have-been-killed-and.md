### [2006-01-18](/news/2006/01/18/index.md)

#  Bangladeshi UN peacekeepers are attacked by Laurent Gbagbo's "Young Patriots" in Cte d'Ivoire. At least three people have been killed, and the UN has warned that the country is sliding towards war.
