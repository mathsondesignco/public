### [2006-01-15](/news/2006/01/15/index.md)

#  President Mwai Kibaki of Kenya has declared the ongoing drought a national disaster and has appealed for {{Nowrap|US$150 million}} to feed the hungry. {{Nowrap|2.5 million}} people have been left close to starvation due to the lack of rains over the last three years and corrupt officials who steal food aid.
