### [2006-06-18](/news/2006/06/18/index.md)

#  The people of Catalonia accept the proposed changes to the Statute of Autonomy of Catalonia, notably a change that would define Catalonia as a nation. 

Catalonia has won semi-autonomy from Madrid in a binding referendum Sunday.
