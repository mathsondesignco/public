### [2006-06-3](/news/2006/06/3/index.md)

#  2006 Toronto terrorism arrests: The Royal Canadian Mounted Police announce the arrest of 17 suspected Islamic terrorists in connection with a planned terrorist attack around Toronto.  The RCMP say that three tons of ammonium nitrate was seized.  In comparison, the 1995 Oklahoma City bombing used one ton.
