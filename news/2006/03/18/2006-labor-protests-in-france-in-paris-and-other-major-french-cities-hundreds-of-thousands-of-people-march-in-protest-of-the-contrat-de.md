### [2006-03-18](/news/2006/03/18/index.md)

#  2006 labor protests in France: In Paris, and other major French cities, hundreds of thousands of people march in protest of the Contrat de premire embauche (First Employment Contract), a labor law set to take effect in April that gives employers the right to fire workers under the age of 26 in the first two years of their employment without justification.
