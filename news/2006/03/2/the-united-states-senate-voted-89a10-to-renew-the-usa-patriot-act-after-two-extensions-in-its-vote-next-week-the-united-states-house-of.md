### [2006-03-2](/news/2006/03/2/index.md)

#  The United States Senate voted 89-10 to renew the USA PATRIOT Act after two extensions. In its vote next week, the United States House of Representatives will likely also vote to renew the Act, analysts say. 

With the Senate voting Thursday to renew the USA Patriot Act on Thursday, the measure moves to the House, which is expected to pass the legislation next week.
