### [2006-11-27](/news/2006/11/27/index.md)

#  Iraq War:. A U.S. F-16 fighter aircraft has gone down near the city of Fallujah in western Iraq. The United States Air Force said the plane's pilot was missing and an investigation into the cause of the incident has been launched.
