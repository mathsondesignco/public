### [2006-11-1](/news/2006/11/1/index.md)

#  The Revolutionary Armed Forces of Colombia (FARC) attack a rural police command in Colombia, killing at least 16 officers as part of a two-week offensive. President lvaro Uribe had earlier withdrawn from negotiations.
