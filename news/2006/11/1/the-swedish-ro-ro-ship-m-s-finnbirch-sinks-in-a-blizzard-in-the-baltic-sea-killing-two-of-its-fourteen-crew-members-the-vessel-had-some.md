### [2006-11-1](/news/2006/11/1/index.md)

#  The Swedish Ro-Ro ship M-S "Finnbirch" sinks in a blizzard in the Baltic Sea, killing two of its fourteen crew members. The vessel had some 260 tons of fuel and lubrication oil onboard which might present a hazard to the environment.
