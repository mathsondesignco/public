### [2006-04-4](/news/2006/04/4/index.md)

#  In the Republic of Ireland, Denis Donaldson a former senior Sinn Fin member, is found shot dead near the village of Glenties, County Donegal, close to where it is believed he had been living since he admitted, in December 2005, to being a paid British agent for over twenty years.
