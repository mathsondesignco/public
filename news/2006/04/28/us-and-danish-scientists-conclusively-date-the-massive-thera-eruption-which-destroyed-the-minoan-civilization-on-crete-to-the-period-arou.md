### [2006-04-28](/news/2006/04/28/index.md)

#  US and Danish scientists conclusively date the massive Thera eruption, which destroyed the Minoan civilization on Crete, to the period around c.1627-1600&nbsp;BC based on sophisticated radio-carbon analysis.

A single olive tree, buried alive during an ancient volcanic eruption, has scientists rethinking one of the earliest chapters of European civilization.
