### [2006-10-9](/news/2006/10/9/index.md)

#  2006 North Korean nuclear testing The Korean Central News Agency of North Korea reports that the nation has tested its first nuclear weapon in an underground facility at Gilju in North Hamgyong province. This report was at least somewhat confirmed by a seismic event that was measured around the world.
