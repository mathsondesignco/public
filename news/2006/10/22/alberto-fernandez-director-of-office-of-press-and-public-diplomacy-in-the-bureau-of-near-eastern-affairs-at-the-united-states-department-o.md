### [2006-10-22](/news/2006/10/22/index.md)

#  Alberto Fernandez, Director of Office of Press and Public Diplomacy in the Bureau of Near Eastern Affairs at the United States Department of State, apologises for saying the United States Government has shown "arrogance and stupidity" in Iraq. 

Alberto Fernandez apologized for saying that the U.S. had acted with “arrogance” and “stupidity” in its campaign in Iraq.
