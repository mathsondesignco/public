### [2006-09-3](/news/2006/09/3/index.md)

#  Kayaking world record: The Swedish adventurer Lasse Schmidt finishes the first solo crossing of the Baltic Sea in a kayak. The crossing between Sweden and Estonia took three days to complete without sleep.
