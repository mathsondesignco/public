### [2006-09-7](/news/2006/09/7/index.md)

# '''Belgian False Flag Terror arrests:''' Police in Belgium make arrests at army barracks and soldiers' homes as part of an investigation into suspected far-right terrorists (Bloed-Bodem-Eer en Trouw) operating inside the military. The soldiers were reportedly planning attacks aimed at destabilizing the country.
