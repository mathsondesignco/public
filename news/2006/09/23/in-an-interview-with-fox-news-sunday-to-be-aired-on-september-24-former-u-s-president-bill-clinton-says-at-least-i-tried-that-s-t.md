### [2006-09-23](/news/2006/09/23/index.md)

#  In an interview with ''Fox News Sunday'', to be aired on September 24, Former U.S. President Bill Clinton says, "At least I tried. That's the difference in me and some, including all of the right-wingers who are attacking me now," responding to those who criticize him for the September 11th terrorist attacks, "They had eight months to try, they did not try. I tried. So I tried and failed."
