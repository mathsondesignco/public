### [2006-09-16](/news/2006/09/16/index.md)

#  A Delta Air Lines flight makes an emergency landing at Shannon Airport in Ireland. The flight was en route to New York from Shannon itself, and had 276 passengers on board. It was reported that there was smoke in the cabin. 

A Delta Airlines aircraft with 276 passengers on board has made a precautionary emergency landing at Shannon Airport.
