### [2006-07-17](/news/2006/07/17/index.md)

#  A 7.7 M<sub>w</sub> earthquake off the coast of Indonesia causes a tsunami to crash into Java, causing significant property damage and killing over 100 people. Concern is raised over lack of a warning system for the south coast despite warnings from international agencies.
