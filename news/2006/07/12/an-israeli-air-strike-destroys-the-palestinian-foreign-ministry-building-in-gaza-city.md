### [2006-07-12](/news/2006/07/12/index.md)

#   An Israeli air strike destroys the Palestinian Foreign Ministry Building in Gaza City. 

Senior wanted Palestinian terror suspect Muhammad Deif, commander of Hamas&#39; military wing, injured in aerial strike on Gaza City building; his condition is unclear. Six Palestinians killed, including civilians. Senior Hamas member involved in kidnapping of Cpl. Gilad Shalit, seriously wounded. IDF enters central Gaza, divides Gaza Strip into two
