### [2007-12-11](/news/2007/12/11/index.md)

#  The United States Federal Reserve cuts federal funds rates and discount rates by a quarter of a percentage point to 4.25% and 4.75% respectively, signaling its concerns about the impact of the subprime mortgage financial crisis on the United States economy. 

In announcing the cut, the Federal Reserve signaled its concern that the credit crisis may be damaging the broader economy. The Dow fell nearly 300 points.
