### [2007-06-28](/news/2007/06/28/index.md)

#  Rivers in Gippsland, Victoria, Australia flood, leading to evacuations in Bairnsdale and Sale as a result of days of heavy rain. 

Emergency services in Gippsland in eastern Victoria are being swamped by calls for help as floodwaters continue to rise.
