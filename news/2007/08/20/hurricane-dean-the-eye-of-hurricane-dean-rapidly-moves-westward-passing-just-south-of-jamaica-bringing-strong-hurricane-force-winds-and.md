### [2007-08-20](/news/2007/08/20/index.md)

#  Hurricane Dean:: The eye of Hurricane Dean rapidly moves westward, passing just south of Jamaica, bringing strong hurricane-force winds and storm surges to bear down on the island nation, though the strongest wind is believed to have been offshore.
