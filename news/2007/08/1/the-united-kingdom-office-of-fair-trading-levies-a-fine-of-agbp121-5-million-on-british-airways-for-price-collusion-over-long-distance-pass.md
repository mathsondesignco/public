### [2007-08-1](/news/2007/08/1/index.md)

#  The United Kingdom Office of Fair Trading levies a fine of GBP121.5 million on British Airways for price collusion over long distance passenger fuel surcharges. British Airways and Korean Air later plead guilty to conspiracies to fix the price of passenger and cargo fees in the United States with fines of $300 million each being levied.
