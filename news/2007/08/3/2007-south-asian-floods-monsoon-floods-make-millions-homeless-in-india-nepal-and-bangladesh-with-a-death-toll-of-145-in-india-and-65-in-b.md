#  2007 South Asian floods: Monsoon floods make millions homeless in India, Nepal and Bangladesh with a death toll of 145 in India and 65 in Bangladesh. 

Monsoon floods have forced almost 20 million people out of their homes in northern India, Bangladesh and Nepal.
