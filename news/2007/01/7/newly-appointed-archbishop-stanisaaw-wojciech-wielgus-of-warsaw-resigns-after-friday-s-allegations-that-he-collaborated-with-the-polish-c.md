### [2007-01-7](/news/2007/01/7/index.md)

#  Newly appointed Archbishop Stanislaw Wojciech Wielgus, of Warsaw resigns after Friday's allegations that he collaborated with the Polish communist government's secret police. He was due to be officially appointed by Pope Benedict XVI at 10:00 GMT, and announced his decision only one hour before.
