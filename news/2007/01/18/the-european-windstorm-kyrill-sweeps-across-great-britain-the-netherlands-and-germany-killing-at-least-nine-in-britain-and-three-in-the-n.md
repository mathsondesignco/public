### [2007-01-18](/news/2007/01/18/index.md)

#  The European windstorm Kyrill sweeps across Great Britain, the Netherlands and Germany, killing at least nine in Britain and three in the Netherlands. The container ship "MSC Napoli" has to be abandoned in the English channel because of the wind. British and French rescue services pick up 26 crew members.
