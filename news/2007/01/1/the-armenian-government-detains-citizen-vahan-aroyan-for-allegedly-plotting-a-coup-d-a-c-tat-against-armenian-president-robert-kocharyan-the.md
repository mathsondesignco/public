### [2007-01-1](/news/2007/01/1/index.md)

#  The Armenian government detains citizen Vahan Aroyan for allegedly plotting a coup d'etat against Armenian President Robert Kocharyan. The Association of Armenian Volunteers, a political opposition group, denounces Aroyan's detainment, saying the move is an attempt to silence dissidence.
