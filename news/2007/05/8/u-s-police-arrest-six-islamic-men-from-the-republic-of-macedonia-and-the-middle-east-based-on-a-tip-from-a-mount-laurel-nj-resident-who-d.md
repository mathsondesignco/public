### [2007-05-8](/news/2007/05/8/index.md)

#  U.S. police arrest six Islamic men from the Republic of Macedonia and the Middle East based on a tip from a Mount Laurel, NJ resident who discovered their plot to attack Fort Dix, New Jersey, and "kill as many soldiers as possible." 

Six people have been arrested on allegations of plotting to kill soldiers at an army installation, an official said today.
