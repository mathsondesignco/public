### [2007-07-7](/news/2007/07/7/index.md)

#  The New Seven Wonders of the World are announced. These are The Great Wall of China, Petra in Jordan, the Christ the Redeemer statue in Brazil, Machu Picchu in Peru, Mexico's Chichen Itza Mayan site, the Colosseum in Rome and the Taj Mahal in India. 

Sydney&#039;s Opera House failed to make the cut as the organisers of a huge online poll unveiled their new Seven Wonders of the World in the Portuguese capital Lisbon this morning.
