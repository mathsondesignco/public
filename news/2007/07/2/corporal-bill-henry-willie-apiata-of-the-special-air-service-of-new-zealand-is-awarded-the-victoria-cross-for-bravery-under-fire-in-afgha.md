### [2007-07-2](/news/2007/07/2/index.md)

#  Corporal Bill Henry "Willie" Apiata of the Special Air Service of New Zealand is awarded the Victoria Cross for bravery under fire in Afghanistan in 2004. 

A New Zealand soldier has been awarded the Victoria Cross for the first time since World War II.
