#  Gordon Brown, the Prime Minister of the United Kingdom, holds an Emergency Cabinet meeting to discuss floods with the British Army called in to help in the worst affected areas of England and Wales. 

British Prime Minister Gordon Brown has held an emergency Cabinet meeting as severe floods continue to affect large areas of central England and Wales.
