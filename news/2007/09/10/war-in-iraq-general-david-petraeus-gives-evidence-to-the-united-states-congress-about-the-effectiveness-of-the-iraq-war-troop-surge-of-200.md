#  War in Iraq: General David Petraeus gives evidence to the United States Congress about the effectiveness of the Iraq War troop surge of 2007. General Petraeus warned against a rapid pullback of troops. 

Gen. David H. Petraeus has recommended deferring decisions on reducing the main body of troops in Iraq.
