### [2007-09-30](/news/2007/09/30/index.md)

#  The Topps Meat Company recalls 21700000 lb of frozen beef patties because of potential contamination with "E. coli". Twenty-five cases of illness due to "E. coli" have been reported in the Northeastern United States. 

The Topps Meat Company, based in Elizabeth, N.J., said it was recalling 21.7 million pounds of ground beef products, which may have sickened 25 people in eight states.
