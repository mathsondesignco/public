### [2007-09-3](/news/2007/09/3/index.md)

#   Hurricane Felix reaches Category 5 strength as it heads towards  Honduras issues a hurricane warning for an area from Limon on the north coast to the border with Nicaragua. The Bay Islands are placed on red alert, and the departments of Gracias a Dios, Colon, Atlantida, Olancho, Cortes and Yoro are placed on yellow alert.
