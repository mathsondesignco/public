### [2007-09-6](/news/2007/09/6/index.md)

#  APEC: The President of the People's Republic of China Hu Jintao and Prime Minister of Australia John Howard agree to six deals including an agreement whereby the PRC will purchase A$35 billion worth of liquefied natural gas from Woodside Petroleum. 

Australia and China have signed a series of new agreements for closer ties between the two nations, including a $35 billion deal with Woodside to buy liquefied natural gas (LNG).
