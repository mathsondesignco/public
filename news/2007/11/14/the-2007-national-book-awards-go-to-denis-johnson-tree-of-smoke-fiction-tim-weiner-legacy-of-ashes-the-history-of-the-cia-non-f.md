### [2007-11-14](/news/2007/11/14/index.md)

#  The 2007 National Book Awards go to Denis Johnson ("Tree of Smoke"), fiction, Tim Weiner ("Legacy of Ashes: The History of the CIA"), non-fiction, Sherman Alexie ("The Absolutely True Diary of a Part-Time Indian"), young people's literature, and Robert Hass ("Time and Materials"), poetry.
