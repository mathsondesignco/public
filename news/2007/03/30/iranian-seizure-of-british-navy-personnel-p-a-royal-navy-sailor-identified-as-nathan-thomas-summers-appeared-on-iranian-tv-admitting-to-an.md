### [2007-03-30](/news/2007/03/30/index.md)

#  Iranian seizure of British Navy personnel: A Royal Navy sailor identified as Nathan Thomas Summers appeared on Iranian TV, admitting to and apologizing for entering Iranian territory and his government's wartime actions in general. Tony Blair dismisses the statement as forced.
