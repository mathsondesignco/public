### [2007-03-8](/news/2007/03/8/index.md)

#  Georgia increases the size of its contingent in the multinational force in Iraq from 850 to 2000. 

Georgian President Mikhail Saakashvili said the former Soviet republic will raise the level of troops serving in Iraq from 850 to more than 2,000 for one year.
