### [2007-04-30](/news/2007/04/30/index.md)

#  The United States Supreme Court decides the case of "Scott v. Harris," holding that a "police officer's attempt to terminate a dangerous high-speed car chase that threatens the lives of innocent bystanders does not violate the Fourth Amendment, even when it places the fleeing motorist at risk of serious injury or death."
