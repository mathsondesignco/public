# Beverly Hills police confirm Whitney Houston was underwater and unconscious when found in a bath in her Los Angeles hotel room last Saturday. 

Whitney Houston was unconscious and underwater in a bath when found on Saturday morning, police say, as her body is flown to New Jersey.
