### [2012-02-17](/news/2012/02/17/index.md)

# Security forces in Sudan raid student dormitories at the University of Khartoum, arresting and beating students after they demanded the right to form a union. 

Raid at Khartoum university leads to arrest of 350 students who previously staged sit-in demanding right to form union.
