### [2012-02-17](/news/2012/02/17/index.md)

# Archaeological Museum of Olympia robbery:: Thieves break into the Archaeological Museum of Olympia in Greece. stealing between 60 and 70 artifacts of the ancient Olympic Games. 

Armed robbers steal dozens of priceless artefacts from a Greek museum in Olympia dedicated to the history of the ancient Olympic Games.
