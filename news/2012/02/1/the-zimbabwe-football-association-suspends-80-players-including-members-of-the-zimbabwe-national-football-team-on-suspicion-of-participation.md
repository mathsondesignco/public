# The Zimbabwe Football Association suspends 80 players including members of the Zimbabwe national football team on suspicion of participation in match-fixing. 

The Zimbabwe Football Association (ZIFA) has suspended 80 players, including the core of the national team, on suspicion of match-fixing.
