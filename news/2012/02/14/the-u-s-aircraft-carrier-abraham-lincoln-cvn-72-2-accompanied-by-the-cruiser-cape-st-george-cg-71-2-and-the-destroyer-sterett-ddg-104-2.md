### [2012-02-14](/news/2012/02/14/index.md)

# The U.S. aircraft carrier Abraham Lincoln|CVN-72|2, accompanied by the cruiser Cape St. George|CG-71|2 and the destroyer Sterett|DDG-104|2, sails through the Strait of Hormuz, close to the coast of Iran, for the second time in recent weeks. 

A US aircraft carrier passes through the Strait of Hormuz for the second time in recent weeks, amid heightened tension with Iran.
