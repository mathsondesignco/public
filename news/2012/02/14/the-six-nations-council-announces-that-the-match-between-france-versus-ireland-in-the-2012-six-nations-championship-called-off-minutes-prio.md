### [2012-02-14](/news/2012/02/14/index.md)

# The Six Nations Council announces that the match between France versus Ireland in the 2012 Six Nations Championship, called off minutes prior to kick-off on Saturday night, has been rescheduled for Sunday 4 March, with a 3.00pm kick-off, Irish time. 

The Six Nations game between France and Ireland will now be played on Sunday, 4 March in Paris
