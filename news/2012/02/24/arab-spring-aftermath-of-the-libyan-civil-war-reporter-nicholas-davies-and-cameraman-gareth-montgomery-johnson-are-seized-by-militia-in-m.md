### [2012-02-24](/news/2012/02/24/index.md)

# Arab Spring:. Aftermath of the Libyan civil war: Reporter Nicholas Davies and cameraman Gareth Montgomery-Johnson are seized by militia in Misrata while working for the English language Press TV news network. Two local residents are also seized. 

World and Arab powers are urging Syria to immediately cease all violence but have not endorsed a military intervention.
