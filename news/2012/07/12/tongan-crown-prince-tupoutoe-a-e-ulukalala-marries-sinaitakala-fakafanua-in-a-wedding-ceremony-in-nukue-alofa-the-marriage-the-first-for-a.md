### [2012-07-12](/news/2012/07/12/index.md)

# Tongan Crown Prince Tupouto`a `Ulukalala marries Sinaitakala Fakafanua in a wedding ceremony in Nuku`alofa. The marriage, the first for a Crown Prince of Tonga in 65 years, sparks controversy as the couple are second cousins. 

Tongans have celebrated the royal wedding of the Crown Prince to his second-cousin but the marriage has raised concerns about the practice of marriages between close relatives.
