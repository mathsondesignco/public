# Syrian uprising (2011-present):Major General Hisham Ikhtiyar, the head of Syrian national security, dies of wounds he received from a bombing in the capital Damascus on July 18. 

A fourth top Syrian official dies from injuries incurred in a bombing on Wednesday, as the army ousts rebels from a Damascus neighbourhood.
