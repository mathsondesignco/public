### [2012-07-18](/news/2012/07/18/index.md)

# Kim Jong-un is officially announced as the Supreme Leader of North Korea, a position that he occupied "de facto" since his father's death. He also gains the rank of Marshal in the Korean People's Army. 

NORTH Korea has made &quot;an important announcement&quot; - Kim Jong-un is the Supreme Leader.
