### [2012-07-6](/news/2012/07/6/index.md)

# Andy Murray makes it to the final of the 2012 Wimbledon Championships - Men's Singles, becoming the first Briton to do so in 74 years. 

Andy Murray beats Jo-Wilfried Tsonga in four sets to become the first British man in 74 years to reach the Wimbledon final.
