### [2012-07-4](/news/2012/07/4/index.md)

# A gunman, who was facing eviction from his apartment, is believed to have seized and killed four hostages in the German city of Karlsruhe before turning the gun on himself. 

A man shot dead four people including his partner before killing himself in a siege in the German city of Karlsruhe, police say.
