### [2012-07-4](/news/2012/07/4/index.md)

# The Scottish Premier League refuses to admit Newco Rangers, the team that emerged from the ashes of Rangers F.C. following its disintegration. 

Ten out of 12 chairmen vote to reject the application from Rangers newco to join the Scottish Premier League.
