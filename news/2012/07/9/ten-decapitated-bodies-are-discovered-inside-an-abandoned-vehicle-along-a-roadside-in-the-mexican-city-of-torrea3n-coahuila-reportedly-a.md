### [2012-07-9](/news/2012/07/9/index.md)

# Ten decapitated bodies are discovered inside an abandoned vehicle along a roadside in the Mexican city of Torreon, Coahuila. Reportedly, a written message was left behind by the perpetrators. 

Police in northern Mexico find 10 decapitated bodies in the city of Torreon, believed to be the victims of a turf war between drug gangs.
