# Blind Chinese human rights activist Chen Guangcheng &ndash; who fled from house arrest &ndash; reportedly takes refuge in the U.S. embassy in Beijing. 

Supporters of blind legal advocate Chen Guangcheng believe he is in the US embassy after escape from home imprisonment.
