# A British government pathologist suggests Secret Intelligence Service officer Gareth Williams "possibly died" from poisoning or asphyxiation. 

MI6 officer Gareth Williams possibly died from poisoning or asphyxiation, a Home Office pathologist tells an inquest into the death.
