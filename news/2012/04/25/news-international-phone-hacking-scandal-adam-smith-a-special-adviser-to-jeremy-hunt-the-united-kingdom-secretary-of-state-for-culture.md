### [2012-04-25](/news/2012/04/25/index.md)

# News International phone hacking scandal:: Adam Smith, a special adviser to Jeremy Hunt, the United Kingdom Secretary of State for Culture, Olympics, Media and Sport, resigns over his role in Rupert Murdoch's News Corporation's attempt to take over satellite broadcaster BSkyB. 

Ex-Prime Minister Gordon Brown denies he &quot;declared war&quot; on News Corporation after Rupert Murdoch made the claim in his evidence to the Leveson Inquiry.
