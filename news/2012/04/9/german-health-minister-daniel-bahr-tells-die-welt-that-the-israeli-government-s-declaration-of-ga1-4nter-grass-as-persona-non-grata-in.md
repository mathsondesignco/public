### [2012-04-9](/news/2012/04/9/index.md)

# German Health Minister Daniel Bahr tells ''Die Welt'' that the Israeli government's declaration of Gunter Grass as ''persona non grata'' in reaction to his poem "What Must Be Said" is "exaggerated". Meanwhile, a sculpture celebrating freedom of speech in Gottingen is daubed in red paint telling the recipient of the 1999 Nobel Prize in Literature to "shut your mouth".
