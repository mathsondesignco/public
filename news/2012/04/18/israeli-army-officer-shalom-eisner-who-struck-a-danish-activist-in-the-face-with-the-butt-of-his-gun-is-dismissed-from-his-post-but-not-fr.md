### [2012-04-18](/news/2012/04/18/index.md)

# Israeli army officer Shalom Eisner, who struck a Danish activist in the face with the butt of his gun, is dismissed from his post but not from the Israeli Defense Forces. 

Israel dismisses from his post a soldier who hit a pro-Palestinian activist in the face, after footage of the incident was published online.
