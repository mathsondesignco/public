### [2012-04-10](/news/2012/04/10/index.md)

# War in Afghanistan (2001-present):. A suicide bomber attacks a government office building in Afghanistan's Herat Province with 15 people killed and 22 wounded. 

Taliban claim responsibility for suicide attacks targeting government buildings in Herat and Helmand provinces.
