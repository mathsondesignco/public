### [2012-04-7](/news/2012/04/7/index.md)

# The 158th Boat Race between Oxford and Cambridge is stopped mid-race due to a swimmer in the water. After a clash of oars at the restart, Cambridge won easily. 

A man is charged over an incident which led to the 158th University Boat Race being halted half-way through.
