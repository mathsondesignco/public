# Arab Spring:: Bahrain Centre for Human Rights's president Nabeel Rajab is arrested by representatives of Bahrain's Interior Ministry on arrival at Manama airport from Lebanon ahead of his scheduled court appearance. 

Nabeel Rajab has been arrested on the eve of his court appearance.
