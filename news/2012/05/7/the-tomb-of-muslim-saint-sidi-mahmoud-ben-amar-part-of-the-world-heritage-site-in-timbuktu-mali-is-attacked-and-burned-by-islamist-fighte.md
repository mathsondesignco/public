# The tomb of Muslim saint Sidi Mahmoud Ben Amar, part of the World Heritage Site in Timbuktu, Mali, is attacked and burned by Islamist fighters. 

Tomb of revered Muslim in ancient town attacked and burned by Islamist fighters said to be linked to al-Qaeda.
