### [2012-05-12](/news/2012/05/12/index.md)

# Nigerian police claim to have captured a senior commander of the radical Islamist militant group Boko Haram. 

Nigerian police say they have arrested a senior commander of the Islamist militant group Boko Haram in the troubled northern city of Kano.
