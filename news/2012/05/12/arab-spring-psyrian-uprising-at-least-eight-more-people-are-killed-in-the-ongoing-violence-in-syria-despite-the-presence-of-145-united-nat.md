# Arab Spring:Syrian uprising: At least eight more people are killed in the ongoing violence in Syria, despite the presence of 145 United Nations ceasefire monitors. 

Several people reported dead in latest clashes even as number of UN monitors charged with overseeing truce reaches 145.
