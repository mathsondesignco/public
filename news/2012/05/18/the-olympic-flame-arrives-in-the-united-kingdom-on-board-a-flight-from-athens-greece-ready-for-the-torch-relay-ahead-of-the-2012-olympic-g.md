# The Olympic Flame arrives in the United Kingdom on board a flight from Athens, Greece, ready for the torch relay ahead of the 2012 Olympic Games in July. 

David Beckham lights a cauldron in Cornwall after the flame touches down, ready for the start of the London 2012 torch relay.
