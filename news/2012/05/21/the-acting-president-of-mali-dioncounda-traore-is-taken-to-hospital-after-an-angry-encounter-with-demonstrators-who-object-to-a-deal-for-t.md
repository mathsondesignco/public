### [2012-05-21](/news/2012/05/21/index.md)

# The Acting President of Mali, Dioncounda Traor, is taken to hospital after an angry encounter with demonstrators who object to a deal for the 70-year-old to remain in office for a year. 

Interim Mali President Dioncounda Traore is briefly hospitalised with a head wound after being attacked in his office by demonstrators, officials say.
