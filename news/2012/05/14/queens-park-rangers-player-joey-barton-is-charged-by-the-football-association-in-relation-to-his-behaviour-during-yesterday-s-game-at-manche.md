### [2012-05-14](/news/2012/05/14/index.md)

# Queens Park Rangers player Joey Barton is charged by the Football Association in relation to his behaviour during yesterday's game at Manchester City, in which he assaulted several opposing players. 

QPR midfielder Joey Barton accepts one Football Association charge of violent conduct, but denies a second.
