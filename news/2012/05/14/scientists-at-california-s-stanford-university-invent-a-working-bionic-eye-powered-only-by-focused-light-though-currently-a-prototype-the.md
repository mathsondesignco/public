### [2012-05-14](/news/2012/05/14/index.md)

# Scientists at California's Stanford University invent a working bionic eye powered only by focused light. Though currently a prototype, the device could eventually restore the sight of millions of people suffering from eye diseases such as macular degeneration and retinal pigmentosa. 

A retinal implant - or bionic eye - which is powered by light has been invented by scientists at Stanford University in California.
