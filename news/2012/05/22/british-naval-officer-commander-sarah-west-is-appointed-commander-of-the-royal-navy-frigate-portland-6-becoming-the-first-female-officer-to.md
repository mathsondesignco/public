### [2012-05-22](/news/2012/05/22/index.md)

# British naval officer Commander Sarah West is appointed commander of the Royal Navy frigate Portland 6, becoming the first female officer to take command of a major British warship. 

The first female commander of a major Royal Navy (RN) warship is taking up her post today.
