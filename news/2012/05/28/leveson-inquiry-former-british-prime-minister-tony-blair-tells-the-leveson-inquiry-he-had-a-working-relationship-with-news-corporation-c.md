# Leveson Inquiry:: Former British Prime Minister Tony Blair tells the Leveson Inquiry he had a "working relationship" with News Corporation chairman Rupert Murdoch, but that Murdoch did not influence policy during his time in office. 

Ex-Prime Minister Tony Blair tells the Leveson Inquiry his friendship with Rupert Murdoch was &quot;a working relationship&quot; until he left office.
