### [2012-05-15](/news/2012/05/15/index.md)

# Thousands of Palestinians demonstrate on the 64th anniversary of their "Nakba" (en|"Catastrophe"), in remembrance of their exodus from the former Mandate of Palestine after Israel's declaration of independence in 1948. Demonstrators and Israeli policemen are lightly injured in several clashes. 

Rallies and demonstrations in West Bank, Gaza and Israel as Palestinians commemorate displacement from lands in 1948.
