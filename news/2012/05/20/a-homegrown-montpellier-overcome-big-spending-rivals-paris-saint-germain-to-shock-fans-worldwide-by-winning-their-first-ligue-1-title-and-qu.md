### [2012-05-20](/news/2012/05/20/index.md)

# A homegrown Montpellier overcome big-spending rivals Paris Saint-Germain to shock fans worldwide by winning their first Ligue 1 title and qualifying for the UEFA Champions League for the first time in their history. 

Minnows Montpellier focus on keeping home-grown stars after outlasting big-spending rivals PSG to the Championnat crown.
