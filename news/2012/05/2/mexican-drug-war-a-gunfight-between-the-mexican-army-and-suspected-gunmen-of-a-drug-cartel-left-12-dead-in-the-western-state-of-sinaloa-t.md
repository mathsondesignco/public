### [2012-05-2](/news/2012/05/2/index.md)

# Mexican Drug War:. A gunfight between the Mexican Army and suspected gunmen of a drug cartel left 12 dead in the western state of Sinaloa, the homestate of Joaquin Guzman Loera, Mexico's most-wanted drug lord. 

At least 12 people - including two soldiers - are killed in a gunfight between the Mexican army and suspected drug gang members in Sinaloa state.
