### [2012-05-2](/news/2012/05/2/index.md)

# Arab Spring:: At least 20 people are killed in Cairo as unidentified attackers unleash rocks, clubs, firebombs and shotguns on a protest near the Egyptian Ministry of Defence. Police and the military intervene after six hours. 

At least 20 people are killed in Cairo amid an attack on people protesting over the barring of a candidate from presidential polls.
