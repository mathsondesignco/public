### [2012-08-15](/news/2012/08/15/index.md)

# Mexican Drug War:Mexican authorities confirm that the Jalisco New Generation Cartel is responsible for murdering five journalists in the state of Veracruz. This information was confirmed after the arrest of some of the members in the criminal organization.
