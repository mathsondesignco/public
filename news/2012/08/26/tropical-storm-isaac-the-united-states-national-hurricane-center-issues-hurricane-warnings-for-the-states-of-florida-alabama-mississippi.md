### [2012-08-26](/news/2012/08/26/index.md)

# Tropical Storm Isaac:. The United States National Hurricane Center issues hurricane warnings for the states of Florida, Alabama, Mississippi and Louisiana. A state of emergency is in effect in these four areas. 

Forecasters say Tropical Storm Isaac has gained added strength and should be at or near hurricane force when it reaches the Florida Keys later in the day.
