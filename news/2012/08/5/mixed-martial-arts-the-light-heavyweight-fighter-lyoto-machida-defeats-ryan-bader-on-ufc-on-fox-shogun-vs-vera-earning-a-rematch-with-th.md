# Mixed martial arts: The light-heavyweight fighter Lyoto Machida defeats Ryan Bader on UFC on Fox: Shogun vs. Vera, earning a rematch with the current champion in the division, Jon Jones. 

A four-man shootout for the next UFC light-heavyweight title shot ensued on Saturday night, and at the end of it Lyoto Machida was the man who shone brightly above the others
