### [2012-08-13](/news/2012/08/13/index.md)

# Iran announces that the death toll from the 2012 Ahar-Varzaqan earthquakes is 306 with 3,037 injured. 

The number of people killed in twin earthquakes in Iran at the weekend has risen to 306, following the deaths of more victims in hospital.
