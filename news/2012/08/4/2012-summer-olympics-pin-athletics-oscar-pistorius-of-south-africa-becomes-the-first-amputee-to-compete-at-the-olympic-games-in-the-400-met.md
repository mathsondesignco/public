### [2012-08-4](/news/2012/08/4/index.md)

# 2012 Summer Olympics:In athletics, Oscar Pistorius of South Africa becomes the first amputee to compete at the Olympic Games in the 400 meters. 

Oscar Pistorius has made history by becoming the first double amputee to compete at the Olympic Games, crossing the line second in the men&#039;s 400 metres heat.
