# The Italian Olympic Committee requests a four-year doping ban for former race walk Olympic champion Alex Schwazer. He was excluded from the 2012 London Olympics after testing positive for EPO before the games. 

The Italian Olympic Committee has requested a four-year doping ban for former Olympic race walk champion Alex Schwazer.
