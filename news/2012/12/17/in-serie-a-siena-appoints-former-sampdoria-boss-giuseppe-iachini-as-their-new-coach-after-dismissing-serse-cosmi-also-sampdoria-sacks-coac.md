### [2012-12-17](/news/2012/12/17/index.md)

# In Serie A, Siena appoints former Sampdoria boss Giuseppe Iachini as their new coach after dismissing Serse Cosmi. Also Sampdoria sacks coach Ciro Ferrara after five months in charge on and replaces him with former Lazio and Palermo boss Delio Rossi. 

UC Sampdoria have replaced coach Ciro Ferrara with Delio Rossi following their 3-1 defeat at Calcio Catania on Sunday, while Serse Cosmi has left bottom club AC Siena.
