# 2012 Central African Republic rebellion:Chad sends troops to the Central African Republic to help defend the town of Bria from the Seleka rebel coalition. 

Chad troops enter the Central African Republic (CAR) to help fight rebels who have seized several towns and threaten to overthrow the government.
