### [2012-12-19](/news/2012/12/19/index.md)

# The High Court of England and Wales quashes the original inquest verdicts returned on 96 Liverpool football fans who died as a result of the 1989 Hillsborough disaster. 

Hillsborough campaigners welcomed the news of a new inquest into the deaths of 96 Liverpool fans, saying: “Justice is on its way.”
