### [2012-12-8](/news/2012/12/8/index.md)

# The President of Afghanistan Hamid Karzai claims that a suicide bombing attack that injured his security chief was planned in the Pakistani city of Quetta. 

Afghan President Hamid Karzai saidon Saturday that a suicide bombing attack that wounded hisintelligence chief was planned in the Pakistani city of Quetta.
