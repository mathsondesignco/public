### [2012-12-9](/news/2012/12/9/index.md)

# Violent clashes occur in Bangladesh as protesters stage a nationwide blockade of roads to press for an independent body to oversee the next general election. At least two people were killed and about 100 injured after police fire rubber bullets and tear gas. 

Violent clashes erupt in Bangladesh as protesters stage a nationwide blockade of roads to demand an independent body to oversee elections.
