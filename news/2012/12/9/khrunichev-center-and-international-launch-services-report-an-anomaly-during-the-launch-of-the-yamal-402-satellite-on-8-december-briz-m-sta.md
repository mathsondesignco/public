### [2012-12-9](/news/2012/12/9/index.md)

# Khrunichev Center and International Launch Services report an anomaly during the launch of the Yamal-402 satellite on 8 December. Briz-M stage failure 4 minutes before scheduled shut down on its fourth burn.
