### [2012-12-30](/news/2012/12/30/index.md)

# The Egyptian Football Association says the launch 2012-13 Egyptian Premier League is postponed for a third time, this time until 1 February. The first round of matches will be played behind closed doors following the Port Said Stadium disaster.
