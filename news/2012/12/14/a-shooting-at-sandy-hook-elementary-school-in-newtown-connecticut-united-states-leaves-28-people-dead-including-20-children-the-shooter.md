# A shooting at Sandy Hook Elementary School in Newtown, Connecticut, United States, leaves 28 people dead, including 20 children, the shooter, and his mother. 

A gunman kills 20 children and six adults at a primary school in Connecticut - in one of the deadliest mass shootings in recent US history.
