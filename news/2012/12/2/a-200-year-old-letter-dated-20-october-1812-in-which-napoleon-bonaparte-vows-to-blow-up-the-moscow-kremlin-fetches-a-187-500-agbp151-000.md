# A 200-year-old letter dated 20 October 1812 in which Napoleon Bonaparte vows to blow up the Moscow Kremlin fetches EUR187,500 (GBP151,000, $233,800) at a French auction in Fontainebleau. 

A coded letter written by Napoleon containing his plan to defeat the Russians sells for more than 10 times the estimated price.
