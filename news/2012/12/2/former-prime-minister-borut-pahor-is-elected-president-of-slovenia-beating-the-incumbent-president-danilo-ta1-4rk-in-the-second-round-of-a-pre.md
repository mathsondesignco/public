### [2012-12-2](/news/2012/12/2/index.md)

# Former Prime Minister Borut Pahor is elected President of Slovenia beating the incumbent President Danilo Turk in the second round of a presidential election after anti-austerity protests in the capital, Ljubljana, erupted in clashes that left 15 people injured.
