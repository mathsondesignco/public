### [2012-12-16](/news/2012/12/16/index.md)

# War in North-West Pakistan:: Taliban militants launch a coordinated raid on Bacha Khan International Airport in Peshawar, Pakistan, killing at least four civilians and injuring more than 40 others. 

The Taliban launch an attack on Peshawar airport in north-west Pakistan, killing at least four civilians and injuring 30, officials say.
