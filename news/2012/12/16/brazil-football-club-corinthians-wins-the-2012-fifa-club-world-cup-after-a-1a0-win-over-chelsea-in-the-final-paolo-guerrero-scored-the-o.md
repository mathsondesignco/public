### [2012-12-16](/news/2012/12/16/index.md)

# Brazil football club Corinthians wins the 2012 FIFA Club World Cup, after a 1-0 win over Chelsea in the final. Paolo Guerrero scored the only goal of the game in Yokohama, Japan. Brazilian player Cassio Ramos is named as MVP of the tournament.
