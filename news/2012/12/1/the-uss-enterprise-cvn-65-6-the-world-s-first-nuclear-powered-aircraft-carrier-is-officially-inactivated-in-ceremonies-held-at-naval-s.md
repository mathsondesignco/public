### [2012-12-1](/news/2012/12/1/index.md)

# The {{USS|Enterprise|CVN-65|6}}, the world's first nuclear-powered aircraft carrier, is officially inactivated in ceremonies held at Naval Station Norfolk, Virginia, completing a 51-year career in the United States Navy. In a pre-recorded speech, U.S. Secretary of the Navy Ray Mabus announces that the U.S. Navy's third {{sclass|Gerald R. Ford|aircraft carrier}}, CVN-80, will be named ''Enterprise''.
