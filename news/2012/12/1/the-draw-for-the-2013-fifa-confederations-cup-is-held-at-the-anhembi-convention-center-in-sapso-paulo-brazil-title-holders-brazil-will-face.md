### [2012-12-1](/news/2012/12/1/index.md)

# The draw for the 2013 FIFA Confederations Cup is held at the Anhembi Convention Center in Sao Paulo, Brazil. Title holders Brazil will face Japan, Mexico, Italy in Group A while World Champions Spain will play Uruguay, Tahiti and the 2013 African Champion in Group B.
