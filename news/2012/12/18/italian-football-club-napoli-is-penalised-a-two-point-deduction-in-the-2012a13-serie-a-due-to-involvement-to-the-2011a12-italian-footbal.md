# Italian football club Napoli is penalised a two-point deduction in the 2012-13 Serie A due to involvement to the 2011-12 Italian football scandal. 

Napoli have been docked two points in the Scommessopoli scandal, while Paolo Cannavaro and Giuanluca Grava were handed six-month bans
