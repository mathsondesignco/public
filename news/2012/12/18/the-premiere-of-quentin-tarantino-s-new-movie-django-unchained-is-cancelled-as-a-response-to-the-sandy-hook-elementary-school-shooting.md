### [2012-12-18](/news/2012/12/18/index.md)

# The premiere of Quentin Tarantino's new movie "Django Unchained" is cancelled as a response to the Sandy Hook Elementary School shooting. 

The decision comes following the Newtown, Conn., tragedy on Friday.
