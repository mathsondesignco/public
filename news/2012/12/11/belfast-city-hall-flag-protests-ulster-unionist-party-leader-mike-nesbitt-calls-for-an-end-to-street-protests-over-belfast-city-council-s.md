### [2012-12-11](/news/2012/12/11/index.md)

# Belfast City Hall flag protests:. Ulster Unionist Party leader Mike Nesbitt calls for an end to street protests over Belfast City Council's decision to restrict the flying of the union flag after loyalists threw a petrol bomb into a police vehicle in which a female officer was sitting at the time.
