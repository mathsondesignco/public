# Sir Desmond de Silva's report into the 1989 murder of Northern Ireland solicitor Pat Finucane is published, detailing "shocking state collusion" but, it says, absence of an "overarching conspiracy". 

A review into the murder of Belfast solicitor Pat Finucane in 1989 has found that actions by employees of the British state &quot;actively facilitated&quot; the killing.
