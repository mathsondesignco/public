# Virgin Trains is asked to continue running the rail franchise for UK's West Coast Main Line following the cancellation of a deal to award the contract to another company when errors were made in the way in which the process was conducted. 

Virgin Trains will be asked to continue to run services on the West Coast Mainline for at least another nine months following the decision to scrap the latest franchise decision.
