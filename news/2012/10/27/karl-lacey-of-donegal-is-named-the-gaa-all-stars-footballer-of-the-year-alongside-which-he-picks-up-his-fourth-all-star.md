# Karl Lacey of Donegal is named the GAA All Stars Footballer of the Year, alongside which he picks up his fourth All Star. 

Karl Lacey edges out Donegal team-mates Colm McFadden and Frank McGlynn to be named Gaelic Footballer of 2012.
