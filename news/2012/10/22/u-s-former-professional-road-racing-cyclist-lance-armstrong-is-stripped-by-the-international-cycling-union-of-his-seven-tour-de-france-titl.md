### [2012-10-22](/news/2012/10/22/index.md)

# U.S. former professional road racing cyclist Lance Armstrong is stripped by the International Cycling Union of his seven Tour de France titles and banned for life from participating in UCI-sanctioned events. 

The International Cycling Union (UCI) strips Lance Armstrong of his seven Tour de France titles and bans him for life.
