### [2012-10-28](/news/2012/10/28/index.md)

# In Formula One racing, Sebastian Vettel wins the 2012 Indian Grand Prix, with Fernando Alonso and Mark Webber in second and third place. 

Sebastian Vettel dominates the Indian GP to take a fourth win in a row and extend his title lead over Fernando Alonso.
