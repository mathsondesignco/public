### [2012-10-23](/news/2012/10/23/index.md)

# Sheikh Hamad bin Khalifa Al Thani, the Emir of Qatar, visits the Gaza Strip, the first head of state to visit there since Hamas seized control of the territory five years ago. 

Sheikh Hamad is the first head of state to visit embattled Palestinian enclave since international blockade imposed.
