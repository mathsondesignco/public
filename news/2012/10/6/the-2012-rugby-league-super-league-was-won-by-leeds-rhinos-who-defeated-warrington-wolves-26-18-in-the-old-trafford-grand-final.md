### [2012-10-6](/news/2012/10/6/index.md)

# The 2012 rugby league Super League was won by Leeds Rhinos who defeated Warrington Wolves 26-18 in the Old Trafford Grand Final. 

Defending Super League champions Leeds beat Warrington in the Grand Final to claim a record sixth title.
