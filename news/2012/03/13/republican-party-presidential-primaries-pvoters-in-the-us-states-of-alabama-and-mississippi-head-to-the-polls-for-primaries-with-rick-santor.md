### [2012-03-13](/news/2012/03/13/index.md)

# Republican Party presidential primaries:Voters in the US states of Alabama and Mississippi head to the polls for primaries with Rick Santorum winning in both states. 

Rick Santorum won the Alabama and Mississippi primaries, holding off stiff challenges from Newt Gingrich and Mitt Romney.
