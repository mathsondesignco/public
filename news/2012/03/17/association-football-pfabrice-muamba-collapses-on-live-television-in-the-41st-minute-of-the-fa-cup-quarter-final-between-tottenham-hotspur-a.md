### [2012-03-17](/news/2012/03/17/index.md)

# Association football:Fabrice Muamba collapses on live television in the 41st minute of the FA Cup quarter-final between Tottenham Hotspur and Bolton Wanderers. The match is abandoned, and Muamba is rushed to a London hospital in critical condition after being resuscitated for several minutes on the pitch. 

Bolton Wanderers midfielder Fabrice Muamba collapses on the pitch during an FA Cup quarter-final against Tottenham.
