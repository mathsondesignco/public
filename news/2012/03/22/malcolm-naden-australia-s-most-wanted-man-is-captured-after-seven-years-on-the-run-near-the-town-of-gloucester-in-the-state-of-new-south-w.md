### [2012-03-22](/news/2012/03/22/index.md)

# Malcolm Naden, Australia's most wanted man, is captured after seven years on the run near the town of Gloucester in the state of New South Wales and will face murder charges today. 

Murder suspect, charged over 2005 death of his cousin, apprehended by police after more than seven years on the run.
