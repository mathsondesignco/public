# 2011-2012 Syrian uprising:Turkish intelligence reports claim that approximately 20,000 Syrian soldiers have deserted from the government forces in less than a month. 

Turkey threatened to launch a military incursion into northern Syria after refugees fleeing alleged massacres by pro-Assad forces poured across its frontiers.
