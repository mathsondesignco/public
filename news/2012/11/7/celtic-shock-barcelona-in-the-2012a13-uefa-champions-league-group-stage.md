# Celtic shock Barcelona in the 2012-13 UEFA Champions League group stage. 

Celtic produced a stunning 2-1 victory over Barcelona in the Champions League on Wednesday, rewarded for a disciplined defensive display in Glasgow against the European heavyweight and making the most of their rare attacks.
