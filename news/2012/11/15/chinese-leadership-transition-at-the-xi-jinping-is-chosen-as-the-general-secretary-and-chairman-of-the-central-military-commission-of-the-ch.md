### [2012-11-15](/news/2012/11/15/index.md)

# Chinese leadership transition at the Xi Jinping is chosen as the General Secretary and Chairman of the Central Military Commission of the Chinese Communist Party and the next leader of the People's Republic of China. 

Xi Jinping is confirmed as the man to lead China for the next decade, as reformists miss out on membership of the top decision-making body.
