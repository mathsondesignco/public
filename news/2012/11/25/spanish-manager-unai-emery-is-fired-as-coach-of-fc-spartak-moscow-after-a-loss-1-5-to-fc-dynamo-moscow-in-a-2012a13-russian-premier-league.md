### [2012-11-25](/news/2012/11/25/index.md)

# Spanish manager Unai Emery is fired as coach of FC Spartak Moscow after a loss 1-5 to FC Dynamo Moscow in a 2012-13 Russian Premier League local derby and elimination from 2012-13 UEFA Champions League. 

Spartak Moscow have sacked coach Unai Emery after just six months following their thrashing at the hands of rivals Dinamo Moscow
