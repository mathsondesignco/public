# Harry Redknapp is appointed as the manager of Queens Park Rangers, taking over from Mark Hughes whose contract was terminated after fail to win any of their 12 Premier League games this season. 

Harry Redknapp is the new Queens Park Rangers manager after the club finally decided to sack Mark Hughes.
