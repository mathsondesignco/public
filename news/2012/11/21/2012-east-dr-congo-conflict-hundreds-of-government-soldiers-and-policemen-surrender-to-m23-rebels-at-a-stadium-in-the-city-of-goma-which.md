# 2012 East DR Congo conflict:. Hundreds of government soldiers and policemen surrender to M23 rebels at a stadium in the city of Goma, which fell to rebel forces on November 20. 

Thousands of soldiers and police surrender to M23 at a stadium in Goma after the group takes control of the city.
