# Kazakh opposition leader Vladimir Kozlov will face seven-and-a-half years in jail on charges relating to causing the 2011 Mangystau riots in Zhanaozen, Mangystau Province. 

Vladimir Kozlov will face seven-and-a-half years in jail on charges relating to causing unrest in December last year.
