# In the 2012 Davis Cup final Czech Republic defeats Spain 3-2 in Prague. Radek Stepanek wins a decisive fifth match against Nicolas Almagro. 

Radek Stepanek is the hero as the Czech Republic beat defending champions Spain 3-2 in the Davis Cup final in Prague.
