### [2012-11-29](/news/2012/11/29/index.md)

# Train services in the Channel Tunnel are suspended after a freight train fire on the French side. Everybody on the train is safely evacuated, traffic is resumed two hours after. 

Train services resume in the Channel Tunnel after a fire on a freight train on the French side caused traffic to be suspended.
