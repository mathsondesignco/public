### [2012-06-19](/news/2012/06/19/index.md)

# The Supreme Court of Pakistan disqualifies prime minister Yousaf Raza Gillani from holding office after Gillani was found guilty in a contempt-of-court case on April 26. 

Supreme court rules that current prime minister cannot hold office two months after convicting Gilani for contempt.
