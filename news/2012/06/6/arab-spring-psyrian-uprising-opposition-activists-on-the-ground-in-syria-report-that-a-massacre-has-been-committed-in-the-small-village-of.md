### [2012-06-6](/news/2012/06/6/index.md)

# Arab Spring:Syrian uprising: Opposition activists on the ground in Syria report that a massacre has been committed in the small village of Qubair, Hama, by the government-hired Shabiha militia. Activists report 78 dead, mostly women and children. 

Activists accuse Syrian government forces of massacring 78 in a village near Hama, condemned by the US as &quot;unconscionable&quot;.
