# Britain's Queen Elizabeth II and former IRA commander and Sinn Fein deputy First Minister of Northern Ireland Martin McGuinness shake hands at a historic meeting in Belfast. 

The Queen and former IRA commander Martin McGuinness shake hands for the first time, in what is being seen as a ground-breaking moment in Anglo-Irish history.
