### [2012-06-27](/news/2012/06/27/index.md)

# UEFA Euro 2012:Spain beat Portugal in a penalty shoot-out, with Fabregas as game winner, to go through to Sunday's final in Kiev. 

Cesc Fabregas is the hero again as Spain keep alive their hopes of defending their European title with a dramatic shootout win over Portugal.
