### [2012-06-28](/news/2012/06/28/index.md)

# At the 2012 Wimbledon Championships, World No. 100 Lukas Rosol upsets World No. 2 and two-time Wimbledon champion Rafael Nadal in five sets, to achieve one of the biggest upsets in Grand Slam history. 

Rafael Nadal was upset in the second round by Lukas Rosol, while Andy Murray, Andy Roddick and Brian Baker advanced to the third round at Wimbledon.
