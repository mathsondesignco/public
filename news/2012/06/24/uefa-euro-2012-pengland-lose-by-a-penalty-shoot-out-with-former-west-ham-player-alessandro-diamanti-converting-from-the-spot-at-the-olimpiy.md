### [2012-06-24](/news/2012/06/24/index.md)

# UEFA Euro 2012:England lose by a penalty shoot-out, with former West Ham player Alessandro Diamanti converting from the spot at the Olimpiyskiy National Sports Complex in Kiev to take Italy through to the semi-final against Germany.  

England are once again knocked out on penalties as the Azzuri make it through to the last four of Euro 2012.
