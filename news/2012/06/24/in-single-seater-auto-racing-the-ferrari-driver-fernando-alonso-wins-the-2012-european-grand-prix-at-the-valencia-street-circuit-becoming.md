### [2012-06-24](/news/2012/06/24/index.md)

# In single-seater auto racing, the Ferrari driver Fernando Alonso wins the 2012 European Grand Prix at the Valencia Street Circuit, becoming the first driver this season to win two races. 

Fernando Alonso becomes first driver of season to win two races as he reigns in front of home support at European GP.
