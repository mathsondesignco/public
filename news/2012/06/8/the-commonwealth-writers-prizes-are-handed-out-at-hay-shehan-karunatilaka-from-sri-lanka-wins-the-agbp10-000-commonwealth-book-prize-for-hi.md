### [2012-06-8](/news/2012/06/8/index.md)

# The Commonwealth Writers prizes are handed out at Hay: Shehan Karunatilaka from Sri Lanka wins the GBP10,000 Commonwealth Book Prize for his debut novel "Chinaman: The Legend of Pradeep Mathew", while Emma Martin from New Zealand wins the GBP5,000 Commonwealth Short Story Prize for "Two Girls in a Boat".
