### [2012-06-12](/news/2012/06/12/index.md)

# Voters in the 8th congressional district in the US state of Arizona go to the polls for a special election caused by the resignation of Gabrielle Giffords due to health reasons with Democrat Ron Barber duly elected. 

Democrat Ron Barber defeated Republican Jesse Kelly in an emotionally charged and nationally watched special election.
