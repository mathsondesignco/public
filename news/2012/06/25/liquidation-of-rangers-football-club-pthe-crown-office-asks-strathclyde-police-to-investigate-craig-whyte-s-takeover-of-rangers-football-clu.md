# Liquidation of Rangers Football Club:The Crown Office asks Strathclyde Police to investigate Craig Whyte's takeover of Rangers Football Club in May last year. 

A newco Rangers will be refused admittance to the Scottish Premier League, with six clubs publicly opposed to the move.
