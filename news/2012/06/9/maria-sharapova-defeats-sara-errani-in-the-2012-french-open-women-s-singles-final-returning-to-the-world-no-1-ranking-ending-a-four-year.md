### [2012-06-9](/news/2012/06/9/index.md)

# Maria Sharapova defeats Sara Errani in the 2012 French Open Women's Singles final, returning to the World No. 1 ranking, ending a four-year major title drought and completing a career Grand Slam. Sharapova had been as low as World No. 126 during the drought, having suffered a serious shoulder injury in 2008. 

Maria Sharapova beats Sara Errani 6-3, 6-2 to win the French Open and complete a career Grand Slam.
