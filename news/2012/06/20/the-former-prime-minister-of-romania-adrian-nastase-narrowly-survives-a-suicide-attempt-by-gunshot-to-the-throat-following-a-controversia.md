### [2012-06-20](/news/2012/06/20/index.md)

# The former Prime Minister of Romania, Adrian Nastase, narrowly survives a suicide attempt by gunshot to the throat following a controversial court decision sentencing him to two years in jail. 

Former Romanian Prime Minister Adrian Nastase shoots himself in the neck hours after a jail sentence against him on corruption charges is upheld.
