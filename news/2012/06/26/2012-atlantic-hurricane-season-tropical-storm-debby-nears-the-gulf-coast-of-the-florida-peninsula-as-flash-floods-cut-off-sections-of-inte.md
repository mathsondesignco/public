### [2012-06-26](/news/2012/06/26/index.md)

# 2012 Atlantic hurricane season:. Tropical Storm Debby nears the Gulf Coast of the Florida peninsula as flash floods cut off sections of Interstate 10 and Interstate 75 in the state. 

The National Hurricane Center says Debby has weakened to a tropical depression as it continues to move across Florida.
