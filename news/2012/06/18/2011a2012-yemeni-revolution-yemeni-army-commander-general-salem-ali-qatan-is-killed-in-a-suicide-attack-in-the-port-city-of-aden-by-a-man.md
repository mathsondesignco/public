# 2011-2012 Yemeni revolution: Yemeni Army commander General Salem Ali Qatan is killed in a suicide attack in the port city of Aden by a man wearing an explosives belt. 

The Yemeni army commander leading the fight against militants in the south of the country has been killed in a suicide attack, officials say.
