### [2012-09-20](/news/2012/09/20/index.md)

# Syrian civil war:. The Local Coordination Committees of Syria report that the Syrian army has bombed a gas station in the town of Ain Issa in northern Syria, killing at least 30 people and wounding dozens more. 

Syrian opposition activists say at least 30 people have been killed and dozens wounded.
