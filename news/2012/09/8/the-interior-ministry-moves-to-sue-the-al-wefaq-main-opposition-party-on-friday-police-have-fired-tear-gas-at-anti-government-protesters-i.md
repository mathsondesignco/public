### [2012-09-8](/news/2012/09/8/index.md)

# The Interior Ministry moves to sue the Al Wefaq main opposition party. On Friday, police have fired tear gas at anti-government protesters in the capital Manama. The protestors allegedly have defied a ban on demonstrations. 

Dozens of anti-government protesters defied ban on unauthorised demonstrations to call for prisoner releases in Manama.
