### [2012-09-11](/news/2012/09/11/index.md)

# Barcelona's streets are paralyzed as protesters peacefully demand independence on the National Day of Catalonia. Police say the demonstrators numbered 1.5 million; organizers estimate the numbers at 2 million. No incidences of violence are reported. The protest is the largest in Catalonia's history.
