### [2012-09-12](/news/2012/09/12/index.md)

# In his yearly State of the Union speech at the European Parliament, President of the European Commission Jose Manuel Barroso appeals for more European integration and a supervision mechanism for the 6,000 banks in Europe.  

A proposal to give the European Union more control over banking has stirred up dissent from countries that have doubts about the plan’s practicality.
