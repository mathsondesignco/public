### [2012-09-4](/news/2012/09/4/index.md)

# Beijing and Shanghai place orders for the world's longest bus, the 101|foot|m, five-axle, four-steering-axle, three-tiered ''AutoTram''. It carries 256 passengers and costs about $10 million a piece. Due to advanced electronics, it is said to be as maneuverable and precise as a conventional bus.
