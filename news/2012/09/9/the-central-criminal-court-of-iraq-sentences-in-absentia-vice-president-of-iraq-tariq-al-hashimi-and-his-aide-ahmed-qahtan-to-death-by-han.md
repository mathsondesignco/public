### [2012-09-9](/news/2012/09/9/index.md)

# The Central Criminal Court of Iraq sentences "in absentia" Vice president of Iraq Tariq al-Hashimi and his aide Ahmed Qahtan to death by hanging for, among other charges, the assassination of a lawyer and that of an Army general.
