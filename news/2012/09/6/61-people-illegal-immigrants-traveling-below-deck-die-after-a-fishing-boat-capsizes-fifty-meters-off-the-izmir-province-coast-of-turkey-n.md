### [2012-09-6](/news/2012/09/6/index.md)

# 61 people, illegal immigrants traveling below deck, die after a fishing boat capsizes fifty meters off the Izmir Province coast of Turkey, near the Greek Aegean islands. 

A boat carrying migrants capsizes off the coast of western Turkey killing at least 60 people, about half of them said to be children.
