### [2012-09-30](/news/2012/09/30/index.md)

# In hurling, Kilkenny defeats Galway in the 2012 All-Ireland Senior Hurling Championship Final replay at Croke Park. Hurling legend Henry Shefflin breaks another record, becoming the first Gaelic athlete to win nine All-Ireland Senior Championship medals on the field of play.
