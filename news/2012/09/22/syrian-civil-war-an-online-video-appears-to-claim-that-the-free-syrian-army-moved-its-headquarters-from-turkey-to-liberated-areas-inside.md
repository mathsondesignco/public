# Syrian civil war:: An online video appears to claim that the Free Syrian Army moved its headquarters from Turkey to "liberated areas" inside Syria; Reuters says in Idlib or Aleppo. 

The rebel Free Syrian Army announces it has moved its command centre from Turkey to &quot;liberated areas&quot; inside Syria.
