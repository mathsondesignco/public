# Apple Inc. announces that the technology generated over $127 billion in revenue during 2011. Apple sold over 37 million iPhones and over 15 million iPads during the fourth quarter of 2011. 

Apple records $46.3 billion in sales during the past quarter, which is the best total ever for a technology company.
