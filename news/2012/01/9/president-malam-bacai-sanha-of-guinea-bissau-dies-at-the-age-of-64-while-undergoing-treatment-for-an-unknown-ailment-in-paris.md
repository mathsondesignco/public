# President Malam Bacai Sanh of Guinea-Bissau dies at the age of 64 while undergoing treatment for an unknown ailment in Paris. 

The president of the West African country of Guinea-Bissau, Malam Bacai Sanha, dies in a French hospital at the age of 64.
