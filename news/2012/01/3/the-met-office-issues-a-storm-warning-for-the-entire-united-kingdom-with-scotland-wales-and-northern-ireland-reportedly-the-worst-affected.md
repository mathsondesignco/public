### [2012-01-3](/news/2012/01/3/index.md)

# The Met Office issues a storm warning for the entire United Kingdom, with Scotland, Wales and Northern Ireland reportedly the worst-affected areas. Two people are later reported killed in separate weather-related incidents. 

Two men are killed as storms cause damage and result in power cuts across many parts of the UK, with gusts of up to 100mph in Scotland.
