### [2002-08-12](/news/2002/08/12/index.md)

#  Extreme weather: The death toll in Europe caused by flooding has risen to at least 74, with 58 deaths in Russia, 3 in Germany, 3 in Austria, and one in the Czech Republic. Prime Minister Vladimir Spidla declared a state of emergency in Prague, Bohemia, Plzen and Karlovy Vary. All shipping on the Danube has been halted. Premier Silvio Berlusconi approved {{Nowrap|$50 million}} in emergency aid in response to the {{Nowrap|$300 million}} in damage of northern Italy's crops.
