### [2002-10-22](/news/2002/10/22/index.md)

#  Canadian author Yann Martel won the Booker Prize for his "quirky fable" "Life of Pi". The prize is worth GBP50,000 ($77,300). Martel's work was picked from 130 novels from Britain, Ireland.
