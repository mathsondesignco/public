## [2002-03-31](/news/2002/03/31/index.md)

### [Israeli-Palestinian conflict: Israeli troops exchange gunfire with guards of Yasir Arafat in Ramallah. A suicide bomber identified as Shadi Tubasi, a resident of the refugee camp Jenin, kills 14 and wounds more than 40 in Haifa. Later, a suicide bomber wounds four members of an intensive-care unit, one critically, in a paramedics' dispatch station in Efrat. In the past 18 months, according to the Associated Press, 1262 people have been killed on the Palestinian side and on 401 on the Israeli side.](/news/2002/03/31/israeli-palestinian-conflict-israeli-troops-exchange-gunfire-with-guards-of-yasir-arafat-in-ramallah-a-suicide-bomber-identified-as-shadi.md)
