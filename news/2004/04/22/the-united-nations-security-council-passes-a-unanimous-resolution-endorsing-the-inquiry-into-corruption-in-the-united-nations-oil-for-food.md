### [2004-04-22](/news/2004/04/22/index.md)

#  The United Nations Security Council passes a unanimous resolution endorsing the inquiry into corruption in the United Nations Oil for food program for Iraq calling upon all 191 member states to cooperate. 

Secretary General Kofi Annan said today that U.N. critics were treating unproven charges as facts and ignoring the positive aspects of the Iraqi oil-for-food program.
