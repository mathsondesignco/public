### [2004-04-5](/news/2004/04/5/index.md)

#  Swedish business weekly "Veckans Affarer" argues that Ingvar Kamprad, a Swede who founded the furniture retail chain store IKEA, has overtaken Microsoft's Bill Gates as the world's richest person. IKEA disagrees, stating that he does not personally own the company.
