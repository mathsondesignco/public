### [2004-04-28](/news/2004/04/28/index.md)

#  Intense fighting breaks out in Fallujah as U.S. forces respond to attacks on their positions by insurgentss. Artillery and AC-130 gunships are used to bombard guerrilla positions, but the number of casualties is as yet unknown.
