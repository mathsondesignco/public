### [2004-11-16](/news/2004/11/16/index.md)

#  A possible war crime by a U.S. Marine in Fallujah, Iraq, is caught on film by an NBC camera crew. The marine shot and killed an apparently unarmed Iraqi while in a mosque. 

Pool correspondent Kevin Sites of NBC News videotapes United States marine shooting and killing apparently unarmed Iraqi prisoner in mosque, Falluja; says three other previously wounded prisoners in mosque also had apparently been shot again by marines; Marine Corp probes incident; Sites was embedded with one group of marines when second unit, which had previously come under fire from mosque, stormed building, killing 10 men and wounding 5 others; says marine in same unit had been killed earlier tending to booby-trapped dead body of insurgent; photo (M)
