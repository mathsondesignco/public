### [2004-01-30](/news/2004/01/30/index.md)

#  Former French Prime Minister and current Mayor of Bordeaux Alain Jupp is convicted of a party funding scam in the 1980s and early 1990s, and is given an 18-month suspended jail sentence and disqualified from elected office for 10 years, although he retains his mayoralty pending his appeal. He had been viewed as Jacques Chirac's likely successor in the 2007 Presidential election.
