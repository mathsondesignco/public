### [2004-12-12](/news/2004/12/12/index.md)

#  At least five Israeli soldiers are killed and 10 are injured as a tunnel rigged with 1500&nbsp;kg of explosives exploded near the Rafah crossing between Rafah and Egypt. Hamas and an al-Aqsa Martyrs' Brigades' offshoot called "Fatah Hawk" claimed joint responsibility.
