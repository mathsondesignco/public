### [2004-12-8](/news/2004/12/8/index.md)

#  Civil unions in New Zealand: Parliament passes civil union legislation by 65 votes to 55. The new law provides a way for "de facto" couples, including same-sex couples, to gain legal recognition of their relationships, but stops short of same-sex marriage.
