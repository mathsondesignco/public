### [2004-12-15](/news/2004/12/15/index.md)

#  Sprint Corporation announces a US$35 billion deal to acquire Nextel Communications. With about $40 billion in combined yearly revenue the resulting company (called Sprint Nextel) will be the third largest wireless telephone service provider in the U.S.. 

Nextel Communications Inc. and Sprint Corp. &#160;have agreed to merge in a deal that would create the nation&#8217;s third largest wireless telephone provider
