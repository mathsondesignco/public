### [2004-12-1](/news/2004/12/1/index.md)

# 2004 Ukrainian presidential election. The Verkhovna Rada (parliament of Ukraine) passes a vote of no confidence to dismiss Viktor Yanukovich as Prime Minister. The opposition led by Viktor Yushchenko agrees to continue negotiations and end the blockade of official buildings.
