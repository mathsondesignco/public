### [2004-06-22](/news/2004/06/22/index.md)

#  Francisco Ortiz Franco, editor of Mexican newsweekly "Zeta," is ambushed and killed by gunmen in Tijuana. Ortiz Franco and "Zeta" were particularly well known for their work in investigating drug trafficking and reporting government corruption.
