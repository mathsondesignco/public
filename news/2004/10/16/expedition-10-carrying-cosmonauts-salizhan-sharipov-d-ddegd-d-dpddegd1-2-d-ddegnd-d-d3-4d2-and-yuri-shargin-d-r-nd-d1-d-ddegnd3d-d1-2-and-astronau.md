### [2004-10-16](/news/2004/10/16/index.md)

#  Expedition 10, carrying cosmonauts Salizhan Sharipov (Salizhan Sharipov) and Yuri Shargin (Iurii Shargin), and astronaut Leroy Chiao, docks successfully at the International Space Station. Chiao and Sharipov will be relieving Mike Fincke and Gennady Padalka (Gennadi Padalka) from Expedition 9, and will spend six months aboard the station.
