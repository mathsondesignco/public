### [2004-07-24](/news/2004/07/24/index.md)

#  In Mexico Judge Csar Flores refuses to authorize an arrest warrant for former president Luis Echeverra and other officials under the accusations of genocide for the killing of students during the "dirty war". Prosecutors are expected to appeal the decision.
