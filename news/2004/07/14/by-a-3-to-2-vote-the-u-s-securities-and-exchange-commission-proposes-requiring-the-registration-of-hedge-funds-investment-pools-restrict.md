### [2004-07-14](/news/2004/07/14/index.md)

#  By a 3-to-2 vote, the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission proposes requiring the registration of hedge funds (investment pools restricted to high-net-worth individuals and institutions). Although many hedge funds are already registered, that has thus far been voluntary.
