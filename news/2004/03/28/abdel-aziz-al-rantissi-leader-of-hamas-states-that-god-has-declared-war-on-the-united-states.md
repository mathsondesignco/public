### [2004-03-28](/news/2004/03/28/index.md)

#  Abdel Aziz al-Rantissi, leader of Hamas, states that God has declared war on the United States. 

The new Hamas leader in the Gaza Strip, Dr. Abdel Aziz Rantisi, called President Bush the enemy of Muslims.
