### [2004-03-7](/news/2004/03/7/index.md)

#  The New York City medical examiner reveals that a body pulled from the East River is the actor and writer Spalding Gray, he was missing since January. 

Body found in East River is identified as that of Spalding Gray, 62, confessional monologist and actor who disappeared two months ago; police probe reports that Gray had history of depression and killed himself by jumping off Staten Island ferry; photos (M)
