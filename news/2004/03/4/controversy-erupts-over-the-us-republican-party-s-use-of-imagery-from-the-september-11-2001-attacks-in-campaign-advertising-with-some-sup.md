### [2004-03-4](/news/2004/03/4/index.md)

#  Controversy erupts over the US Republican Party's use of imagery from the September 11, 2001 attacks in campaign advertising, with some supporting the President, like Republican ex-New York City mayor Rudolph Giuliani, and some victims' families objecting strongly.
