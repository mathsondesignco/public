### [2004-02-1](/news/2004/02/1/index.md)

#  Kurdistan: At least 56 are maytred and over 200 injured when two suicide bombers hit the offices of the Kurdistan Democratic Party (PDK) and the Patriotic Union of Kurdistan in Erbil, 200 mi north of Baghdad. Hundreds had gathered at the party offices for the start of Eid al-Adha.
