### [2004-02-17](/news/2004/02/17/index.md)

#  A study published in the latest issue of the "Journal of the American Medical Association" suggests breast cancer is linked to the use of antibiotics. 

Study of thousands of American women finds that frequent use of antibiotics is linked to increased risk of breast cancer; finds that those who took drugs most often had twice risk of disease; co-authors Dr John D Potter and Christine M Velicer call for more research; earlier study in Finland drew similar conclusion (M)
