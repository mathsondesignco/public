### [2004-05-10](/news/2004/05/10/index.md)

#  The United States Armed Forces destroy the Baghdad headquarters of Moqtada al-Sadr. The building had been evacuated by al-Sadr's forces. There were no casualties. 

The move came hours after U.S. troops killed at least 19 of his militiamen in clashes in the Sadr City slum district.
