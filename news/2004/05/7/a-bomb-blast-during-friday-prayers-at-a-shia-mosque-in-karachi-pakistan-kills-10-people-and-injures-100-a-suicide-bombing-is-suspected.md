### [2004-05-7](/news/2004/05/7/index.md)

#  A bomb blast during Friday prayers at a Shia mosque in Karachi, Pakistan, kills 10 people and injures 100. A suicide bombing is suspected. The head cleric of the mosque is among the dead. 

After the blast, angry groups of young men roamed the city, setting fire to a gas station and destroying cars.
