### [2004-05-25](/news/2004/05/25/index.md)

#  Tennis: At the French Open, a new world record for the longest match in the sport's recorded history is set when Frenchman Fabrice Santoro beats Arnaud Clment 6-4, 6-3, 6-7 (5), 3-6, 16-14 after playing for 6 hours and 33 minutes, split over two days. 

Fabrice Santoro and Arnaud Clement finished the longest match in the history of tennis Tuesday.
