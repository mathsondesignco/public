### [2003-11-27](/news/2003/11/27/index.md)

#  Peruvian police clash with campesinos in the town of Carhuamayo (department of Junin), leaving two dead and more than 20 people injured, during a protest against mining pollution. Strikers are demanding the government hand over $58 million from the privatization of a state electricity company for the cleanup.
