### [ The Diana, Princess of Wales Memorial Fund announces that on legal advice it has frozen its funds as it faces a demand for GBP15&nbsp;million ($25&nbsp;million) damages for alleged "malicious prosecution" from the "Franklin Mint" in the US. The Mint had won a courtcase over its right to manufacture a Diana, Princess of Wales lookalike doll. Hundreds of charities are expected face financial difficulties as a result of the freeze.
