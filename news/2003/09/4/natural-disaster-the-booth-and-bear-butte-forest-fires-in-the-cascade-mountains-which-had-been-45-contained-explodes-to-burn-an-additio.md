### [2003-09-4](/news/2003/09/4/index.md)

#  Natural disaster: The Booth and Bear Butte forest fires in the Cascade Mountains, which had been 45% contained, explodes to burn an additional 20,000 acres (80&nbsp;km<sup>2</sup>). Estimates of the size of this fire vary between 62,000 and 80,000 acres (250 and 320&nbsp;km<sup>2</sup>). The resort community of Camp Sherman, where authorities allowed residents to return, is once again evacuated.
