### [ Ain es Saheb airstrike: Israeli warplanes attack an alleged Islamic Jihad training base deep in Syria in retaliation for a suicide bombing at a Haifa restaurant that killed 19 people, the army said Sunday. Israeli media state this is the first Israeli attack on Syrian soil in more than two decades. An emergency session of the UN Security Council is scheduled to debate the action. France and Germany condemn the attack. The international community calls for restraint by all parties involved.](/news/2003/10/5/ain-es-saheb-airstrike-israeli-warplanes-attack-an-alleged-islamic-jihad-training-base-deep-in-syria-in-retaliation-for-a-suicide-bombing.md)
