### [2003-10-2](/news/2003/10/2/index.md)

#  European Union: The first bilateral meeting between France and Austria since 1998 is marked by disagreements over the European Constitution. Austria heads the group of small countries seeking to make changes to the text, but France wants to leave the draft by Valery Giscard d'Estaing broadly untouched.
