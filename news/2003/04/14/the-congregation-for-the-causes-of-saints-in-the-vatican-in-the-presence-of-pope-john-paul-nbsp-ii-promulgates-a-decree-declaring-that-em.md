### [2003-04-14](/news/2003/04/14/index.md)

#  The Congregation for the Causes of Saints in the Vatican, in the presence of Pope John Paul&nbsp;II, promulgates a decree declaring that Emperor-King Karl of Austria-Hungary (r: 1916-1918) possessed "heroic virtues". This decree marks a significant step towards canonisation in the Roman Catholic Church for the last Austrian emperor and king of Hungary.
