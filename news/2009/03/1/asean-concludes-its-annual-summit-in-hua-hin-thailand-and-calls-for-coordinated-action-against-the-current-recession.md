### [2009-03-1](/news/2009/03/1/index.md)

#  ASEAN concludes its annual summit in Hua Hin, Thailand, and calls for coordinated action against the current recession. 

Southeast Asianleaders called on Sunday for coordinated policies and jointactions at the regional level to deal with a worsening globalfinancial crisis that is battering their export-dependenteconomies, but did not spell out any specific actions the groupwould take.
