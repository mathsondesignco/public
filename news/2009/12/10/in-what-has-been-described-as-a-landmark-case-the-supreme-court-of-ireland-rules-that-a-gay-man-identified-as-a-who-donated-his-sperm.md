### [2009-12-10](/news/2009/12/10/index.md)

#  In what has been described as a landmark case, the Supreme Court of Ireland rules that a gay man, identified as "A", who donated his sperm to a lesbian couple shall have access to the resulting boy child, overturning the original High Court decision. GLEN expresses concern at the Supreme Court's rejection of the lesbian couple as a "de facto family". 

The Supreme Court has ruled the gay friend of a lesbian couple who donated his sperm to one of them so she could have a child&hellip;
