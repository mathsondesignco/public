### [2009-12-9](/news/2009/12/9/index.md)

#  The Irish Bishops Conference apologises for the sexual abuse suffered by its children after spending the first day of its winter conference examining the Murphy Report. 

The Irish Bishops&#39; Conference has issued an apology to all those who were abused by priests.
