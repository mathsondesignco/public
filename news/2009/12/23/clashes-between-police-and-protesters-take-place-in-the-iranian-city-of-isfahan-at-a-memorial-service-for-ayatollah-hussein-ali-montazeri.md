### [2009-12-23](/news/2009/12/23/index.md)

#  Clashes between police and protesters take place in the Iranian city of Isfahan at a memorial service for Ayatollah Hussein-Ali Montazeri. 

Incidents occur as crowds in Isfahan mourn death of dissident Hossein Ali Montazeri.
