### [2009-12-29](/news/2009/12/29/index.md)

#  Iran's Nobel Peace Prize winner Shirin Ebadi says her sister Nooshin, a medical professor and human rights activist, was arrested by authorities the previous evening. Sources indicate journalists have also been detained. 

The sister of Nobel laureate Shirin Ebadi has been detained by Iranian intelligence officials, the Iranian lawyer and human rights activist said.
