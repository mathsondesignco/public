### [2009-12-18](/news/2009/12/18/index.md)

#  A Paris court rules that Google is infringing copyright, sentencing it to pay 300,000&nbsp;euros in damages and interest to French publisher Editions de la Martinire, and 10,000&nbsp;euros a day until it removes extracts of the books from its database.
