### [2009-09-21](/news/2009/09/21/index.md)

#  Rescuers scour the sea off the Morocco coast for dozens of African migrants who are missing and feared dead after their boat capsized in an accident that kills eight others. 

At least eight people drowned when a small boat carrying dozens of migrants from sub-Saharan Africa sank off the coast of Morocco.
