### [2009-09-22](/news/2009/09/22/index.md)

#  Ireland's National Ploughing Championships, the largest ploughing championship in the world and the largest outdoor agricultural event in Europe, begins in Athy, County Kildare. 

An estimated 49,000 people attended the National Ploughing Championship today.
