### [2009-09-8](/news/2009/09/8/index.md)

#  Sudanese journalist Lubna al-Hussein is freed from one day in jail after being prosecuted for wearing trousers &ndash; the fine was paid by the Journalist Union. 

Journalist union pays $200 fine to release woman jailed for indecency.
