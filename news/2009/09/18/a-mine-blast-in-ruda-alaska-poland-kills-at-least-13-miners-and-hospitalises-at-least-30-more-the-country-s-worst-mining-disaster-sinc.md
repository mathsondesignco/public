### [2009-09-18](/news/2009/09/18/index.md)

#  A mine blast in Ruda Slaska, Poland, kills at least 13 miners and hospitalises at least 30 more. The country's worst mining disaster since 2006, two days of national mourning are declared the following day. 

At least 12 miners were killed today in a methane explosion at a coal mine in southern Poland, the government said.
