### [2009-09-18](/news/2009/09/18/index.md)

#  Two people are killed and a further 12 injured in a stabbing attack in Beijing. 

A man stabbed two security guards to death with a knife and wounded 12 other people in central Beijing, despite increased security ahead of the 60th anniversary of Communist rule in China.
