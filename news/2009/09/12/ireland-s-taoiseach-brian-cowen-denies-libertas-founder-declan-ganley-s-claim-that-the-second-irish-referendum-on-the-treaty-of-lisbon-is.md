#  Ireland's Taoiseach Brian Cowen denies Libertas founder Declan Ganley's claim that the second Irish referendum on the Treaty of Lisbon is "profoundly undemocratic". 

The Taoiseach has rejected a claim that the holding of a second referendum on the Lisbon treaty is profoundly undemocratic.
