### [2009-07-26](/news/2009/07/26/index.md)

#  Seven people are killed in hurricane-force winds reaching 130 km-h in Poland and two more are killed by wind in the Czech Republic. Hail falls on 60,000 hectares of crops in Austria, causing damage of at least EUR20 million. 

EXTREME weather has killed and injured dozens of people across Europe, with Spain, France and Italy hit by summer wildfires and parts of central Europe deluged by storms.
