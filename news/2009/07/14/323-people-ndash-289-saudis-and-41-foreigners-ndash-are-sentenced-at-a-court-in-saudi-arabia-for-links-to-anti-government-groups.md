### [2009-07-14](/news/2009/07/14/index.md)

#  323 people &ndash; 289 Saudis and 41 foreigners &ndash; are sentenced at a court in Saudi Arabia for links to anti-government groups. 

First publicly reported trials since the 2003 attacks involved suspected al-Qaeda members.
