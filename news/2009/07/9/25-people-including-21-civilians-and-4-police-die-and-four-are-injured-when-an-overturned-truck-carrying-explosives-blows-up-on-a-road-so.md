#  25 people, including 21 civilians and 4 police, die and four are injured when an overturned truck carrying explosives blows up on a road south of Kabul, Afghanistan. 

An overturned truck carrying explosives has blown up on a road south of Kabul in Afghanistan, killing 25 people.
