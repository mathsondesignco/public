### [2009-08-6](/news/2009/08/6/index.md)

#  Micro-blogging website Twitter is knocked offline by a distributed denial-of-service attack targetted at a Georgian blogger. 

Twitter and Facebook suffered service problems today, raising speculation that they had come under a pre-planned coordinated attack by hackers.
