### [2009-08-22](/news/2009/08/22/index.md)

#  Cardinal Sean Brady, Archbishop of Armagh and Primate of All Ireland, criticises Ireland's Civil Partnership Bill, which gives statutory partnership rights to same-sex couples. 

The legislation which gives statutory partnership rights to gay and lesbian couples has been criticised by Cardinal Sean Brady.
