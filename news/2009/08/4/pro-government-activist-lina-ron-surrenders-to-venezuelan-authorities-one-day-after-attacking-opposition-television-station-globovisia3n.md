### [2009-08-4](/news/2009/08/4/index.md)

#  Pro-government activist, Lina Ron, surrenders to Venezuelan authorities one day after attacking opposition television station Globovision. President Hugo Chavez condemns the attack, saying it helps his opponents brand him as a tyrant.
