#  "MS Oasis of the Seas", the world's largest cruise ship, is officially unveiled at Fort Lauderdale, Florida, live on an American morning television show. 

The world&#039;s biggest cruise ship - boasting its own open air park and amphitheatre - was officially unveiled today.
