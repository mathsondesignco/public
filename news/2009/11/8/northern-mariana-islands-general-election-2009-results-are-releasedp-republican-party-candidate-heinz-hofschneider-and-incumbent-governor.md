### [2009-11-8](/news/2009/11/8/index.md)

#  Northern Mariana Islands general election, 2009 results are released Republican Party candidate Heinz Hofschneider and incumbent Governor Benigno Fitial virtually tie in the closest gubernatorial election in history. A runoff election will be held in the next 14 days to determine the winner.
