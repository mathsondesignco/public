### [2009-11-30](/news/2009/11/30/index.md)

#  A court in Argentina blocks Latin America's first gay marriage, overturning an earlier ruling, saying the Supreme Court should decide on its constitutionality. 

The wedding had been authorized by Judge Gabriela Seijas
