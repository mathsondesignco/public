### [2009-11-30](/news/2009/11/30/index.md)

#  On St. Andrew's Day, the Scottish Government concludes the National Conversation into Scotland's constitutional future by publishing a white paper for their proposed referendum on independence. 

The SNP set out its vision for a referendum on Scottish independence today.
