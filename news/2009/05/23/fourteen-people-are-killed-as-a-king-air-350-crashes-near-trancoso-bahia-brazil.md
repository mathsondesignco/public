### [2009-05-23](/news/2009/05/23/index.md)

#  Fourteen people are killed as a King Air 350 crashes near Trancoso, Bahia, Brazil. 

Authorities say 14 people were killed in the crash of a small executive jet at a luxury beach resort in north-eastern Brazil, raising the death toll from 11.
