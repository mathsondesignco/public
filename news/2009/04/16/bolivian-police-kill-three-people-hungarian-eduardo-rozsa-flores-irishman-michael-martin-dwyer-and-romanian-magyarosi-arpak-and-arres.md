### [2009-04-16](/news/2009/04/16/index.md)

#  Bolivian police kill three people - Hungarian Eduardo Rzsa-Flores, Irishman Michael Martin Dwyer, and Romanian Magyarosi Arpak - and arrest two others over an alleged plot to assassinate President Evo Morales and Vice President lvaro Garca Linera.
