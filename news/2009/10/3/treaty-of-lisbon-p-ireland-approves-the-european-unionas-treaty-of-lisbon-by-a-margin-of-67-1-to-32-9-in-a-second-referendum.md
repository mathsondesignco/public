### [2009-10-3](/news/2009/10/3/index.md)

#  Treaty of Lisbon: Ireland approves the European Union's Treaty of Lisbon by a margin of 67.1% to 32.9% in a second referendum. 

Opposition campaigners concede defeat as results of referendum start to come in.
