### [2009-10-18](/news/2009/10/18/index.md)

#  Two foreign aid workers Sharon Commins and Hilda Kawuki &ndash; one Irish and one Ugandan &ndash; working for the GOAL charity captured in Sudan's Darfur region more than three months ago are released. 

GOAL aid worker Sharon Commins has thanked the people of Sudan for their work to secure her release and that of her Ugandan colleague Hilda Kawuki.
