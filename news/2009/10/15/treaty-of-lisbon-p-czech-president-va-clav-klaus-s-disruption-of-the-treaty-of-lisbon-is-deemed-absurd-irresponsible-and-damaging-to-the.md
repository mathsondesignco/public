### [2009-10-15](/news/2009/10/15/index.md)

#  Treaty of Lisbon: Czech President Vaclav Klaus's disruption of the Treaty of Lisbon is deemed "absurd, irresponsible and damaging to the country" by his predecessor Vaclav Havel. 

The President has signed the bill reconciling the Lisbon Treaty with the Constitution.
