### [2009-01-28](/news/2009/01/28/index.md)

#  Winter storms across the Midwestern United States have killed 19 people and cut electricity to 600,000 homes and businesses from Oklahoma to West Virginia. 

A large winter storm has left 23 people dead as snow and ice cover 1,400 miles.
