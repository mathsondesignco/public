### [2009-06-10](/news/2009/06/10/index.md)

#  Google Inc. announced that it has received notice from the United States Department of Justice that antitrust investigators are studying its settlement with publishers designed to put millions of books online consistent with the property rights of authors. 

Google Inc (GOOG.O) hasreceived formal notice from the U.S. Justice Department thatantitrust investigators are looking into its settlement withpublishers that would help make millions of books availableonline.
